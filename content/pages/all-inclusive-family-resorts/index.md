## The best beaches for families

There are so many great beaches for families around the world, it can be hard to choose just one! But whether you’re looking for a quiet spot to relax with your little ones, or a bustling beach town with plenty of activities for everyone, we’ve got you covered.

Here are some of our top picks for the best beaches for families:

###### Clearwater Beach, Florida

![Clearwater Beach, Florida](/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/robert-linder-75h5bEIItRw-unsplash.jpg)

This sunny spot on Florida’s Gulf Coast is perfect for families who want to enjoy the beach without venturing too far from home. With its sugar-sand beaches and crystal-clear water, Clearwater Beach is a top choice for families. Plus, there’s plenty to keep everyone entertained, with a lively boardwalk, countless shops and restaurants, and even a marine aquarium.

###### Corpus Christi, Texas

![Corpus Christi, Texas](/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/joshua-j-cotten-qytJUmJezz0-unsplash.jpg)

Corpus Christi is another terrific option for families looking to enjoy the sunny Gulf Coast. The city’s wide range of beaches offer something for everyone, from calm bayside beaches perfect for swimming and kayaking to waves ideal for surfing and boogie boarding. And don’t forget about the must-see Texas State Aquarium and USS Lexington Museum on the Bay!

###### Lahaina, Maui

![Lahaina, Maui](/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/jordan-crawford-xNFOATm-PW4-unsplash.jpg)

Maui is often called paradise for good reason—and Lahaina is one of its jewels. This historic whaling town offers beautiful golden sand beaches flanked by coral reefs, perfect for snorkeling or simply enjoying the views. Plus, there’s no shortage of activities to keep everyone happy, from shopping and dining in charming Front Street shops to taking a sunset cruise or learning about Hawaiian culture at one of Lahaina’s many museums.

Whether you're looking for a beachfront property in the Caribbean or an action-packed resort in Mexico, we've rounded up the best all-inclusive resorts for families.

## The best all-inclusive family resorts

Whether you're looking for a resort with a water park, family-friendly activities, or luxurious amenities, there's an all-inclusive resort that's perfect for your next family vacation.

Here are some of the best all-inclusive family resorts:

1\. Beaches Turks & Caicos Resort Villages & Spa

![Turks & Caicos](/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/angela-smart-ax9rcpYAe74-unsplash.jpg)

With 12 miles of white sand beaches, this resort is a paradise for beach lovers. The property has 21 restaurants, 18 bars, a water park, and a kids' club. Families can also enjoy daily activities such as snorkeling, kayaking, and windsurfing.

2\. Club Med Punta Cana

![Club Med Punta Cana](/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/danny-de-groot-45LNRNIzs30-unsplash.jpg)

This resort is located on a pristine stretch of beach in Punta Cana. It features an on-site water park, Kids' ClubMed programs for children of all ages, and unique dining experiences. Families can also enjoy plenty of activities such as tennis, archery, and flying trapeze.

3\. The Westin Playa Bonita Panama

![Westin Playa Bonita Panama](/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/polina-kuzovkova-KQAR3zlWRkU-unsplash.jpg)

This oceanfront resort is located just 30 minutes from Panama City. It features three infinity pools overlooking the Pacific Ocean, two kids' clubs, and six restaurants. Families can also enjoy plenty of beach activities such as kayaking, stand-up paddle boarding, and boogie boarding.
