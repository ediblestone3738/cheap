## The top 5 things to do at the Disney Caribbean Beach Resort

If you’re looking for a fun and relaxing vacation, look no further than the Disney Caribbean Beach Resort. This beautiful resort is located in the heart of the Walt Disney World® Resort, and it’s the perfect place to stay if you want to be close to all the action. Here are the top five things to do at the Disney Caribbean Beach Resort:

1\. Relax by the pool.
The pool at the Disney Caribbean Beach Resort is truly a oasis. With its white sand beaches, palm trees and crystal-clear water, you’ll feel like you’re in the tropics. There are plenty of lounge chairs and cabanas around the pool so you can relax in comfort while you soak up the Florida sun.

2\. Discover new dining options.
The Disney Caribbean Beach Resort is home to several great restaurants, including Sebastian’s Bistro, Old Port Royale Food Court and Centertown Market. Sebastian’s Bistro is a table-service restaurant that serves up fresh seafood dishes inspired by the Caribbean. The Old Port Royale Food Court offers quick-service dining options like burgers, pizzas and sandwiches. And at Centertown Market, you can find street food from different cultures from around the world.

3\. Enjoy a drink at Banana Cabana Pool Bar & Grill.
Banana Cabana Pool Bar & Grill is a poolside bar that serves up refreshing cocktails, beer and wine as well as light snacks like flatbreads and nachos. It’s the perfect place to relax with a drink in hand while you enjoy the tropical surroundings.

4\. Go for a swim in Barefoot Bay pool complex.
Barefoot Bay is an interactive water play area that features two zero-depth entry pools, fountains, spray jets, water cannons and more! It’s perfect for both children and adults who want to cool off on a hot day.

5\. Rent a boat or go fishing at Barefoot Bay Marina.
If you want to explore Barefoot Bay beyond the resort grounds, you can rent a boat or go fishing at Barefoot Bay Marina. They offer pontoon boats, kayaks and more for rental, and they also have fishing equipment available for rent if you want to go fishing offshore
