Enjoying the Nightlife of Boston: Where to Find the City's Hottest Spots

Boston is known for its rich history, but the city also has a vibrant nightlife scene. Whether you're looking for craft cocktails, live music, dancing, or just a good time with friends, Boston has something for everyone. In this article, we'll explore the city's hotspots and help you plan a night out you'll never forget.

Best bars in Boston:

1\. The Hawthorne - located in the Hotel Commonwealth, this bar offers sophisticated cocktails made with fresh ingredients.
2\. Drink - this speakeasy-style bar has a no-menu policy and instead offers customized drinks based on your preferences.
3\. No. 9 Park - not only does this bar have a great cocktail list, but it also has a fantastic wine selection.
4\. The Beehive - this bohemian style bar offers live jazz music and eclectic international cuisine.
5\. The Last Hurrah - located in the Omni Parker House Hotel, this classic bar offers classic cocktails in a historic setting.

Best clubs in Boston:

1\. Venu - known for its large dance floor and VIP sections, Venu is the place to go for a night of dancing.
2\. Royale - this massive club has multiple levels and offers top DJs and live performances.
3\. Icon - located in the Theatre District, Icon offers a luxurious experience with its VIP tables and extensive bar menu.
4\. Bijou - this upscale club is known for its stylish decor and trendy crowd.
5\. The Grand - located in the Seaport District, The Grand offers three levels of partying with its multiple bars and dance floors.

Best live music venues in Boston:

1\. House of Blues - this popular venue offers a mix of national and local acts, ranging from rock to hip hop.
2\. Paradise Rock Club - this intimate venue hosts up and coming acts and offers a great view from anywhere in the house.
3\. The Sinclair - located in Cambridge, The Sinclair offers a diverse range of music from indie to electronic.
4\. Brighton Music Hall - this venue offers a mix of live music and DJ sets in a more casual setting.
5\. The Middle East - this Cambridge venue offers live music as well as a dinner and drinks menu.

Best comedy clubs in Boston:

1\. Laugh Boston - this club is known for its stand-up comedy acts and celebrity guests.
2\. ImprovBoston - located in Cambridge, this club offers improvisational comedy and sketch comedy performances.
3\. The Comedy Studio - this club is known for its intimate setting and weekly shows featuring local and national comedians.
4\. Nick's Comedy Stop - located in the Theatre District, this club has a long history of hosting top comedians.
5\. Dick Doherty's Beantown Comedy Vault - this club offers nightly comedy shows featuring both up-and-coming comedians and established ones.

Best neighborhood for nightlife:

The best neighborhood for nightlife in Boston is undoubtedly the Fenway-Kenmore area. This neighborhood is home to several colleges and universities, which means there are plenty of bars catering to the college crowd. But there are also plenty of more sophisticated options, such as Eastern Standard, which offers craft cocktails, and The Hawthorne, which is known for its extensive whiskey selection. The area is also home to Fenway Park, which means it's a great place to hang out before or after a game.

FAQs:

Q: What is the legal drinking age in Boston?
A: The legal drinking age in Boston, as in the rest of the United States, is 21.

Q: What is the dress code for Boston nightclubs?
A: Most Boston nightclubs have a dress code of "smart casual," which means no sneakers or athletic wear.

Q: Are Boston nightclubs open late?
A: Boston nightclubs typically close at 2am, but some may stay open later on weekends.

Q: How do I get around Boston at night?
A: Boston has a public transportation system known as the MBTA, which includes buses and trains that run late into the night. Taxis and ride-share services such as Uber and Lyft are also readily available.

Q: Are there any rooftop bars in Boston?
A: Yes, there are several rooftop bars in Boston, including Lookout Rooftop at The Envoy Hotel, Legal Harborside, and Sky Lounge at Yotel Boston.
