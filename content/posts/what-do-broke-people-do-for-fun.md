# What Do Broke People Do For Fun?

Being broke doesn't mean you can't have fun. There are plenty of activities that don't cost a lot of money, or any money at all. Here are some ideas for having fun without breaking the bank.

## Check the Library or Your Local Parks and Recreation for Free or Reduced Cost Classes

Many libraries and parks and recreation departments offer free or reduced cost classes. From yoga to cooking classes, there are plenty of activities that won't cost you a dime. Check your local library or parks and recreation department for more information.

## Take Up Hiking at Your Local Nature Preserve

Hiking is a great way to get out and explore nature. Many nature preserves offer free or reduced cost hikes. Check with your local nature preserve for more information. Hiking is a great way to get exercise and it's better than a gym membership.

## Just Chillax and Window Shop with Your Best Friend

Sometimes the best thing to do is just hang out with your best friend. Go window shopping and check out the latest trends. You don't have to buy anything, just enjoy looking at the latest fashions.

## Take a Trip to the Beach

If you live near the beach, take a trip there. You can relax in the sun, take a dip in the ocean, and enjoy the scenery. Best of all, it won't cost you a dime.

## Go to a Free Concert

Many cities offer free concerts in the summer. Check your local newspaper or city website for more information. You can enjoy some great music without spending a dime.

## Go to a Free Museum

Many museums offer free admission days or reduced admission prices. Check your local museum for more information. You can enjoy some great art and culture without spending a lot of money.

## Go to a Free Movie Night

Many cities offer free movie nights in the summer. Check your local newspaper or city website for more information. You can enjoy a movie without spending a dime.

## Go for a Picnic

Pack a picnic lunch and head to your local park. Enjoy the fresh air and sunshine while you eat. Best of all, it won't cost you a dime.

## Go for a Bike Ride

If you have a bike, take it out for a spin. Enjoy the fresh air and sunshine while you ride. Best of all, it won't cost you a dime.

## Go to a Free Festival

Many cities offer free festivals in the summer. Check your local newspaper or city website for more information. You can enjoy some great music and entertainment without spending a dime.

## Go for a Walk

Sometimes the best thing to do is just take a walk. Enjoy the fresh air and sunshine while you walk. Best of all, it won't cost you a dime.

Being broke doesn't mean you can't have fun. There are plenty of activities that don't cost a lot of money, or any money at all. From free classes to free festivals, there are plenty of ways to have fun without breaking the bank. So get out there and enjoy yourself!
