# Where Should I Go for a 3 Week Holiday?

When it comes to planning a 3 week holiday, there are a variety of destinations to choose from. Whether you’re looking for a tailor-made holiday or an island hopping adventure, there’s something for everyone. Here are some of the best 3 week holiday destinations to consider.

## South Pacific Island Hopping Holiday in Tahiti

Tahiti is a stunning destination for a 3 week holiday. With its pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and stunning lagoons, it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind. For a truly unique experience, consider a tailor-made island hopping holiday. This will allow you to explore the various islands of the South Pacific, from Tahiti to Bora Bora and beyond. You’ll have the opportunity to experience the unique culture and customs of each island, as well as the breathtaking natural beauty.

## Vines, Bays and Beaches: North Island New Zealand and Samoa Holiday

For a truly unforgettable 3 week holiday, consider a tailor-made holiday to the North Island of New Zealand and Samoa. This is the perfect destination for wine lovers, as the region is home to some of the best vineyards in the world. You’ll also have the opportunity to explore the stunning bays and beaches of the region, as well as the vibrant culture and customs of the local people.

## Highlights of Japan and Beach Holiday in Fiji

For a truly unique 3 week holiday, consider a tailor-made holiday to Japan and Fiji. In Japan, you’ll have the opportunity to explore the vibrant cities of Tokyo and Kyoto, as well as the stunning countryside. You’ll also have the chance to experience the unique culture and customs of the Japanese people. In Fiji, you’ll be able to relax on the stunning beaches and explore the lush rainforests.

## Skyscrapers to Islands: Tokyo and New Caledonia Holiday

For a truly unforgettable 3 week holiday, consider a tailor-made holiday to Tokyo and New Caledonia. In Tokyo, you’ll have the opportunity to explore the vibrant city, from the bustling streets to the stunning skyscrapers. You’ll also have the chance to experience the unique culture and customs of the Japanese people. In New Caledonia, you’ll be able to relax on the stunning beaches and explore the lush rainforests.

No matter what type of 3 week holiday you’re looking for, there’s something for everyone. From island hopping in Tahiti to exploring the highlights of Japan and beach holidays in Fiji, you’re sure to find the perfect destination for your 3 week holiday. So, why not start planning your dream holiday today?
