# Top Places To Travel For Christmas

Christmas is a magical time of year, filled with festive cheer, twinkling lights, and the spirit of giving. For many, it's also the perfect opportunity to travel and experience the holiday season in different parts of the world. Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or a tropical getaway, here are some of the top places to travel for Christmas that will make your holiday season unforgettable.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is iconic during the Christmas season, offering a dazzling array of festive activities and sights.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Witness the famous Christmas tree lighting ceremony and enjoy ice skating beneath its sparkling branches.
- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Experience the magic of the Rockettes in this annual holiday show.
- **Fifth Avenue**: Stroll down this famous street to see the elaborate holiday window displays.
- **Central Park**: Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride through a winter wonderland.

## 2. Lapland, Finland

For a truly enchanting Christmas experience, head to Lapland, the official home of Santa Claus.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa himself and cross the magical Arctic Circle.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking aurora borealis in the clear, dark skies.
- **Reindeer Sleigh Rides**: Glide through snowy forests on a traditional reindeer sleigh.
- **Snow Hotels**: Spend a night in a hotel made entirely of snow and ice.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna transforms into a winter wonderland during the Christmas season, with its historic charm and festive markets.

- **Vienna Christmas Markets**: Explore the numerous markets offering handmade crafts, mulled wine, and festive treats.
- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit the Christmas village set against the backdrop of this stunning baroque palace.
- **Vienna Boys' Choir**: Attend a concert to hear the angelic voices of this world-renowned choir.
- **St. Stephen's Cathedral**: Experience a Christmas Eve mass in this iconic Gothic cathedral.

## 4. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City offers a picturesque setting for a traditional Christmas celebration with its European charm and snowy landscapes.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the cobblestone streets adorned with festive decorations.
- **German Christmas Market**: Enjoy the festive atmosphere with traditional German food and crafts.
- **Tobogganing**: Slide down the historic toboggan run on Dufferin Terrace.
- **Ice Hotel**: Visit the Hôtel de Glace, a unique hotel made entirely of ice and snow.

## 5. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations, making it a fascinating destination during the holidays.

- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Experience the park's special Christmas events and parades.
- **Illuminations**: Marvel at the stunning light displays throughout the city, such as the ones in Roppongi Hills and Shibuya.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit European-style markets offering festive foods and gifts.
- **Christmas Eve Dinner**: Enjoy a traditional Japanese Christmas meal, often featuring fried chicken and strawberry shortcake.

## 6. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's fairy-tale architecture and festive atmosphere make it a perfect destination for a magical Christmas getaway.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market and admire the towering Christmas tree.
- **Charles Bridge**: Take a stroll across this historic bridge, beautifully lit for the holidays.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore the castle grounds and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Midnight Mass**: Attend a Christmas Eve mass at the stunning St. Vitus Cathedral.

## 7. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking a warm Christmas, Sydney offers a unique experience with its sunny weather and festive spirit.

- **Bondi Beach**: Celebrate Christmas Day with a beach barbecue and a swim in the ocean.
- **Carols in the Domain**: Join thousands for a night of caroling under the stars.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Take a cruise to see the city’s iconic landmarks adorned with Christmas lights.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the markets for unique gifts and delicious treats.

## 8. Strasbourg, France

Known as the "Capital of Christmas," Strasbourg offers a charming and traditional holiday experience.

- **Christkindelsmärik**: Visit one of the oldest Christmas markets in Europe, dating back to 1570.
- **Petite France**: Wander through this picturesque district with its half-timbered houses and festive decorations.
- **Strasbourg Cathedral**: Admire the stunning Gothic architecture and the nativity scenes inside.
- **Alsatian Cuisine**: Indulge in local specialties such as tarte flambée and spiced mulled wine.

## 9. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique and magical Christmas experience with its stunning natural beauty and festive traditions.

- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's mischievous Christmas characters who bring gifts to children.
- **Northern Lights**: Chase the aurora borealis for a truly unforgettable sight.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the markets for handmade crafts and local delicacies.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters surrounded by snow-covered landscapes.

## 10. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh's historic charm and festive celebrations make it a wonderful destination for a Christmas getaway.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Enjoy the festive atmosphere with rides, food stalls, and unique gifts.
- **Hogmanay**: Celebrate New Year's Eve with one of the world's most famous street parties.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the iconic castle and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Royal Mile**: Stroll down this historic street, beautifully decorated for the holidays.

## Conclusion

Whether you're seeking a traditional Christmas experience or a unique holiday adventure, these destinations offer something for everyone. From the snowy landscapes of Lapland to the sunny beaches of Sydney, each place provides its own special way to celebrate the season. So pack your bags, embrace the festive spirit, and create unforgettable memories in one of these top places to travel for Christmas.
