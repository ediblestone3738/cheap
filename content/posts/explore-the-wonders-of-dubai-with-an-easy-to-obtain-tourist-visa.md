# Explore the Wonders of Dubai with an Easy-to-Obtain Tourist Visa

Dubai is a city of dreams and luxury. From its skyscrapers and shopping malls to its desert safaris and stunning beaches, there is something for everyone in this vibrant city. With an easy-to-obtain tourist visa, you can experience all that Dubai has to offer and explore its many wonders.

## Visa Requirements

The first step to exploring the wonders of Dubai is to obtain a tourist visa. Most visitors to Dubai are eligible for a visa-on-arrival, which is valid for up to 30 days. Citizens of certain countries, such as the United States, Canada, Australia, and the United Kingdom, are eligible for a 90-day visa-on-arrival.

The visa-on-arrival can be obtained at the Dubai International Airport. Visitors must have a valid passport, a return ticket, and proof of sufficient funds. The visa fee is AED 100 ($27 USD). It is important to note that the visa-on-arrival is only valid for 90 days from the date of issue and cannot be extended.

## Accommodation

There are a variety of accommodation options in Dubai to suit all budgets. From luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels, there is something for everyone. Most hotels in Dubai offer great amenities, such as swimming pools, gyms, and spas.

For those looking for a more unique experience, there are a variety of boutique hotels and serviced apartments available. These offer a home-away-from-home experience and are perfect for those looking for a more authentic experience.

## Transportation

Dubai has an excellent public transportation system, which includes metro, buses, and taxis. The Dubai Metro is the most efficient way to get around the city and is a great option for those looking to explore the city on a budget.

Taxis are also a great option for getting around the city. Taxis are metered and fares start at AED 5 ($1.40 USD). It is important to note that there is an additional charge for luggage and for any additional passengers.

## Things to Do

Dubai is a city of wonders and there is no shortage of things to do. From shopping to sightseeing, there is something for everyone.

For those looking to experience the city’s vibrant nightlife, there are a variety of nightclubs and bars to choose from. For those looking for a more cultural experience, there are a variety of museums, galleries, and historic sites to explore.

For those looking to experience the desert, there are a variety of desert safaris and dune bashing tours available.

## Top 5 Tourist Attractions in Dubai

1\. Burj Khalifa: The world’s tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, is a must-see attraction in Dubai. The building stands at 828 meters tall and offers stunning views of the city from its observation deck.

2\. Dubai Mall: The world’s largest shopping mall, the Dubai Mall, is a must-visit destination for shopaholics. The mall is home to over 1,200 stores, as well as a variety of restaurants and entertainment venues.

3\. Dubai Desert Safari: Experience the beauty of the Arabian desert on a desert safari tour. Tour operators offer a variety of safari packages, including dune bashing, camel rides, and sand skiing.

4\. Palm Jumeirah: Take a stroll along the world’s largest man-made island, the Palm Jumeirah. The island is home to a variety of luxury resorts, restaurants, and attractions.
