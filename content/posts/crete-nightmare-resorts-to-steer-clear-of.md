The Belle Mare Resort may boast luxury accommodations, but don't be fooled. The resort is rundown, the staff is rude, and the beds are uncomfortable. The food is bland and uninspired, and the amenities are outdated and poorly maintained. Guests are warned to stay clear of this resort at all costs.

The Delphin Palace Resort is a disaster waiting to happen. The rooms are dirty and poorly maintained, and the staff is unhelpful and rude. The food is inedible, and the amenities are outdated and poorly maintained. Guests are warned to steer clear of this resort if they value their sanity.
