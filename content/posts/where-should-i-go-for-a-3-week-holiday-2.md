# Where Should I Go for a 3 Week Holiday?

Are you looking for the perfect 3 week holiday destination? With so many options out there, it can be hard to decide where to go. To help you out, we’ve put together a list of the best 3 week holiday destinations for your next getaway.

## South Pacific Island Hopping Holiday in Tahiti

Tahiti is the perfect destination for a 3 week holiday. With its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and vibrant culture, Tahiti is the ideal place to relax and explore. For the ultimate 3 week holiday, why not try a tailor-made island hopping tour? You’ll get to experience the best of Tahiti, including the stunning Bora Bora, the picturesque Moorea, and the charming Huahine. You’ll also get to explore the unique culture of the South Pacific, from traditional Polynesian cuisine to vibrant music and dance.

## Vines, Bays and Beaches: North Island New Zealand and Samoa Holiday

If you’re looking for a 3 week holiday with a mix of adventure and relaxation, then a tailor-made North Island New Zealand and Samoa holiday is the perfect choice. You’ll get to explore the stunning landscapes of the North Island, from the lush vineyards of Hawke’s Bay to the rugged beaches of the Coromandel. You’ll also get to experience the unique culture of Samoa, from the vibrant markets of Apia to the traditional villages of Savai’i.

## Highlights of Japan and Beach Holiday in Fiji

For a 3 week holiday that combines culture and relaxation, why not try a tailor-made Highlights of Japan and Beach Holiday in Fiji? You’ll get to explore the bustling cities of Japan, from the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to the traditional temples of Kyoto. You’ll also get to experience the stunning beaches of Fiji, from the white sands of the Mamanuca Islands to the crystal-clear waters of the Yasawa Islands.

## Skyscrapers to Islands: Tokyo and New Caledonia Holiday

For a 3 week holiday that combines city and beach, why not try a tailor-made Tokyo and New Caledonia holiday? You’ll get to explore the vibrant city of Tokyo, from the neon-lit streets of Shinjuku to the traditional temples of Asakusa. You’ll also get to experience the stunning beaches of New Caledonia, from the white sands of the Loyalty Islands to the crystal-clear waters of the Isle of Pines.

No matter what type of 3 week holiday you’re looking for, there’s something for everyone. From island hopping in Tahiti to exploring the cities of Japan, there’s a destination out there that’s perfect for you. So what are you waiting for? Start planning your 3 week holiday today!
