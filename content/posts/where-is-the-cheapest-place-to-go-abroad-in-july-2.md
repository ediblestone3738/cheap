# Where is the Cheapest Place to Go Abroad in July?

July is a great time to travel abroad and explore the world. Whether you’re looking for a beach getaway, a cultural experience, or a unique adventure, there are plenty of options available. But with so many destinations to choose from, it can be hard to decide where to go.

Fortunately, there are plenty of places around the world that offer great value for money. Here are 13 of the best cheap places to travel in July:

## Niagara Falls, Canada

Niagara Falls is one of the most iconic natural wonders in the world. Located on the border between Canada and the United States, the falls are a spectacular sight to behold. The best way to experience the falls is to take a boat ride on the Maid of the Mist, which will take you right up to the base of the falls. There are also plenty of other attractions in the area, such as the Skylon Tower and the Niagara Parks Butterfly Conservatory.

## Phuket, Thailand

Phuket is a popular destination for beach lovers. The island is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, such as Patong Beach and Kata Beach. There are also plenty of other attractions in the area, such as the Big Buddha and the Wat Chalong temple.

## Phoenix, Arizona

Phoenix is a great destination for those looking for a desert adventure. The city is home to some of the most stunning desert landscapes in the world, and there are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as hiking, biking, and hot air balloon rides.

## Montego Bay, Jamaica

Montego Bay is a great destination for beach lovers. The city is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the Caribbean, such as Doctor’s Cave Beach and Cornwall Beach. There are also plenty of other attractions in the area, such as the Rose Hall Great House and the Montego Bay Marine Park.

## The Bahamas

The Bahamas is a great destination for those looking for a tropical getaway. The islands are home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, such as Cable Beach and Paradise Island. There are also plenty of other attractions in the area, such as the Atlantis Paradise Island Resort and the Ardastra Gardens.

## Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

Punta Cana is a great destination for beach lovers. The city is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the Caribbean, such as Bavaro Beach and Macao Beach. There are also plenty of other attractions in the area, such as the Punta Cana Ecological Park and the Manati Park Bavaro.

## Mazatlan, Mexico

Mazatlan is a great destination for those looking for a unique cultural experience. The city is home to some of the most stunning colonial architecture in the world, and there are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as fishing, surfing, and whale watching.

## New Orleans, Louisiana

New Orleans is a great destination for those looking for a unique cultural experience. The city is home to some of the most vibrant music and art scenes in the world, and there are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as jazz music, Mardi Gras celebrations, and Cajun cuisine.

## Conclusion

These are just a few of the best cheap places to travel in July. Whether you’re looking for a beach getaway, a cultural experience, or a unique adventure, there are plenty of options available. So why not take advantage of the great deals available and start planning your summer vacation today?
