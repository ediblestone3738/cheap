# Where is the Cheapest Place to Go Abroad in August?

August is a great time to take a vacation, and if you’re looking for a cheap place to go abroad, you’re in luck. ## Las Vegas, Nevada

Las Vegas is a great place to visit in August, as the city is usually quite warm and sunny. The city is full of exciting attractions, from the iconic Las Vegas Strip to the Grand Canyon. ## Cancun, Mexico

Key West is a great destination for beach lovers. The city is full of stunning beaches, and you can also explore the nearby Ernest Hemingway House. There are plenty of affordable hotels and restaurants, and you can find cheap flights to Key West from many cities around the world.

## Quito, Ecuador

Quito is a great destination for budget travelers. The city is full of fascinating attractions, from the colonial architecture to the equator line. There are plenty of affordable hotels and restaurants, and you can find cheap flights to Quito from many cities around the world.

## New York City, New York, USA

New York City is a great destination for budget travelers. The city is full of fascinating attractions, from the Empire State Building to the Statue of Liberty. There are plenty of affordable hotels and restaurants, and you can find cheap flights to New York City from many cities around the world.

## Conclusion

August is a great time to take a vacation, and there are plenty of affordable destinations around the world that offer great value for money. From vibrant cities to stunning beaches, there are plenty of cheap places to travel in August. Whether you’re looking for a beach vacation or a city break, you’re sure to find a great destination that fits your budget.
