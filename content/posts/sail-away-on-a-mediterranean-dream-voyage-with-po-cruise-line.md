Sail Away on a Mediterranean Dream Voyage with P&O Cruise Line

P&O Cruise Line is considered one of the best cruise lines for those looking for a beautiful and immersive experience in the Mediterranean. With a variety of ships and routes, P&O Cruise Line makes it easy to plan the perfect Mediterranean dream voyage. And with its exceptional service, delicious cuisine, and luxurious amenities, P&O Cruise Line will take you on a journey you will never forget.

From beautiful scenic towns to bustling cities with rich culture, the Mediterranean has something to offer for everyone no matter what your interests are. The P&O Cruise Line has a special sail-away Mediterranean Dream Voyage programme that covers the Mediterranean region's iconic cities and towns.

So, what can you expect when you Sail Away on a Mediterranean Dream Voyage with P&O Cruise Line? In this article, we will share travel tips and highlights to help plan your dream escape to the Mediterranean.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Sail Away on a Mediterranean Dream Voyage with P&O Cruise Line

1\. Dubrovnik, Croatia: Dubrovnik is a stunning city with a rich cultural heritage. The entire old town is sheltered inside the walls of the fortress. A walk through the city will take you back in time. Be sure to visit the picturesque marina and the historic Stari Grad neighbourhood.

2\. Venice, Italy: Who can resist a visit to romantic Venice? Explore the canals and alleys of this city, admire the Bell Tower, and enjoy a relaxed evening – a perfect blend of history and entertainment.

3\. Kotor, Montenegro: Kotor is one of the most beautiful ports in Europe. The stunning mountains surround this ancient town of winding streets and medieval buildings. Be sure to explore the old town, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

4\. Mykonos, Greece: Mykonos is legendary for its parties, but it is not just for partygoers. Be sure to visit the white-washed buildings and picturesque alleys of the town. Relax along the golden sandy beaches, sample local cuisine and participate in watersports.

5\. Barcelona, Spain: One of the highlights of any Mediterranean cruise is Barcelona. Explore the incredible Gaudi architecture, soak up the sun on a beach, and visit bustling markets. From the Sagrada Familia to Park Guell, there's so much to see in the city.

6\. Florence, Italy: Renaissance art lover? Visit the Uffizi gallery – the world's top art museum. Explore the numerous cathedrals, Piazzas and palaces. A walking tour of the city will take you to all the top tourist attractions quickly.

7\. Santorini, Greece: Santorini is one of the most visited islands in Greece. Take a donkey ride, watch a dazzling sunset, explore the archaeological sites or relax in the natural hot waters.

8\. Naples, Italy: Visit Pompeii and learn about its tragic history or head to the Amalfi Coast, the most picturesque Italian coastline.

9\. Istanbul, Turkey: Istanbul, the former capital of the Ottoman Empire, is the cultural, artistic and economic hub of Turkey. The city's history goes back to before the Byzantine period, and you'll find historic buildings, mosques, and museums in abundance. Shop in the Grand Bazaar and enjoy the Turkish delights.

10\. Corfu, Greece: A picture-perfect port that will appeal to all ages. Take a walk through the alleys of the old town, visit the fortresses and experience the local culture at the market.
