Make Your Dreams a Reality with Our Incredible Rhodes Package Deals!

Rhodes is a Greek island located in the Aegean Sea with its history stretching back to ancient times. Known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, spectacular chapels and castles, Rhodes is one of the most popular vacation destinations in the world.

Our Rhodes package deals offer a wide range of options that cater to different needs and budgets, from all-inclusive beach resorts to luxury villas with private pools. Whatever your travel style, we have something for everyone.

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Rhodes?
A: The best time to visit Rhodes is from May to October when the weather is warm and sunny.

Q: What are the most popular tourist attractions in Rhodes?
A: The most popular tourist attractions in Rhodes are the Acropolis of Lindos, the Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes, the Medieval Old Town of Rhodes, and the Valley of the Butterflies.

Q: What is the currency used in Rhodes?
A: The currency used in Rhodes is the Euro.

Q: What is the language spoken in Rhodes?
A: The language spoken in Rhodes is Greek, but English is widely spoken in the tourist areas.

Q: Is Rhodes safe for tourists?
A: Yes, Rhodes is a safe destination for tourists.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Rhodes

1\. Acropolis of Lindos - An ancient Greek temple dedicated to the goddess Athena.

2\. Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes - A castle built in the 14th century by the Knights of Rhodes.

3\. Medieval Old Town of Rhodes - A UNESCO World Heritage Site that features medieval walls, cobblestone streets and a palace of the Knights of Rhodes.

4\. Valley of the Butterflies - A natural reserve that is home to thousands of butterflies.

5\. Anthony Quinn Bay - A stunning beach named after the famous actor who once owned the beach.

6\. Kallithea Springs - A historic spa complex that was built in the early 20th century.

7\. Ancient Kamiros - An ancient city on the west coast of the island.

8\. Tsambika Beach - A beautiful beach with turquoise waters.

9\. Filerimos Monastery - A monastery located on a hilltop with stunning panoramic views.

10\. Lindos Town - A picturesque village that has a beautiful beach and narrow alleys lined with shops and restaurants.
