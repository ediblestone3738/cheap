# Best Places To Visit At Christmas In The South

The Southern United States, with its unique blend of cultures, traditions, and landscapes, offers a magical setting for Christmas celebrations. From charming small towns to vibrant cities, the South is filled with destinations that transform into winter wonderlands during the holiday season. Whether you're seeking festive lights, historical charm, or unique holiday traditions, the South has something special to offer. Here are some of the best places to visit at Christmas in the South.

## Asheville, North Carolina

Nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains, Asheville is a picturesque destination that comes alive with holiday spirit.

- **Biltmore Estate**: Experience the grandeur of America's largest home, beautifully decorated with thousands of lights, ornaments, and Christmas trees. The Candlelight Christmas Evenings offer a magical experience with live music and candlelit tours.
- **Downtown Asheville**: Stroll through the charming streets adorned with festive decorations, and enjoy the local shops and restaurants that offer special holiday treats and gifts.
- **Winter Lights at the North Carolina Arboretum**: Wander through a dazzling display of lights and enjoy the beauty of nature illuminated in the winter night.

## Charleston, South Carolina

Charleston's historic charm and Southern hospitality make it a perfect Christmas destination.

- **Holiday Festival of Lights**: Held at James Island County Park, this event features millions of lights, holiday-themed displays, and family-friendly activities.
- **Historic District**: Explore the cobblestone streets and antebellum homes, many of which are decorated for the holidays. Don't miss the special holiday tours and events at historic sites.
- **Christmas Parades and Events**: Enjoy the festive parades, concerts, and performances that take place throughout the city during the holiday season.

## Savannah, Georgia

Savannah's historic squares and moss-draped oaks provide a stunning backdrop for Christmas celebrations.

- **Savannah Christmas Market**: Located at Plant Riverside District, this European-style market offers holiday shopping, live performances, and festive food and drinks.
- **Holiday Tours of Homes**: Experience the beauty of Savannah's historic homes, decorated in their holiday finest, with special tours offered throughout December.
- **River Street Celebrations**: Enjoy the holiday lights and events along Savannah's famous River Street, including fireworks and live entertainment.

## Nashville, Tennessee

Known as Music City, Nashville offers a lively and musical Christmas experience.

- **Gaylord Opryland Resort**: This iconic resort transforms into a winter wonderland with over three million lights, ice sculptures, and holiday-themed activities.
- **Christmas at the Ryman**: Enjoy holiday concerts and performances at the historic Ryman Auditorium, featuring some of the biggest names in music.
- **Holiday Markets and Events**: Explore the various holiday markets, craft fairs, and festive events happening throughout the city.

## New Orleans, Louisiana

New Orleans offers a unique blend of holiday traditions with its rich cultural heritage.

- **Celebration in the Oaks**: Held in City Park, this event features stunning light displays, amusement rides, and holiday-themed attractions.
- **Reveillon Dinners**: Experience a traditional Creole Christmas feast at one of the city's renowned restaurants, offering special holiday menus.
- **Bonfires on the Levee**: Witness the unique tradition of lighting bonfires along the Mississippi River levee, guiding Papa Noel to New Orleans.

## San Antonio, Texas

San Antonio's vibrant culture and festive spirit make it a great destination for Christmas.

- **River Walk**: The famous River Walk is adorned with thousands of lights, creating a magical atmosphere for a holiday stroll or a festive boat ride.
- **La Gran Tamalada**: Participate in this cultural event at the Historic Market Square, where you can learn to make traditional tamales and enjoy live music and entertainment.
- **Holiday in the Park**: Visit Six Flags Fiesta Texas for thrilling rides, holiday shows, and a spectacular light display.

## Williamsburg, Virginia

Step back in time and experience a colonial Christmas in Williamsburg.

- **Colonial Williamsburg**: Enjoy the historic charm of this living history museum, with traditional decorations, holiday tours, and special events.
- **Grand Illumination**: Witness the spectacular fireworks display and candlelit processions that kick off the holiday season.
- **Busch Gardens Christmas Town**: Experience the magic of Christmas with millions of lights, festive shows, and holiday-themed attractions.

## Gatlinburg, Tennessee

Located in the heart of the Smoky Mountains, Gatlinburg offers a cozy and festive holiday experience.

- **Winterfest**: Enjoy the stunning light displays and festive events that take place throughout the town and the surrounding area.
- **Great Smoky Arts & Crafts Community**: Explore the unique shops and galleries offering handmade gifts and holiday decorations.
- **Ober Gatlinburg**: Hit the slopes or enjoy the indoor ice skating rink at this popular ski resort, which also offers holiday-themed activities.

## Dallas, Texas

Dallas offers a mix of modern attractions and traditional holiday celebrations.

- **Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden**: Experience the 12 Days of Christmas display, featuring elaborate Victorian-style gazebos decorated with holiday scenes.
- **Enchant Christmas**: Visit this magical light maze and village, offering ice skating, holiday shopping, and festive treats.
- **Holiday Parades and Events**: Enjoy the various parades, concerts, and events happening throughout the city during the holiday season.

## St. Augustine, Florida

As the nation's oldest city, St. Augustine offers a unique and historic Christmas experience.

- **Nights of Lights**: Witness the city illuminated with millions of tiny white lights, creating a breathtaking display that has been recognized as one of the best holiday light displays in the world.
- **Colonial Night Watch**: Experience a reenactment of a colonial Christmas, complete with parades, musket drills, and festive celebrations.
- **Holiday Regatta of Lights**: Watch as boats decorated with lights and holiday decorations parade along the Matanzas Bay.

## Conclusion

The Southern United States offers a diverse array of Christmas destinations, each with its own unique charm and traditions. Whether you're looking for a cozy mountain retreat, a historic city with colonial charm, or a vibrant cultural experience, the South has something to offer every traveler during the holiday season. Embrace the warmth and hospitality of the South, and create unforgettable memories with your loved ones this Christmas.
