Indulge in a Luxurious Corfu Getaway at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace

Nestled amongst the lush greenery of Corfu Island, lies the luxurious Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace. This five-star accommodation is the perfect destination for those seeking a luxurious retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The hotel boasts an enviable location, breathtaking views of the sea, and top-notch amenities sure to please even the most discerning traveler.

Rooms and Suites

Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace has a vast array of comfortable rooms and suites, all of which are designed with the utmost luxury in mind. Each room is equipped with a comfortable bed, a flat-screen TV, a mini-fridge, air conditioning, first-class toiletries, and fast Wi-Fi. The rooms come in various sizes and shapes, and each one takes inspiration from the surrounding nature.

The Penthouse Suite is the most luxurious option available, featuring two separate rooms, a separate living area, and a private terrace with a hot tub. The suite is ideal for guests wanting a little more privacy, and it tops our list of highly recommended accommodation options.

Facilities

The hotel amenities available at the Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace are second to none. The on-site Spa offers a range of therapeutic treatments, such as relaxation massages, facials, and detox programs. The spa also features a wellness program with a gym, a sauna, and a Jacuzzi. All of these facilities are available free of charge to hotel guests.

In addition to the spa, the hotel contains several outdoor pools with stunning views of the sea, and a private beach that is exclusively available to hotel guests. If you'd like to spice things up, check out the Casino Corfu, located within the hotel, where you can test your luck at your favorite table games.

Dining

The Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace takes dining seriously, featuring an excellent range of dining options available throughout the day. The hotel's main restaurant, Vassilis, serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner in a luxurious setting. If you prefer, the hotel also has a pizzeria and pool bar that serves fresh and delicious dishes perfect for a quick and casual meal.

The hotel's rooftop bar is where you can enjoy the sunset with the best panoramic views of the sea. The bar serves a range of cocktails, beers, wines, and with a variety of snacks and mouth-watering finger foods.

FAQs

1\. What is the check-in time for Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace?

The hotel's check-in time is 3:00 PM local time.

2\. What is the checkout time for Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace?

The hotel's checkout time is 11:00 AM local time.

3\. Is there an airport shuttle service available?

Yes, the hotel provides airport shuttle service at an additional cost. Guests can book this service directly with the hotel when they make the reservation.

4\. Is the hotel located near any tourist attractions?

Yes, the hotel is located within proximity of several top tourist destinations, including Corfu Old town, Mon Repos Museum, and Kanoni Peninsula.

5\. What amenities are included in the room rate?

The room rate includes Wi-Fi, toiletries, breakfast, and access to the Spa, gym, and outdoor pool area.
