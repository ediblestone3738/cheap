Unpacking Zante Laganas: A Relaxing Escape from Reality

3\. Turtle Island: This small island, located off the coast of Laganas, is a protected wildlife sanctuary and is home to the endangered loggerhead turtles. Visitors can take a boat tour around the island to see the turtles and their nests.

4\. Marathonisi Island: This small island, also known as “Turtle Island,” is located off the coast of Laganas and is home to the endangered loggerhead turtles. The island offers beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning sea views.
