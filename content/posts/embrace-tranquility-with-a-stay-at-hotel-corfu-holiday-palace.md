Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in Embrace Tranquility with a Stay at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace

1) Corfu Old Town

Corfu Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring an elegant blend of Venetian, French, and English architecture.

2) The Palace of St. Michael and St. George

The Palace of St. Michael and St. George is now the Museum of Asian Art and is situated in a beautiful garden, the ideal place to stroll and appreciate history and nature.

3) Achilleion

Achilleion is a Neoclassical Palace with breathtaking views of Corfu Island that was built for Sissy, Empress of Austria.

4) Corfu's Canal d'Amour

Corfu's Canal d'Amour is a sleep seaside village on the north-west coast of Corfu.

5) Mountain Biking

Mountain biking is a popular activity in Corfu, with many scenic routes to explore.

6) Aqualand Water Park

Aqualand Water Park is a fantastic option to entertain the whole family with more than 35 water slides and various activities.

7) Paleokastritsa Beach

Paleokastritsa Beach is a hidden gem with turquoise sea, steep cliffs as a background and a Monastery overlooking the beach.

8) Mouse Island

Mouse Island is a small islet situated just opposite the southern coast of Corfu, legend claims Ulysses turned to stone the ship of the Pirate that raided the island.

9) The Village of Messonghi

The village of Messoghi is a small colorful village which owes its fame to the sandy beach and shallow waters.

10) The Achillion Brewery

The Achillion Brewery is a thriving business in Corfu, where visitors can sample locally crafted beer and learn about the brewing process.
