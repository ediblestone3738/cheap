It is an ideal location for travelers who want to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and indulge in a luxurious and comfortable vacation. Luxury Accommodation

One of the highlights of Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is its luxurious accommodations. Every room is designed with utmost elegance and sophistication, promising guests the highest levels of comfort and convenience. Whether you opt for a sea view room or a garden view room, you will have an idyllic and peaceful environment to retreat to after a long day of exploring the island. Recreational Activities

Apart from its exceptional accommodations, the hotel has a wide range of recreational activities that cater to all preferences. There are four swimming pools, including the cascade pool, which offers a stunning view of the sea, as well as a children's pool. There is also a private beach, where you can escape for a moment of sun, sea, and sand.

Guests can also indulge in a range of water sports, including paddleboarding, windsurfing, and scuba diving. There is also a tennis court and a mini-golf course, and fitness enthusiasts can take advantage of the gym facilities.
