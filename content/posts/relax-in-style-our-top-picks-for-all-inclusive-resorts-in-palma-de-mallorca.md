Palma de Mallorca, located in the Balearic Islands of Spain, is a picturesque destination known for its gorgeous beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant atmosphere. One of the best ways to enjoy this stunning region is by indulging in an all-inclusive resort experience. Relaxing in style is exactly what Palma de Mallorca can offer visitors. Here are our top picks for all-inclusive resorts in Palma de Mallorca.

1\. Iberostar Selection Playa de Palma

Nestled in the heart of Playa de Palma, Iberostar Selection Playa de Palma is a luxury 5-star resort that is perfect for those seeking a relaxing and elegant vacation. The resort has direct access to the beach and boasts a range of amenities including a spa, fitness center, and outdoor pool. The resort's all-inclusive package includes an array of high-end dining options, cocktails, and activities for guests to enjoy.

2\. Zafiro Palace Palmanova

This 5-star resort is located in the stunning resort town of Palmanova and boasts some of the best vacation amenities in the region, including fully-equipped kitchens in their suites, a choice of multiple pools to sunbathe by, and exclusive access to two private beaches. The resort's all-inclusive package includes all meals, drinks, activities, and water sports, making it an ultimate all-inclusive resort experience.

3\. Iberostar Albufera Park

This 4-star resort is located in Playa de Muro and is perfect for families looking for a Mediterranean getaway. The resort has four outdoor pools, a playground, and an entertainment club to keep kids and adults alike occupied. The all-inclusive package includes meals, drinks, and a range of activities that cater to guests of all ages.

4\. Zafiro Palace Alcudia

Zafiro Palace Alcudia is a 5-star resort located in the north of the island of Palma de Mallorca. The hotel boasts a range of modern facilities such as a luxury spa, fitness center, and stunning indoor and outdoor pools. The resort's all-inclusive package includes delicious meals with a range of local and international cuisine, unlimited drinks, and a range of activities available for guests to enjoy.

5\. Be-Live Collection Palace de Muro

This 5-star resort is located in the heart of Playa de Muro and has an exclusive beach location with stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort's all-inclusive package boasts unlimited food and drinks, including a selection of wine, beer, and spirits. The hotel also has many facilities such as two pools, a spa area, a mini-club for kids, and a gym.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1\. What does the all-inclusive package include?

Each resort has its own all-inclusive package, but most include unlimited meals, drinks, and access to resort activities such as water sports, concerts, and fitness classes. Check with your chosen resort for the specific details.

2\. What is the weather like in Palma de Mallorca?

Palma de Mallorca enjoys a Mediterranean climate, with hot summers and mild winters. The best time to visit the island is from May to October as the weather is mild, and the sea is warm.

3\. What are the top tourist attractions in Palma de Mallorca?

There are a range of tourist attractions in Palma de Mallorca such as:
\- The Gothic Cathedral of Santa Maria
\- The Royal Palace of La Almudaina
\- Bellver Castle
\- The Arab Baths
\- The Fish Market
\- The Paseo Maritimo waterfront
\- The historic district of Old Town Palma
\- Palma Aquarium
\- Marineland
\- Western Water Park.
