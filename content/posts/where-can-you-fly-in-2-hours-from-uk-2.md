# 8 Great European Cities With Short Flight Times From The UK

Are you looking for a quick getaway from the UK? Whether you’re looking for a weekend break or a longer holiday, there are plenty of European cities that you can reach in two hours or less from the UK. Here are eight great cities that you can reach in two hours or less from the UK.

## Amsterdam, Netherlands

Amsterdam is the capital of the Netherlands and one of the most popular tourist destinations in Europe. It’s known for its picturesque canals, beautiful architecture, and vibrant nightlife. The city is easily accessible from the UK, with direct flights from London taking just over an hour.

## Paris, France

Paris is one of the most romantic cities in the world and a popular destination for couples. The city is known for its iconic landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and the Champs-Élysées. Flights from London to Paris take just over an hour, making it a great destination for a weekend break.

## Antwerp, Belgium

Antwerp is a charming city in Belgium that is known for its cobbled streets, stunning architecture, and vibrant nightlife. The city is easily accessible from the UK, with direct flights from London taking just over an hour.

## Cologne, Germany

Cologne is a vibrant city in Germany that is known for its beautiful architecture, lively beer halls, and historic sites. Flights from London to Cologne take just over an hour, making it a great destination for a weekend break.

## Geneva, Switzerland

Geneva is a stunning city in Switzerland that is known for its picturesque lake, beautiful parks, and lively nightlife. Flights from London to Geneva take just over an hour, making it a great destination for a weekend break.

## Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is a vibrant city in Spain that is known for its stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and beautiful beaches. Flights from London to Barcelona take just over an hour, making it a great destination for a weekend break.

## Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a stunning city in the Czech Republic that is known for its beautiful architecture, vibrant nightlife, and lively pubs. Flights from London to Prague take just over an hour, making it a great destination for a weekend break.

## Salzburg, Germany

Salzburg is a charming city in Germany that is known for its stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and picturesque lakes. Flights from London to Salzburg take just over an hour, making it a great destination for a weekend break.

Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway or a weekend break, there are plenty of European cities that you can reach in two hours or less from the UK. From Amsterdam to Salzburg, these eight cities offer something for everyone. So, why not book your flight today and start planning your next European adventure?
