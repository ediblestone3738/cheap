Orlando: A Great Place to Visit During the Pandemic

Amidst the global pandemic, travelling may seem like a distant dream. However, with the right precautions and a well-planned itinerary, you can still make the most out of your vacation. Orlando, Florida, is one such destination that offers a safe and enjoyable experience for tourists. With its theme parks, natural attractions, and several other activities, Orlando can make for an unforgettable trip.

In this article, we will discuss why Orlando is an excellent choice for a vacation during the pandemic, the top tourist attractions to visit, and the most frequently asked questions about travelling to Orlando.

Why Choose Orlando for your Vacation During the Pandemic?

Orlando has always been a go-to destination for tourists due to its theme parks and attractions. In recent times, it has also emerged as a safe option during a pandemic. Hereâ€™s why:

1\. Safety Precautions

With the pandemic still affecting several parts of the world, increased safety measures are being taken throughout Orlando. The theme parks and attractions have implemented strict protocols to ensure the safety of guests and employees. They include temperature checks, mandatory masks, social distancing, enhanced hygiene practices, and reduced capacity.

2\. Wide Open Spaces

Orlando is blessed with vast natural attractions such as national parks, lakes, and gardens. These outdoor spaces provide ample opportunities for social distancing, which is crucial during the pandemic.

3\. Reduced Crowds

Although Orlando is a popular destination, the pandemic has caused a decline in the number of tourists. This means that the attractions are less crowded, offering a more exclusive experience for visitors.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions located in Orlando: A Great Place to Visit During the Pandemic

1\. Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World is the epitome of family entertainment. The park offers multiple theme parks, water parks, resorts, and restaurants. With reduced capacity and increased safety measures, visiting Disney World during the pandemic is a safe option for families.

2\. Universal Studios

Universal Studios is another theme park that offers various all-age attractions such as Harry Potter World, Jurassic Park, and Despicable Me Minion Mayhem. With its safety protocols in place, it is a must-visit attraction in Orlando.

3\. SeaWorld Orlando

SeaWorld Orlando is a marine theme park that features animal exhibits, roller coasters, and water rides. With its spacious outdoor areas and safety measures, it is an excellent place to visit during the pandemic.

4\. Gatorland

Gatorland is an outdoor attraction that showcases numerous alligators and crocodiles. It offers several shows, exhibits, and a petting zoo. Gatorland also emphasizes safety measures such as mandatory masks and social distancing.

5\. Kennedy Space Center

The Kennedy Space Center is a significant landmark in the history of space exploration. The centre offers visitors an opportunity to learn about NASA's accomplishments, space travel, and even meet astronauts.

6\. Orlando Science Centre

The Orlando Science Centre is a hands-on museum that features interactive exhibits on science and technology. With several precautions such as reduced capacity and increased cleaning procedures in place, the Science Centre is a safe and educational outing.

7\. Discovery Cove

Discovery Cove is a unique attraction that offers visitors the chance to swim with dolphins, snorkel with tropical fish, and explore a coral reef. With its well-managed capacity and hygiene measures, it is a must-visit for marine enthusiasts.

8\. The Wheel at ICON Park

The Wheel at ICON Park is a 400-foot observation wheel that provides panoramic views of Orlando. With its advanced cleaning protocols and reduced capacity, it is a safe and enjoyable experience for visitors.

9\. Legoland Florida

Legoland Florida is a theme park that caters to families and children. It features several attractions such as water parks, roller coasters, and Lego exhibits. Legoland Florida has implemented safety procedures such as reduced capacity and increased hygiene practices.

10\. Harry P. Leu Gardens

Harry P. Leu Gardens is a 50-acre botanical garden located in Orlando. It features several plants and flowers from around the world and is an excellent destination for nature lovers. With outdoor spaces and social distancing protocols in place, it is a safe and peaceful retreat.

FAQs about Travelling to Orlando During the Pandemic

Q. Do I need to wear a mask in Orlando?

A. Yes, it is mandatory to wear a mask in all public places in Orlando, including theme parks, hotels, restaurants, and shops.

Q. Are the theme parks and attractions open in Orlando?

A. Yes, most of the theme parks and attractions in Orlando are open. However, it is recommended to check their websites before visiting as some may have altered hours or reduced capacity.

Q. Are the restaurants in Orlando open?

A. Yes, the restaurants in Orlando are open, but with reduced capacity and safety measures such as masks, social distancing, and enhanced hygiene practices.

Q. Do I need to quarantine when I arrive in Orlando?

A. No, there is no mandatory quarantine requirement when arriving in Orlando. However, it is recommended to follow local guidelines and safety protocols.

Q. Is it safe to travel to Orlando during the pandemic?

A. Yes, Orlando has implemented numerous safety protocols, reduced capacity, and increased hygiene practices to ensure a safe experience for tourists.

Conclusion

Orlando remains a popular destination for tourists worldwide. The pandemic may have reduced travel, but with the right precautions in place, you can still enjoy all that Orlando has to offer. With its theme parks, natural attractions, and several other activities, a trip to Orlando can be an unforgettable vacation during the pandemic. Just remember to wear your mask, practice social distancing, and check for safety protocols before visiting.
