From Serenity to Adventure: Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace Has It All

Are you seeking a vacation that allows you to unwind, recharge and explore different terrains, from sandy beaches to lush green forests all in one place? If you are nodding your head in approval, make your way to Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace located in Kanoni, Corfu, Greece.

Surrounded by mountains and the Ionian Sea, this resort is considered as a jewel of the Greek islands with a magical combination of sun and sea, guaranteed to make your trip unforgettable. In this travel article, you will find out why Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is the ultimate destination for travelers seeking both serenity and adventure.

Serenity
Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace has 261 rooms and suites, all with a balcony or a terrace, ensuring each guest gets a picturesque view of the Ionian Sea, Corfu island, or the resort's tropical gardens. The rooms are air-conditioned and have modern appliances, ensuring your comfort throughout your stay.

The resort's private beach, found in a sheltered cove, provides an exclusive atmosphere for travelers wanting to relax and unwind. The golden sand and clear waters call out for a swim, and water sports such as windsurfing, and canoeing, are also available for the more adventurous guests. The resort's staff is available to serve refreshing drinks and snacks throughout the day.

At the Corfu Holiday Palace, be sure to explore their luxurious spa, armed with a heated indoor pool, a hot tub, a sauna, and a steam room. The spa also offers massage and beauty treatments for a more invigorating rejuvenation experience. For keen fitness enthusiasts, head to the resort's two tennis courts, or the gym. These facilities ensure each guest caters to their fitness needs during their stay.

Dining at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace provides an exceptional culinary experience. With four restaurants and three bars, the resort offers a diverse culinary menu. With the 'Symposium' restaurant, serving an array of international dishes, the 'Taverna' restaurant that provides a traditional Greek menu, the 'Gardena' pool bar that offers drinks and snacks throughout the day, and the 'Pantheon' roof-top restaurant, which serves dinner and offers breathtaking views at night. Guests should ensure that they try the signature cocktails, made using fresh fruits, while enjoying the stunning scenery.

Adventure
Corfu Holiday Palace is nestled at the foot of Kanoni Hill, with its rich flora and fauna species, making it the ultimate destination for avid hikers and adventurers. Hike up to the top of the hill and discover the spectacular view of the resort and the surrounding areas. The Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace offers a more personalized excursion with a personal guide to make your outing more meaningful.

For water sports enthusiasts, there are countless activities, including water-skiing, wakeboarding, banana boat rides, and parasailing. The resort's staff and qualified instructors offer beginners lessons making it an opportunity for anyone to explore and experience these activities.

If you are more inclined towards a cultural experience, at only ten kilometers away, the Old Town of Corfu, a UNESCO World Heritage site, offers tours guided by historians to give you a deep insight into the history of Corfu. Also within a close proximity, the 'Achilleion' palace, built by the Empress Elizabeth of Austria (known as "Sissy"), offers visitors a peek into the luxurious lives of the royalties of the 19th century. The palace hosts vast gardens with statues of mythical heroes like Achilles, and the Muses of music, poetry, history, and dance, each standing tall, portraying various arts.

FAQs

Q: Does the resort offer child care services?
A: Yes, the resort offers a kid's club, providing creative games and activities, which cater to children aged 4 to 12.

Q: How can I book for an excursion?
A: Visitors can request excursions through the resort's reception. A personal guide will assist and advise on possible locations according to individual preferences.

Q: How far is the resort from the airport, and how can I reach it?
A: Corfu International Airport is at a distance of six kilometers from the resort. Taxis, private pickups, or car rentals from the airport are accessible means of arrival.

Q: Which language do the resort staff speak?
A: The hotels' staff members speak fluent English, German, Italian, and French.

Q: Does the hotel have disabled access rooms?
A: Yes, the resort has rooms that cater to the needs of the disabled, including wheelchair accessibility.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in From Serenity to Adventure: Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace Has It All

1\. Kanoni Hill: A great hiking spot that displays Corfu's lush green vegetation

2\. Old Town of Corfu: This historical site boasts charming Venetian buildings, a Palace, and a Fortress.

3\. Achilleion Palace: An extraordinary 19th-century palace with vast gardens and awe-inspiring statues

4\. Mon Repos: A neoclassical palace, that was the birthplace of the Duke of Edinburgh, Prince Phillip

5\. Byzantine Museum of Antivouniotissa: A museum displaying the Byzantine art collection dating to the 15th century.

6\. Corfu Museum of Asian Art: Provides a rare opportunity to view a vast collection of Asian art

7\. Agni Bay: A tranquil bay with crystal clear waters, perfect for a leisure swim

8\. Glyfada Beach: A golden sand beach that provides exciting water activities like banana boat rides, and windsurfing

9\. Corfu Golf Club: One of the most picturesque golf courses in Europe

10\. Corfu Aqua Park: A waterpark that promises to provide a fun-filled day for all ages.

Conclusion
In summary, the Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace promises to provide guests with an unforgettable experience. With both adventure and serenity activities, it is a great vacation spot for all, from solo travelers to families with kids. The resort has a diverse menu of activities, with four restaurants offering gastronomy cuisine, and three bars offering cocktails and drinks throughout the day. And with fantastic tour guides that offer personalized excursions, visitors can explore Corfu's most iconic spots fully. For a great vacation that caters to both relaxation and exploration of nature and culture, the Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is the ultimate destination.
