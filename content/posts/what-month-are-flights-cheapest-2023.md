# What Month are Flights Cheapest in 2023?

Are you planning a trip for 2023 and looking for the best deals on flights? Knowing when to book your flights can save you a lot of money. To help you get the best deals on airfare, we’ve put together a guide to the cheapest months to fly in 2023.

## Cheapest Days to Fly in 2023

The cheapest days to fly in 2023 are during the winter months, from January 7 to February 28. The single cheapest travel day is January 21. The best days of the week to fly are Tuesdays and Wednesdays, followed by Thursdays. Prices are higher February 14-20 due to Valentine's Day and President's Day weekends.

## Cheapest Fares in 2023

January is the cheapest month to fly in 2023, with fares up to 20% lower than the average. February is also a good month to book flights, with fares up to 10% lower than the average. March is the next cheapest month, with fares up to 5% lower than the average. April is the most expensive month to fly in 2023, with fares up to 10% higher than the average.

## Tips for Finding the Cheapest Flights in 2023

To get the best deals on flights in 2023, it’s important to be flexible with your travel dates. Try to book your flights at least three weeks in advance to get the best prices. Also, consider flying on Tuesdays and Wednesdays, as these are the cheapest days of the week to fly. Finally, avoid flying on major holidays such as Christmas and New Year’s Eve, as prices are usually higher during these times.

## Conclusion

Booking your flights in advance is the best way to get the cheapest fares in 2023. The cheapest months to fly are January and February, with the single cheapest travel day being January 21. The best days of the week to fly are Tuesdays and Wednesdays, followed by Thursdays. Prices are higher February 14-20 due to Valentine's Day and President's Day weekends. By following these tips, you can save money on your airfare and enjoy your trip even more.
