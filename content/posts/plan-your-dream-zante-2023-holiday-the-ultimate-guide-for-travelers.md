Zante, also known as Zakynthos, is a stunning island located in the Ionian Sea. Known for its breathtaking landscapes and crystal-clear waters, it is a top destination for travelers seeking a dream holiday experience. Plan your Dream Zante 2023 Holiday with our ultimate guide to help you make the most of your trip.

When to Go

Zante is a versatile destination that offers different experiences depending on the time of year you decide to visit. The summer months of July and August are the most popular seasons, but they can also be the hottest and most crowded. If you're looking to avoid the crowds and enjoy pleasant weather, consider visiting in May, June, September, or October.

How to Get There

Traveling to Zante is easy, with various airlines offering flights to the island's airport all year round. During the peak season, there are daily flights from many European cities, making it possible to reach Zante from almost anywhere in the world. Alternatively, you can take a ferry from Athens, Kefalonia, or Italy.

Where to Stay

Zante has a wide range of accommodation options available, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly hostels. Popular areas to stay include Zakynthos Town, Laganas, and Tsilivi. If you're looking for seclusion and relaxation, consider renting a villa in Vassilikos or Agios Sostis.

What to Do

Zante has something for everyone. Beach lovers will be spoilt for choice, as the island boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in Europe, including Navagio (Shipwreck) Beach, Keri Beach, and Porto Limnionas. Water sports enthusiasts can enjoy activities such as diving, snorkeling, water skiing, and banana boating. Another fun activity is taking a boat tour to visit the Blue Caves or to see the turtles.

The island is also rich in history and culture, with numerous historical sites dating back to ancient times. Some popular places to visit are the Byzantine Museum, the Venetian Fortress, and the Solomos Museum. There are plenty of tours available for those interested in learning more about the island's heritage.

For a fun night out, head to Laganas, Zante's party capital, where you can find a variety of bars and clubs to dance the night away. For a more relaxed evening, enjoy a traditional Greek meal at a waterfront restaurant and take in the beautiful sunset views.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Zante

1\. Navagio (Shipwreck) Beach - One of the most photographed beaches in the world, where you can see a rusty shipwreck washed up on the beach.

2\. Blue Caves - A series of natural sea caves, known for their striking blue color.

3\. Keri Lighthouse - A famous landmark located on the southern tip of the island, offering breathtaking views of the sea.

4\. Marathonisi Island - A small uninhabited island known for its crystal-clear waters and sandy beaches. It is a popular spot for turtle spotting and swimming.

5\. Gerakas Beach - A beautiful sandy beach on the eastern coast of the island, a nesting site for loggerhead sea turtles.

6\. Solomos Museum - A museum dedicated to the life and work of Zante's national poet, Dionysios Solomos.

7\. Zakynthos Town - The island's capital, with a charming old town center, Venetian fortress, and numerous restaurants and cafes.

8\. Agios Dionysios Church - A beautiful church located in the heart of Zakynthos Town, dedicated to the island's patron saint.

9\. Porto Limnionas - A small secluded cove surrounded by cliffs and turquoise waters, perfect for swimming and snorkeling.

10\. Water Village - A water park located in the center of the island, offering a range of slides and pools for all ages.

FAQs

1\. Is Zante a family-friendly destination?

Yes, Zante is a family-friendly destination, with plenty of activities and attractions suitable for children of all ages. Many of the beaches are safe and shallow, and there are several water parks and adventure playgrounds on the island.

2\. What is the best way to get around Zante?

The best way to get around Zante is by car or scooter. Many rental companies offer affordable rates, and driving is relatively easy, with well-maintained roads and plenty of parking. Alternatively, you can use public transport or hire a taxi.

3\. Is English commonly spoken in Zante?

Yes, most people in the tourist areas of Zante speak English, and many menus and signs are also in English.

4\. Do I need a visa to travel to Zante?

No, if you are a citizen of the European Union or the United States, you do not need a visa to travel to Zante. However, it is always advisable to check the latest visa regulations before you travel.

5\. Can I swim with turtles in Zante?

Yes, Zante is one of the few places in Europe where you can swim with the loggerhead sea turtles. The best time to spot them is between May and October, and there are several tours available that offer a chance to swim with the turtles in their natural habitat.

Conclusion

Zante is a beautiful island that offers a perfect balance of natural beauty, history, and nightlife. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or a fun family holiday, Zante has something to offer. With our ultimate guide, you'll be able to plan your dream Zante 2023 holiday with ease and make memories that will last a lifetime.
