Q: When is the best time to visit Rhodes?
A: The best time to visit Rhodes is from May to October, when the weather is warm and sunny.

Q: Do I need a visa to visit Rhodes?

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Rhodes

1\. Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes - this medieval castle was built in the 14th century and has been beautifully restored.

2\. Lindos - a picturesque village known for its whitewashed houses, narrow alleys, and stunning views of the sea.

3\. Rhodes Old Town - a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a labyrinth of cobbled streets, medieval walls, and historic buildings.

4\. Acropolis of Rhodes - a hilltop citadel with some of the best views of Rhodes.

5\. Valley of the Butterflies - a tranquil nature reserve with a stunning display of butterflies during the summer months.

6\. Ancient Kamiros - an archaeological site that was once a thriving ancient Greek city.

7\. Faliraki Water Park - a fun-filled water park with slides and rides for all ages.

8\. Anthony Quinn Bay - a picturesque cove with crystal-clear waters, named after the actor who starred in the movie "The Guns of Navarone".

9\. Tsambika Beach - a beautiful sandy beach with clear blue waters, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

10\. Lindos Beach - a secluded and pristine beach that offers breathtaking views of the Lindos Acropolis.
