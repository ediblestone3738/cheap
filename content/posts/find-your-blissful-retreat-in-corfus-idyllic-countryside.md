Find Your Blissful Retreat in Corfu's Idyllic Countryside

If you're searching for a destination that offers unspoiled natural beauty, breathtaking panoramic views, and peaceful surroundings, Corfu's idyllic countryside is the perfect getaway. Tucked away in the lush greenery of Greece's Ionian island, Find Your Blissful Retreat lies nestled in the hills, boasting a serene mix of comfort, tranquility, and luxury. Encompassing an enchanting expanse of hilltops, olive groves, and pines that converge with the cerulean Ionian sea, Find Your Blissful Retreat provides a unique vacation experience where indulgence meets nature.

About Find Your Blissful Retreat

Find Your Blissful Retreat is a collection of luxury villas tucked into three hectares of privately owned land, located in the serene hills of Corfu's countryside. The villas offer a perfect retreat for couples, families, and groups wanting supreme relaxation, privacy, and tranquility, away from the everyday hustle and bustle. The blissful retreat is a stunning mix of modern design and rustic charm, with each villa thoughtfully designed to offer a private sanctuary with panoramic views of the Mediterranean sea and surrounding hills.

The villas at Find Your Blissful Retreat are set apart from each other, making it a perfect choice for guests who desire solitude or privacy. Each villa has its private entrance, security gate, parking area, and an infinity pool with a stunning view of Corfu's lush greenery. Inside the villas, you’ll find stylish decor, modern amenities, and functional layouts that provide comfortable living spaces for guests. Each villa features multiple bedrooms, fully equipped kitchens, living rooms, and state-of-the-art entertainment systems to ensure that guests have everything they need to enjoy their stay.

Find Your Blissful Retreat's Location

Located in the northwest corner of Corfu, Find Your Blissful Retreat is easily accessible by car, rental or taxi from Corfu International Airport – the only international airport on the island – which is 35 kilometers away. The blissful retreat is also just 10 minutes away by car from the historical town of Paleo, a charming town steeped in history dating back to the 13th century. The town features stunning Venetian architecture, picturesque alleys, and charming restaurants. Guests can also explore Kassiopi village, another nearby town, and witness remnants of ancient Greece, including a Venetian castle, Roman baths, and Byzantine ruins.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in Find Your Blissful Retreat in Corfu's Idyllic Countryside

1\. Old Fortress: Located in the heart of the old town of Corfu, the fortress was built in the 15th century by the Venetians, and now serves as a popular tourist attraction.

2\. The Liston: The Liston is an elegant building that stretches the length of the Spianada, Corfu's most famous square.

3\. Achilleion Palace: The palace was built in 1890 by the Austro-Hungarian Empress Elisabeth and is one of the most popular tourist attractions on the island.

4\. Corfu Town: Corfu Town is a UNESCO World Heritage site and is renowned for its Venetian architecture, historic landmarks, and sophisticated shops.

5\. Mon Repos Palace: Mon Repos Palace is a neoclassical-style building surrounded by a beautiful park and is known for its stunning views of the Ionian Sea.

6\. Paleokastritsa: This picturesque village is situated on the west coast of Corfu and is famous for its beaches, clear waters, and limestone cliffs.

7\. Sidari: Sidari is a popular resort town that draws visitors with its turquoise waters and white sandy beaches.

8\. The Canal d'Amour: The Canal d'Amour is a unique formation of sandstone cliffs and caves that are the result of the region's geological movements.

9\. Mount Pantokrator: Mount Pantokrator is the highest peak of Corfu, and hiking or taking guided tours to the summit is a popular activity for tourists.

10\. The beaches: With crystalline waters and golden sand, the beaches in Corfu are a significant attraction for tourists, and some of the most popular ones include Glyfada, Canal d'Amour, Barbati, and Paleokastritsa.
