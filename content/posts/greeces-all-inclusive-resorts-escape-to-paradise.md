Greece's All-Inclusive Resorts: Escape to Paradise

Greece’s all-inclusive resorts are a great way to enjoy a luxurious holiday without the hassle of having to individually book flights, accommodation, food, drinks, and other amenities. All-inclusive resorts provide guests with a convenient way to have everything they need to relax and have a good time in a single, all-inclusive package. Among the numerous all-inclusive resorts in Greece, the Escape to Paradise is one that stands out from the rest due to its world-class facilities and exceptional services.

Escape to Paradise is located in the southwest of the Peloponnese region of Greece, specifically in the town of Petrohori by the Ionian Sea. This all-inclusive resort is popular for its serene and picturesque landscape that features the crystal-clear waters of the Ionian Sea and lush greenery. The resort is ideal for families, couples, and solo travelers who seek a luxurious, all-inclusive vacation experience.

The resort features various types of rooms, including standard rooms, family accommodation, and suites, amongst others. With over 75 rooms, guests can enjoy varying degrees of luxury and comfort while staying at the resort. The rooms are spacious, well-furnished, and equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, satellite TV, a mini-bar, and WiFi, amongst others. The rooms are cleaned daily, and the bedding is changed at the guests' request to improve their level of comfort.

All-inclusive packages are an excellent feature of Escape to Paradise, which includes all meals, snacks, drinks, and a range of leisure activities. Guests can enjoy a wide range of authentic Greek dishes and international cuisine as the resort’s restaurants provide a diverse range of dishes that cater to all dietary requirements. Additionally, guests can enjoy drinks, including non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks, and various types of beers, wines, and spirits, amongst others.

Escape to Paradise provides various leisure activities to keep guests entertained during their stay. These leisure activities include a swimming pool, a playground, a tennis court, and volleyball court, amongst other games. Additionally, guests can enjoy an array of water sports such as kayaking, windsurfing, and paddle boats, amongst others. The resort also has a spa center that offers a wide range of services such as massages, facials, pedicure, and manicure, amongst others.
