Why choose Santorini for your next holiday destination?
Santorini is one of the most beautiful and serene islands in the world, and it has so much to offer. Here are some reasons why you should choose Santorini as your next holiday destination;

1\. Gorgeous Sunsets: Santorini is renowned for its breathtaking sunset views. The sunset on this island is so stunning that people flock from all over the world to watch it.

2\. Lovely Beaches: Santorini has some lovely beaches, and the waters are incredibly clear. Whether you fancy a private cove or a public beach with amenities, Santorini has it all.

3\. Fantastic food: Santorini offers a range of delicious Greek delicacies that will leave your taste buds craving for more. From fresh seafood to traditional Greek cuisine, Santorini won’t disappoint you.

4\. Fascinating History: Santorini has a rich history dating back to prehistoric times, and the island offers a plethora of archaeological sites, museums, and ancient ruins.

Discovering the hidden gems of Santorini with our best package holidays
Although Santorini is widely known for its picturesque villages and stunning views, there are many hidden gems waiting to be discovered, and the best way to do so is by booking our best package holidays. Here are some places you can explore:

1\. Akrotiri Archaeological Site: Akrotiri is a prehistoric settlement built in the Late Neolithic period that was preserved by volcanic ash. This archaeological site is well-preserved, and visitors can walk through the ancient city streets to discover its secrets.

2\. Mesa Gonia: This abandoned village is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. The village was destroyed by an earthquake in 1956, and many of the buildings and structures remain intact.

3\. The Lost Atlantis Experience: This fascinating museum in Santorini offers visitors an insight into the legend of the lost city of Atlantis. The museum features interactive exhibits and multimedia displays that bring the myth of Atlantis to life.

4\. Pyrgos Village: Pyrgos is a charming village located at the highest point of Santorini. Visitors can enjoy exploring the narrow streets, admiring the traditional houses, and taking in the panoramic views of the island.

5\. Perissa Black Sand Beach: This beach is located on the southeast coast of Santorini and is well-known for its black sand. The beach is surrounded by cliffs, which offer a picturesque backdrop for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

6\. Cape Columbo Beach: This beach is located on the northeast coast of Santorini and is one of the most secluded beaches on the island. The water is crystal clear, and the beach is surrounded by dramatic cliffs.

7\. Vlychada Beach: Vlychada is a unique beach located on the southwest coast of Santorini. The beach is famous for its distinct lunar landscape, and its white cliffs and rock formations.

8\. Megalochori Village: This traditional village is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Visitors can wander the cobbled streets, admire the old houses, and soak in the authentic Greek charm.

9\. Wine Tasting at Wineries: Santorini is known for its excellent wines, and visitors can enjoy wine tasting tours at the numerous vineyards and wineries located on the island.

10\. Caldera Hiking Trail: For the adventurous visitors, the Caldera Hiking Trail is a must-do activity. This trail takes visitors through some of Santorini’s most stunning landscapes, including cliffs, calderas, and volcanic eruptions.

FAQs:

1\. What is the best time of year to visit Santorini?
The best time to visit Santorini is from April to November, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the peak season is July and August, and the island can become very crowded.

2\. How long should I stay in Santorini?
We recommend staying at least three to four days to explore the island and discover its hidden gems.

3\. What is the main language spoken in Santorini?
The official language of Greece is Greek, and most people in Santorini speak it. However, English is widely spoken in tourist areas.

4\. Do I need a visa to visit Santorini?
If you are a citizen of the European Union or the United States, you do not need a visa to visit Greece for up to 90 days.

5\. Is it safe to travel to Santorini?
Yes, Santorini is a safe destination for tourists. However, it is always recommended to take normal safety precautions while traveling, including safeguarding your belongings and staying aware of your surroundings.
