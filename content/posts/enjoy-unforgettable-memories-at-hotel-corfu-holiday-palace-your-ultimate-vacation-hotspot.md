Located in Kanoni, a picturesque coastal village on the island of Corfu, Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is the ultimate vacation hotspot that promises to offer an unforgettable holiday experience for travelers seeking a luxurious stay in a scenic location. The hotel features a unique architectural design that incorporates traditional Corfiot elements with modern aesthetics, while the lush surroundings offer breathtaking views of the Ionian Sea and the Pontikonisi Island. From a variety of accommodation options to its multiple dining and recreational facilities, Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is an ideal choice for travelers who wish to explore the rich history and culture of Corfu while enjoying a luxurious holiday atmosphere.

Accommodation Options:

Guests at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace can choose from a range of accommodation options that cater to their needs and preferences. All the rooms are elegantly decorated with modern amenities that provide a comfortable and restful stay. From the classic standard rooms to the deluxe superior sea view rooms, the hotel offers options that suit travelers' different budgets and requirements. For those seeking a more spacious and luxurious stay, the Executive Suites and the Presidential Suite come with separate living areas, private balconies, and unparalleled sea views that make for an indulgent stay.

Dining Options:

Food enthusiasts are guaranteed a delightful culinary experience at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace, as the hotel's multiple dining options cater to varied tastes and preferences. The Main Restaurant offers a lavish breakfast buffet with a choice of hot and cold dishes, while the a la carte lunch and dinner menus serve Greek and International cuisine using fresh local ingredients. The Panoramic Restaurant, situated on the tenth floor of the hotel, offers a more upscale dining experience with its gourmet Mediterranean menu and panoramic sea views. For those looking for a casual drink, the Lobby Bar and the Pool Bar serve refreshing cocktails and beverages throughout the day.

Recreational Facilities:

Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is equipped with multiple recreational facilities that promise to keep guests entertained throughout their stay. Guests can enjoy a refreshing swim in the hotel's two outdoor swimming pools, including one with a water slide. The hotel boasts a private beach accessible by a cable car, where guests can bask in the sun, indulge in water sports and enjoy the crystal-clear waters of the Ionian Sea. The in-house Wellness Center offers a range of spa treatments, including massages, facials, and aromatherapy, making for a rejuvenating experience. The hotel also provides a fully equipped gym and a tennis court, catering to fitness enthusiasts.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions near Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace:

1\. Achilleion Palace - A neoclassical palace built by Empress Elisabeth of Austria, located 6 km from the hotel.
2\. Old Town of Corfu - A UNESCO World Heritage site that captures the island's rich history and culture, located 5 km from the hotel.
3\. Vlacherna Monastery - A picturesque 17th-century monastery situated on a small island just off the coast, within walking distance from the hotel.
4\. Mon Repos Palace - A restored neoclassical summer palace built for the British Commissioner, located 7 km from the hotel.
5\. Sidari Beach - A popular sandy beach with crystal-clear waters and stunning rock formations, located 35 km from the hotel.
6\. Paleokastritsa Beach - A scenic beach that offers spectacular views of the Ionian sea, located 17 km from the hotel.
7\. Corfu Island Cruise - A full-day excursion to explore the stunning coastline and hidden coves of Corfu, departing from the hotel.
8\. Glyfada Beach - A picturesque beach with fine white sand and turquoise waters, located 10 km from the hotel.
9\. Canal d'Amour - A unique rock formation that has become one of Corfu's top attractions, located 35 km from the hotel.
10\. Mount Pantokrator - The highest mountain on Corfu, offering panoramic views of the island, located 30 km from the hotel.
