Sandy Beaches, Crystal Waters, and Sunsets like No Other – Welcome to Corfu!

If you're looking for a European destination that offers warm weather, stunning beaches, and crystal clear waters, then look no further than Corfu, Greece. This small island is a treasure trove of natural beauty, historical landmarks, and delicious cuisine.

With its long coastline, Corfu is blessed with an abundance of sandy beaches, each one offering something unique. Whether you're a fan of secluded coves or lively resorts, you'll find a beach to suit your tastes here. And with so many options, you can easily spend a week exploring and sunbathing on Corfu's beaches. Here are just a few of the best.

1\. Paleokastritsa Beach

Paleokastritsa Beach is one of the most famous beaches in Corfu, known for its crystal clear waters and picturesque surroundings. Located in the north-west coast of the island, this beach boasts a stunning backdrop of lush greenery and towering cliffs.

2\. Glyfada Beach

If you're looking for a wide sandy beach with plenty of facilities, then Glyfada Beach is the place to go. Located on the west coast of Corfu, this lively beach offers sun beds, umbrellas, water sports, and a range of cafes and restaurants.

3\. Sidari Beach

Sidari Beach is located in the north of Corfu and is known for its unique rock formations. Visitors can swim through the natural tunnel in the rocks, giving this beach a sense of adventure and excitement.

4\. Agios Gordios Beach

Agios Gordios Beach is located in the west of Corfu and is a popular choice for families. The beach is wide and sandy, with plenty of shallow waters for children to play in. There are also several restaurants and cafes nearby.

5\. Myrtiotissa Beach

Myrtiotissa Beach is a popular choice for those seeking a quiet, secluded beach. Located on the west coast of Corfu, this beach is hidden away behind steep cliffs, giving it a sense of privacy and seclusion.

6\. Kavos Beach

Kavos Beach is located in the south of Corfu and is known for its lively atmosphere. With plenty of beach bars and restaurants, this is the perfect place to go if you're looking for a party scene.

7\. Issos Beach

Issos Beach is located in the south of Corfu and is known for its stunning sand dunes. This beach is a great choice for those looking for something unique and different.

8\. Kontogialos Beach

Kontogialos Beach is located in the west of Corfu and is a great choice for those seeking a laid-back atmosphere. This beach is surrounded by trees, giving it a natural feel.

9\. Agios Georgios North Beach

Agios Georgios North Beach is located in the north of Corfu and is a great choice for those seeking something peaceful and quiet. This beach is surrounded by stunning scenery and offers a great chance to relax and unwind.

10\. Arillas Beach

Arillas Beach is located in the north-west of Corfu and is known for its crystal clear waters. This beach is a great choice for those looking for a relaxing getaway, with plenty of sun beds and umbrellas available.

After a long day at the beach, be sure to check out Corfu's stunning sunsets. Watching the sun go down on this island is a truly magical experience, with the orange and pink hues reflecting off the sea.

FAQs

Q: What airport should I fly into to visit Corfu?

A: The international airport of Corfu "Ioannis Kapodistrias" (CFU) is located just a few kilometres south of the capital city of Corfu.

Q: What are the best months to visit Corfu?

A: The best time to visit Corfu is between June and September when the weather is sunny and warm.

Q: What is the currency used in Corfu?

A: The currency used in Corfu is the Euro.

Q: Is Corfu a safe travel destination?

A: Yes, Corfu is considered a safe travel destination with low crime rates.

Q: What are the must-try dishes in Corfu?

A: Some must-try dishes in Corfu include Pastitsada (a beef casserole with pasta), Sofrito (veal stew), and Bourdeto (spicy fish stew).

A visit to Corfu offers a blend of adventure, relaxation and a chance to escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, so be sure to add this beautiful island to your travel bucket list.
