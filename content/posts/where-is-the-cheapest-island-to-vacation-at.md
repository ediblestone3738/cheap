Known as “The Garden Isle”, Kauai is one of the best cheap islands to visit in the USA. With its lush tropical rainforest, stunning beaches, and stunning mountain views, Kauai is a great destination for a budget-friendly getaway. The island offers plenty of activities, from snorkeling and kayaking to hiking and exploring the local culture. There are also plenty of affordable accommodation options, from camping and glamping to budget-friendly hotels.
