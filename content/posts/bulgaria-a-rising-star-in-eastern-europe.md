# Discover Bulgaria: A Rising Star in Eastern Europe

Bulgaria is an Eastern European country with a rich cultural heritage, stunning scenery, and a vibrant atmosphere. It is a rapidly growing tourist destination, offering a wealth of activities and attractions for visitors to explore. From the bustling capital city of Sofia to the picturesque Black Sea coast, Bulgaria is a country that has something for everyone.

## History and Culture

Bulgaria has a long and fascinating history that dates back to the 7th century. The country was once part of the powerful Ottoman Empire and later became part of the Soviet Union. This rich cultural heritage has left a lasting impression on the country and its people. Visitors can explore the ancient ruins of Thracian tombs and medieval churches, or visit the many museums and galleries dedicated to the country’s past.

## Outdoor Activities

Bulgaria is home to some of the most stunning natural landscapes in Europe. From the majestic Pirin Mountains to the beautiful Black Sea Coast, there are plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities. Hiking, mountain biking, and skiing are popular activities, while the country’s many rivers and lakes provide the perfect setting for kayaking and rafting.

## Food and Drink

Bulgaria is known for its delicious cuisine, with a range of dishes that combine traditional Bulgarian flavors with influences from the country’s past. Popular dishes include banitsa (a pastry filled with cheese and spinach) and musaka (a potato and meat casserole). The country is also home to some of the best wines in the region, with local varieties such as Mavrud and Melnik becoming increasingly popular.

## FAQs

### What is the best time to visit Bulgaria?

The best time to visit Bulgaria is during the summer months (May to September). This is when the weather is at its best and the country is at its busiest.

### What is the currency in Bulgaria?

The currency in Bulgaria is the Bulgarian Lev (BGN).

### What language is spoken in Bulgaria?

The official language of Bulgaria is Bulgarian, although English is widely spoken in the major cities.
