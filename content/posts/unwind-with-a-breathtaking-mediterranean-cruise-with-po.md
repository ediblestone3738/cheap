As you step aboard P&O's Mediterranean cruise, you know that you're in for an unforgettable experience. If you're looking to unwind and explore diverse cultures, tastes and landscapes, this trip is perfect for you!

The Mediterranean boasts some of the world's most breathtaking views and P&O offer a unique and extraordinary way to experience it all. What sets P&O apart from other cruising experiences is the attention to detail and service that can be found throughout the entire journey. From the moment you step foot on the ship to the last farewell, you will be treated like royalty.

Here's what you can expect from a breathtaking Mediterranean cruise with P&O:

Relaxation at Its Best

A Mediterranean cruise with P&O is the perfect vacation to unwind and relax. The ship offers a wide range of amenities that cater to your every desire. You can enjoy a luxurious spa treatment, unwind in the pool, or enjoy a cocktail while watching the sunset on the deck.

For those who enjoy beachside activities, the P&O Mediterranean cruise offers a chance to soak up the sun and explore the sand and sea. You'll have the opportunity to stop at some of the Mediterranean's most beautiful beaches, with crystal clear waters and an array of water sports activities.

Exquisite Dining

Perhaps the biggest draw of a P&O Mediterranean cruise is the dining options available. The ship offers an array of restaurants to cater to every taste and palette. Whether you're in the mood for a juicy steak, a rich pasta dish or a light Mediterranean salad, you'll find it on board.

The ship also has a variety of bars and lounges, perfect for socializing, relaxing, and enjoying some world-class drinks. You can also indulge in some of the finest wines and spirits from around the world, with sommeliers on hand to help you make the best selection.

Adventurous Destinations

A Mediterranean cruise with P&O is also an opportunity to visit some of the most diverse and exotic destinations in Europe. You'll get to stop at some of the Mediterranean's most iconic locations, including the French and Italian Riviera, the Spanish coast, Croatia, and Greece.

Each port of call offers a unique experience, culture, and cuisine. You'll have the chance to explore ancient ruins, breathtaking landscapes, and sample authentic Mediterranean food, all while soaking up the Mediterranean sun.

FAQs

1\. What's the best time to go on a Mediterranean cruise with P&O?

The best time to go on a P&O Mediterranean cruise is between May and October, which is the peak season for traveling in the region. The weather during this time is generally sunny and warm, making it perfect for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

2\. What's the dress code like for a P&O Mediterranean cruise?

When it comes to the dress code on a P&O Mediterranean cruise, it's all about comfort and style. During the day, you can wear casual clothes, such as shorts, t-shirts, and sandals. In the evenings, there is a smart-casual dress code, with men required to wear collared shirts and women wearing dresses or skirts.

3\. Are there any excursions that are included on a P&O Mediterranean cruise?

There are a range of excursions that are available on a P&O Mediterranean cruise, but they are not included in the cost of the cruise. However, guests can book excursions through P&O's website or through the onboard excursion desk.

4\. Do I need a visa for a P&O Mediterranean cruise?

It depends on your nationality and the countries you will be visiting. It's important to check with the relevant authorities to see if you need a visa for the countries you will be visiting.

5\. Can I bring my own alcohol onboard a P&O Mediterranean cruise?

P&O has a strict policy on bringing alcohol onboard the ship. It's not allowed, and any alcohol found will be confiscated.
