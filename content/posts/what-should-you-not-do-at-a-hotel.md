# What Should You Not Do at a Hotel?

Hotels are a great place to stay for a night or two, but there are certain things you should not do in a hotel room. Doing any of these things can result in fines, damage to the hotel, or even legal trouble. Here are the top 10 things you must not do in a hotel room.

## \#10 Lying After Damaging Something

It is never a good idea to lie about damaging something in a hotel room. If you break something, you should always be honest and tell the hotel staff. Lying about it can lead to fines and other penalties.

## \#9 Leaving Jewelry and Money

Leaving jewelry and money in a hotel room is a bad idea. It is always best to keep these items with you or in a safe. Hotel staff may not be able to protect your items from theft, so it is best to take them with you.

## \#8 Keeping Bathroom Door Unlocked when Taking a Shower

It is important to keep the bathroom door locked when taking a shower. This is for your own safety and the safety of others. It is also important to make sure the door is locked when you leave the room.

## \#7 Sneaking In Your Pets

Most hotels do not allow pets in the rooms. If you try to sneak your pet into the room, you could be fined or even asked to leave. It is best to check with the hotel before bringing your pet.

## \#6 Smoking

Smoking is not allowed in most hotel rooms. If you do smoke in the room, you could be fined or asked to leave. It is best to check with the hotel before smoking in the room.

## \#5 Stealing from the Hotel

Stealing from the hotel is a serious offense and can result in legal trouble. If you take anything from the hotel, you should always pay for it.

## \#4 Drinking Tap Water

It is not a good idea to drink the tap water in a hotel room. The water may not be safe to drink and could make you sick. It is best to buy bottled water or use a filter.

## \#3 Cheating the Minibar

Cheating the minibar is never a good idea. If you are caught, you could be fined or asked to leave. It is best to pay for what you take and not try to cheat the system.

## \#2 Taking Unauthorized Photos

Taking unauthorized photos in a hotel room is not allowed. If you are caught, you could be fined or asked to leave. It is best to ask permission before taking photos in the room.

## \#1 Making Too Much Noise

Making too much noise in a hotel room is not allowed. If you are caught, you could be fined or asked to leave. It is best to keep the noise level down and be respectful of other guests.

# Conclusion

Hotels are a great place to stay for a night or two, but there are certain things you should not do in a hotel room. Doing any of these things can result in fines, damage to the hotel, or even legal trouble. It is important to be respectful of the hotel and its guests when staying in a hotel room.
