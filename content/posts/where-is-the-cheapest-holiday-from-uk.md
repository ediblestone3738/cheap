# Where is the Cheapest Holiday from the UK?

The UK is a great place to take a holiday, but if you’re looking for a cheap holiday destination, you’ll have to look further afield. Fortunately, there are plenty of options for budget-conscious travellers. Here are some of the best and cheapest holiday destinations for 2023.

## Turkey

Turkey is a great option for a cheap holiday. The Turkish lira has been struggling against the British pound in recent years, making it a great value destination. Turkey is also home to some stunning beaches, historical sites, and delicious cuisine. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an adventure-filled cultural experience, Turkey has something for everyone.

## Lisbon, Portugal

Lisbon is a great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The Portuguese capital is a beautiful city, with stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and plenty of cultural attractions. The city is also very affordable, with plenty of budget-friendly accommodation and dining options.

## Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is another great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The country is home to some stunning beaches, ancient temples, and plenty of cultural attractions. The Sri Lankan rupee is also very weak against the British pound, making it a great value destination.

## The Gambia

The Gambia is a great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The country is home to some stunning beaches, lush jungles, and plenty of wildlife. The Gambian dalasi is also very weak against the British pound, making it a great value destination.

## Mauritius

Mauritius is a great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The island nation is home to some stunning beaches, lush jungles, and plenty of wildlife. The Mauritian rupee is also very weak against the British pound, making it a great value destination.

## Belize

Belize is a great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The country is home to some stunning beaches, lush jungles, and plenty of wildlife. The Belizean dollar is also very weak against the British pound, making it a great value destination.

## Jamaica

Jamaica is a great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The country is home to some stunning beaches, lush jungles, and plenty of wildlife. The Jamaican dollar is also very weak against the British pound, making it a great value destination.

## Bolivia

Bolivia is a great option for a cheap holiday from the UK. The country is home to some stunning landscapes, ancient ruins, and plenty of cultural attractions. The Bolivian boliviano is also very weak against the British pound, making it a great value destination.

No matter where you choose to go, there are plenty of great options for a cheap holiday from the UK. With the British pound gaining ground on some other faltering currencies, now is the perfect time to plan your next budget-friendly getaway. So, what are you waiting for?
