Current Situation in Syria

The Syrian opposition is a loosely organized and diverse group of political and military factions dedicated to eliminating the regime of President Bashar al-Assad. Since the start of the Syrian civil war in 2011, the opposition has been fighting against the Syrian government and its allies, including Russia and Iran.

In March 2021, the Biden administration announced that it was increasing support for the Syrian opposition by providing aid and assistance worth $10 million. This came after years of limited engagement with the opposition under the previous administration.

The US has also been working with Turkey, which hosts a large number of Syrian refugees, to create a safe zone within Syria's borders. This zone would be a sanctuary for the opposition to operate and would also offer a haven for Syrian civilians. The ultimate goal of these efforts is to create a stable and democratic Syria that respects human rights and offers a better future for its citizens.

Q: What is the best time to visit Syria?

A: The best time to visit Syria is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. Summers can be oppressively hot, while winters can be cold and wet.
