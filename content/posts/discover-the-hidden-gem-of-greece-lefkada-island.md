Discover the Hidden Gem of Greece: Lefkada Island

Lefkada Island is one of the most intriguing hidden gems in Greece that everyone should experience. This island is located in the Ionian Sea, connected to the mainland Greece by a bridge, and it is part of the Eptanisa island group. The island possesses a unique charm that will leave any visitor in awe. The crystal-clear turquoise water, the stunning beaches, the serene atmosphere, and the picturesque villages, will make your trip to Lefkada Island worth every penny. Here's everything you need to know about this excellent Greek Island.

How to Get to Lefkada Island?

To get to Lefkada Island, you can either fly directly to the island or take a direct road from Athens, which takes about 4-5 hours. The island has no airport, but the nearest airport to the island is the Aktion National Airport, which is approximately 20 km away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi, take a bus or a rental car to the island.

What are the Best Times to Visit Lefkada Island?

The best times to visit Lefkada Island are during the months of May to October, when the weather is warmer and the climate is mild. During these months, the island is filled with a myriad of events and festivals that showcase the unique way of life and rich culture of the Lefkadian people.

What is the Local Currency Used in Lefkada Island?

The local currency used in Lefkada Island is the Euro.

What Are the Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Lefkada Island?

1\. Porto Katsiki Beach- This beach is one of the most beautiful beaches on Lefkada Island, known for its crystal clear waters, soft white sands, and natural rock formations.

2\. Egremni Beach- Another breathtaking beach, which is considered one of the best beaches in Europe. Egremni Beach offers a stunning view of the surrounding mountain ranges and the deep blue sea.

3\. Nidri Waterfalls- A natural wonder on Lefkada Island, Nidri Waterfalls is hidden among lush green vegetation and is a haven for nature lovers.

4\. Agiofili Beach- This beach can only be reached by boat or by foot, making it a secluded and tranquil spot. Agiofili Beach is surrounded by towering cliffs and turquoise waters that offer an unforgettable swimming experience.

5\. Lefkada Town- The capital of the island, Lefkada Town, is a picturesque town with a Venetian canal and colorful houses. The town has cafes, restaurants, and shops that offer a unique shopping experience, offering local handmade crafts and souvenirs.

6\. Kathisma Beach- This beach offers a magnificent panoramic view of the sea, with its crystal-clear waters and natural surroundings. Kathisma Beach is one of the best places on the island to enjoy a peaceful swim.

7\. Meganisi Island- This island is located just a short distance away from Lefkada Island and can be reached by ferry. Meganisi Island is a peaceful and secluded place, with natural beauty and small villages.

8\. Karya Village- This village is known for its famous embroidery and weaving industry. Karya Village has a unique atmosphere, with picturesque stone houses, mountainous landscapes and traditional taverns that offer local cuisine.

9\. Palairos- This village is a fishing village and popular tourist destination that offers unique and breathtaking views of the Ionian Sea. Palairos is a perfect spot for swimming, fishing, and boating activities.

10\. Aenos Monastery- Located on top of a hill in Lefkada Town, this monastery offers stunning views of the island, as well as the Ionian sea. The sanctuary is an important place of worship for the Orthodox Christians and a popular attraction for tourists.

How is the Accommodation on Lefkada Island?

Lefkada Island has a range of accommodation options that cater to every taste, style, and budget. From luxury hotels, villas, and resorts to traditional guesthouses, rental apartments, and camping sites, there is something for everyone. Some of the popular hotels on the island include the Ionian Blue Hotel, the Nirikos Hotel, and the Ionian Islands Hotel.

In Conclusion

Lefkada Island is a hidden gem in Greece that offers a unique insight into the authentic Greek way of life. From the beautiful beaches, the natural landscapes, and the picturesque villages to the cultural landmarks, the mouth-watering cuisine, and the hospitable locals, every aspect of this island is a treat for the senses. Whether you're looking for a peaceful escape, a romantic getaway or an adventure-packed holiday, Lefkada Island is the perfect destination.
