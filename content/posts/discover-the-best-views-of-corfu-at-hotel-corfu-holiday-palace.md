With its stunning coastline, crystal-clear waters, and picturesque villages, Corfu is a truly enchanting destination. One of the best places to discover the island's beauty is Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace. Nestled on a hillside overlooking the sea, this luxurious resort provides guests with some of the best views of Corfu. Whether you are looking to relax on the beach, explore the island's rich cultural heritage, or indulge in the local cuisine, Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is the perfect base for your Corfu adventure.

The resort's well-appointed rooms and suites are designed to offer guests the ultimate in comfort and luxury. Each room is equipped with all the necessary amenities to make your stay as comfortable as possible, including air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, and a minibar. The guest rooms are tastefully decorated in a modern style and feature balconies or terraces with breathtaking sea views.

At Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace, guests can enjoy a wide range of dining options. The hotel's main restaurant, Nautilus, serves exquisite local and international cuisine in a relaxed and elegant setting. The restaurant's terrace overlooks the sea, providing guests with a truly memorable dining experience. The hotel's other dining options include the poolside Mediterraneo restaurant, which serves light snacks and drinks throughout the day, and the sophisticated Piano Bar, which offers a wide range of cocktails and live music in the evenings.

If you are looking to relax and unwind during your stay at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace, there are plenty of options to choose from. The hotel features a large swimming pool surrounded by sun loungers and parasols, as well as a private beach with crystal-clear waters. The hotel's spa offers a range of treatments and therapies to help you relax and rejuvenate, including massages, facials, and body wraps. For those looking to stay active, the hotel also offers a gym, tennis court, and mini-golf course.

Corfu is an island rich in history and culture, and Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is the perfect base from which to explore its many attractions. Some of the island's top tourist attractions include:

1\. Achilleion Palace - This stunning palace was built by Empress Elisabeth of Austria in the 19th century and features beautiful gardens and impressive architecture.

2\. Old Town of Corfu - This UNESCO World Heritage site is a maze of narrow streets and alleys lined with traditional houses and shops.

3\. Mon Repos Palace - Once the summer residence of the Greek royal family, this elegant palace now houses a museum and art gallery.

4\. Canal d'Amour - Located on the northwest coast of the island, this series of coves and rock formations is a popular spot for swimming and snorkeling.

5\. Mount Pantokrator - The highest point on the island offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and the sea.

6\. Paleokastritsa Beach - One of the most popular beaches on the island, this sandy beach offers crystal-clear waters and plenty of facilities for visitors.

7\. Vlacherna Monastery - This picturesque monastery is located on a small island just off the coast of Corfu and is a popular spot for photos.

8\. Corfu Museum of Asian Art - This museum houses a large collection of Asian art and artifacts from China, Japan, and Southeast Asia.

9\. The Liston - This elegant promenade in the heart of Corfu Town is lined with cafes, restaurants, and shops and is a popular spot for people-watching.

10\. Mouse Island - Located off the southern coast of Corfu, this small island is home to a charming church and offers stunning views of the sea.
