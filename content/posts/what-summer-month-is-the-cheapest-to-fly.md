# What Summer Month is the Cheapest to Fly?

Summertime is a great time to take a vacation and explore the world. But, with all the added costs of flights, it can be difficult to find a good deal. The good news is that there are certain months during the summer that are cheaper to fly than others. Here, we’ll discuss the best months to fly during the summer and how to get the best deals.

## June

The first half of June is a relatively cheap time to fly, compared to prices that kick in June 22, which marks the start of summer's peak season. Prices tend to be lower in the first half of June, so if you’re looking for a good deal, try to book your flight before June 22.

## August

August is another great month to fly if you’re looking for a good deal. Prices tend to be lower after August 25, when cheaper fall fares start to kick in. It gets even cheaper in September and October, so if you’re looking for the best deal, try to book your flight after August 25.

## Tips for Finding the Best Deals

When it comes to finding the best deals on flights, there are a few tips you can follow. First, try to be flexible with your dates. Prices tend to fluctuate, so if you’re able to be flexible with your dates, you may be able to find a better deal.

Second, try to book your flight in advance. Prices tend to go up the closer you get to the date of the flight, so if you’re able to book your flight a few weeks in advance, you may be able to get a better deal.

Finally, try to compare prices. There are a lot of different websites and apps that allow you to compare prices, so take the time to compare prices and find the best deal.

## Conclusion

Finding the best deals on flights during the summer can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find the best deals. The best months to fly during the summer are June and August, and you can get the best deals by being flexible with your dates, booking in advance, and comparing prices.

So, if you’re looking for a good deal on a flight this summer, keep these tips in mind and you’ll be sure to find the best deal.
