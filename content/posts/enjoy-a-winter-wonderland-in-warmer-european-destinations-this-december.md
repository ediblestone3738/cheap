# Enjoy a Winter Wonderland in Warmer European Destinations This December

The winter season is upon us, and many of us are dreaming of a white Christmas. But if you’re looking to escape the cold winter weather, there are plenty of warmer European destinations you can visit this December. From sunny beaches to charming cities, these destinations will make you feel like you’re in a winter wonderland.

## Malta

Malta is a small island nation located in the Mediterranean Sea. With its mild climate and sunny beaches, Malta is the perfect destination for a winter getaway. December is a great time to visit Malta, as temperatures remain mild and the island is relatively quiet. Spend your days lounging on the beach and exploring the island’s many sights. Malta is also home to some of the best diving spots in Europe, so be sure to take a dive and explore the underwater world.

## Portugal

Portugal is a great destination for those looking for a winter escape. With its mild climate and beautiful beaches, Portugal is the perfect place to enjoy a winter wonderland. In December, temperatures remain mild and the days are usually sunny. There are plenty of activities to enjoy in Portugal, from exploring the charming cities of Lisbon and Porto to visiting the stunning beaches of the Algarve. Portugal is also home to some of the best seafood in Europe, so be sure to sample some of the local delicacies.

## Spain

Spain is another great destination for those looking for a winter escape. With its warm climate and stunning beaches, Spain is the perfect place to enjoy a winter wonderland. In December, temperatures remain mild and the days are usually sunny. There are plenty of activities to enjoy in Spain, from exploring the charming cities of Madrid and Barcelona to visiting the stunning beaches of the Costa Brava. Spain is also home to some of the best food in Europe, so be sure to sample some of the local delicacies.
