# 11 Awesome Ways To Sleep Completely For Free While Travelling The World

Travelling the world can be an amazing experience, but it can also be expensive. One of the biggest costs when travelling is accommodation, so it’s no surprise that many people are looking for ways to save money on their accommodation costs. If you’re looking for ways to sleep for free while travelling the world, then you’ve come to the right place. Here are 11 awesome ways to sleep for free while travelling the world.

## \#1 - Use Hospitality Exchange Platforms

Hospitality exchange platforms are a great way to find free accommodation while travelling. These platforms allow you to stay with local hosts in exchange for a cultural exchange. You can stay with a host for a few days or even a few weeks, depending on the arrangement. Popular hospitality exchange platforms include Couchsurfing, BeWelcome, and Hospitality Club.

## \#2 - Camp Outdoors

If you’re travelling in an area where camping is allowed, then camping outdoors is a great way to save money on accommodation. All you need is a tent, sleeping bag, and some basic camping gear. You can camp in national parks, forests, or even on the beach. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

## \#3 - Stay With a Lover

If you’re lucky enough to have a lover in the city you’re travelling to, then you can stay with them for free. This is a great way to save money on accommodation and also to spend some quality time with your loved one. Just make sure to be respectful of their space and follow any house rules they may have.

## \#4 - Couchsurf

Couchsurfing is a great way to find free accommodation while travelling. All you need to do is sign up for a free account on the Couchsurfing website and search for hosts in the city you’re travelling to. You can then contact potential hosts and arrange to stay with them for free.

## \#5 - Sleep Under the Stars on Beach Sun Loungers

If you’re travelling to a beach destination, then you can sleep under the stars on beach sun loungers. All you need is a sleeping bag and some basic camping gear. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

## \#6 - Stay in Hostels

Hostels are a great way to save money on accommodation while travelling. Hostels offer dorm-style rooms with shared bathrooms and kitchens. They are usually much cheaper than hotels and can be a great way to meet other travellers.

## \#7 - Sleep in Airports

If you’re stuck in an airport overnight, then you can sleep in the airport. Most airports have comfortable chairs and couches that you can sleep on. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

## \#8 - Sleep in Monasteries, Nunneries & Churches

If you’re travelling to a religious destination, then you can stay in monasteries, nunneries, and churches for free. Most religious institutions offer free accommodation to travellers in exchange for a donation. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

## \#9 - Take Advantage of Sleeper Buses and Trains

Sleeper buses and trains are a great way to save money on accommodation while travelling. These buses and trains offer comfortable beds and are usually much cheaper than hotels. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

## \#10 - Sleep in Bus and Train Stations

If you’re stuck in a bus or train station overnight, then you can sleep in the station. Most stations have comfortable chairs and couches that you can sleep on. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

## \#11 - Stay in Free Accommodation

If you’re travelling to a city or town, then you can often find free accommodation. This could be in the form of a free hostel, a friend’s house, or even a spare room in someone’s house. Just make sure to check the local laws and regulations before you set up camp.

Travelling the world can be an amazing experience, but it can also be expensive. However, with these 11 awesome ways to sleep for free while travelling the world, you can save money on accommodation and still have an amazing experience. So what are you waiting for? Get out there and start exploring!
