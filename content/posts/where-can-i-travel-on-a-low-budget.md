# 10 Best Places to Travel on a Budget

Budget travel is an attractive option for those who want to explore the world without breaking the bank. Fortunately, there are a handful of destinations out there that make budget travel easy, fun, and safe. Here are 10 of the best places to travel on a budget.

## Thailand

Thailand is one of the most popular budget travel destinations in the world. It has something for everyone, from the stunning beaches of the south to the bustling cities of the north. Accommodation is cheap, and food is even cheaper. Plus, the locals are incredibly friendly and welcoming.

## Central America

Central America is a great place to explore on a budget. From the ancient ruins of Mexico to the lush jungles of Costa Rica, there is something for everyone. Accommodation is cheap, and you can find great deals on flights. Plus, the locals are incredibly friendly and welcoming.

## Cambodia

Cambodia is a great destination for budget travelers. It has stunning temples, beautiful beaches, and friendly locals. Accommodation is cheap, and food is even cheaper. Plus, there are plenty of activities to keep you busy, from exploring the ancient temples to taking a boat ride down the Mekong River.

## The Balkans

The Balkans are a great place to explore on a budget. From the stunning beaches of Croatia to the majestic mountains of Bosnia and Herzegovina, there is something for everyone. Accommodation is cheap, and you can find great deals on flights. Plus, the locals are incredibly friendly and welcoming.

## China

China is a great destination for budget travelers. It has stunning cities, beautiful countryside, and friendly locals. Accommodation is cheap, and food is even cheaper. Plus, there are plenty of activities to keep you busy, from exploring the Great Wall to taking a boat ride down the Yangtze River.

## India

India is a great destination for budget travelers. It has stunning temples, beautiful beaches, and friendly locals. Accommodation is cheap, and food is even cheaper. Plus, there are plenty of activities to keep you busy, from exploring the ancient temples to taking a boat ride down the Ganges River.

## Georgia

Georgia is a great destination for budget travelers. It has stunning mountains, beautiful beaches, and friendly locals. Accommodation is cheap, and food is even cheaper. Plus, there are plenty of activities to keep you busy, from exploring the ancient churches to taking a boat ride down the Black Sea.

## Portugal

Portugal is a great destination for budget travelers. It has stunning cities, beautiful countryside, and friendly locals. Accommodation is cheap, and food is even cheaper. Plus, there are plenty of activities to keep you busy, from exploring the ancient castles to taking a boat ride down the Douro River.

## Conclusion

Budget travel doesn't have to be boring or difficult. There are plenty of destinations out there that make budget travel easy, fun, and safe. From Thailand to Portugal, there is something for everyone. So, if you're looking for a great place to travel on a budget, these 10 destinations are sure to fit the bill.

Happy travels!
