The Caribbean is a magical region that boasts of sunny skies, pristine beaches, turquoise waters, and lush landscapes. It is a place where you can unwind, soak up the sun, and indulge in the laid-back island style. If you're looking for a perfect Caribbean getaway, then look no further than the Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace.

Nestled on the stunningly beautiful island of Corfu, Greece, Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace offers the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and breathtaking views. Whether you're traveling solo, with family or friends or for a romantic getaway, this hotel promises to provide a memorable vacation.

Accommodation

The hotel features 256 elegantly appointed rooms and suites that are designed for your comfort and relaxation. The rooms are spacious and come with modern amenities such as air conditioning, LCD TV, minibar, safe, and free Wi-Fi. Some of the rooms also have balconies that offer spectacular views of the Ionian Sea.

Food and Drink

Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace has an array of dining options that cater to all tastes and preferences. There are three restaurants that serve a variety of dishes ranging from traditional Greek cuisine to international cuisine. The hotel also has three bars where you can enjoy delicious cocktails and other drinks while soaking up the stunning sea views.

Facilities

The hotel has a range of facilities that ensure you have a comfortable and pleasant stay. There are two outdoor swimming pools, a children's pool, and a private beach area. You can relax and soak up the sun on the sun loungers or cool off with a dip in the sea or the pool.

For those who like to stay active, the hotel has a fitness center, a tennis court, and a mini-golf course. There's also a beauty salon and a spa where you can indulge in a range of beauty and wellness treatments.

FAQs

1\. Is there a shuttle service from the airport to the hotel?

Yes, the hotel offers a shuttle service from the airport to the hotel for an additional fee.

2\. Does the hotel have a kids' club?

Yes, the hotel has a kids' club that offers a range of activities for children.

3\. Is there a dress code for the restaurants?

Yes, there is a dress code in the restaurants during dinner. Gentlemen are required to wear long trousers.

4\. Is the hotel wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the hotel is wheelchair accessible, and there are rooms specially designed for guests with disabilities.

5\. Is there a car rental service at the hotel?

Yes, the hotel has a car rental service that allows you to explore the island at your own pace.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in Find Your Perfect Caribbean Getaway at Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace

1\. Achilleion Palace
This is a stunning palace that was built by Empress Elisabeth of Austria in 1890. It's located just 10 km from the hotel and is a must-visit attraction.

2\. Corfu Old Town
This town is a UNESCO World Heritage site and is famous for its narrow streets, colorful buildings, and historic landmarks.

3\. Paleokastritsa
This is a beautiful coastal village that is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and scenic views.

4\. Kanoni Peninsula
This is a beautiful peninsula that offers incredible views of the Ionian Sea and the picturesque Mouse Island.

5\. Mon Repos Palace
This is a beautiful palace that was used as a summer residence by British royalty. It's located just 4 km from the hotel and is a popular tourist attraction.

6\. Corfu Aquarium
This is a great family-friendly attraction that features a range of marine animals, including sharks, sea turtles, and exotic fish.

7\. Mount Pantokrator
This mountain offers stunning views of the island and is a popular hiking destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

8\. The Liston
This is a beautiful promenade that is lined with cafes, restaurants, and shops. It's a great place to relax and soak up the atmosphere.

9\. Vlacherna Monastery
This is a beautiful monastery that is located on a small island near Kanoni Peninsula. It's a picturesque and peaceful place that's worth a visit.

10\. Museum of Asian Art
This museum is located in the heart of Corfu Old Town and is home to a fascinating collection of Asian art and artifacts.
