# 15 Tips To Book The Cheapest Hotels

Are you looking for the best and cheapest way to book a hotel? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we’ll be discussing 15 tips to help you book the cheapest hotels. From using credit cards with huge travel cash back perks to downloading hotel booking smartphone apps, we’ll cover everything you need to know to get the best deals. So, let’s get started!

## Use Credit Cards With Huge Travel Cash Back Perks

One of the best ways to get the cheapest hotel deals is to use credit cards with huge travel cash back perks. Many credit cards offer rewards such as cash back on hotel stays, airline tickets, and other travel expenses. Look for cards that offer the highest rewards and use them to pay for your hotel stays.

## Sign Up To Hotel Booking Engines Loyalty / Rewards Programs

Another great way to get the best deals on hotel stays is to sign up for loyalty and rewards programs with hotel booking engines. Many of these programs offer discounts and other perks for frequent customers. Take advantage of these programs to get the best deals on your hotel stays.

## Download Hotel Booking Smartphone Apps

Another great way to get the best deals on hotel stays is to download hotel booking smartphone apps. These apps often offer exclusive discounts and other perks that you won’t find anywhere else. Plus, they make it easy to compare prices and book your stay in just a few clicks.

## Check Prices Directly With Hotels

It’s always a good idea to check prices directly with hotels. Many hotels offer discounts for booking directly with them, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

## Book In Advance With Free Cancellation

If you’re looking for the best deals on hotel stays, it’s always a good idea to book in advance. Many hotels offer discounts for booking in advance, and some even offer free cancellation if you need to change your plans.

## Book In Advance With Non-Refundable Rate

If you’re looking for the cheapest hotel deals, it’s always a good idea to book in advance with a non-refundable rate. This is often the cheapest way to book a hotel, as you won’t be able to get a refund if you need to cancel your stay.

## Look For Package Deals

Another great way to get the best deals on hotel stays is to look for package deals. Many hotels offer discounts when you book a package that includes flights, car rentals, and other services.

## Look For Last-Minute Deals

If you’re looking for the best deals on hotel stays, it’s always a good idea to look for last-minute deals. Many hotels offer discounts for last-minute bookings, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

## Look For Deals On Group Bookings

If you’re traveling with a group, it’s always a good idea to look for deals on group bookings. Many hotels offer discounts for group bookings, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

## Look For Deals On Extended Stays

If you’re planning an extended stay, it’s always a good idea to look for deals on extended stays. Many hotels offer discounts for extended stays, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

## Look For Deals On Weekend Stays

If you’re planning a weekend stay, it’s always a good idea to look for deals on weekend stays. Many hotels offer discounts for weekend stays, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

## Look For Deals On Off-Season Stays

If you’re planning a stay during the off-season, it’s always a good idea to look for deals on off-season stays. Many hotels offer discounts for off-season stays, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

## Look For Deals On Business Travel

If you’re traveling for business, it’s always a good idea to look for deals on business travel. Many hotels offer discounts for business travelers, so it’s worth checking out their websites or giving them a call to see if they have any special offers.

From using credit cards with huge travel cash back perks to looking for deals on group bookings, there are plenty of ways to get the best deals on your hotel stays.
