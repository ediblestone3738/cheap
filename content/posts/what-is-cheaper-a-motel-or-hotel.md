# What is Cheaper: A Motel or Hotel?

When it comes to travelling, there are a lot of factors to consider. One of the most important is the cost. When you are on a budget, you want to make sure you are getting the best deal possible. When it comes to lodging, motels and hotels are two of the most popular options. But which one is cheaper?

## Cost

Motels are often less expensive than hotels due to high guest turnover, minimal staffing requirements, and lack of amenities. This is because motels are designed to be more of a budget-friendly option. Hotels, on the other hand, tend to have more amenities and higher staffing requirements, which makes them more expensive.

## Room Door Location

In hotels, guest room doors usually open to interior hallways. This is because hotels are typically more secure than motels. Many motels do not have shared interior hallways and guest room doors open directly to the parking lot. This can be a security concern for some travellers.

## Conclusion

When it comes to cost, motels are usually the cheaper option. However, if you are looking for more amenities and security, then a hotel may be the better choice. Ultimately, it comes down to your personal preferences and budget.
