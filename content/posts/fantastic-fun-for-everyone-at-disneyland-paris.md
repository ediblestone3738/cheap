Fantastic Fun for Everyone at Disneyland Paris!

Disneyland Paris is the most visited theme park in all of Europe, and it's not hard to see why. The park has a lively and magical atmosphere that delights visitors of all ages with its elaborate mix of thrilling rides, live shows, themed restaurants, shops and other attractions. Known as the kingdom of magic, Disneyland Paris is divided into two theme parks - Disneyland Park and Walt Disney Studios Park - and they are filled with over 50 incredible shows and attractions that visitors can immerse themselves in.

If you're looking for a place to make magic memories with your loved ones, there's no better destination than Disneyland Paris, where the possibilities are endless. Here's a detailed guide to what you can expect from Disneyland Paris and why it’s a Fantastic Fun for Everyone!

The Top 10 Tourist Attractions at Disneyland Paris

1\. Space Mountain: Mission 2
This is considered one of Disneyland Paris’s most thrilling attractions, Space Mountain: Mission 2 takes guests on a high-speed adventure through space complete with stunning special effects, twists, and turns.

2\. Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast
This interactive ride takes guests on a journey through the galaxy with Buzz and Woody from Toy Story, where they use their laser guns to zap enemy targets and rack up points.

3\. Pirates of the Caribbean
Embark on a magical journey through the Golden Age of Piracy as you sail through a Caribbean pirate's den, with realistic animatronics and special effects that make you feel as though you're in the middle of a swashbuckling adventure.

4\. Ratatouille: The Adventure
This attraction puts you in the middle of Paris as you journey alongside Remy and his pals from Ratatouille, taking guests on an exhilarating adventure filled with thrilling high-speeds, tight turns and a range of unexpected surprises.

5\. Phantom Manor
Venture inside the eerie Phantom Manor, where you'll encounter various supernatural occurrences, special effects, and illusions. This attraction lends an interesting insight into the dark and mysterious side of Disneyland Paris.

6\. It's a Small World
Imagine a tour around the world, through miniatures and models of iconic landmarks, and encountering the music and national costumes of different countries on this charming ride that celebrates unity and peace.

7\. Big Thunder Mountain Railroad
One of the original Disneyland Paris rides, this Wild West-themed roller coaster is all about the twisting turns and high speeds through the mountaintops- an exhilarating ride for thrill-seekers.

8\. The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror
This ominous and spooky ride takes you to the top floor of the Hollywood Tower Hotel and drops you at lightning speed down through five floors. Terrifying but exhilarating.

9\. Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain
For Star Wars Fans, this Space Mountain transformed into a thrilling journey that takes you on an epic adventure through space for an illusory encounter with the legendary X-wing and Tie Fighters.

10\. The Lion King: Rhythms of the Pride Lands
The newest addition to Walt Disney Studios, this show is a visual marvel, combining an impressive display of African rhythms, percussions, costume and dance performances from the cherished movie The Lion King.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Disneyland Paris open every day?
A: Disneyland Paris is open all year round, every day from 10.00 am to 10.00 pm. However, there may be slight differences in park hours on holidays and during the low season.

Q: How much does a ticket to Disneyland Paris cost?
A: Ticket prices vary depending on the season and type of ticket, with a day ticket for a single park starting at €52 per adult, according to Disneyland Paris.

Q: How long should I stay at Disneyland Paris?
A: You can easily make this a 2-3 day trip to cover both Disneyland Park and Walt Disney Studios Park in one visit. There's a lot to see, do and eat, so it's a good idea to plan your visit accordingly.

Q: Can I bring snacks and drinks into Disneyland Paris?
A: Yes, visitors are allowed to bring their food and drinks inside the park, but glass containers and alcohol are prohibited.

Q: Can I wear costumes to Disneyland Paris?
A: Guests over 14 years of age are not permitted to wear a costume inside the park but are allowed to wear Disney-inspired outfits or accessories.

Q: What is the best time to visit Disneyland Paris?
A: Disneyland Paris is beautiful at any time of the year, however, generally, the best time to visit when the park is less crowded is between January to April or after September.

Fantastic Fun at Disneyland Paris for Everyone

Disneyland Paris is one of the most magical and memorable destinations for people of all ages. The attraction has many different areas, including Main Street USA, Adventureland, Fantasyland and Discoveryland. With so much to see and do, it's impossible to see everything in a single day.

From thrilling rides to live shows to themed restaurants, you are spoilt for choice with endless options for fun and excitement. Meet your favorite characters, get a selfie with them, and be sure to watch the vibrant parade and spectacular fireworks during the evenings.

At Disneyland Paris, you'll be immersed in a magical and unforgettable world where memories are made that last a lifetime.
