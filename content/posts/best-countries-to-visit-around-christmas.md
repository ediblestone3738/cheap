# Best Countries To Visit Around Christmas

The festive season is a magical time of year, and there's no better way to experience the joy and wonder of Christmas than by traveling to some of the most enchanting destinations around the world. From snow-covered landscapes to vibrant holiday markets, these countries offer unique and unforgettable Christmas experiences. Here are some of the best countries to visit around Christmas.

## Germany

Germany is renowned for its traditional Christmas markets, which are a quintessential part of the holiday season. The country transforms into a winter wonderland, with twinkling lights, festive decorations, and the aroma of mulled wine filling the air.

- **Nuremberg Christmas Market**: One of the oldest and most famous Christmas markets in Germany, featuring over 180 wooden stalls selling handmade gifts, gingerbread, and traditional German foods.
- **Dresden Striezelmarkt**: Known for its giant Christmas pyramid and Stollen Festival, this market offers a delightful array of crafts and culinary delights.
- **Berlin Christmas Markets**: With over 70 markets to choose from, Berlin offers a diverse range of experiences, from traditional to modern.

## Austria

Austria is a picturesque destination during the Christmas season, with its snow-capped mountains and charming villages. The country is known for its classical music, and many cities host concerts and events celebrating the holiday spirit.

- **Vienna**: The capital city is adorned with festive lights and decorations, and its Christmas markets are a must-visit. The Rathausplatz market is particularly popular, offering a magical atmosphere with its ice rink and festive stalls.
- **Salzburg**: Famous for its baroque architecture and musical heritage, Salzburg hosts a beautiful Christmas market in the historic Old Town, where visitors can enjoy traditional Austrian treats and crafts.
- **Innsbruck**: Nestled in the Alps, Innsbruck offers a stunning backdrop for its Christmas markets. The city is also a great destination for winter sports enthusiasts.

## Finland

For a truly magical Christmas experience, Finland is the place to be. Known as the home of Santa Claus, the country offers a unique blend of holiday traditions and natural beauty.

- **Rovaniemi**: Located in the Arctic Circle, Rovaniemi is the official hometown of Santa Claus. Visitors can meet Santa, explore the Santa Claus Village, and even take a reindeer sleigh ride.
- **Lapland**: Experience the enchanting Northern Lights and enjoy activities such as dog sledding, snowmobiling, and ice fishing in this winter wonderland.
- **Helsinki**: The capital city offers a mix of modern and traditional Christmas celebrations, with markets, concerts, and festive events throughout December.

## United States

The United States offers a diverse range of Christmas experiences, from the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of the Rocky Mountains.

- **New York City**: Known for its iconic Christmas tree at Rockefeller Center, ice skating in Central Park, and the dazzling window displays along Fifth Avenue, NYC is a must-visit during the holiday season.
- **Chicago**: The Christkindlmarket in Chicago is inspired by traditional German markets and offers a festive atmosphere with its wooden stalls and holiday treats.
- **Aspen**: For a white Christmas, head to Aspen, Colorado, where you can enjoy skiing, snowboarding, and cozying up by the fire in a charming mountain lodge.

## Canada

Canada's winter landscapes and festive cities make it an ideal destination for a Christmas getaway. From vibrant urban centers to tranquil countryside settings, there's something for everyone.

- **Quebec City**: With its European charm and historic architecture, Quebec City transforms into a fairy tale setting during the holidays. The German Christmas Market and the illuminated Old Quebec are highlights.
- **Toronto**: The Toronto Christmas Market in the Distillery District is a popular attraction, offering a mix of traditional and contemporary holiday experiences.
- **Banff**: Nestled in the Canadian Rockies, Banff offers stunning natural beauty and a range of winter activities, from skiing to hot springs.

## United Kingdom

The United Kingdom is steeped in Christmas traditions, from caroling to festive feasts. The country's historic cities and charming countryside provide a perfect backdrop for holiday celebrations.

- **London**: The capital city comes alive with Christmas lights, markets, and events. Highlights include the Winter Wonderland in Hyde Park and the Christmas at Kew Gardens light display.
- **Edinburgh**: The Scottish capital offers a magical Christmas experience with its European Christmas Market, ice skating rink, and the iconic Hogmanay celebrations.
- **Bath**: Known for its Georgian architecture, Bath hosts a charming Christmas market set against the backdrop of the historic Bath Abbey.

## Italy

Italy offers a unique blend of religious and cultural Christmas traditions, with its beautiful churches, nativity scenes, and festive markets.

- **Rome**: The Vatican City is a focal point for Christmas celebrations, with Midnight Mass at St. Peter's Basilica being a highlight. The city also hosts several Christmas markets and events.
- **Florence**: Known for its Renaissance art and architecture, Florence offers a magical Christmas atmosphere with its festive lights, markets, and the famous Christmas Eve concert at the Duomo.
- **Venice**: Experience a unique Christmas in Venice, where the city's canals and historic buildings are adorned with festive decorations and lights.

## Japan

While Christmas is not a traditional holiday in Japan, the country has embraced the festive spirit with its own unique twist. Cities are adorned with stunning light displays, and Christmas-themed events are held throughout the country.

- **Tokyo**: The capital city is known for its extravagant Christmas illuminations, with popular spots including the Marunouchi area and the Tokyo Midtown complex.
- **Osaka**: Universal Studios Japan hosts a spectacular Christmas event, featuring themed attractions and entertainment.
- **Sapporo**: The Sapporo White Illumination transforms the city into a winter wonderland, with beautiful light displays and a festive atmosphere.

## Australia

For those seeking a warm and sunny Christmas, Australia offers a unique holiday experience with its beaches, outdoor activities, and festive events.

- **Sydney**: Enjoy a Christmas by the beach, with events such as Carols in the Domain and the spectacular New Year's Eve fireworks over Sydney Harbour.
- **Melbourne**: The city hosts a range of festive events, including the Christmas Festival at Federation Square and the Myer Christmas Windows.
- **Gold Coast**: Experience a laid-back Christmas with beach picnics, surfing, and festive events along the coast.

## Conclusion

Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or looking to escape to a sunny destination, these countries offer a diverse range of experiences to make your holiday season truly special. From traditional markets and festive events to stunning natural landscapes and cultural celebrations, there's something for everyone to enjoy. So pack your bags and get ready to explore the best countries to visit around Christmas!
