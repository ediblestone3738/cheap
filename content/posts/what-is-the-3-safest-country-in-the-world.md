# What are the 3 Safest Countries in the World?

Traveling can be an exciting and rewarding experience, but it’s important to consider safety when planning a trip. Every country has its own unique set of risks, and it’s important to research the safety of a destination before booking a trip. With that in mind, here are the three safest countries in the world according to the Global Peace Index (GPI).

## 1\. Iceland

Iceland takes the GPI crown for the safest country in the world. This Nordic island nation is known for its stunning landscapes, geothermal pools, and the northern lights. It’s also known for its low crime rate and peaceful atmosphere. Iceland has a long history of peacefulness, and its government is committed to maintaining a safe and secure environment for its citizens and visitors.

## 2\. New Zealand

New Zealand is the second safest country in the world according to the GPI. This beautiful country is known for its stunning landscapes, vibrant cities, and friendly people. New Zealand has a low crime rate and is generally considered to be a safe destination for travelers. The government is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for its citizens and visitors.

## 3\. Ireland

Ireland is the third safest country in the world according to the GPI. This small island nation is known for its stunning landscapes, friendly people, and vibrant culture. Ireland has a low crime rate and is generally considered to be a safe destination for travelers. The government is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for its citizens and visitors.

## Conclusion

When it comes to safety, Iceland, New Zealand, and Ireland are the three safest countries in the world according to the Global Peace Index. These countries have a long history of peacefulness and are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for their citizens and visitors. If you’re looking for a safe destination for your next trip, these countries are a great option.
