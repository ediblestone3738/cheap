Top 10 Tourist Attractions:

1\. Bellver Castle

Bellver Castle is a Gothic style castle located in Palma de Mallorca. The castle is one of the best-preserved examples of Gothic architecture in Spain and has a unique circular design. The castle is now a museum and offers amazing views of the city.

2\. Palma Cathedral

Palma Cathedral is a stunning Gothic cathedral located in the heart of Palma de Mallorca. The cathedral is one of the most famous landmarks on the island and is known for its stunning stained glass windows and intricate design.

3\. Serra de Tramuntana

Serra de Tramuntana is a mountain range located on the northwest coast of Mallorca. The mountain range is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers amazing hiking trails and stunning views of the sea.

4\. Formentor Beach

Formentor Beach is a stunning beach located on the northeast coast of Mallorca. The beach is surrounded by pine forests and offers clear blue waters and white sand.

5\. Port de Soller

Port de Soller is a picturesque port located on the northwest coast of Mallorca. The port is surrounded by mountains and offers stunning views of the sea. The port has a wide range of restaurants and cafes, and is a popular spot for boat trips.

6\. Valldemossa

Valldemossa is a picturesque hill town located in the heart of the Tramuntana Mountains. The town is known for its historic buildings, narrow streets, and stunning views of the mountains.

7\. Deia

Deia is a picturesque village located on the northwest coast of Mallorca. The village is known for its stunning views of the sea and the mountains, and is a popular spot for artists and writers.

8\. Sa Calobra

Sa Calobra is a stunning cove located on the northwest coast of Mallorca. The cove is surrounded by high cliffs and offers clear blue waters and white sand.

9\. Alcudia Old Town

Alcudia Old Town is a historic town located on the northeast coast of Mallorca. The town is known for its narrow streets, historic buildings, and stunning views of the sea.

10\. Palma Aquarium

Palma Aquarium is a large aquarium located in Palma de Mallorca. The aquarium has a wide range of marine life, including sharks, stingrays, and dolphins.
