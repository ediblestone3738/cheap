Top Exciting Activities to Do in Zante Laganas Holidays

1\. Watersports – Zante Laganas is the perfect destination for watersport enthusiasts, with a variety of activities on offer. You can go jet skiing, parasailing, windsurfing or simply rent a boat and explore the beautiful coastline.

2\. Walk to Keri Caves – The Keri Caves are a series of sea caves located near the village of Keri, about 12km from Zante Laganas. The caves are accessible by boat, but if you're feeling adventurous you can also hike to them. The walk takes about an hour and offers stunning views of the coastline.

3\. Turtle spotting – Zante Laganas is home to the endangered loggerhead turtle, which lays its eggs on the beaches of the island. Take a boat tour to spot these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

4\. Visit Shipwreck Beach – Located on the north-west coast of the island, Shipwreck Beach is one of the most famous in Greece. The beach is only accessible by boat, and features crystal clear waters and a stunning view of a rusted shipwreck on the shore.

5\. Explore the Blue Caves – The Blue Caves are a series of sea caves located on the north-east coast of the island, near the village of Skinari. The caves are famous for their bright blue waters and stunning rock formations.

6\. Party at Laganas Beach – Laganas Beach is the main party destination in Zante Laganas, with a wide variety of bars, clubs and restaurants along its promenade. The nightlife here is lively and fun, with a party atmosphere that lasts well into the night.

7\. Go on a Wine Tasting Tour – Zante Laganas is also known for its excellent wineries, which produce a variety of delicious wines. Take a tour to learn about the local wine-making process and taste some of the island's best wines.

8\. Visit the Venetian Castle – The Venetian Castle is a historic site located in the town of Zakynthos, about 10km from Zante Laganas. The castle dates back to the 15th century and offers stunning views of the surrounding area.

9\. Go on a Scuba Diving Adventure – Zante Laganas is home to some of the most beautiful and diverse marine life in the Mediterranean. Take a scuba diving tour to explore the underwater world and discover hidden treasures.

10\. Take a Cooking Class – Greek cuisine is known for its delicious flavors and fresh ingredients. Take a cooking class to learn how to make traditional Greek dishes and bring a piece of Greece home with you.
