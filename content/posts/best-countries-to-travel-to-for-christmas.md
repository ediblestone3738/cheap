# Best Countries To Travel To For Christmas

Christmas is a magical time of year, and what better way to celebrate than by exploring some of the best countries around the world that truly embrace the festive spirit? From enchanting winter wonderlands to sun-kissed beaches, each destination offers its own unique take on the holiday season. In this article, we'll explore some of the best countries to travel to for Christmas, each offering a memorable experience that captures the essence of the holiday.

## 1. Germany

Germany is renowned for its traditional Christmas markets, which are a quintessential part of the holiday season. These markets, known as "Weihnachtsmärkte," are filled with festive stalls offering handcrafted gifts, delicious treats, and warm beverages.

- **Nuremberg**: Home to one of the oldest and most famous Christmas markets, the Nuremberg Christkindlesmarkt, which dates back to the 16th century.
- **Dresden**: Known for the Striezelmarkt, one of the oldest documented Christmas markets in the world.
- **Cologne**: Offers several markets, including the iconic market at the Cologne Cathedral, featuring a stunning backdrop and a wide array of festive goods.

## 2. Austria

Austria is another European country that embraces the Christmas spirit with open arms. Its charming cities and picturesque landscapes make it a perfect destination for a festive getaway.

- **Vienna**: The capital city is famous for its Christmas markets, especially the Vienna Christmas World at Rathausplatz, which transforms the city into a winter wonderland.
- **Salzburg**: Known for its baroque architecture and the birthplace of Mozart, Salzburg offers a magical Christmas atmosphere with its traditional markets and festive concerts.
- **Innsbruck**: Nestled in the Alps, Innsbruck provides a stunning backdrop for Christmas celebrations, with markets set against the snow-capped mountains.

## 3. Finland

For those dreaming of a white Christmas, Finland is the ultimate destination. Known as the home of Santa Claus, this Nordic country offers a truly enchanting holiday experience.

- **Rovaniemi**: Located in the Arctic Circle, Rovaniemi is the official hometown of Santa Claus. Visitors can meet Santa, explore the Santa Claus Village, and enjoy activities like reindeer sleigh rides.
- **Lapland**: Offers a winter wonderland experience with opportunities to see the Northern Lights, go dog sledding, and stay in cozy log cabins.
- **Helsinki**: The capital city hosts a variety of Christmas markets and events, including the St. Thomas Christmas Market, known for its unique Finnish crafts and delicacies.

## 4. United States

The United States offers a diverse range of Christmas experiences, from bustling cities to serene countryside retreats.

- **New York City**: Famous for its extravagant holiday displays, including the Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree and the festive window displays along Fifth Avenue.
- **Chicago**: Known for the Christkindlmarket, a traditional German-style market offering a variety of holiday treats and gifts.
- **Aspen**: For a snowy Christmas, Aspen provides a picturesque setting with its ski resorts and charming holiday decorations.

## 5. Canada

Canada's vast landscapes and vibrant cities make it an ideal destination for a Christmas getaway.

- **Quebec City**: Offers a European-style Christmas experience with its cobblestone streets, historic architecture, and the German Christmas Market.
- **Vancouver**: Known for the Vancouver Christmas Market and the Capilano Suspension Bridge Park's Canyon Lights display.
- **Banff**: Located in the Canadian Rockies, Banff provides a stunning winter wonderland with opportunities for skiing, snowboarding, and enjoying the natural beauty.

## 6. United Kingdom

The United Kingdom is steeped in holiday traditions, offering a mix of historic charm and modern festivities.

- **London**: Famous for its Christmas lights, markets, and ice skating rinks, including the Winter Wonderland in Hyde Park.
- **Edinburgh**: Known for its Christmas markets, festive events, and the Hogmanay celebration, which marks the New Year with fireworks and street parties.
- **Bath**: Offers a picturesque setting with its Georgian architecture and the Bath Christmas Market, known for its artisanal crafts and festive atmosphere.

## 7. Japan

Japan offers a unique take on Christmas, blending Western traditions with its own cultural influences.

- **Tokyo**: Known for its dazzling illuminations, including the Tokyo Midtown Christmas and the Caretta Shiodome Winter Illumination.
- **Osaka**: Offers festive light displays and Christmas markets, including the Osaka German Christmas Market.
- **Kyoto**: Known for its serene temples and gardens, Kyoto provides a peaceful setting for a reflective Christmas experience.

## 8. Australia

For those seeking a warm Christmas, Australia offers a sun-soaked holiday experience with its beautiful beaches and vibrant cities.

- **Sydney**: Known for its Christmas lights, including the Martin Place Christmas Tree and the Christmas projections on St Mary's Cathedral.
- **Melbourne**: Offers a variety of festive events, including the Myer Christmas Windows and the Christmas Festival at Federation Square.
- **Gold Coast**: Provides a beachside Christmas experience with opportunities for surfing, sunbathing, and enjoying festive events.

## 9. Italy

Italy's rich history and culture make it a captivating destination for a Christmas holiday.

- **Rome**: Known for its beautiful nativity scenes, including the life-sized nativity at St. Peter's Square, and the Christmas markets in Piazza Navona.
- **Florence**: Offers a charming Christmas atmosphere with its festive lights, markets, and the famous Christmas tree in Piazza del Duomo.
- **Venice**: Known for its unique Christmas markets and the Festa di San Nicolò, a traditional celebration on December 6th.

## 10. Iceland

Iceland's stunning natural beauty and unique holiday traditions make it a memorable destination for Christmas.

- **Reykjavik**: Known for its Christmas markets, festive lights, and the Yule Lads, Iceland's mischievous holiday figures.
- **Akureyri**: Offers a charming Christmas atmosphere with its picturesque setting and festive events.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Provides a unique experience of soaking in geothermal waters surrounded by snow-covered landscapes.

## Conclusion

Whether you're dreaming of a snowy Christmas or a sun-drenched holiday, these countries offer a diverse range of experiences that capture the magic of the season. From traditional markets and festive lights to unique cultural celebrations, each destination provides an unforgettable Christmas experience. So pack your bags, embrace the holiday spirit, and explore the best countries to travel to for Christmas.
