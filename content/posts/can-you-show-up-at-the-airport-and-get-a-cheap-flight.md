# Can You Show Up at the Airport and Get a Cheap Flight?

As a travel expert, I'm often asked if it's possible to show up at the airport without a ticket and expect to get somewhere for cheap. The short answer is no. Airlines no longer allow you to wait around at the counter in hopes of snagging a "standby flight" and filling the last seat of a departing flight.

## Why Airlines Don't Allow Standby Flights Anymore

The practice of standby flights used to be quite common. It was a way for travelers to get discounted tickets if they were willing to take a chance and show up at the airport without a ticket. Airlines used to be willing to offer discounted tickets to fill the last few seats on a flight.

However, in recent years, airlines have become more strict about their policies. This is due to the fact that the cost of fuel has increased significantly, making it more expensive for airlines to operate. As a result, airlines are no longer willing to offer discounted tickets to travelers who show up at the airport without a ticket.

## How to Get Cheap Flights

If you're looking for a cheap flight, the best way to do it is to plan ahead. Booking your flight in advance is the best way to get the best prices. Airlines often offer discounts for travelers who book their tickets at least two weeks in advance.

You can also take advantage of special deals and promotions offered by airlines. Many airlines offer discounts for travelers who book their tickets during certain times of the year. For example, airlines often offer discounts during the summer months or during the holiday season.

## Tips for Finding Cheap Flights

If you're looking for a cheap flight, there are a few tips that can help you find the best deals. First, it's important to be flexible with your travel dates. Airlines often offer discounts for travelers who are willing to fly on certain days or at certain times.

Second, it's also important to compare prices between different airlines. Different airlines may offer different prices for the same flight, so it's important to compare prices to make sure you're getting the best deal.

Finally, it's also important to be aware of any special promotions or discounts that airlines may be offering. Airlines often offer discounts for travelers who book their tickets during certain times of the year or who use certain payment methods.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, it's no longer possible to show up at the airport without a ticket and expect to get a cheap flight.
