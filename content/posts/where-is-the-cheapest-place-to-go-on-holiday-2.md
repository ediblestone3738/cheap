# Where is the Cheapest Place to Go on Holiday?

When it comes to planning a holiday, budget is often a major factor. Fortunately, there are a number of destinations around the world that offer a great holiday experience without breaking the bank. Here are some of the best places to go on holiday if you’re looking for a bargain.

## Thailand

There’s a reason why Thailand remains so popular with backpackers – it’s got idyllic islands, a rich culture, beach-huts aplenty, tantalising cuisine and adventures galore, and all available at often staggeringly low prices. From Bangkok to the islands of Phuket and Koh Samui, Thailand is a great place to explore without spending a fortune.

## South Africa

South Africa is a great destination for those looking for a bargain holiday. From the bustling cities of Johannesburg and Cape Town to the stunning wildlife of Kruger National Park, there’s plenty to explore here. Accommodation and food are both reasonably priced, and you can even find some great deals on flights if you book in advance.

## Vietnam

Vietnam is a great destination for budget travellers. The country is full of stunning scenery, from the mountains of Sapa to the bustling cities of Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Accommodation and food are both incredibly cheap, and you can find some great deals on flights if you’re willing to shop around.

## Uruguay

Uruguay is often overlooked as a holiday destination, but it’s a great place to visit if you’re looking for a bargain. The country is full of stunning beaches, colonial towns and bustling cities, and you can find some great deals on accommodation and food. Flights to Uruguay can be expensive, but if you’re willing to shop around you can find some great deals.

## Cuba

Cuba is a great destination for budget travellers. The country is full of stunning beaches, colonial towns and bustling cities, and you can find some great deals on accommodation and food. Flights to Cuba can be expensive, but if you’re willing to shop around you can find some great deals.

## Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a great destination for budget travellers. The city is full of stunning architecture, cobbled streets and bustling markets, and you can find some great deals on accommodation and food. Flights to Prague can be expensive, but if you’re willing to shop around you can find some great deals.

## Greece

Greece is a great destination for budget travellers. The country is full of stunning beaches, ancient ruins and bustling cities, and you can find some great deals on accommodation and food. Flights to Greece can be expensive, but if you’re willing to shop around you can find some great deals.

## Guatemala

Guatemala is a great destination for budget travellers. The country is full of stunning scenery, colonial towns and bustling cities, and you can find some great deals on accommodation and food. Flights to Guatemala can be expensive, but if you’re willing to shop around you can find some great deals.

No matter where you’re looking to go on holiday, there are plenty of destinations around the world that offer great value for money. From Thailand to Guatemala, there’s something for everyone. So, if you’re looking for a bargain holiday, these are some of the best places to go.
