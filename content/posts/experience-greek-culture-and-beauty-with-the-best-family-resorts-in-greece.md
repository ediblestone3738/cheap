Experience Greek Culture and Beauty with the Best Family Resorts in Greece

Greece is a land full of ancient history, rich culture, and breathtaking beauty. It is known for its stunning beaches, picturesque islands, and warm hospitality. If you’re looking for a family vacation that encompasses all these things, then Greece is the perfect destination for you. With its many family-friendly resorts, you can experience Greek culture and beauty with your loved ones in a comfortable and fun atmosphere. In this article, we will introduce you to the best family resorts in Greece that offer exceptional service, accommodations, and amenities for families.

Best Family Resorts in Greece:

1\. Sani Resort
Located in the pine forests of the Kassandra peninsula, Sani Resort is a luxurious five-star resort that offers a wide range of facilities and activities for the whole family. From sandy beaches to pools to water sports, your family will have the time of their lives here. The resort has a kids’ club for children from 4 months to 16 years old, where they can engage in various activities such as treasure hunts, arts and crafts, and cooking classes.

2\. Ikos Olivia
Ikos Olivia is another luxurious five-star resort located in Halkidiki. The resort offers stunning sea views, white sandy beaches, and crystal-clear waters. It has a range of amenities for families, including a kids’ club, teens’ club, and a creche for infants from 4 months old. The resort also offers various activities for adults, including yoga, cooking classes, and tennis lessons.

3\. Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort
Located on the island of Crete, Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort is a luxurious five-star resort that offers breathtaking views of the Mirabello Bay. The resort has a range of facilities for families, including a kids’ club, a creche for infants, and a range of activities such as water sports, golf, and spa treatment. The resort also offers a range of dining options, from buffet to a la carte restaurants.

4\. Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa
Located on the island of Crete, Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa is a five-star resort that offers breathtaking sea views, sandy beaches, and crystal clear waters. The resort has a range of facilities for families, including a kids’ pool, a kids’ club, and a range of activities such as water sports, tennis, and mini-golf. The resort offers a range of dining options, from buffet to a la carte restaurants.

5\. Grecotel Olympia Riviera & Aqua Park
Located on the west coast of the Peloponnese peninsula, Grecotel Olympia Riviera & Aqua Park is a five-star resort that offers a range of facilities for families, including a water park, a kids’ club, and a range of activities such as water sports, tennis, and archery. The resort also offers a range of dining options, from buffet to a la carte restaurants.

FAQs about Best Family Resorts in Greece

1\. What are the best family-friendly beaches in Greece?
Some of the best family-friendly beaches in Greece are Elafonissi Beach, Tsambika Beach, St. George Beach, Myrtos Beach, and Navagio Beach.

2\. What is the best time to visit Greece with family?
The best time to visit Greece with family is from June to September as the weather is warm, and the sea is perfect for swimming.

3\. What are the best family-friendly activities in Greece?
Some of the best family-friendly activities in Greece include visiting ancient ruins, taking a boat tour, swimming in the sea, visiting museums, and trying out Greek cuisine.

4\. Do the family resorts in Greece offer kids’ clubs?
Yes, many family resorts in Greece offer kids’ clubs that provide a range of activities such as arts and crafts, sports, and cooking classes.

5\. Is Greece a safe destination for families?
Yes, Greece is a safe destination for families. Crime rates are low, and people are friendly and welcoming.

Conclusion

Greece is a great destination for families looking to experience ancient history, rich culture, and breathtaking beauty. The best family resorts in Greece offer luxurious accommodations, excellent service, and a wide range of family-friendly amenities and activities. Along with the top tourist attractions in Greece, your family will have a fantastic vacation that will create unforgettable memories.
