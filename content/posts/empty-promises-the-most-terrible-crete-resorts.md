If you're planning to have a holiday in Crete, then you must steer clear of the resorts in the area that are known for making empty promises. For various reasons, these resorts have a reputation for unfulfilled promises and sub-par service that visitors can do without. Here's the deal on Empty Promises: The Most Terrible Crete Resorts.

Empty Promises: The Most Terrible Crete Resorts

The following four are some of the most commonly regarded resorts as the bottom of the barrel in Crete:

1\. Hersonissos

Hersonissos is frequently labeled as one of Crete's most disappointing resorts. It's a destination that many holidaymakers quickly forget after they discover that it's neither pleasant nor attractive.

2\. Malia

Malia is a well-known Crete town that hosts one of Europe's best universities for international studies. However, it's also known as a party town or destination for older people mourning their youth. Various reports claim that the town is characterized by noisy clubs and intoxicated holidaymakers.

3\. Chania

Chania is regarded as Crete's second city, which sits on the northwest coast, with a population of 55,000. However, in recent years, it has become increasingly overrun with tourists, and some complain that its streets are now filled with souvenir shops and high-priced bars.

4\. Elounda

Elounda is known among the world's most elite and exclusive resorts, which is also one of the island's most expensive. Its guests sometimes find the resort dull, leaving them wishing they had chosen a more dynamic location.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about Empty Promises: The Most Terrible Crete Resorts

Is Crete safe?

Yes, Crete is one of the safest holiday destinations in Europe, attracting millions of travelers every year.

When Should I visit Crete?

Crete is a year-round destination, depending on whether you prefer heat or cooler temperatures. However, the peak season runs from May to October.

What are the best tourist attractions in Crete?

Crete is a large and diverse island, with much to see and do. Here are the top 10 tourist attractions:

1\. The Palace of Knossos

2\. The Samaria Gorge

3\. The Venetian Fortifications at Rethymnon

4\. The Monastery of Arkadi

5\. The Elafonissi pink beach

6\. The Palace of Phaistos

7\. The Lasithi Plateau

8\. The Minoan Archaeological Site of Gortyna

9\. The Historical Museum of Crete

10\. The Agia Triada Monastery

What are the best resorts to stay in Crete?

If you're looking for a refined island getaway, then try the Agios Nikolaos or the Domes of Elounda.

Meanwhile, If your preference is for a lively resort with tons of activities and an animated nightlife, you might enjoy Chania or Malia.

What is the best way to travel to Crete?

Getting to Crete is relatively easy, with different airlines offering direct flights from various European cities. You could travel via ferry from Athens, Santorini, or other smaller islands. Alternatively, some travelers opt to hire a car and take the ferry from mainland Greece to southern Crete.

Conclusion

It's essential to research destinations before making any bookings, and with the list above, you should be able to eliminate the resorts that have a reputation for empty promises. As stated earlier, the best way to create lasting and unforgettable vacation memories in Crete is by choosing a place that offers quality service and suits your taste. Remember that it's essential to keep safety in mind at all times and stay informed about the latest travel restrictions and health regulations.
