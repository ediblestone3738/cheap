# What Are the Cheapest Days to Fly Internationally?

When it comes to planning a trip abroad, one of the most important factors to consider is the cost of airfare. Fortunately, there are certain days of the week that are cheaper than others when it comes to international flights. Berg explains that the days that other people are less likely to want to travel are always going to be the cheapest days. This means that Tuesdays and Wednesdays are the least popular days for people to fly, and therefore, airlines tend to offer discounted fares on these days.
