# What Time of Day is Best to Book Hotels?

Are you looking for the best time to book a hotel? Whether you are planning a holiday or just a weekend away, you want to make sure you get the best deal. The time of day you book your hotel can make a big difference in the price you pay.

## Best Time to Book a Hotel for Last Minute Deals

If you are looking for last minute hotel deals, the best time to book a hotel is after 4pm. This is when hotels will drop their prices to entice travelers to fill their rooms for the night. Many hotels will offer discounts of up to 50% off the regular price for last minute bookings.

## Best Time to Book a Hotel for Lowest Prices

For those who are dedicated budget hunters, the best time to book hotel for lowest prices is after 8pm. This is when hotels are desperate to fill their rooms and will offer discounts of up to 70% off the regular price.

## Tips for Booking Hotels at the Best Time

Here are some tips to help you get the best deal when booking a hotel:

- Book in advance – Booking your hotel in advance will give you the best chance of getting the lowest price. Hotels usually offer discounts for early bookings.
- Compare prices – Use online travel sites to compare prices from different hotels. This will help you find the best deal.
- Check for discounts – Many hotels offer discounts for certain days of the week or for members of loyalty programs. Make sure to check for any discounts you may be eligible for.
- Be flexible – If you are flexible with your dates, you may be able to find better deals. Hotels may offer discounts for bookings on certain days of the week.

## Conclusion

Booking a hotel at the right time can make a big difference in the price you pay. The best time to book a hotel for last minute deals is after 4pm, while the best time to book a hotel for lowest prices is after 8pm. Be sure to compare prices and check for discounts to get the best deal.

Happy travels!
