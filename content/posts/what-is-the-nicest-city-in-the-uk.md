The United Kingdom is home to some of the most beautiful cities in the world, and it can be difficult to decide which one is the nicest. Fortunately, the 2022 Readers' Choice Awards have made it easier for you to decide. After surveying thousands of people, the results are in and the top seven cities in the UK have been determined.

London is the clear winner, with an impressive score of 84.07. It's no surprise that the capital of the UK is the most popular city in the country, as it is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the world, such as Big Ben, the London Eye, and Buckingham Palace. There's also an abundance of culture, history, and entertainment to explore in London, making it the perfect destination for any traveler.

In second place is Edinburgh, with a score of 83.01. This beautiful city is renowned for its stunning architecture, historic castles, and cobbled streets. It's also the perfect place to experience Scotland's unique culture, as it is home to the world-famous Edinburgh Festival, the Royal Mile, and the National Museum of Scotland.

Coming in third is Cardiff, with a score of 82.81. This vibrant city is the capital of Wales and is home to some of the most stunning Victorian architecture in the UK. It's also a great place to experience Welsh culture, with its many pubs, restaurants, and shops.

Manchester is fourth on the list, with a score of 82.22. This bustling city is known for its vibrant nightlife, its football teams, and its many music venues. It's also home to some of the best shopping in the UK, with the iconic Arndale Centre and the Trafford Centre.

Fifth on the list is Liverpool, with a score of 76.78. This city is famous for its Beatles connections, but it's also home to some of the best museums and galleries in the UK. It's also a great place to experience the unique culture of the North West, with its many pubs, clubs, and music venues.

Sixth on the list is Belfast, with a score of 75.42. This city is the capital of Northern Ireland and is home to some of the most beautiful Victorian architecture in the UK. It's also a great place to experience Irish culture, with its many pubs, restaurants, and shops.

Seventh on the list is Bath, with a score of 75.40. This stunning city is home to some of the most beautiful Georgian architecture in the UK. It's also a great place to experience the unique culture of the South West, with its many pubs, restaurants, and shops.

Finally, eighth on the list is Oxford, with a score of 72.82. This city is the ultimate university town and has long been a honeypot for creatives and academics. It's also home to some of the most beautiful architecture in the UK, with its many colleges, libraries, and museums.

So, which is the nicest city in the UK? Well, according to the 2022 Readers' Choice Awards, London is the clear winner. But all of the cities on this list are beautiful in their own right and are well worth a visit. Whether you're looking for culture, history, entertainment, or just a great night out, the UK has something for everyone.
