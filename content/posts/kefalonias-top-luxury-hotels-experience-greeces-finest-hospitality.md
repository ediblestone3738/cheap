Kefalonia is the largest of the Ionian Islands, located in the west of Greece. Known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear turquoise waters, majestic cliffs and dramatic scenery, it has become one of the most popular destinations for luxury travel. The island is home to some of the finest hotels in Greece, offering an unmatched level of luxury and comfort. In this article, we will go over Kefalonia's top luxury hotels and the top 10 tourist attractions located in these hotels.

Kefalonia's Top Luxury Hotels

1\. Emelisse Nature Resort

The Emelisse Nature Resort is a strikingly beautiful hotel located in the charming town of Fiskardo. Set on a hill overlooking the Ionian Sea, this hotel combines luxurious comfort with an unspoiled natural environment. The hotel boasts of two private beaches, stunning sea views, and two stunning swimming pools. It’s an ideal starting point for exploring the nearby islands of Ithaca and Lefkada.

2\. Kefalonia Bay Palace

The Kefalonia Bay Palace is one of the most luxurious hotels on the island. Slightly hidden and with a private beach, the Palace is the epitome of luxury accommodations. The hotel is located only 5km away from the center of the city of Argostoli. Its exclusive vibe is perfect for couples and the extravagant spa treatments help to relax and unwind.

3\. Avithos Resort

The Avithos Resort is a boutique-style hotel offering luxury accommodations and impeccable service. The resort is located near the village of Svoronata, which is known for its tranquil surroundings and peaceful atmosphere. The resort features an outdoor swimming pool and has direct access to Avithos Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches on the island.

4\. Petani Bay Hotel

Petani Bay Hotel is a hidden gem overlooking the famous Petani beach. The hotel provides a tranquil atmosphere within a picturesque region that is well-loved for its sunsets. The hotel has impressive features, such as a fabulous restaurant offering locally sourced dishes and stylish, sea-facing rooms.

5\. F Zeen Retreat

F Zeen Retreat is a high-end boutique hotel located in the charming village of Lourdas. It is perfect for those who seek a private sanctuary for relaxation, yoga and meditation. Due to its wellness programs, it has been known as a haven for stress relief with people coming far and wide to enjoy the facilities.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in Kefalonia's Top Luxury Hotels

1\. Private Beaches

All of the hotels located in Kefalonia's top luxury hotels boast of several private beaches. They offer guests the chance to enjoy the crystal-clear turquoise waters without having to compete for space. Lie back and relax without being surrounded by crowds.

2\. Multisensory spa treatments

Indulge in a multisensory spa experience at any one of the luxury hotels on the island. The hotels feature different customizable spa treatments and wellness programs boasting of the latest spa treatments to indulge and rejuvenate in.

3\. World-class restaurants

Kefalonia is known for its gastronomic delights, and hotels like Petani Bay Hotel and Avithos Resort offer some of the best restaurants on the island. The restaurants have extensive menus comprising locally sourced produce and seafood.

4\. Infinity Pools

Luxury hotels such as Kefalonia Bay Palace have infinity pools that allow you to enjoy the stunning sea view while gliding through the water. It’s the perfect place to kick back and relax.

5\. Personalized service

The personalized service offered by the staff at these luxury hotels is exemplary. You can be sure to have everything you need at the tip of your fingers with friendly and accommodating staff always available to attend to your needs.

6\. Cultural tours

Luxury hotels like Emelisse Nature Resort offer cultural tours and activities to enrich your overall travel experience. Join a cultural tour led by a knowledgeable guide and learn about the history and culture of Kefalonia.

7\. Kayak tours

Kayaking tours are also available at some of Kefalonia's luxury hotels. You can explore hidden coves and sea caves in a kayak, accompanied by knowledgeable tour guides who will ensure you get the most out of the experience.

8\. Scenic hiking and nature walks

For nature lovers, scenic hiking and nature walks are available at most of the top luxury hotels in Kefalonia. Keen hikers can immerse themselves in the island's natural beauty and enjoy breath-taking views of the stunning coastline.

9\. Water sports activities

Most of the luxurious hotels in Kefalonia offer an array of water sports activities such as surfing, windsurfing, and parasailing. These activities provide guests with a unique and unforgettable experience.

10\. Sunset views

Finally, what better way to end the day than to watch its closure with a picturesque sunset view? Each of Kefalonia's luxury hotels offers an outstanding site to behold the sunset. Make sure to visit Sky Lounge and Bar at Kefalonia Bay Palace or Emelisse Nature Resort's Rooftop Lounge for the perfect sunset experience.

Kefalonia's Top Luxury Hotels: FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Kefalonia?
A: The best time to visit Kefalonia is from June to September, with July and August being the hottest months.

Q: Are the luxury hotels in Kefalonia child-friendly?
A: Some of the luxurious hotels in Kefalonia, such as Emelisse Nature Resort, are child-friendly and offer activities tailored for children.

Q: Are pets allowed in Kefalonia’s luxury hotels?
A: Most of Kefalonia's luxury hotels do not allow pets.

Q: How much does it cost to stay in the top luxury hotels in Kefalonia?
A: The cost of staying in one of Kefalonia's top luxury hotels varies, depending on the season and the type of room you book. Expect to pay at least €300 per night.

Q: What are the most popular tourist attractions in Kefalonia?
A: The most popular tourist attractions in Kefalonia are the Myrtos Beach, Melissani Cave, Assos Village, and the Fiscardo Port.

In conclusion, Kefalonia is a beautiful island that offers a unique luxury travel experience. Its top luxury hotels, complete with world-class restaurants, multisensory spa treatments, private beaches, infinity pools and personalized services, ensure that visitors have an unforgettable experience. Knowing the tourist attractions located in these luxury hotels will help visitors to plan their trip accordingly. Whether you are on a romantic getaway, taking a chance on some "me time" or planning a family vacation, Kefalonia offers an exclusive luxury travel experience that is hard to match.
