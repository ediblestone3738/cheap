Majorca is an island in the Mediterranean Sea and is the largest of the Balearic Islands. Located off the coast of Spain, Majorca is a popular tourist destination known for its sandy beaches, picturesque landscapes, and cultural attractions. The island has a proud history dating back to prehistoric times, and its diverse landscape ranges from the rugged mountains in the north to the golden beaches in the south.

If you are looking to escape from the monotony of city life and relax on a pristine beach, Majorca is the perfect destination for you. The island has a variety of beach resorts that cater to all budgets, from luxurious five-star hotels to budget-friendly options.

In this article, we will take a closer look at the best beach resorts Majorca has to offer, along with the top tourist attractions on the island.

Best Beach Resorts Majorca Has to Offer

1\. Belmond La Residencia, Deia

Tucked away in the charming village of Deia, Belmond La Residencia is a luxurious beach resort that has been attracting discerning travelers for decades. The resort features two pools, a spa, tennis courts, and several restaurants. The property is set amidst lush gardens overlooking the Tramuntana Mountains, and the rooms are designed in a traditional Mallorcan style.

2\. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa, Soller

Perched on a cliff overlooking the picturesque port of Soller, Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa is a stunning beach resort that offers unparalleled views of the sea and mountains. The resort features several swimming pools, a spa, and a range of restaurants serving delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

3\. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort, Costa d'en Blanes

Located in the upscale neighborhood of Costa d'en Blanes, St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a luxurious beach resort that offers the ultimate in relaxation and indulgence. The resort features three swimming pools, a spa, and several restaurants serving international cuisine.

4\. Gran Hotel Son Net, Puigpunyent

Nestled amidst the rolling hills of Puigpunyent, Gran Hotel Son Net is a charming beach resort that offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The resort features a swimming pool, a spa, and a restaurant serving delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

5\. Park Hyatt Mallorca, Canyamel

Located in the northeastern part of the island, Park Hyatt Mallorca is a luxurious beach resort that offers the perfect mix of relaxation and adventure. The resort features several swimming pools, a spa, and a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, horse riding, and cycling.

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Majorca?

A: The best time to visit Majorca is during the spring (March to May) or fall (September to November) when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller.

Q: What are the most popular beaches in Majorca?

A: The most popular beaches in Majorca are Cala d'Or, Cala Millor, Cala Ratjada, and Palma Nova.

Q: Is it safe to swim in the sea in Majorca?

A: Yes, it is safe to swim in the sea in Majorca. However, it is important to follow the instructions of lifeguards and avoid swimming in areas marked by warning signs.

Q: What is the currency used in Majorca?

A: The currency used in Majorca is the Euro.

Q: What is the official language spoken in Majorca?

A: The official language spoken in Majorca is Catalan. However, Spanish and English are also widely spoken.
