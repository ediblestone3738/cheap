Majorca, located at the Eastern coast of Spain, is the largest of Balearic Islands. It is well-known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters and charming beachfront resorts. The island offers the perfect destination for all kinds of travelers looking for a relaxing getaway, and it is not difficult to understand why. With this in mind, we have put together this guide to help you Experience the Best of Majorca's Beaches with These Outstanding Resorts!

Best Beachfront Resorts

1\. Belmond La Residencia
Belmond La Residencia is a luxury hotel located in Deia, on the Western Coast of Majorca. It is surrounded by the Tramuntana Mountains with stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. It has 2 outdoor pools, one of which is heated and a spa that offers various wellness treatments.

2\. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa
Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa is nestled on a cliff, overlooking the stunning Port de Soller bay in Soller. It is a luxurious resort with three swimming pools, a spa, and a fitness center. It offers a wide range of dining services, including Michelin-starred options.

3\. Hotel Formentor
Hotel Formentor is a historic hotel located on the northern tip of Majorca, in the Bay of Pollensa. It is known for its luxury amenities, as it has a private beach, two outdoor pools, rooftop terrace dining, a golf course, and a spa.

4\. Iberostar Grand Portals Nous
Iberostar Grand Portals Nous is a luxury hotel situated in the exclusive area of Portals Nous. It has been designed by world-renowned Dutch designer Marcel Wanders and has a unique and modern design. It offers two outdoor pools, a spa, and a range of dining options.

5\. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort
St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is located in the southwest of the island near Portals Nous. The resort has two outdoor pools, a spa, a fitness center, and a beach club. It also offers a range of luxurious dining experiences, including a sushi bar and a Michelin-starred restaurant.

FAQs:

Q. What is the best time to visit Majorca?
A. The best time to visit Majorca is between April and September when the weather is sunny and dry.

Q. What is the official language spoken in Majorca?
A. The official language spoken in Majorca is Spanish, but English and German are widely spoken.

Q. What are some must-visit attractions while in Majorca?
A. Must-visit attractions in Majorca include: Palma Cathedral, Serra de Tramuntana, Cap de Formentor, Es Trenc Beach, Alcudia Old Town, and Caves of Drach.

Q. What is the currency used in Majorca?
A. The currency used in Majorca is Euro.

Q. Is Majorca a safe destination for travelers?
A. Majorca is a safe destination for travelers, but like any other tourist destination, travelers should take necessary precautions and be aware of their surroundings.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions

1\. Palma Cathedral
Palma Cathedral, also known as La Seu, is a Gothic-style cathedral located in the heart of Palma de Mallorca. It is one of the most recognizable landmarks of the city.

2\. Serra de Tramuntana
The Serra de Tramuntana mountain range offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and is a popular destination for hiking and cycling.

3\. Cap de Formentor
Cap de Formentor is a scenic cliff located at the northern tip of the island. It offers breathtaking views of the coastline and is a popular destination for photography enthusiasts.

4\. Es Trenc Beach
Es Trenc Beach is a stunning beach located on the southeastern coast of Majorca. It is known for its crystal clear waters and long stretches of white sand.

5\. Alcudia Old Town
Alcudia Old Town is a charming medieval town located in the north of Majorca. It is known for its well-preserved centuries-old buildings and narrow streets.

6\. Caves of Drach
The Caves of Drach is a stunning natural underground cave system located near Portocristo. It has underground lakes and a stunning light show making it a popular tourist attraction.

7\. Cala Millor Beach
Cala Millor Beach is a popular beach located on the east coast of Majorca. It is known for its bustling promenade, water sports activities, and family-friendly atmosphere.

8\. Bellver Castle
Bellver Castle is a Gothic-style castle located on a hill overlooking the city of Palma. It was built in the 14th century and today it houses the Museum of History of the City of Palma.

9\. Royal Palace of La Almudaina
The Royal Palace of La Almudaina is a Gothic-style castle located in the heart of Palma. It was built in the 13th century and today it serves as the official residence of the Spanish royal family.

10\. Palma Aquarium
Palma Aquarium is an aquarium located in Palma and is home to over 8,000 specimens of marine life from around the world.
