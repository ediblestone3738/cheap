# Good Places To Travel During Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year, and there's no better way to celebrate than by traveling to a destination that truly embraces the Christmas spirit. Whether you're looking for a winter wonderland, a cultural experience, or a tropical escape, there are countless places around the world that offer unique and memorable Christmas experiences. In this article, we'll explore some of the best places to travel during Christmas, each offering its own special charm and festive atmosphere.

## 1. Lapland, Finland

Lapland is often considered the ultimate Christmas destination, and for good reason. This northern region of Finland is home to Santa Claus Village, where you can meet the man himself and experience the magic of Christmas firsthand.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Visit Santa's official residence, meet his reindeer, and send postcards from the Santa Claus Main Post Office.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking Aurora Borealis, a natural light display that illuminates the Arctic skies.
- **Husky and Reindeer Safaris**: Embark on a thrilling ride through the snowy landscapes with a team of huskies or reindeer.
- **Snow Hotels**: Spend a night in a hotel made entirely of snow and ice, complete with intricate ice sculptures.

## 2. New York City, USA

New York City is a bustling metropolis that transforms into a winter wonderland during the holiday season. The city's iconic landmarks and festive events make it a must-visit destination for Christmas travelers.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Marvel at the towering Christmas tree and enjoy ice skating at the famous rink.
- **Holiday Window Displays**: Stroll along Fifth Avenue to see the elaborate holiday window displays at department stores like Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue.
- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Catch a performance of the Rockettes in this beloved holiday show.
- **Central Park**: Take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the snow-covered park for a romantic experience.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a city that exudes elegance and charm, especially during the Christmas season. Its historic architecture and festive markets create a magical atmosphere that is perfect for holiday celebrations.

- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the traditional Christkindlmarkt at Rathausplatz, where you can find handmade crafts and delicious treats.
- **Vienna State Opera**: Attend a performance of a classic opera or ballet in this world-renowned venue.
- **St. Stephen's Cathedral**: Visit this stunning Gothic cathedral and enjoy a concert of Christmas carols.
- **Schönbrunn Palace**: Wander through the beautifully decorated palace grounds and enjoy a cup of mulled wine.

## 4. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations. The city's vibrant lights and festive events make it an exciting destination for holiday travelers.

- **Illuminations**: Experience the dazzling light displays in areas like Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Roppongi.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit European-style Christmas markets, such as the Tokyo Christmas Market in Hibiya Park.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Enjoy the special Christmas-themed parades and decorations at this magical theme park.
- **Shopping**: Explore the bustling shopping districts of Ginza and Harajuku for unique gifts and souvenirs.

## 5. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City is a charming destination that offers a taste of European Christmas traditions in North America. Its cobblestone streets and historic architecture create a picturesque setting for holiday festivities.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the UNESCO World Heritage site, adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations.
- **German Christmas Market**: Discover the traditional market in the heart of the city, offering crafts, food, and entertainment.
- **Tobogganing**: Experience the thrill of tobogganing down the iconic Dufferin Terrace slide.
- **Ice Hotel**: Stay in the Hôtel de Glace, a stunning ice hotel that is rebuilt each year.

## 6. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a city that captivates visitors with its fairy-tale charm and rich history. During Christmas, the city's medieval architecture and festive markets create a magical atmosphere.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market in this historic square, featuring a towering Christmas tree and live nativity scenes.
- **Charles Bridge**: Stroll across this iconic bridge, adorned with holiday lights and offering stunning views of the city.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore the castle grounds and enjoy a concert of classical music in the St. Vitus Cathedral.
- **Traditional Czech Cuisine**: Indulge in holiday treats like trdelník (sweet pastry) and vánočka (Christmas bread).

## 7. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking a warm-weather Christmas, Sydney offers a unique holiday experience with its sunny beaches and festive events.

- **Bondi Beach**: Celebrate Christmas with a beachside barbecue and a swim in the ocean.
- **Carols by Candlelight**: Attend this beloved event at the Domain, featuring performances by local artists and a festive atmosphere.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Take a cruise on the harbor to see the city's iconic landmarks, including the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
- **Christmas Lights**: Explore the neighborhoods of Sydney to see the impressive light displays and decorations.

## 8. Strasbourg, France

Strasbourg is known as the "Capital of Christmas" and is home to one of the oldest and most famous Christmas markets in Europe. The city's half-timbered houses and festive decorations create a charming holiday atmosphere.

- **Christkindelsmärik**: Explore the historic Christmas market, offering a wide range of crafts, food, and gifts.
- **Cathedral of Notre Dame**: Visit this stunning Gothic cathedral and enjoy a concert of Christmas music.
- **Petite France**: Wander through this picturesque district, adorned with holiday lights and decorations.
- **Alsatian Cuisine**: Sample traditional dishes like choucroute (sauerkraut) and tarte flambée (thin-crust pizza).

## 9. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes and festive traditions. The city's cozy atmosphere and winter activities make it a perfect destination for holiday travelers.

- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's unique Christmas folklore, featuring mischievous trolls known as the Yule Lads.
- **Northern Lights**: Take a tour to see the mesmerizing Aurora Borealis in the Icelandic skies.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters of this famous spa, surrounded by snow-covered landscapes.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the markets in downtown Reykjavik for handmade crafts and local delicacies.

## 10. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh is a city that combines history, culture, and festive cheer during the Christmas season. Its stunning architecture and lively events make it a must-visit destination for holiday travelers.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Explore the market in East Princes Street Gardens, offering a variety of gifts, food, and entertainment.
- **Hogmanay**: Celebrate New Year's Eve with the famous Hogmanay festival, featuring fireworks, concerts, and street parties.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit this iconic landmark and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Royal Mile**: Stroll along this historic street, adorned with holiday lights and decorations.

## Conclusion

Whether you're dreaming of a snowy escape, a cultural adventure, or a sunny retreat, there are countless destinations around the world that offer unforgettable Christmas experiences. From the enchanting landscapes of Lapland to the vibrant streets of Tokyo, each location provides its own unique charm and festive atmosphere. As you plan your holiday travels, consider these incredible places to make your Christmas truly magical.
