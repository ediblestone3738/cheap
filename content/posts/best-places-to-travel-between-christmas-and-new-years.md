# Best Places To Travel Between Christmas And New Year's

The period between Christmas and New Year's is a magical time to travel. The world is still aglow with festive lights, and there's a palpable sense of anticipation in the air. Whether you're looking to escape the cold or embrace it, there are countless destinations that offer unique experiences during this special time of year. Here are some of the best places to travel between Christmas and New Year's.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is a classic destination for the holiday season. The city is famous for its extravagant Christmas decorations and New Year's Eve celebrations.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Witness the iconic Christmas tree and go ice skating on the famous rink.
- **Times Square**: Experience the world-renowned New Year's Eve ball drop.
- **Broadway Shows**: Catch a holiday-themed show or a classic Broadway production.
- **Central Park**: Enjoy a winter walk or a horse-drawn carriage ride through the snow-covered park.

## 2. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a winter wonderland during the holiday season, with its historic architecture and festive markets.

- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the Christkindlmarkt at Rathausplatz for traditional Austrian treats and crafts.
- **New Year's Concert**: Attend the famous New Year's Concert by the Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra.
- **Schönbrunn Palace**: Explore the beautifully decorated palace and its gardens.
- **Viennese Coffeehouses**: Warm up with a cup of coffee and a slice of Sachertorte.

## 3. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern celebrations during the holiday season.

- **Illuminations**: Marvel at the stunning light displays in areas like Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Roppongi.
- **Oshogatsu**: Experience the Japanese New Year with visits to shrines and temples.
- **Shopping**: Take advantage of post-Christmas sales in Tokyo's bustling shopping districts.
- **Traditional Cuisine**: Enjoy seasonal dishes like osechi ryori and toshikoshi soba.

## 4. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City is a charming destination that feels like stepping into a European village during the holidays.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the cobblestone streets adorned with festive lights and decorations.
- **Winter Carnival**: Get a taste of the upcoming Quebec Winter Carnival with pre-festival activities.
- **Ice Hotel**: Visit the Hôtel de Glace, a stunning hotel made entirely of ice and snow.
- **Skiing**: Hit the slopes at nearby Mont-Sainte-Anne or Stoneham Mountain Resort.

## 5. Sydney, Australia

For those looking to escape the winter chill, Sydney offers a warm and vibrant holiday experience.

- **Sydney Harbour**: Watch the spectacular New Year's Eve fireworks over the iconic Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
- **Beaches**: Relax on Bondi Beach or Manly Beach and enjoy the summer sun.
- **Taronga Zoo**: Spend a day exploring the zoo and its special holiday events.
- **The Rocks**: Discover the historic district with its markets, pubs, and festive atmosphere.

## 6. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik is a unique destination for those seeking adventure and natural beauty during the holiday season.

- **Northern Lights**: Chase the aurora borealis for a breathtaking natural light show.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Soak in the geothermal waters surrounded by snow-covered landscapes.
- **New Year's Eve Bonfires**: Join the locals in celebrating with bonfires and fireworks.
- **Golden Circle**: Explore the stunning natural wonders of Thingvellir National Park, Geysir, and Gullfoss.

## 7. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh is known for its lively Hogmanay celebrations, making it a top destination for New Year's festivities.

- **Hogmanay Street Party**: Join thousands of revelers for one of the world's biggest New Year's Eve parties.
- **Torchlight Procession**: Participate in the annual procession through the city streets.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the historic castle and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Festive Markets**: Explore the Christmas markets for unique gifts and delicious treats.

## 8. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a fairy-tale city that becomes even more enchanting during the holiday season.

- **Old Town Square**: Admire the Christmas market and the stunning Astronomical Clock.
- **Charles Bridge**: Stroll across the iconic bridge with its festive decorations.
- **Prague Castle**: Visit the castle complex and enjoy the holiday lights.
- **Traditional Czech Cuisine**: Savor dishes like roast pork, dumplings, and mulled wine.

## 9. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Rio de Janeiro offers a vibrant and energetic atmosphere for those looking to celebrate the New Year in style.

- **Copacabana Beach**: Join millions of people for the famous New Year's Eve celebration on the beach.
- **Fireworks**: Enjoy the spectacular fireworks display over the ocean.
- **Samba Music**: Dance to the rhythm of samba at street parties and clubs.
- **Christ the Redeemer**: Visit the iconic statue for breathtaking views of the city.

## 10. Lapland, Finland

Lapland is a magical destination for families and those seeking a true winter wonderland experience.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa Claus and his reindeer in Rovaniemi.
- **Husky Sledding**: Embark on an exhilarating husky sledding adventure through snowy forests.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the aurora borealis in one of the best locations for viewing.
- **Snow Hotels**: Stay in a unique snow hotel for a memorable experience.

## Conclusion

Traveling between Christmas and New Year's offers a unique opportunity to experience the world in a festive and joyful atmosphere. Whether you're seeking the excitement of a bustling city, the tranquility of a snowy landscape, or the warmth of a beachside celebration, there's a destination to suit every traveler's desires. Embrace the holiday spirit and create unforgettable memories in one of these incredible locations.
