# Best Places To Go To Escape Christmas

The holiday season can be a magical time for many, but not everyone wants to be surrounded by Christmas cheer. Whether you're looking to escape the hustle and bustle, avoid the festive decorations, or simply seek a different kind of adventure, there are plenty of destinations around the world that offer a respite from the holiday frenzy. Here are some of the best places to go to escape Christmas.

## 1. Marrakech, Morocco

Marrakech offers a vibrant and culturally rich escape from the traditional Christmas celebrations. The city is a sensory delight with its bustling souks, aromatic spices, and stunning architecture.

- **Explore the Medina**: Wander through the narrow alleyways of the Medina, where you can shop for unique handicrafts and enjoy the lively atmosphere.
- **Visit Jardin Majorelle**: This beautiful garden offers a peaceful retreat with its exotic plants and striking blue buildings.
- **Experience a Hammam**: Relax and rejuvenate in a traditional Moroccan bathhouse, a perfect way to unwind.

## 2. Tokyo, Japan

While Japan does celebrate Christmas, it is more of a commercial event rather than a religious one, making Tokyo an ideal destination for those looking to avoid traditional festivities.

- **Discover Shibuya and Shinjuku**: These bustling districts are perfect for shopping, dining, and experiencing the vibrant nightlife.
- **Visit Meiji Shrine**: Escape the city's hustle and bustle with a serene walk through this peaceful Shinto shrine.
- **Explore Akihabara**: Dive into the world of anime, manga, and electronics in this iconic district.

## 3. Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai offers a luxurious escape with its modern architecture, stunning beaches, and endless shopping opportunities. While Christmas is celebrated, it is not as pervasive as in Western countries.

- **Relax on Jumeirah Beach**: Enjoy the sun and sea on one of Dubai's most famous beaches.
- **Visit the Burj Khalifa**: Take in breathtaking views from the world's tallest building.
- **Explore the Dubai Mall**: Shop till you drop in one of the largest malls in the world, complete with an indoor aquarium and ice rink.

## 4. Buenos Aires, Argentina

In Buenos Aires, Christmas falls during the summer, offering a unique twist on the holiday season. The city is lively and full of energy, making it a great place to escape traditional Christmas celebrations.

- **Stroll through Palermo**: This trendy neighborhood is filled with parks, cafes, and boutiques.
- **Experience Tango**: Immerse yourself in the passionate world of tango with a live performance or dance class.
- **Visit La Boca**: Explore this colorful neighborhood known for its vibrant street art and historic architecture.

## 5. Bangkok, Thailand

While Christmas is celebrated in Bangkok, it is not a public holiday, and the city offers a myriad of activities that have nothing to do with the festive season.

- **Explore the Grand Palace**: Discover the opulent architecture and rich history of this iconic landmark.
- **Visit Wat Arun**: Enjoy the stunning views from this riverside temple, especially at sunset.
- **Shop at Chatuchak Market**: Browse through thousands of stalls offering everything from clothing to antiques.

## 6. Sydney, Australia

Christmas in Sydney is a summer affair, making it a perfect destination for those looking to escape the traditional winter festivities.

- **Relax on Bondi Beach**: Soak up the sun and enjoy the laid-back atmosphere of this famous beach.
- **Explore the Sydney Opera House**: Take a guided tour or catch a performance at this iconic venue.
- **Visit the Blue Mountains**: Escape the city with a day trip to this stunning natural wonder.

## 7. Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town offers a diverse range of activities and experiences, making it an ideal destination for those looking to escape Christmas.

- **Hike Table Mountain**: Enjoy panoramic views of the city and coastline from the summit.
- **Explore the V&A Waterfront**: Discover a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options.
- **Visit Robben Island**: Learn about South Africa's history with a tour of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

## 8. Reykjavik, Iceland

For those who want to embrace winter without the Christmas festivities, Reykjavik offers a unique and magical experience.

- **Witness the Northern Lights**: Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the aurora borealis.
- **Relax in the Blue Lagoon**: Unwind in the warm, mineral-rich waters of this famous geothermal spa.
- **Explore the Golden Circle**: Discover Iceland's natural wonders, including geysers, waterfalls, and national parks.

## 9. Buenos Aires, Argentina

In Buenos Aires, Christmas falls during the summer, offering a unique twist on the holiday season. The city is lively and full of energy, making it a great place to escape traditional Christmas celebrations.

- **Stroll through Palermo**: This trendy neighborhood is filled with parks, cafes, and boutiques.
- **Experience Tango**: Immerse yourself in the passionate world of tango with a live performance or dance class.
- **Visit La Boca**: Explore this colorful neighborhood known for its vibrant street art and historic architecture.

## 10. Goa, India

While Goa does celebrate Christmas, it is more of a cultural event, and the region offers plenty of opportunities to escape the festivities.

- **Relax on the Beaches**: Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea on Goa's beautiful beaches.
- **Explore Old Goa**: Discover the region's rich history with a visit to its colonial-era churches and buildings.
- **Experience the Nightlife**: Dance the night away at one of Goa's famous beach parties or clubs.

## Conclusion

Escaping Christmas doesn't mean you have to miss out on adventure and excitement. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene landscapes of Iceland, there are countless destinations around the world where you can enjoy a different kind of holiday season. Whether you're looking for relaxation, cultural experiences, or thrilling adventures, these destinations offer something for everyone seeking to escape the traditional Christmas celebrations.
