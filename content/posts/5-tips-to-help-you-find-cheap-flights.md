# 5 Tips to Help You Find Cheap Flights

Travelling by air can be expensive, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find great deals on flights. Here are five tips to help you find cheap flights:

## 1\. Book in Advance

Booking your flight in advance is the best way to get a good deal. Airlines usually offer discounts for those who book their tickets months ahead of time. Try to book at least two months in advance for the best prices.

## 2\. Use Flight Comparison Sites

Using flight comparison sites like Skyscanner or Kayak can help you find the best deals. These sites compare prices from different airlines and help you find the cheapest option.

## 3\. Be Flexible with Your Dates

## 4\. Sign Up for Email Alerts

Signing up for email alerts from airlines and flight comparison sites can help you stay up-to-date on deals and discounts. You can also follow airlines on social media to get notifications of special offers.

## 5\. Book Connecting Flights

Booking connecting flights can often be cheaper than direct flights. It may take a bit more time, but the savings can be worth it.
