# Is it Cheaper to Book a Hotel in Advance or Walk in?

As a travel expert, I'm often asked if it's cheaper to book a hotel in advance or to walk in. While it's almost always cheaper to book a room last-minute, how much cheaper is it, really? Turns out, though a bit cheaper, don't expect the bargain bin here. Rooms average 13% less when booked last-minute than booked four months in advance.

## Why Book a Hotel in Advance?

Booking a hotel in advance is a great way to ensure you get the best rate and the room you want. It also gives you peace of mind knowing that you have a place to stay when you arrive. Plus, if you book a hotel in advance, you can take advantage of any special offers or discounts that may be available.

## Advantages of Booking a Hotel in Advance

Booking a hotel in advance has several advantages. First, you can get the best rate available. Hotels often offer discounts for booking in advance, so you can save money by booking early. Additionally, you can be sure that the room you want is available. You can also take advantage of any special offers or discounts that may be available.

## Disadvantages of Booking a Hotel in Advance

Booking a hotel in advance also has some disadvantages. First, you may be stuck with the rate you booked if the hotel's prices go down after you book. Additionally, if you need to cancel your reservation, you may be charged a cancellation fee.

## Advantages of Walking In

Walking in to a hotel has some advantages. First, you can take advantage of any last-minute discounts or special offers that may be available. Additionally, you can often negotiate a better rate if you're willing to haggle. Finally, you can also check out the room before you commit to staying.

## Disadvantages of Walking In

Walking in to a hotel also has some disadvantages. First, you may not be able to get the room you want. Additionally, you may not be able to get the best rate available. Finally, if the hotel is full, you may have to find another place to stay.

## Conclusion

When it comes to booking a hotel, it's almost always cheaper to book a room last-minute than four months in advance. However, don't expect the bargain bin here. Rooms average 13% less when booked last-minute than booked four months in advance. Ultimately, it depends on your individual needs and preferences. If you're looking for the best rate, booking in advance is usually the best option. However, if you're looking for a last-minute deal, walking in may be the way to go.
