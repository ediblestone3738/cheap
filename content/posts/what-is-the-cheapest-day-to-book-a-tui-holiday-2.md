# What is the Cheapest Day to Book a TUI Holiday?

When it comes to booking a holiday, everyone wants to get the best deal possible. Whether you’re looking for a beach holiday, a city break, or an adventure holiday, you want to make sure you’re getting the best value for your money. So, what is the cheapest day to book a TUI holiday?

## Book Package Holidays on Monday

It turns out that the best day to [book a package holiday](/uncategorized/whats-the-cheapest-time-to-book-a-holiday/ "What’s the cheapest time to book a holiday?") is on a Monday. This is because the prices are sometimes dropped to try and entice customers who have had a quote at the weekend, but not completed a booking. So, if you’re looking to get the best deal on a TUI holiday, it’s worth checking out the prices on a Monday.

## Book Early for the Best Deals

It’s also worth noting that the earlier you book your holiday, the better the deal you’re likely to get. This is because the prices tend to increase the closer you get to the departure date. So, if you’re looking to get the best deal on a TUI holiday, it’s worth booking as early as possible.

## Look Out for Special Offers

TUI often has special offers and discounts available, so it’s worth keeping an eye out for these. They can be anything from discounts on flights, to discounts on accommodation, to discounts on activities. So, if you’re looking to get the best deal on a TUI holiday, it’s worth checking out the special offers.

## Book During the Low Season

The prices of holidays tend to be higher during the peak season, so it’s worth booking during the low season if you’re looking to get the best deal. The low season is usually from October to April, so if you’re looking to get the best deal on a TUI holiday, it’s worth booking during this time.

## Conclusion

So, to summarise, the best day to book a package holiday is on a Monday, as the prices are sometimes dropped to try and entice customers who have had a quote at the weekend, but not completed a booking. It’s also worth booking early for the best deals, keeping an eye out for special offers, and booking during the low season.

Overall, if you’re looking to get the best deal on a TUI holiday, it’s worth following these tips. Happy holiday planning!
