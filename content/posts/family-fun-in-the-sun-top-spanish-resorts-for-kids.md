Family Fun in the Sun: Top Spanish Resorts for Kids

Spain is known for its fabulous warm weather, friendly people, and family-friendly attractions. With so many world-class resorts offered across the country, it's no wonder that Spain is a hotspot for family vacations. The joy of traveling with family is that a break in one of Spain's picturesque beach resorts is the perfect solution for families of all ages. Whether you are looking for adventure, relaxation or a family-friendly atmosphere, you can be sure to find it in one of these top Spanish resort destinations with your kids in tow.

Most Asked FAQs:

Q: Is Spain safe for kids?
A: Yes, Spain is one of the safest countries in Europe. The locals are friendly and welcoming towards families, and crime rates are low. It's mostly safe to explore and experience the country with your children comfortably.

Q: Is there any kid-friendly activity to do in Spain?
A: Yes, there are many activities to do with kids in Spain, such as visiting the themed parks, enjoying the beaches, having a family picnic, exploring cultural activities, and many more.

Q: Will the locals speak English?
A: The majority of Spaniards speak some English, and it is straightforward to navigate your way around the resorts' touristic areas. However, it is always useful to learn some basic Spanish phrases.

Q: Is the weather good enough to sunbathe and swim throughout the year?
A: Spain generally has a Mediterranean climate, which means that in the summertime, temperatures can reach up to 40°C. However, it is warm enough to sunbathe and swim from around May to September. In winter, the temperature drops, but it’s still possible to get out and about.

Q: Can I bring my child's car seat to the resort?
A: Yes, you can bring your car seat to the resort. Spain's car seat law demands that children under the age of 12 or a height of 135cm ride in an approved car seat.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions located in Family Fun in the Sun: Top Spanish Resorts for Kids

1\. Costa Brava
Discover the beautiful coastline of Costa Brava with your loved ones while soaking up the sun. Prepare to see beautiful coves, pristine beaches, and butterfly parks.

2\. Barcelona
Barcelona is a superb destination for a family trip, with loads of activities to keep everyone entertained. Famous attractions include, but not limited to, Barcelona Aquarium, Park Guell, La Sagrada Familia, The Magic Fountain Show or Montserrat Monastery on the outskirts of the city.

3\. PortAventura World
Do not miss the fun at PortAventura World, a theme park with five distinctive areas that cater to all ages with its exceptional range of activities, including some of Europe’s most impressive rollercoasters.

4\. Seville
Discover the historical city of Seville, with its stunning cathedrals, huge parks, and breathtaking architecture.

5\. Tenerife Beaches
Relax and unwind on the beautiful white sand beaches in Tenerife, the largest of the Canary Islands. Also, be sure to experience its carnival, one of the largest and most extravagant in the world.

6\. Water Parks
A family-friendly must-do while in Spain is a day out at a water park. Many resorts boast their water-parks, including Costa Adeje in Tenerife, Aqualandia in Benidorm, and the Aquopolis in Madrid.

7\. Madrid
Spain’s exciting capital is home to an array of exciting activities, such as the Tren de la Fresa, Warner Bros Madrid and the amazing Spanish aquarium.

8\. Pirate Shows
Enjoy the fun and exhilarating pirate shows offered in different resorts, particularly in Mallorca, Lanzarote, and Tenerife, provide a unique and thrilling family-friendly show.

9\. The Costa Del Sol
Experience the Costa Del Sol and make unforgettable memories at resorts like Marbella, with its sandy beaches and fascinating old town, or with Torremolinos's stunning Mediterranean views.

10\. Valencia
Visit Valencia, the city of arts, to witness the futuristic architecture of the City of Arts and Sciences, take on the fun Torre de Emoción or pamper your sweet tooth with a chocolate factory tour.

Conclusion

Spain offers an endless array of opportunities for families to enjoy and have fun. With its warm climate, stunning landscapes, and a wide range of family-friendly activities and destinations, it's no wonder that Spain is a hotspot for family vacations. From playing on the white sandy beaches to experiencing thrilling amusement parks, exploring historical cities to enjoying pirate shows, there’s an endless adventure waiting for you and the kids in Spain.
