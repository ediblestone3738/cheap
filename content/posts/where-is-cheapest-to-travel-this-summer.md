# Where is Cheapest to Travel This Summer?

Summer is the perfect time to get away and explore the world. But, if you’re on a budget, you may be wondering where is cheapest to travel this summer?

Fortunately, there are plenty of destinations around the world that offer great value for money. From sunny beach holidays in Europe to exotic getaways in Asia, here are 10 of the top destinations for cheap beach holidays this summer.

## Malaga, Spain

Malaga is a great choice for budget-conscious travellers. This beautiful city on the Costa del Sol is home to some of the best beaches in Spain, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the city is full of culture and history, with plenty of museums and monuments to explore.

## Ibiza, Spain

Ibiza is a great destination for those looking for a cheap beach holiday. The island is known for its vibrant nightlife, but it also has some stunning beaches and a relaxed atmosphere. Plus, it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food, making it a great choice for budget travellers.

## Algarve, Portugal

The Algarve is one of the most popular beach destinations in Europe, and it’s easy to see why. The region is home to some of the best beaches in Portugal, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the region is full of culture and history, with plenty of museums and monuments to explore.

## Nice, France

Nice is a great destination for those looking for a cheap beach holiday. The city is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the city is full of culture and history, with plenty of museums and monuments to explore.

## Pula, Croatia

Pula is a great destination for those looking for a cheap beach holiday. The city is known for its stunning beaches and relaxed atmosphere, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the city is full of culture and history, with plenty of museums and monuments to explore.

## Antalya, Turkey

Antalya is a great destination for those looking for a cheap beach holiday. The city is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the city is full of culture and history, with plenty of museums and monuments to explore.

## Sharm el Sheikh, Egypt

Sharm el Sheikh is a great destination for those looking for a cheap beach holiday. The city is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the city is full of culture and history, with plenty of museums and monuments to explore.

## Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a great destination for those looking for a cheap beach holiday. The island is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife, and it’s easy to find cheap accommodation and food. Plus, the island is full of culture and history, with plenty of temples and monuments to explore.

## Conclusion

These are just some of the top destinations for cheap beach holidays this summer. Whether you’re looking for a sunny beach holiday in Europe or an exotic getaway in Asia, there are plenty of great options for budget-conscious travellers. So, start planning your summer getaway today and enjoy a great holiday without breaking the bank.
