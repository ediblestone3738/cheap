Mykonos is easily one of the best destinations in Greece, thanks to its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and picturesque villages. It has been a favorite among travelers for decades and for a good reason. The island is home to some of the most beautiful resorts, hotels, and vacation rentals, offering a plethora of all-inclusive deals for every budget.

So whether you are looking for a luxury escape or a budget-friendly getaway, Mykonos has something for everyone. In this travel article, we will explore the best all-inclusive deals that the island has to offer, along with the top ten tourist attractions that you must visit during your Mykonos getaway.

All-Inclusive Deals for Every Budget

Mykonos is home to a vast range of accommodations, ranging from ultra-luxurious villas to budget-friendly studios. There are all-inclusive deals to suit all budgets, which offer guests the convenience of having everything taken care of in one go. Here are some of the best all-inclusive deals that you can avail of during your stay on Mykonos island.

Luxury All-Inclusive Deals

Staying in one of the luxurious resorts in Mykonos is a memorable experience in itself. The island boasts some of the world's most lavish accommodations, such as the Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos or the Bill & Coo Coast Suites. These resorts offer world-renowned amenities such as private infinity pools, spa treatments, and personal butler services.

Mid-Range All-Inclusive Deals

Many mid-range hotels and resorts in Mykonos offer all-inclusive packages at a reasonable price. The Old Windmill Studios, Yakinthos Residence, and Mykonos Kosmoplaz Beach Resort Hotel are just a few of the options available for travelers on a budget.

Budget-Friendly All-Inclusive Deals

Travelers seeking budget-friendly all-inclusive deals can opt for self-catering villas, studios, or apartments. These types of accommodations come equipped with a kitchen, allowing you to prepare your meals, keeping your food expenses low. The studios at Kyma, Manoula's Beach Mykonos Resort, and Acrogiali Hotel are all excellent budget-friendly options.

Top Ten Tourist Attractions in Mykonos

Mykonos is a hotspot for tourists, and it's no surprise thanks to its beautiful beaches, quaint villages, and ancient landmarks. Here are the top ten tourist attractions that you shouldn't miss during your Mykonos getaway.

1\. Little Venice

Little Venice is a picturesque neighborhood in Mykonos that is famous for its beautiful houses built right up to the water's edge. It's the perfect place to catch the sunset with your loved ones.

2\. Panagia Paraportiani

The Panagia Paraportiani is one of the most photographed churches in Greece, thanks to its beautiful architecture. The church dates back to the 15th century and is a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

3\. Delos Island

Delos Island is an archaeological site that is home to some of the most well-preserved ancient landmarks in the world. It's a UNESCO World Heritage site and is considered to be the birthplace of Apollo and Artemis.

4\. Paradise Beach

Paradise Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Mykonos, famous for its crystal-clear waters and beautiful scenery. It's the perfect place to relax and soak up the sun.

5\. Tria Pigadia

Tria Pigadia is a charming square in the heart of Mykonos Town, featuring three wells that were used as a water source in the past. It's the perfect spot to enjoy a cup of coffee while watching the world go by.

6\. Ftelia Beach

Ftelia Beach is a secluded beach located on the north coast of Mykonos. It's an ideal choice for those looking for a more peaceful and relaxing beach experience.

7\. The Windmills

One of the most iconic landmarks in Mykonos, the Windmills date back to the 16th century and are a testament to the island's rich history. They are situated on a hill overlooking Mykonos Town.

8\. Agios Ioannis Beach

Agios Ioannis Beach is known for its crystal-clear waters and beautiful scenery, and it's the perfect place to unwind and soak up the sun.

9\. Mykonos Town

Mykonos Town is the island's capital and is known for its picturesque streets, white-washed buildings, and traditional architecture. It's a must-visit for anyone traveling to Mykonos.

10\. Psarou Beach

Psarou Beach is one of the most glamorous beaches in Mykonos, offering some of the best amenities on the island, such as beachside restaurants, bars, and luxury lounges.

FAQs

Q. Is Mykonos expensive?

Mykonos can be an expensive destination, particularly during peak season. However, there are plenty of budget-friendly options for accommodation, dining, and entertainment.

Q. How do I get to Mykonos?

Mykonos is easily accessible via ferry or flight. Several airlines fly to the island from major cities across the world, and ferries run from Athens and other nearby islands.

Q. What is the best time to visit Mykonos?

The best time to visit Mykonos is during the months of May to September, when the weather is warm and sunny. During peak season, the island can be crowded, so some prefer to visit during the shoulder season (May and September).

In Conclusion

Mykonos is truly one of the most beautiful destinations in Greece. Its picturesque villages, stunning beaches, and rich history make it a must-visit place for travelers worldwide. Whether you're looking for luxury or a budget-friendly getaway, there are all-inclusive deals for every budget. Pair this with the top ten tourist attractions that the island has to offer, and you are sure to have an unforgettable experience on your next Mykonos getaway.
