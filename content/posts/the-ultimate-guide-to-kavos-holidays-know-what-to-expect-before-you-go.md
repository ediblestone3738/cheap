Kavos is a small town located in the southern part of the Greek Island of Corfu. Known for its lively nightlife, Kavos has become a popular destination for young travelers looking to party and have a good time. However, there is much more to Kavos than just its clubs and bars. From its pristine beaches to its rich history and culture, Kavos has something to offer for every type of traveler. If you're planning a trip to Kavos, here is your ultimate guide to everything you need to know before you go.

Getting There

The easiest way to get to Kavos is by plane. Corfu’s international airport is located just 30 minutes away from Kavos by car. You can find direct flights from several European cities to Corfu. Once you arrive at the airport, you can hire a taxi or rent a car to reach Kavos.

If you prefer to travel by sea, there are several ferry services that operate between Corfu and the mainland. The journey time is approximately four hours.

Accommodations

Kavos has a wide range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. From budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels, there is something for everyone. Most of the accommodation in Kavos is located close to the beach and the town center. Some popular options include:

\- Morfeas Hotel: Located just 100 meters from the beach, this hotel offers a wide range of rooms and suites with sea views.
\- Ionian Sea View: This budget-friendly hotel is perfect for backpackers and budget travelers. It is located just a few minutes’ walk from the beach.
\- Kavos Panorama Complex: This aparthotel offers self-catering apartments with sea views. It is located close to the beach and the town center.

Nightlife

Kavos is known for its lively nightlife, with several clubs, bars, and pubs that stay open until the early hours of the morning. Some popular nightlife spots include:

\- Future Club: This is one of the largest and most popular nightclubs in Kavos. It has a capacity of over 3,000 people and hosts international DJs.
\- Atlantis: This club is located right on the beach and offers stunning sea views. It has a large dance floor and hosts regular foam parties.
\- Buzz Bar: This is a popular spot for pre-drinks before hitting the clubs. It offers a wide range of cocktails and has a lively atmosphere.

Food and Drink

Greek cuisine is famous for its fresh ingredients and delicious flavors, and Kavos doesn't disappoint when it comes to food and drink. Some must-try dishes include:

\- Souvlaki: This is a popular Greek fast food consisting of grilled meat served on a skewer.
\- Moussaka: This is a traditional Greek dish made with layers of eggplant, minced meat, potatoes, and béchamel sauce.
\- Tzatziki: This is a dip made with yogurt, garlic, and cucumber, and is perfect for dipping pita bread or vegetables.

For drinks, try some of the local spirits such as ouzo and tsipouro. And of course, no visit to Kavos is complete without trying a mythos beer or two.

Beaches

Kavos is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in Greece. With crystal clear waters and golden sandy beaches, these beaches are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. Some popular beaches include:

\- Kavos Beach: This is the main beach in Kavos and is the perfect place for swimming and sunbathing. It is also a popular spot for water sports.
\- Issos Beach: This is a long stretch of golden sand beach, perfect for sunbathing and relaxing.
\- Arkoudilas Beach: This beach is located on the south of Kavos and is well known for its crystal clear waters and beautiful scenery.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions

1\. Achilleion Palace: This beautiful palace was built for the Empress of Austria in the 19th century and offers stunning sea views.

2\. Old Town of Corfu: The Old Town of Corfu is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to several historic monuments and museums.

3\. Paleokastritsa Beach: This beautiful beach is surrounded by lush green hills and offers crystal clear waters perfect for swimming.

4\. Angelokastro Castle: This castle was built in the 13th century and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

5\. Kassiopi Castle: This castle was built in the 13th century and overlooks the beautiful Kassiopi Bay.

6\. Canal d'Amour: This beautiful beach offers unique geological formations and stunning rock formations.

7\. Glyfada Beach: This beautiful beach offers crystal clear waters and is perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

8\. Sidari Beach: This beautiful beach offers crystal clear waters and is known for its unique rock formations.

9\. Agios Georgios Beach: This beautiful beach offers crystal clear waters and is perfect for swimming and relaxation.

10\. Corfiot Cuisine: Explore the cuisine of Corfu and enjoy some of the delicious Greek dishes.

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Kavos?

A: The best time to visit Kavos is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Q: Is Kavos expensive?

A: Kavos can be expensive, especially during the peak tourist season. However, there are several budget-friendly options for food and accommodation.

Q: Is Kavos safe?

A: Kavos is generally a safe destination for tourists. However, as with any tourist hotspot, it is essential to take precautions and be aware of your surroundings.

In conclusion, Kavos has something to offer for every type of traveler. Whether you're looking for a lively nightlife, beautiful beaches, or rich history and culture, Kavos has it all. Hopefully, this guide has provided you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Kavos and have an unforgettable holiday experience.
