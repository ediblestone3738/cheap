Spain is a country that is synonymous with luxury and high-end holiday destinations. Pristine sandy beaches, warm sunshine, and a rich cultural history are just some of the reasons why Spain is a popular choice for those seeking a luxury holiday experience. However, with so many holiday resorts on offer, it can be difficult to know where to start when planning a luxurious getaway. Here, we offer the ultimate guide to Spain's most luxurious holiday resorts, to help you make the most of your escape.

Marbella

Marbella is one of the most famous luxury holiday destinations in Spain, thanks to its glamourous reputation and stunning location. Located on the Costa del Sol, Marbella is a thriving cultural hub which boasts a beautiful old town, designer boutiques, world-class dining, and a stunning marina. Perfect for those who want to soak up the sun and enjoy a luxurious atmosphere, Marbella is a must-visit destination for any luxury holiday.

Ibiza

Ibiza may be famous for its party scene, but this iconic island is also a paradise for those who are seeking a luxurious break. With its turquoise waters, rocky coves, and sandy beaches, Ibiza is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the finer things in life. Whether you prefer secluded, designer villas or five-star hotels, Ibiza has everything you need for a luxury getaway.

Mallorca

Mallorca is an island of contrasts, with rugged mountains, golden beaches, and crystal-clear waters. As one of the most popular holiday destinations in Spain, it's no surprise that Mallorca boasts some of the most luxurious resorts in the country. From spa treatments to private villas, Mallorca offers everything you need to unwind and recharge.

Costa del Sol

The Costa del Sol is a region that spans the southern coast of Spain, and it's been a favourite destination for luxury holidays for decades. With its stunning waterfront promenades, designer boutiques, world-class golf courses, and top-rated restaurants, the Costa del Sol is the perfect place to enjoy a luxurious break. Whether you're looking for a private villa or a five-star hotel, the Costa del Sol has a wide range of options to suit all tastes.

Costa Brava

The Costa Brava is located on the northeast coast of Spain and boasts some of the most spectacular scenery in the country. If you're looking for a luxury break away from the crowds, the Costa Brava is the perfect destination. With its unspoiled beaches, secluded coves, and picturesque villages, this region offers a unique and luxurious experience that's hard to find anywhere else.
