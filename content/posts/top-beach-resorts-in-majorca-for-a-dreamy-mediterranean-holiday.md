Majorca, also known as Mallorca, is one of the largest and most beautiful islands in the Mediterranean Sea. The island is a paradise of stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and brilliant sunshine that ensures any traveler will have a dreamy Mediterranean holiday. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway or an action-packed adventure, Majorca offers some of the best beach resorts that are perfect for a memorable holiday.

Here, we have rounded up the top beach resorts in Majorca that offer breathtaking sceneries, excellent amenities, and outstanding service that will make your vacation a time of relaxation, adventure, and rejuvenation.

1\. Gran Melia de Mar

Gran Melia de Mar is one of the most exclusive beach resorts in Majorca located in the town of Illetas, right on the beachfront. The resort boasts of a luxurious spa, four excellent restaurants, and a rooftop terrace that offers panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea. Apart from relaxation, guests can indulge in a range of activities that include water sports, tennis, golf, and hiking.

2\. Park Hyatt Mallorca

The Park Hyatt Mallorca is a luxurious retreat nestled on the island's northeast coast. The resort is surrounded by stunning hills and valleys and boasts of an infinity pool that offers beautiful views of the sea. The resort also features a range of facilities, including a golf course, spa, tennis courts, and a variety of dining and lounging options.

3\. Carrossa Hotel Spa Villas

The Carrossa Hotel Spa Villas is a beautiful resort located in the north of the island. The resort offers stunning views of the Tramuntana Mountains and the sea, making it the perfect place for those seeking calm and serenity. Guests can relax in the resort's luxurious spa, indulge in a round of golf at the nearby golf courses or take a dip in the beautiful infinity pool.

4\. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort

St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a luxurious beach resort located in the town of Costa d'en Blanes. The resort offers luxurious accommodations, a state-of-the-art spa, and several dining options that include Mediterranean and Asian cuisine. The resort also features a marina, where guests can take boat trips and a range of water sports.

5\. Belmond La Residencia

Belmond La Residencia is a beautiful hotel that offers a unique blend of traditional and modern decor. The hotel is located in the charming village of Deia, which boasts of beautiful beaches, turquoise waters, and rugged cliffs. The hotel features luxurious accommodations, a spa, a fitness center, and two excellent restaurants that serve local and international cuisine.

6\. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa

Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa is a five-star luxury resort located in Port de Soller that offers breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort features luxurious accommodations, an award-winning spa, and two superb restaurants that offer fresh seafood and traditional Mediterranean cuisine. While staying at the resort, guests can enjoy a range of activities such as mountain biking, kayaking, and hiking.

7\. Castell Son Claret

Castell Son Claret is a luxurious estate that is located in the southern part of the island, surrounded by immense natural beauty. The estate features a spa, tennis court, and two excellent restaurants that offer local and international cuisine. Guests can also indulge in a range of activities such as cycling, bird-watching, and hiking.

8\. Cap Rocat

Cap Rocat is a luxurious fortress located in the Bay of Palma that has been transformed into a timeless luxury resort. The fortress features luxurious rooms, a saltwater pool, a spa, and an excellent restaurant that offers local and international cuisine. Guests can take a dip in the clear blue waters of the Mediterranean Sea or unwind with a relaxing massage or an invigorating workout.

9\. Gran Hotel Son Net

Gran Hotel Son Net is located in a tranquil countryside setting, surrounded by romantic gardens and stunning views. The hotel offers luxurious accommodations, an outdoor pool, a spa, and two excellent restaurants that serve local and international cuisine. While staying at the hotel, guests can indulge in a range of activities leisurely walks in the countryside and horse riding.

10\. Iberostar Selection Playa de Palma

Iberostar Selection Playa de Palma is a luxurious beach resort located in the popular resort town of Playa de Palma. The resort features spacious rooms, an infinity pool, a spa, and several dining options that range from traditional Mediterranean cuisine to international favorites. Guests can indulge in a range of activities such as cycling, surfing, and paddle tennis.

FAQs

Q. What is the best time to visit Majorca?

A. The best time to visit Majorca is from May to October since the weather is warm and sunny.

Q. What are the best beaches in Majorca?

A. Some of the best beaches in Majorca are Es Trenc, Cala Varques, Cala Deia, and Calo des Moro.

Q. Which beach resorts in Majorca are the most luxurious?

A. Gran Melia de Mar, St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort, and Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa are some of the most luxurious beach resorts in Majorca.

Q. What activities can I do in Majorca?

A. There are a range of activities that you can do in Majorca that include water sports, hiking, cycling, visiting historic sites, and indulging in local cuisine.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in Top Beach Resorts in Majorca for a Dreamy Mediterranean Holiday!

1\. Bellver Castle

Bellver Castle is a Gothic-style castle that is located in the city of Palma. The castle is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Majorca that offers stunning views of the city and the Mediterranean Sea.

2\. Valldemossa

Valldemossa is a charming village that is located in the west of the island. The village is famous for its beautiful architecture, picturesque streets, and stunning valleys.

3\. Coves del Drach

Coves del Drach is a stunning cave system that is located on the east coast of Majorca. The caves feature stalactites and stalagmites that are illuminated and offer a breathtaking experience.

4\. Tramuntana Mountains

The Tramuntana Mountains are one of the most beautiful landscapes in Majorca. The mountains offer stunning views of the island and are ideal for activities such as hiking, mountain biking, and bird-watching.

5\. Palma Cathedral

Palma Cathedral, also known as La Seu, is a magnificent Gothic-style cathedral that dominates the city's skyline. The cathedral is located in the heart of Palma and is a must-visit tourist attraction in Majorca.

6\. Puig de Randa

Puig de Randa is a monastery that is located in the center of the island. The monastery was founded in the 13th century and offers stunning views of the island.

7\. Serra de Tramuntana

Serra de Tramuntana is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that is located in the north of the island. The site features stunning scenery, traditional settlements, and a rich culture and history.

8\. Alcudia Old Town

Alcudia Old Town is a preserved medieval town that is located in the north of the island. The town features narrow streets, traditional houses, and ancient architecture that showcases the island's rich history and culture.

9\. Es Cap de Formentor

Es Cap de Formentor is a stunning headland located in the north of the island. The headland offers panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and is home to a lighthouse that is a popular tourist attraction in Majorca.

10\. Palma Aquarium

Palma Aquarium is a modern and unique facility that is located in the city of Palma. The aquarium features a range of marine life, including sharks, turtles, and dolphins, and offers an educational and interactive experience for all visitors.
