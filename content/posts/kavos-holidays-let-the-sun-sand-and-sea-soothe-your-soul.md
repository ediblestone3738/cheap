Kavos Holidays: Let the Sun, Sand, and Sea Soothe Your Soul!

Kavos, located on the southernmost tip of Corfu island, is a popular holiday destination for tourists, especially for those looking for a lively party scene. The resort town boasts of pristine beaches, crystal clear waters, and a vibrant nightlife. Kavos is the perfect place to unwind, relax and soak up the sun.

Getting to Kavos

The easiest way to reach Kavos is by flying into Corfu International Airport, which serves a range of airlines from various European cities. From there, a taxi or private transfer can be taken to reach Kavos. The journey takes approximately 45 minutes.

Accommodation in Kavos

Kavos offers a variety of accommodation options ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. For those looking for a peaceful retreat, the town also has a few villas and apartments that are located away from the hustle-bustle of the main strip.

The main strip of Kavos is lined with bars, clubs, and restaurants, making it an ideal location for party-goers. However, for those seeking a peaceful retreat, it is best to choose accommodation that is further away from the main strip.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Kavos

1\. Issos Beach - Issos beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Kavos, known for its clear waters, golden sand, and scenic views of the mountains.

2\. Kavos Beach - Kavos beach is a bustling beach located in the heart of the town, and is perfect for people-watching and soaking up the sun.

3\. Aqualand Water Park - Aqualand Water Park is a fun-filled day out for the whole family. With a range of water slides and pools, it’s the perfect place to beat the heat in summer.

4\. Achilleion Palace - This palace was built as a retreat for Empress Elisabeth of Austria in the late 19th century. It is now open to the public and features stunning gardens and architecture.

5\. Kanoni – Kanoni is a scenic location that provides a panoramic view of Corfu town and the coastline. It's the perfect spot for a romantic sunset view.

6\. The Corfu Trail - The Corfu Trail is a 220-kilometer hiking path that runs through the island’s diverse landscapes, from olive groves to rugged mountains.

7\. Boat Trips - Boat trips are a great way to explore the coastline of Kavos and the surrounding areas. With a range of tours available, there is something for everyone.

8\. Sidari - Sidari is a popular tourist town located on the northern coast of Corfu. Known for its stunning beaches and rock formations, it’s a perfect day trip from Kavos.

9\. The Achilleas Dive Center - The Achilleas Dive Center offers a range of scuba diving experiences for beginners and experienced divers alike.

10\. The Mon Repos Estate - The Mon Repos Estate is a beautiful park located on the outskirts of Corfu town. It features a botanical garden, a small museum, and a stunning neoclassical villa.

FAQs

Q. Is Kavos suitable for families?

A. Kavos is not ideal for families with young children, as it is known for its party atmosphere. The main strip of Kavos is lined with bars and clubs that can get quite noisy in the night.

Q. What is the weather like in Kavos?

A. The climate in Kavos is typically Mediterranean, with hot and dry summers and mild winters.

Q. What activities are available in Kavos?

A. Kavos offers a range of activities for tourists, including water sports, boat trips, hiking, and scuba diving.

Q. What is the nightlife like in Kavos?

A. Kavos is known for its lively nightlife scene, with a range of bars and clubs that stay open all night.

Q. Is it recommended to rent a car in Kavos?

A. Renting a car is a great way to explore the island of Corfu, as it gives you the freedom to travel at your own pace. However, driving in Kavos can be difficult due to narrow roads and heavy traffic during peak season.

In conclusion, Kavos is an ideal holiday destination for those looking to soak up the sun and take part in lively party scenes. As well as offering a range of activities, Kavos also has a wealth of tourist attractions to explore, from stunning beaches to historical landmarks. Whether you are traveling with friends or solo, Kavos is sure to provide a memorable and vibrant holiday experience.
