The Indonesian island of Bali is known for its lush green landscapes, stunning beaches, and rich cultural heritage. But amidst all the serene beauty, there exists a place that offers an adrenaline-pumping adventure and tons of fun - Waterbom Bali.

Located in the heart of Kuta, Waterbom Bali is one of the top waterparks in Asia and caters to all ages. Spread across eight hectares of land, the park boasts of over 20 water attractions that range from adrenaline-filled slides to soothing waterfalls.

In this article, we take you on a journey through the thrills and chills of Waterbom Bali's water attractions and everything else you need to know before visiting.

Getting to Waterbom Bali

Waterbom Bali is conveniently located in Kuta, only a 15-minute drive away from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

If you're staying in other parts of Bali such as Seminyak or Ubud, you can take a taxi or hire a private car to reach the park. Alternatively, you can book a shuttle service offered by Waterbom Bali, which operates from major tourist areas.

The Attractions

With over 20 water attractions, Waterbom Bali guarantees a day filled with thrilling experiences and endless fun. Here's a list of some of the park's must-try rides -

Pipeline

If you're someone who loves high-speed thrills, then Pipeline is the ride for you. It's a slide that is 150 meters long and takes you through twists and turns at a fast pace. It's not for the faint-hearted, so brace yourself for an exhilarating experience that will leave you screaming for more.

The Climax

Looking for a ride that's both scary and fun? The Climax is a 26-meter drop that will leave you screaming all the way down. It's the tallest ride in the park and is known for its steep drop that gives you an adrenaline rush like no other.

Boomerang

Boomerang is one of the newest additions to Waterbom Bali and has quickly become a favorite among thrill-seekers. It's a ride that starts with a plunge from a height of 20 meters and takes you on a thrilling journey through a tube before shooting you up a curved wall. You'll experience weightlessness as you slide back and forth – a ride that's sure to leave you wanting more.

Superbowl

Superbowl is a ride that takes you on a whirlwind journey through a giant bowl-shaped slide. It's a ride that combines speed with precarious drops and spins. You'll find yourself spinning around in circles before being plunged into the pool below. It's a ride that's not for the faint of heart.

Green Vipers

The Green Vipers is a ride that's perfect for thrill-seekers who love twists and turns. It's a slide that takes you through a series of corkscrews and turns before dropping you into the pool. The ride is fast-paced and fun, and you'll experience several moments of weightlessness as you slide down.

The Constrictor

The Constrictor is a ride that's not for the faint-hearted. It's a slide that takes you through a series of twists and turns that will leave you feeling like you're being constricted. The ride is dark, so you won't see what's coming, and the twists and turns will leave you feeling disoriented. But if you're up for the challenge, the Constrictor is an experience that's well worth it.

The Lazy River

If you're looking for a ride that's more relaxed, then head over to the Lazy River. It's a river that takes you on a slow and soothing ride through the park. You can float along and take in the sights or just relax and soak up some sun.

Funtastic

Funtastic is a playground that's perfect for kids. It's a vibrant and colorful area that has several water guns, slides, and a giant bucket that drenches you with water when it tips over. It's a fun place for kids to exert their energy and enjoy a day of water-filled adventures.

Twin Racers

Twin Racers is a slide that allows two riders to race down side-by-side. It's an exhilarating ride that's perfect for friends or family members to compete against each other. The slide is fast-paced, and you'll find yourself zooming down the slide faster than you thought possible.

Smashdown

Smashdown is a slide that takes you through a series of twists and turns before dropping you into a pool of water. The ride is fast-paced and filled with suspense, and the final plunge into the pool will leave you breathless.

Python

Python is a tube slide that takes you through a winding path before dropping you into a pool of water. It's a ride perfect for those who love a mix of speed and twists and turns. You'll find yourself racing through the slide before being plunged into the pool below.

Flowrider

Flowrider is a ride that simulates surfing in a safe environment. It's a wave ride that allows you to catch a wave and surf on a board while maintaining balance. It's a great experience for beginners who want to try surfing without heading out into the ocean.

Tipping Bucket

The Tipping Bucket is an attraction that's perfect for kids. It's a giant bucket that fills with water before tipping over and drenching everyone below. The experience is fun and interactive and perfect for those who want to splash around and play.

Splash Pad

The Splash Pad is another playground that's perfect for kids. It has several water activities such as water guns, fountains, and smaller slides. It's a great place for kids to run around and let off some steam.

Water Blaster

Water Blaster is an interactive ride that allows you to control a blaster and shoot water at other riders passing by. It's a fun and interactive experience that allows you to engage with other riders and have a blast.

Swimming Pools

In addition to all the thrill rides, Waterbom Bali has several swimming pools spread across the park. It's a great place to cool off and take a break from all the excitement.

Food and Beverage Options

Waterbom Bali offers several food and beverage options ranging from fast food to Indonesian cuisine. There are several restaurants and snack bars located throughout the park, and you're sure to find something delicious to munch on.

Safety and Security

Waterbom Bali takes safety very seriously and ensures that all rides are regularly inspected and maintained. There are lifeguards stationed at every ride, and the park has a strict safety protocol in place to ensure the safety of all visitors. The park has several safety signs posted throughout, and visitors are required to follow park rules and regulations to ensure everyone's safety.

FAQs

1\. What time does Waterbom Bali open?

Waterbom Bali is open daily from 9 am to 6 pm.

2\. How much does it cost to enter the park?

The cost of entry varies depending on your age and whether you're a resident of Indonesia or an international visitor. You can find more information about ticket prices on the Waterbom Bali website.

3\. Can I bring my own food into the park?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed in the park. However, Waterbom Bali has several food and beverage options available throughout the park.

4\. Is there a dress code for the park?

There is no specific dress code for Waterbom Bali. However, visitors are advised to wear clothing that is comfortable and appropriate for water activities.

5\. Are there any restrictions on the rides?

Most rides have height and weight restrictions. The park has several signs posted throughout, and visitors are advised to follow these restrictions for their own safety.

Top 5 Tourist Attractions Located in Experience the Thrills and Chills of Waterbom Bali's Water Attractions

1\. Lumba-Lumba Dive Centre

Located just ten minutes away from Waterbom Bali, Lumba-Lumba Dive Centre is a must-visit for those interested in exploring Bali's marine life. The center offers PADI-certified diving courses and organizes trips to Bali's best dive sites.

2\. Kuta Beach

Kuta Beach is one of Bali's most popular beaches and is located just a 5-minute drive away from Waterbom Bali. The beach is known for its stunning sunsets, waves perfect for surfing, and lively atmosphere.

3\. Pura Tanah Lot

Pura Tanah Lot is a temple located on a rock formation off the coast of Bali. It's considered one of Bali's most iconic attractions and is a great place to watch the sunset.

4\. Bali Bird Park

Bali Bird Park is a bird sanctuary that houses over 1,000 birds from Bali and other parts of Indonesia. The park is spread across 2 hectares of land and is perfect for bird enthusiasts and families.

5\. Ubud Monkey Forest

The Ubud Monkey Forest is a nature reserve located in the heart of Ubud. It's a great place to observe Bali's indigenous long-tailed macaques up close, and visitors can also explore the forest's lush vegetation and ancient temples.

Conclusion

Waterbom Bali is more than just a waterpark – it's an experience that's sure to excite and thrill visitors of all ages. With over 20 water attractions, food and beverage options, and several swimming pools, it's a great place for families and friends to spend a day immersed in water-filled adventures. So, the next time you're in Bali, make sure to add Waterbom Bali to your must-visit list!
