# Best Places To Travel Over Christmas Break

The festive season is a magical time to explore new destinations, whether you're seeking a winter wonderland or a sun-soaked getaway. Christmas break offers a perfect opportunity to escape the routine and immerse yourself in the holiday spirit around the world. Here's a guide to some of the best places to travel over Christmas break, each offering unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas magic. The city transforms into a winter wonderland with twinkling lights and festive decorations.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Witness the iconic Christmas tree and go ice skating at the famous rink.
- **Fifth Avenue**: Stroll along this legendary street to see the dazzling holiday window displays.
- **Radio City Music Hall**: Catch the Rockettes in the Christmas Spectacular show.
- **Central Park**: Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride through the snow-covered park.

## 2. Lapland, Finland

For a truly enchanting Christmas experience, visit Lapland, the official home of Santa Claus.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa and cross the Arctic Circle.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking aurora borealis in the clear winter skies.
- **Reindeer Sleigh Rides**: Glide through the snowy forests on a traditional sleigh.
- **Snow Hotels**: Stay in a unique ice hotel for a night to remember.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a city that comes alive with festive cheer during the Christmas season.

- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the traditional markets at Rathausplatz and Schönbrunn Palace.
- **Vienna State Opera**: Enjoy a classical concert or opera performance.
- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit the stunning palace and its gardens, beautifully lit for the holidays.
- **Café Culture**: Warm up with a cup of Viennese coffee and a slice of Sachertorte.

## 4. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations.

- **Illuminations**: Experience the spectacular light displays at Roppongi Hills and Shibuya.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Enjoy the festive parades and decorations at this magical theme park.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the European-style markets in Hibiya Park and Yokohama.
- **Shopping**: Explore the bustling streets of Shibuya and Ginza for holiday shopping.

## 5. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City is a charming destination that feels like stepping into a Christmas card.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the cobblestone streets adorned with festive lights.
- **German Christmas Market**: Discover unique gifts and enjoy traditional treats.
- **Tobogganing**: Slide down the historic toboggan run at Dufferin Terrace.
- **Ice Hotel**: Experience a night in the Hôtel de Glace, made entirely of ice and snow.

## 6. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking a warm Christmas, Sydney offers sun, sand, and festive fun.

- **Bondi Beach**: Celebrate Christmas with a beach barbecue and surfing.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Enjoy a cruise with views of the iconic Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
- **Carols in the Domain**: Join the locals for a night of Christmas carols under the stars.
- **Taronga Zoo**: Visit the zoo for a unique Christmas experience with native wildlife.

## 7. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a fairy-tale city that becomes even more magical during Christmas.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market and admire the stunning tree.
- **Charles Bridge**: Walk across the historic bridge with views of the wintery cityscape.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore the castle grounds and St. Vitus Cathedral.
- **Traditional Cuisine**: Savor Czech holiday dishes like carp and potato salad.

## 8. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes.

- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters surrounded by snow.
- **Golden Circle**: Explore the natural wonders of Thingvellir, Geysir, and Gullfoss.
- **Christmas Village**: Visit the Hafnarfjörður Christmas Village for local crafts and treats.
- **Northern Lights**: Take a tour to see the mesmerizing aurora borealis.

## 9. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh is a city steeped in history and tradition, perfect for a festive getaway.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Browse the stalls for gifts and enjoy festive food.
- **Hogmanay**: Celebrate New Year's Eve with the famous street party and fireworks.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the iconic castle for stunning views of the city.
- **Royal Mile**: Explore the historic street lined with shops and pubs.

## 10. Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town offers a vibrant and sunny Christmas experience with a mix of culture and nature.

- **Table Mountain**: Take a cable car ride for panoramic views of the city.
- **V&A Waterfront**: Enjoy shopping, dining, and festive events by the harbor.
- **Kirstenbosch Gardens**: Attend the Carols by Candlelight concert in the botanical gardens.
- **Cape Winelands**: Explore the nearby wine regions for tastings and tours.

## 11. Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg is home to one of the most famous Christmas markets in the world.

- **Christkindlesmarkt**: Explore the market for handmade crafts and traditional treats.
- **Nuremberg Castle**: Visit the historic castle for a glimpse into the city's past.
- **Toy Museum**: Discover the history of toys in this charming museum.
- **Gingerbread**: Taste the city's renowned Lebkuchen, a traditional gingerbread.

## 12. Bali, Indonesia

For a tropical Christmas escape, Bali offers a serene and beautiful setting.

- **Beaches**: Relax on the stunning beaches of Seminyak and Nusa Dua.
- **Ubud**: Explore the cultural heart of Bali with its temples and rice terraces.
- **Spa Retreats**: Indulge in a rejuvenating spa experience.
- **Festive Dining**: Enjoy Christmas feasts at beachside restaurants and resorts.

## 13. Salzburg, Austria

Salzburg, the birthplace of Mozart, is a city filled with musical and festive charm.

- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the markets at Residenzplatz and Mirabell Palace.
- **Silent Night Chapel**: See the chapel where the famous carol was first performed.
- **Hohensalzburg Fortress**: Explore the medieval fortress with views over the city.
- **Sound of Music Tour**: Discover the filming locations of the beloved movie.

## 14. Rovaniemi, Finland

Rovaniemi, located in the Arctic Circle, is the ultimate Christmas destination.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa and send postcards from the Arctic Circle.
- **Arktikum Museum**: Learn about the culture and history of Lapland.
- **Husky Safaris**: Experience the thrill of a dog sled ride through the snow.
- **Snowmobiling**: Explore the winter landscape on a snowmobile adventure.

## 15. London, England

London is a city that embraces the holiday season with style and splendor.

- **Winter Wonderland**: Visit Hyde Park for rides, markets, and festive fun.
- **Oxford Street**: See the dazzling Christmas lights and shop for gifts.
- **Christmas at Kew**: Explore the illuminated gardens at Kew Gardens.
- **West End Shows**: Enjoy a Christmas-themed performance in London's theater district.

## Conclusion

Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or a sunny escape, these destinations offer something for everyone. From the enchanting markets of Europe to the vibrant celebrations in Asia and the sunny beaches of the Southern Hemisphere, the world is full of magical places to explore over Christmas break. Pack your bags, embrace the holiday spirit, and create unforgettable memories in these incredible destinations.
