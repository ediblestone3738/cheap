# Which Country is Cheapest to Fly From UK?

If you’re looking for the cheapest way to travel from the UK to another country, you’ll want to consider air travel. ## Paris, France

Paris is one of the most popular destinations for travelers from the UK, and for good reason. Because of the competition with the Eurostar and Eurotunnel, air fares to the French capital are usually pretty reasonable. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway or a city break, Paris is an ideal destination.
