# Which European Country is Cheapest?

When it comes to planning a vacation in Europe, one of the biggest considerations is cost. Europe is known for its high cost of living, but there are some countries that are more affordable than others. Here is a look at the cheapest European countries for expats.

## Portugal

Portugal is one of the most popular destinations for expats in Europe. This country is famous for its good food, beautiful beaches, a relaxed lifestyle, and affordable living. The cost of living in Portugal is lower than in other European countries, making it an attractive option for those looking to save money. In addition, Portugal is known for its friendly people and laid-back atmosphere, making it a great place to live.

## Slovenia

Slovenia is a Central European country that became an independent country in 1999. This small country is known for its stunning scenery, friendly people, and affordable living. The cost of living in Slovenia is lower than in other European countries, making it an attractive option for those looking to save money. In addition, Slovenia is known for its outdoor activities, such as skiing, hiking, and mountain biking.

## Spain

Spain is another popular destination for expats in Europe. This country is known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and affordable living. The cost of living in Spain is lower than in other European countries, making it an attractive option for those looking to save money. In addition, Spain is known for its delicious cuisine, lively nightlife, and friendly people.

## Croatia

Croatia is a small country located in the Balkans. This country is known for its stunning coastline, beautiful beaches, and affordable living. The cost of living in Croatia is lower than in other European countries, making it an attractive option for those looking to save money. In addition, Croatia is known for its rich culture, delicious cuisine, and vibrant nightlife.

## Bulgaria

Bulgaria is a small country located in the Balkans. This country is known for its stunning scenery, friendly people, and affordable living. The cost of living in Bulgaria is lower than in other European countries, making it an attractive option for those looking to save money. In addition, Bulgaria is known for its delicious cuisine, vibrant nightlife, and beautiful architecture.

## Italy

Italy is one of the most popular destinations for expats in Europe. This country is known for its stunning scenery, delicious cuisine, and affordable living. The cost of living in Italy is lower than in other European countries, making it an attractive option for those looking to save money. In addition, Italy is known for its vibrant culture, friendly people, and beautiful architecture.

Overall, there are many European countries that are affordable for expats. Portugal, Slovenia, Spain, Croatia, Bulgaria, and Italy are all great options for those looking to save money. Each of these countries has its own unique culture, cuisine, and attractions, making them all great destinations for expats. So, if you're looking for an affordable European vacation, these countries are definitely worth considering.
