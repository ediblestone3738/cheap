# What is the Cheapest National Motel Chain in the United States?

Traveling can be expensive, but it doesn't have to be. If you're looking for an affordable place to stay while you're on the road, you may be wondering what the cheapest national motel chain is in the United States. Fortunately, there are two motel chains that offer some of the lowest prices in the country: Motel 6 and Super 8.

## Motel 6

Motel 6 is one of the most popular budget motel chains in the United States. With over 1,400 locations across the country, it's easy to find a Motel 6 wherever you're traveling. Motel 6 offers basic amenities, such as free Wi-Fi, free local calls, and pet-friendly rooms. Prices vary by location, but you can usually find a room for around $50 per night.

## Super 8

## What to Look for When Choosing a Budget Motel

When choosing a budget motel, it's important to consider more than just the price. Here are some things to look for when choosing a budget motel:

- Location: Make sure the motel is in a safe area and close to the attractions you want to visit.
- Amenities: Make sure the motel offers the amenities you need, such as free Wi-Fi, free local calls, and pet-friendly rooms.
- Reviews: Read reviews from other travelers to get an idea of what to expect.
- Price: Compare prices between different motels to get the best deal.

## Conclusion

Motel 6 and Super 8 are the two cheapest national motel chains in the United States.
