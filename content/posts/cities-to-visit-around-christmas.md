# Cities To Visit Around Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year, and there's no better way to experience its charm than by visiting cities that truly embrace the Christmas spirit. From twinkling lights to festive markets, these destinations offer a unique and memorable holiday experience. Here are some of the best cities to visit around Christmas.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas magic. The city transforms into a winter wonderland, offering a plethora of holiday activities and sights.

- **Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree**: The iconic tree lighting ceremony is a must-see, drawing visitors from around the world.
- **Ice Skating at Central Park**: Glide across the ice while surrounded by the stunning Manhattan skyline.
- **Holiday Window Displays**: Stroll down Fifth Avenue to see the elaborate and creative window displays at stores like Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue.
- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Enjoy the world-famous Rockettes in this festive and high-energy show.

## 2. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is renowned for its elegant architecture and rich cultural heritage, making it a perfect backdrop for Christmas celebrations.

- **Christkindlmarkt at Rathausplatz**: This traditional Christmas market offers handcrafted gifts, delicious treats, and warm drinks.
- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit the Christmas Village at this stunning baroque palace for a unique shopping experience.
- **Concerts and Performances**: Enjoy classical music concerts and operas that capture the essence of the holiday season.
- **St. Stephen's Cathedral**: Attend a Christmas mass in this historic and awe-inspiring cathedral.

## 3. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's fairy-tale charm is amplified during the Christmas season, with its cobblestone streets and Gothic architecture.

- **Old Town Square Christmas Market**: Explore the market stalls offering traditional Czech crafts and foods.
- **Charles Bridge**: Walk across this historic bridge adorned with festive lights and enjoy the view of the city.
- **Prague Castle**: Visit the castle grounds for a taste of history and stunning views of the city.
- **Wenceslas Square**: Another bustling market location with a festive atmosphere and plenty of shopping opportunities.

## 4. Strasbourg, France

Known as the "Capital of Christmas," Strasbourg offers a quintessential holiday experience with its charming Alsatian architecture.

- **Christkindelsmärik**: One of the oldest Christmas markets in Europe, offering a wide range of gifts and culinary delights.
- **Petite France**: Wander through this picturesque district, beautifully decorated for the holidays.
- **Strasbourg Cathedral**: Marvel at the stunning Gothic architecture and attend a Christmas service.
- **Illuminations**: Enjoy the city's enchanting light displays that illuminate the streets and buildings.

## 5. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations, making it a fascinating destination during the holidays.

- **Illuminations**: Experience breathtaking light displays in areas like Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Roppongi.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Enjoy festive parades, decorations, and special holiday-themed attractions.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit European-style markets offering seasonal treats and gifts.
- **Shopping**: Explore the city's vibrant shopping districts for unique gifts and holiday sales.

## 6. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City exudes old-world charm with its cobblestone streets and historic architecture, creating a perfect setting for Christmas festivities.

- **German Christmas Market**: Discover traditional German crafts and foods in the heart of Old Quebec.
- **Château Frontenac**: Stay at this iconic hotel, beautifully decorated for the holiday season.
- **Tobogganing**: Experience the thrill of tobogganing on the Dufferin Terrace slide.
- **Winter Carnival**: Although it takes place after Christmas, the festive spirit continues with this lively event.

## 7. Copenhagen, Denmark

Copenhagen's cozy atmosphere and emphasis on "hygge" (comfort and coziness) make it an ideal Christmas destination.

- **Tivoli Gardens**: Visit this historic amusement park, transformed into a winter wonderland with rides, lights, and markets.
- **Nyhavn**: Stroll along the picturesque harbor, lined with colorful buildings and festive decorations.
- **Royal Copenhagen Christmas Tables**: Admire the beautifully set tables at this annual exhibition.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore markets throughout the city, offering Danish crafts and delicacies.

## 8. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh's historic charm and festive spirit make it a captivating place to celebrate Christmas.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Located in East Princes Street Gardens, this market offers a variety of gifts, food, and entertainment.
- **Ice Skating**: Glide across the ice rink with the stunning backdrop of Edinburgh Castle.
- **Hogmanay**: Although technically a New Year's celebration, the festivities begin in late December, adding to the holiday cheer.
- **The Royal Mile**: Explore this historic street, beautifully lit and decorated for the season.

## 9. Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg is home to one of the most famous Christmas markets in the world, offering a truly authentic holiday experience.

- **Christkindlesmarkt**: This traditional market features handcrafted ornaments, toys, and delicious treats like gingerbread and sausages.
- **Nuremberg Castle**: Visit the castle for panoramic views of the city and a taste of history.
- **Toy Museum**: Explore the history of toys and enjoy special holiday exhibits.
- **St. Sebaldus Church**: Attend a Christmas service in this beautiful medieval church.

## 10. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique and magical Christmas experience, with its stunning natural landscapes and festive traditions.

- **Northern Lights**: Witness the awe-inspiring aurora borealis, a natural wonder that adds to the holiday magic.
- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's unique Christmas folklore, featuring mischievous characters who visit children during the holidays.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore markets offering Icelandic crafts and delicacies.
- **Geothermal Pools**: Relax in the warm waters of the Blue Lagoon or other geothermal pools, a perfect way to unwind during the holidays.

## Conclusion

Each of these cities offers its own unique take on Christmas celebrations, from traditional markets to modern light displays. Whether you're seeking a cozy, intimate experience or a bustling, vibrant atmosphere, there's a destination on this list that will make your holiday season unforgettable. So pack your bags, embrace the festive spirit, and explore the magic of Christmas around the world.
