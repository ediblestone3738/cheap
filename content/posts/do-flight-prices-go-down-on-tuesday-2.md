# Do Flight Prices Go Down on Tuesday?

Are you looking for the best deals on airfare? Have you heard that Tuesdays are the cheapest days to fly? If so, you’re not alone. Many travelers have heard the same advice and are wondering if it’s true. The short answer is yes, flight prices do go down on Tuesdays.

## Average Domestic Airfare Price by Day

According to a study conducted by the Airlines Reporting Corporation (ARC), the average domestic airfare price by day is as follows:

Monday: $385

Tuesday: $361

Wednesday: $361

Thursday: $385

Friday: $395

Saturday: $415

Sunday: $425

As you can see, Tuesdays and Wednesdays are the cheapest days to fly domestically. On average, Tuesdays are 24% lower than peak prices on Sundays, which translates to savings of about $85 per ticket.

## The Cheapest Days to Depart if You're Flying Within the U.S.

If you’re flying within the U.S., the cheapest days to depart are midweek – generally Tuesday or Wednesday. According to the ARC study, Tuesdays are the cheapest day to fly, with an average airfare of $361. Wednesdays are also a good option, with an average airfare of $361.

## Tips for Finding the Best Deals on Airfare

In addition to flying on Tuesdays or Wednesdays, there are a few other tips you can use to find the best deals on airfare.

1\. Be flexible with your travel dates. Airlines often offer discounts for flights on certain days of the week or at certain times of the day.

2\. Sign up for fare alerts. Many airlines and travel websites offer fare alerts, which will notify you when the price of a flight drops.

3\. Book in advance. The earlier you book your flight, the more likely you are to get a good deal.

4\. Use a travel rewards credit card. Many travel rewards credit cards offer bonus points or miles when you book flights with certain airlines.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, Tuesdays and Wednesdays are the cheapest days to fly domestically. On average, Tuesdays are 24% lower than peak prices on Sundays, which translates to savings of about $85 per ticket.
