Zante Island, also known as Zakynthos, is one of the largest islands in the Ionian, located in the south-eastern part of the island group. The Island is famous for its sandy beaches, clear waters, and lush green forests that are home to an array of flora and fauna.

Zante Island is an all-year-round destination, with visitors coming to the island to enjoy the Mediterranean climate, the historical landmarks and the natural beauty of the Island. Zante Island is well connected to the mainland, with regular flights and ferries departing from Athens, Kefalonia and the other Ionian Islands.
