# What is the Cheapest Day of the Week to Book a Holiday?

Are you planning a holiday but want to save money? Have you ever wondered what the cheapest day of the week to book a holiday is? If so, you’re in luck! Norton Finance’s research has found that early/or mid-week flights (Tuesdays and Wednesdays) are cheaper overall. Flying on these days saves an average of 12% compared to flying out on a weekend.

## Why is Tuesday the Cheapest Day to Book a Holiday?

The reason why Tuesday is the [cheapest day to book a holiday](/uncategorized/whats-the-cheapest-time-to-book-a-holiday/ "What’s the cheapest time to book a holiday?") is because airlines are competing for customers on this day. Airlines know that most people prefer to travel on the weekends, so they lower their prices on Tuesdays to attract more customers. This means that if you’re willing to fly out on a Tuesday, you can save a lot of money.

## Tips for Booking a Cheap Holiday on a Tuesday

If you’re looking to book a cheap holiday on a Tuesday, here are some tips to help you save money:

- Book your flight as early as possible. Airlines often offer the best deals for flights booked at least two weeks in advance.
- Be flexible with your travel dates. If you’re willing to travel on different days, you may be able to find cheaper flights.
- Compare prices. Don’t just book the first flight you find. Take the time to compare prices on different airlines and websites to find the best deal.
- Sign up for airline newsletters. Many airlines offer special discounts and deals to their subscribers, so make sure you sign up for their newsletters.
- Take advantage of last-minute deals. Airlines often offer last-minute deals for flights departing on Tuesdays, so keep an eye out for these.

## Conclusion

If you’re looking to save money on your next holiday, consider booking your flight on a Tuesday. Norton Finance’s research has found that early/or mid-week flights (Tuesdays and Wednesdays) are cheaper overall. Flying on these days saves an average of 12% compared to flying out on a weekend. So, if you’re willing to fly out on a Tuesday, you can save a lot of money. Just remember to book your flight as early as possible, be flexible with your travel dates, compare prices, sign up for airline newsletters, and take advantage of last-minute deals.
