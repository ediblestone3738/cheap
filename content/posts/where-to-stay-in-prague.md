# Where to Stay in Prague

![Where to Stay in Prague](/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/kelsey-curtis-oK0PIhO2RPg-unsplash.jpg)

Prague, the capital city of the Czech Republic, is a popular tourist destination known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and vibrant nightlife. If you're planning a trip to Prague, one of the most important decisions you'll have to make is where to stay. In this article, we'll provide you with area and room suggestions to help you choose where to stay in Prague .

## Old Town (Stare Mesto)

If you want to be in the heart of the city, Old Town is the place to stay. This area is filled with historical landmarks, such as the Old Town Square, the Astronomical Clock, and the Charles Bridge. You'll also find plenty of restaurants, cafes, and shops in this area.

Some recommended hotels in Old Town include:

### The Grand Mark Prague:

This luxurious hotel is located in a 17th-century building and offers spacious rooms with modern amenities.

### Hotel Paris Prague:

This elegant hotel is situated in a historic building and features Art Nouveau design elements. The rooms are spacious and well-appointed.

### Hotel Kings Court:

This 5-star hotel offers stylish rooms with contemporary design elements. The hotel also has a spa, fitness center, and a rooftop terrace with views of the city.

## Lesser Town (Mala Strana)

Lesser Town is located on the left bank of the Vltava River and is known for its picturesque streets and Baroque architecture. This area is home to many historical landmarks, such as Prague Castle and St. Nicholas Church. Some recommended hotels in Lesser Town include:

### Augustine, a Luxury Collection Hotel:

This 5-star hotel is located in a former monastery and offers stylish rooms with modern amenities. The hotel also has a spa, fitness center, and a restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine.

### Aria Hotel Prague:

This boutique hotel is located in a former palace and features music-themed rooms. The hotel also has a rooftop terrace with views of the city.

### Alchymist Prague Castle Suites:

This 4-star hotel is located in a historic building and offers spacious suites with elegant furnishings. The hotel also has a spa and a restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine.

## Vinohrady

Vinohrady is a residential area located to the east of the city center. This area is known for its Art Nouveau architecture and tree-lined streets. Vinohrady is a quieter area compared to Old Town and Lesser Town, but still offers plenty of restaurants, cafes, and shops.

Some recommended hotels in Vinohrady include:

### Hotel Julian:

This 4-star hotel is located in a historic building and offers spacious rooms with modern amenities. The hotel also has a restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine.

### Hotel Ametyst:

This 4-star hotel offers stylish rooms with contemporary design elements. The hotel also has a restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine and a wellness center with a sauna and a hot tub.

### Hotel Taurus:

This budget-friendly hotel offers comfortable rooms with basic amenities. The hotel also has a restaurant serving Czech cuisine.

## Helpful Tips for the Traveler:

 **Book your accommodation in advance**, especially if you're traveling during the peak season.
Consider the location and proximity to public transportation when choosing your accommodation.
Check the reviews and ratings of the hotels before making a reservation.
If you're traveling on a budget, consider staying in Vinohrady or other residential areas outside of the city center.
Be aware of tourist traps and avoid overpriced restaurants and shops in the city center.

## Conclusion

### Choosing the right place to stay

Choosing the right place to stay in Prague can greatly enhance your travel experience. Old Town, Lesser Town, and Vinohrady are all great options depending on your preferences and budget. Additionally, be sure to consider the location and amenities of your accommodation when making your decision.

With these tips and recommendations, you should be well on your way to finding the perfect place to stay in Prague. Whether you're looking for luxury or budget-friendly options, there are plenty of great choices in this beautiful city.
