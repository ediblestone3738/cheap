Unforgettable Holidays at All-Inclusive Kos Hotels: Your Ultimate Guide

Kos, the third largest island in the Dodecanese group of islands in Greece is a popular tourist destination famous for its sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and rich history. Many tourists flock to the island in the summer months for a warm and sunny holiday experience. With a variety of hotels and resorts, Kos has become a top spot for all-inclusive package holidays. In this article, we will guide you through the ultimate experience of unforgettable holidays at all-inclusive Kos hotels.

All about all-inclusive Kos hotels

If you are considering an all-inclusive holiday in Kos, there are several options to choose from. All-inclusive resorts cover everything from full-board meals, snacks, drinks, and activities both for adults and children. There is no shortage of activities either, guests can indulge in sports activities like tennis, basketball, golf, and swimming or take part in fitness classes and spas. That means you can sit back, relax, and enjoy your time knowing that everything is taken care of.

Most Kos hotels offer all-inclusive packages, some with their own private beach access, so make sure to research before booking. From luxury hotels to budget-friendly resorts, there is something for every traveler. The setting varies from the lush green landscapes to the turquoise waters and sandy coastline. With plenty to see and do, you will be spoiled for choice.

FAQs about all-inclusive Kos hotels

1\. What is included in the all-inclusive package?

A: The standard package includes all meals, snacks, drinks, and activities within the resort.

2\. Do all-inclusive hotels offer alcohol?

A: Yes, all-inclusive packages include alcoholic drinks as well.

3\. Can I bring guests into the hotel who are not staying there?

A: Most hotels allow guests to visit but they may be restricted to specific areas to maintain the safety of the guests staying at the hotel.

4\. Can I book all-inclusive packages online?

A: Yes, most hotels have online booking facilities available on their website.

5\. What activities are included in the package?

A: The activities vary depending on the hotel but most offer sports activities, fitness classes, and spa treatments.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located in Unforgettable Holidays at All-Inclusive Kos Hotels

1\. Ancient Agora of Kos –Take a trip back in time to the ancient marketplace in Kos, which dates back to the 4th century BC.

2\. Asklepion – A hilltop sanctuary dedicated to the Greek god of healing, filled with historical ruins, temples, and therapeutic springs.

3\. Tree of Hippocrates – An iconic tree that has been a symbol of the Greek healer and physician Hippocrates, located in the capital city of Kos.

4\. Tingaki Beach – With crystal clear waters, it is one of the most popular beaches on the island, perfect for sunbathing and swimming.

5\. Kefalos Beach – A serene and picturesque beach, perfect for lounging and taking in the scenic views.

6\. Limnionas Beach – A secluded beach located in the east of Kos, offering stunning views and clear waters for diving and snorkeling.

7\. Lido Waterpark – A fun water park with slides and attractions suitable for all ages.

8\. Casa Romana – A historical villa that showcases the luxury and grandeur of ancient Rome.

9\. Plaka Forest – A peaceful escape from the busy urban life, ideal for hiking and nature lovers.

10\. Castle of Neratzia Fortification – A medieval fortress built by the Knights of St John, with incredible views of the sea.

In conclusion, an all-inclusive holiday in Kos is an excellent way to fully relax and make the most of your experience. Choose from one of the many hotels on the island and indulge in the variety of activities and facilities provided. Whether you are looking for a luxury getaway or a budget-friendly escape, Kos has it all.
