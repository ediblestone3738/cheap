When choosing your destination, it’s important to consider how much time you have. If you’re only taking four days of annual leave, you’ll want to choose a destination that’s relatively close by. Once you’ve chosen your destination, it’s time to make the most of your time. Make a list of all the activities you want to do and plan your days accordingly. You should also make sure to leave some time for relaxation – after all, that’s what holidays are for!
