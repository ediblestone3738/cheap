Mallorca is an exquisite Balearic island in Spain that is well-known for its sunny skies, endless beaches and crystal-clear waters. It is also a paradise for the discerning traveler seeking the ultimate in luxury, excellence in service, and exquisite amenities. Mallorca has a plethora of exquisite resorts that redefine luxury, offering guests an unforgettable holiday experience.

In this article, we will explore the best resorts in Mallorca, which are perfect for the discerning traveler looking to combine outstanding service with unique architecture, picturesque landscapes, and splendid amenities.

Cap Rocat

Cap Rocat is a former miliary fortress that has been converted into a stunning luxury hotel on the edge of the bay of Palma. This resort redefines luxury with its stunning outdoor pool, private beach, and exceptional views of the Mediterranean Sea. Cap Rocat is the perfect combination of exquisite architecture and natural beauty, allowing you to enjoy the best of both worlds. In addition to the stunning views, Cap Rocat is renowned for its exceptional dining experiences, with a focus on regional cuisine with international influences.

Belmond La Residencia

Belmond La Residencia is a luxury retreat nestled in the charming village of Deia, one of Mallorca's most picturesque villages. The resort is surrounded by idyllic olive groves, stunning mountain ranges, and the shimmering blue sea. Belmond La Residencia redefines luxury with its comfort, privacy, and impeccable service. The resort has two outdoor pools, six tennis courts, two gyms, a sauna, and a sumptuous spa with a wide range of treatments. The resort also has three restaurants, each offering a unique dining experience with regional and international influences.

Carrossa Hotel, Villas & Spa

Carrossa Hotel, Villas & Spa is a luxury resort that offers the ultimate holiday experience for the discerning traveler. The resort is nestled in the picturesque countryside, surrounded by lush green hills and stunning scenery. The resort offers guests a wide range of luxury amenities, including outdoor and indoor pools, a tennis court, a fitness center, and a spa, which offers holistic treatments inspired by the region's natural surroundings and heritage. The resort also boasts an exceptional restaurant, the Bistro Badia, which offers a creative menu inspired by the richness of Mallorca's local produce.

Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa

Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa is a seaside resort that offers the ultimate in luxury and relaxation. The resort is perched on a hilltop, offering stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and the town of Soller. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa offers a wide range of luxurious amenities, including an infinity pool, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a spa, which offers a range of holistic treatments. The resort also has three restaurants, each offering a unique dining experience, with a focus on local and international dishes.

St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort

The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a luxurious resort located in Calvia, one of the island's most prestigious areas. The resort blends traditional Mallorcan architecture with modern elements, creating a serene and elegant atmosphere. The resort offers guests a wide range of luxury amenities, including two outdoor pools, an indoor pool, two tennis courts, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a spa, which offers a range of treatments that combine eastern and western therapies. The resort also has three restaurants, each offering a unique culinary experience.

Hotel Sant Francesc

Hotel Sant Francesc is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Palma's old town, surrounded by stunning architecture and iconic landmarks. The hotel is housed in a former manor house that dates back to the 19th century, and has been transformed into a luxurious hotel with a unique character. The hotel offers guests a wide range of luxury amenities, including an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a spa, which offers a range of treatments that combine ancient therapies with modern techniques. The hotel also has one restaurant, which offers a refined dining experience with a focus on local and international cuisine.

Castell Son Claret

Castell Son Claret is a luxury retreat that offers guests an exclusive holiday experience in a stunning natural setting. Castell Son Claret offers guests a wide range of luxury amenities, including two outdoor pools, a tennis court, a fitness center, and a spa with a range of holistic treatments. The resort also has two restaurants, each offering a unique culinary experience with a focus on regional and international cuisine.

Grand Hotel Portals Nous

Grand Hotel Portals Nous is a luxury hotel located in the town of Portals Nous, just a short distance from the island's capital, Palma. The hotel has a contemporary design that blends perfectly with the natural surroundings, and offers guests a wide range of luxury amenities, including an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a spa, which offers a range of holistic treatments. The hotel also has one restaurant, which offers a creative menu inspired by the richness of local produce.

Park Hyatt Mallorca

Park Hyatt Mallorca is a luxury resort located in the picturesque village of Canyamel, in the northeast of the island.
