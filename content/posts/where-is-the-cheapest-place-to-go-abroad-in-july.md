# Where is the Cheapest Place to Go Abroad in July?

Are you looking for a great destination to travel to in July that won’t break the bank? You’re in luck! There are plenty of great places to visit that are both budget-friendly and full of adventure. Here are 13 of the best cheap places to travel in July.

## Niagara Falls, Canada

Niagara Falls is one of the most iconic and breathtaking destinations in the world. It’s also one of the most affordable places to visit in July. From the awe-inspiring views of the falls to the many activities available in the area, there’s something for everyone. You can take a boat tour, go hiking, or explore the nearby wineries. And with the favorable exchange rate, you’ll get more bang for your buck.

## Phuket, Thailand

If you’re looking for a tropical paradise, look no further than Phuket, Thailand. This island is known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and lush jungles. There’s plenty to do here, from snorkeling and diving to exploring the local markets and temples. And with the low cost of living, you can enjoy all of these activities without breaking the bank.

## Phoenix, Arizona

If you’re looking for a more domestic destination, consider Phoenix, Arizona. This desert city is full of adventure and activities. From hiking in the nearby mountains to exploring the unique desert landscape, there’s something for everyone. Plus, the cost of living is low, so you can enjoy all of these activities without spending a fortune.

## Montego Bay, Jamaica

Montego Bay is a great destination for a beach vacation. This city is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and friendly locals. Plus, the cost of living is low, so you can enjoy all of the activities without breaking the bank. From snorkeling and diving to exploring the local markets and nightlife, there’s something for everyone.

## The Bahamas

The Bahamas is a great destination for a tropical getaway. This archipelago is known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and vibrant culture. Plus, the cost of living is low, so you can enjoy all of these activities without spending a fortune. From snorkeling and diving to exploring the local markets and nightlife, there’s something for everyone.

## Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

Punta Cana is a great destination for a beach vacation. This city is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and friendly locals. Plus, the cost of living is low, so you can enjoy all of the activities without breaking the bank. From snorkeling and diving to exploring the local markets and nightlife, there’s something for everyone.

## Mazatlan, Mexico

Mazatlan is a great destination for a beach vacation. This city is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and friendly locals. Plus, the cost of living is low, so you can enjoy all of the activities without breaking the bank. From snorkeling and diving to exploring the local markets and nightlife, there’s something for everyone.

## New Orleans, Louisiana

New Orleans is a great destination for a city vacation. This city is known for its vibrant culture, unique architecture, and delicious food. Plus, the cost of living is low, so you can enjoy all of the activities without breaking the bank. From exploring the French Quarter to sampling the local cuisine, there’s something for everyone.

## Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for a beach vacation or a city getaway, there are plenty of great places to visit in July that won’t break the bank. From Niagara Falls to New Orleans, there’s something for everyone. So start planning your next vacation and enjoy the adventure!
