## Factors to Consider

When booking flights, there are several factors to consider. The time of year, the destination, and the airline are all important factors that can affect the price of a flight.

### Time of Year

The time of year can have a big impact on the price of a flight. Generally, prices are higher during peak travel times such as holidays and school breaks. If you are able to travel during off-peak times, you can often find lower prices.

### Destination

The destination can also have an impact on the price of a flight. If you are traveling to a popular destination, you can expect to pay more for your flight. On the other hand, if you are traveling to a less popular destination, you may be able to find lower prices.

### Airline

The airline you choose can also affect the price of a flight. Different airlines offer different prices for the same route. It is important to compare prices from different airlines to find the best deal.
