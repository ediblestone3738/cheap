## Top Wowcher Christmas Market Destinations

### 1. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's Christmas markets are a feast for the senses. Set against the backdrop of the city's stunning Gothic architecture, these markets are a must-visit.

- **Old Town Square**: The heart of Prague's Christmas celebrations, featuring a towering Christmas tree and a variety of stalls selling handmade ornaments and traditional Czech treats.
- **Wenceslas Square**: Known for its lively atmosphere, this market offers a wide range of gifts, from wooden toys to intricate glassware.
- **Must-Try Treats**: Indulge in Trdelník, a sweet pastry coated in sugar and nuts, and warm up with a cup of Svařák, the Czech version of mulled wine.

### 2. Vienna, Austria

Vienna's Christmas markets are legendary, offering a blend of elegance and festive cheer.

- **Rathausplatz**: The largest and most famous market, set in front of the stunning City Hall, features over 150 stalls and an ice skating rink.
- **Belvedere Palace**: A more intimate setting, this market offers a romantic atmosphere with its baroque backdrop and artisanal crafts.
- **Must-Try Treats**: Savor the taste of Vanillekipferl, crescent-shaped cookies, and sip on Weihnachtspunsch, a spiced punch that warms the soul.

### 3. Budapest, Hungary

Budapest's Christmas markets are a blend of tradition and innovation, offering a unique experience for visitors.

- **Vörösmarty Square**: The main market in Budapest, known for its high-quality crafts and live folk music performances.
- **St. Stephen's Basilica**: A smaller, yet equally charming market, featuring a light show projected onto the basilica's façade.
- **Must-Try Treats**: Enjoy Kürtőskalács, a chimney cake with a crispy exterior, and Forralt Bor, a spiced wine that is a Hungarian favorite.
