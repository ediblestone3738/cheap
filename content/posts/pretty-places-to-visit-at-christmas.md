# Pretty Places To Visit At Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year, and there's no better way to embrace the festive spirit than by visiting some of the world's most enchanting destinations. From snow-covered villages to vibrant cityscapes adorned with twinkling lights, these pretty places offer a perfect backdrop for your Christmas celebrations. In this article, we'll explore some of the most picturesque locations to visit during the Christmas season.

## 1. Rovaniemi, Finland

Nestled in the heart of Lapland, Rovaniemi is often referred to as the official hometown of Santa Claus. This winter wonderland offers a truly magical Christmas experience.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa Claus himself and explore his charming village, complete with reindeer and elves.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking Aurora Borealis dancing across the Arctic sky.
- **Snow Activities**: Enjoy snowmobiling, husky sledding, and ice fishing in the pristine wilderness.

## 2. Strasbourg, France

Known as the "Capital of Christmas," Strasbourg boasts one of the oldest and most beautiful Christmas markets in Europe.

- **Christkindelsmärik**: Wander through the historic market, filled with handcrafted gifts and delicious Alsatian treats.
- **Gothic Cathedral**: Admire the stunning Strasbourg Cathedral, illuminated with festive lights.
- **Petite France**: Stroll through this picturesque district with its half-timbered houses and cobblestone streets.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna transforms into a fairy-tale city during the Christmas season, with its elegant architecture and festive markets.

- **Vienna Christmas Markets**: Explore the various markets, including the famous Rathausplatz and Schönbrunn Palace markets.
- **Classical Concerts**: Experience a traditional Viennese concert in one of the city's grand concert halls.
- **Ice Skating**: Glide across the ice at the Rathausplatz skating rink, surrounded by festive decorations.

## 4. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's medieval charm is amplified during Christmas, making it a must-visit destination for holiday travelers.

- **Old Town Square Market**: Discover the vibrant market with its towering Christmas tree and nativity scenes.
- **Charles Bridge**: Walk across the iconic bridge, offering stunning views of the city and its festive lights.
- **Prague Castle**: Visit the historic castle, adorned with Christmas decorations and hosting seasonal events.

## 5. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas cheer, offering a dazzling array of holiday attractions.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Marvel at the iconic Christmas tree and enjoy ice skating beneath its twinkling lights.
- **Fifth Avenue**: Explore the elaborate holiday window displays at the city's famous department stores.
- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Experience the magic of the Rockettes in this beloved holiday show.

## 6. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City exudes old-world charm, with its cobblestone streets and historic architecture creating a picturesque Christmas setting.

- **German Christmas Market**: Browse the market's wooden stalls, offering unique gifts and traditional German treats.
- **Château Frontenac**: Stay at this iconic hotel, beautifully decorated for the holiday season.
- **Tobogganing**: Enjoy a thrilling ride down the Dufferin Terrace toboggan slide, offering stunning views of the city.

## 7. Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg is home to one of the most famous Christmas markets in the world, attracting visitors with its festive atmosphere.

- **Christkindlesmarkt**: Explore the market's stalls, offering traditional crafts and delicious gingerbread.
- **Nuremberg Castle**: Visit the historic castle, providing panoramic views of the city and its holiday lights.
- **Toy Museum**: Discover the city's toy-making heritage at this fascinating museum.

## 8. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations, with its vibrant cityscape coming alive with lights.

- **Illuminations**: Experience the city's stunning light displays, including the famous Marunouchi and Shibuya illuminations.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit European-style markets, offering festive foods and handmade crafts.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Enjoy a magical Christmas celebration at the theme park, complete with parades and special events.

## 9. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh's historic charm and festive spirit make it a delightful destination for Christmas travelers.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Explore the market's stalls, offering Scottish crafts and seasonal treats.
- **Hogmanay**: Celebrate New Year's Eve with the city's famous Hogmanay festival, featuring fireworks and live music.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the iconic castle, offering stunning views of the city and its holiday decorations.

## 10. Bruges, Belgium

Bruges is a fairy-tale city, with its medieval architecture and canals creating a magical Christmas setting.

- **Bruges Christmas Market**: Discover the market's charming stalls, offering Belgian chocolates and handmade gifts.
- **Ice Sculpture Festival**: Marvel at the intricate ice sculptures, depicting festive scenes and characters.
- **Canal Cruises**: Enjoy a scenic boat ride through the city's picturesque canals, adorned with holiday lights.

## 11. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique Christmas experience, with its stunning natural landscapes and festive traditions.

- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's mischievous Yule Lads, who bring gifts to children during the holiday season.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the awe-inspiring Aurora Borealis, illuminating the winter sky.
- **Geothermal Pools**: Relax in the city's famous Blue Lagoon, surrounded by snow-covered landscapes.

## 12. Salzburg, Austria

Salzburg's baroque architecture and musical heritage create a charming Christmas atmosphere.

- **Salzburg Christmas Market**: Explore the market's stalls, offering traditional crafts and delicious Austrian treats.
- **Sound of Music Tour**: Discover the city's musical history with a tour of iconic filming locations.
- **Hohensalzburg Fortress**: Visit the historic fortress, offering panoramic views of the city and its festive lights.

## 13. Tallinn, Estonia

Tallinn's medieval charm and festive spirit make it a hidden gem for Christmas travelers.

- **Tallinn Christmas Market**: Discover the market's stalls, offering Estonian crafts and seasonal delicacies.
- **Old Town**: Stroll through the city's cobblestone streets, adorned with holiday decorations.
- **Toompea Castle**: Visit the historic castle, providing stunning views of the city and its festive lights.

## 14. Copenhagen, Denmark

Copenhagen's hygge atmosphere and festive traditions create a cozy Christmas experience.

- **Tivoli Gardens**: Explore the amusement park's festive decorations and enjoy seasonal rides and attractions.
- **Nyhavn**: Stroll along the picturesque waterfront, lined with colorful houses and holiday lights.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the city's various markets, offering Danish crafts and delicious treats.

## 15. Zurich, Switzerland

Zurich's alpine charm and festive markets make it a delightful destination for Christmas travelers.

- **Zurich Christmas Market**: Explore the market's stalls, offering Swiss crafts and seasonal delicacies.
- **Bahnhofstrasse**: Wander along the city's famous shopping street, adorned with holiday lights.
- **Ice Skating**: Enjoy ice skating at the Dolder Sports complex, surrounded by stunning winter landscapes.

## Conclusion

Whether you're seeking a winter wonderland or a vibrant cityscape, these pretty places offer a perfect setting for your Christmas celebrations. From the enchanting markets of Europe to the dazzling lights of New York City, each destination provides a unique and magical holiday experience. So pack your bags, embrace the festive spirit, and create unforgettable memories in these beautiful Christmas destinations.
