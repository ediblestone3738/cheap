# Fun Places To Go Around Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year, filled with joy, laughter, and the spirit of giving. It's also the perfect time to explore new destinations and experience the festive cheer in different parts of the world. Whether you're looking for a winter wonderland, a tropical escape, or a cultural celebration, there are countless places to visit around Christmas that offer unique experiences. In this article, we'll explore some of the most fun and festive destinations to consider for your holiday travels.

## 1. Winter Wonderlands

### New York City, USA

New York City is a quintessential Christmas destination, offering a dazzling array of holiday activities and attractions.

- **Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree**: Witness the iconic tree lighting ceremony and enjoy ice skating at the famous rink.
- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Experience the magic of the Rockettes in this beloved holiday show.
- **Holiday Markets**: Explore festive markets like the Union Square Holiday Market and the Bryant Park Winter Village.
- **Fifth Avenue Window Displays**: Marvel at the elaborate holiday window displays at department stores like Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue.

### Lapland, Finland

For a truly enchanting Christmas experience, head to Lapland, the official home of Santa Claus.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa himself and cross the Arctic Circle in this charming village.
- **Reindeer Sleigh Rides**: Enjoy a traditional sleigh ride through the snowy landscapes.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking aurora borealis in the clear winter skies.
- **Snow Hotels**: Spend a night in a hotel made entirely of snow and ice.

### Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City transforms into a picturesque winter wonderland during the holiday season.

- **Old Quebec**: Stroll through the cobblestone streets adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations.
- **German Christmas Market**: Browse for unique gifts and enjoy traditional German treats.
- **Tobogganing**: Experience the thrill of tobogganing down the historic Dufferin Terrace.
- **Ice Hotel**: Visit the Hôtel de Glace, a stunning ice hotel open from January to March.

## 2. Tropical Escapes

### Honolulu, Hawaii

If you're dreaming of a warm Christmas, Honolulu offers a tropical paradise with a festive twist.

- **Waikiki Beach**: Relax on the sandy shores and soak up the sun.
- **Honolulu City Lights**: Enjoy the annual holiday light display and parade in downtown Honolulu.
- **Christmas Luau**: Experience a traditional Hawaiian luau with a holiday theme.
- **Diamond Head Hike**: Take a scenic hike up Diamond Head for panoramic views of the island.

### Sydney, Australia

Celebrate Christmas in the summer sun in Sydney, where the holiday season is filled with unique traditions.

- **Bondi Beach**: Join the festive crowds for a Christmas Day barbecue on the beach.
- **Carols in the Domain**: Attend Australia's largest free Christmas concert in the heart of Sydney.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Take a cruise on the harbor to see the city skyline and iconic landmarks.
- **Taronga Zoo**: Visit the zoo for special holiday events and meet the resident animals.

### Phuket, Thailand

Phuket offers a blend of relaxation and adventure for a memorable Christmas getaway.

- **Patong Beach**: Enjoy the lively atmosphere and vibrant nightlife.
- **Phi Phi Islands**: Take a day trip to explore the stunning islands and crystal-clear waters.
- **Christmas Markets**: Browse local markets for unique souvenirs and delicious street food.
- **Spa Retreats**: Indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment to unwind during the holidays.

## 3. Cultural Celebrations

### Vienna, Austria

Vienna is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and festive Christmas traditions.

- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the Christkindlmarkt at Rathausplatz for handcrafted gifts and mulled wine.
- **Vienna Boys' Choir**: Attend a concert by the world-famous choir for a magical musical experience.
- **Schönbrunn Palace**: Explore the palace and its Christmas market set in a stunning baroque setting.
- **New Year's Concert**: Extend your stay to enjoy the Vienna Philharmonic's New Year's Concert.

### Mexico City, Mexico

Experience the vibrant culture and traditions of Mexico City during the holiday season.

- **Las Posadas**: Participate in the traditional nine-day celebration reenacting Mary and Joseph's search for shelter.
- **Zócalo**: Visit the main square for festive decorations and an ice-skating rink.
- **Nochebuena**: Enjoy a traditional Mexican Christmas Eve dinner with family and friends.
- **Basilica of Our Lady of Guadalupe**: Attend a mass at this important religious site.

### Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition during the Christmas season.

- **Illuminations**: Marvel at the stunning light displays in areas like Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Roppongi.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore European-style markets offering festive treats and gifts.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Experience the magic of Disney with special holiday-themed parades and shows.
- **New Year's Celebrations**: Stay for the New Year's festivities, including temple visits and traditional meals.

## 4. Unique Experiences

### Reykjavik, Iceland

Iceland offers a unique and adventurous Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes.

- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters surrounded by snow-covered lava fields.
- **Golden Circle Tour**: Explore geysers, waterfalls, and national parks on this popular tour.
- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's mischievous Yule Lads, who bring gifts to children during the holiday season.
- **Northern Lights Tours**: Chase the elusive northern lights on a guided tour.

### Bethlehem, Palestine

For a spiritual and historical Christmas experience, visit Bethlehem, the birthplace of Jesus.

- **Church of the Nativity**: Attend a Christmas Eve mass at this significant religious site.
- **Manger Square**: Join the festive celebrations and processions in the heart of Bethlehem.
- **Shepherds' Field**: Visit the site where angels are said to have announced the birth of Jesus to shepherds.
- **Cultural Tours**: Explore the rich history and culture of the region with guided tours.

### Rovaniemi, Finland

Rovaniemi, located in the Arctic Circle, is the official hometown of Santa Claus.

- **Santa Claus Office**: Meet Santa and send postcards from the Santa Claus Main Post Office.
- **Arktikum Science Museum**: Learn about Arctic history and culture at this fascinating museum.
- **Husky Safaris**: Experience the thrill of a husky sled ride through the snowy wilderness.
- **Snowmobile Adventures**: Explore the Arctic landscapes on a guided snowmobile tour.

## 5. European Charm

### Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's fairy-tale architecture and festive atmosphere make it a perfect Christmas destination.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market and admire the stunning Christmas tree.
- **Charles Bridge**: Stroll across the historic bridge adorned with holiday lights.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore the castle grounds and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Czech Christmas Traditions**: Experience local customs like the Christmas carp and holiday pastries.

### Strasbourg, France

Strasbourg is known as the "Capital of Christmas" and offers a charming holiday experience.

- **Christkindelsmärik**: Explore one of the oldest Christmas markets in Europe.
- **Cathedral of Notre Dame**: Admire the stunning Gothic architecture and attend a holiday concert.
- **Petite France**: Wander through the picturesque district with its half-timbered houses.
- **Alsatian Cuisine**: Savor traditional dishes like choucroute and tarte flambée.

### Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh's historic charm and festive events make it a delightful Christmas destination.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Browse for gifts and enjoy festive treats in the heart of the city.
- **Hogmanay Celebrations**: Ring in the New Year with Scotland's famous Hogmanay festivities.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the iconic castle and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Royal Mile**: Explore the historic street adorned with holiday lights and decorations.

## Conclusion

The holiday season is a time for joy, celebration, and creating lasting memories. Whether you prefer a snowy escape, a sunny retreat, or a cultural adventure, there are countless destinations around the world that offer unique and festive experiences. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of Lapland, each destination has its own charm and traditions that make Christmas truly special. So pack your bags, embrace the holiday spirit, and embark on a journey to discover the magic of Christmas in a new and exciting way.
