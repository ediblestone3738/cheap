Experience Thrilling Adventure with Bali Swing: A Sensational Way to Explore Bali

If you are looking for a new type of adventure that combines adrenaline rush with cultural immersion, then you should try Bali Swing. Bali Swing is not your typical swing, it is a giant swing located in Ubud, Bali, that enables you to soar above the rice paddies, tropical rainforest and breathtaking waterfall. There’s no experience like it, and it’s no wonder that thrill-seekers and nature-lovers alike are flocking to Bali Swing to capture the ultimate Instagram photo while enjoying the thrill of the swing. Keep reading to discover everything you need to know about Bali Swing, including its top tourist attractions.

Bali Swing: What to Expect

Bali Swing is an eco-friendly attraction located in a lush jungle valley in Ubud, Bali. As soon as you arrive, you will be greeted by friendly staff who will present you with a menu of thrilling activities, including the giant swing, bird’s nest, flying fox, and many more. Once you have decided on which activity to engage in, the staff will guide you through the safety measures and give you a helmet and a harness.

The giant swing is undoubtedly the most popular activity, and it’s not hard to see why. It involves a massive 15-meter swing made of bamboo and sturdy rope, which is set on the edge of a valley overlooking the stunning rice terraces. You will be strapped into a secure harness and launched from a height of 20 meters, taking you on an exhilarating ride over the rice terraces and countryside. The adrenaline rush will give you a natural high that will last for days, and the photos you capture while riding will be the envy of all your friends.

If you are not keen on the giant swing, there are many other attractions to enjoy. One of the most popular is the bird’s nest. It’s a bit like a big birdhouse that is suspended over the valley by sturdy bamboo poles. You can sit inside the nest and enjoy a bird’s eye view of the breathtaking scenery around you, including the river, rice paddies, and lush green jungle.

Another popular attraction is the flying fox or zip line. You will be attached to a pulley and sent soaring across the valley, feeling the wind in your hair and the thrill of the ride. The tree houses are also worth exploring, as they give you a unique perspective of the natural environment around you.

FAQs: Everything You Need to Know About Bali Swing

1\. Is Bali Swing safe?

Yes, Bali Swing is super safe. Through the years, the attraction has built a strong reputation for safety and customer satisfaction. All of the equipment is regularly maintained and inspected by a team of professionals. The staff is also trained to the highest safety standards, and safety is always their top priority.

2\. How much is the entrance fee to Bali Swing?

The entrance fee starts at IDR 250,000 (approx. $17 USD) for a basic swing ride package. Once you arrive, you will be given a menu of attractions, which are priced separately. You can choose to buy a package deal, which includes two or three activities, or you can pay for each attraction separately.

3\. Is Bali Swing suitable for kids?

Yes, kids can enjoy Bali Swing, but there are weight and height restrictions for some of the attractions. For example, the giant swing has a minimum weight of 20kg and a maximum weight of 200kg. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. It is important to check the safety guidelines for each attraction before booking.

4\. Do I need to book in advance?

Booking in advance is recommended, especially during peak season. You can book online, through a travel agent, or through Bali Swing’s official website. Walk-ins may have to wait in line or may not be able to get a reservation, especially during busy weekends or holidays.

5\. What should I wear?

It is recommended to wear comfortable clothing and shoes, as you will be engaging in physical activities. Avoid wearing flip-flops or sandals, as they may slip off during the ride. It is also recommended to bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat, as it can get hot and sunny during the day.

Top 5 Tourist Attractions in Bali Swing

1\. Giant Swing: The giant swing is the most popular attraction, and it’s easy to see why. You get to soar over the stunning rice terraces and enjoy a fantastic view of the surrounding countryside.

2\. Bird’s Nest: The bird’s nest is another popular attraction. It’s like sitting in a giant birdhouse, high above the valley. It’s perfect for those who want to relax and enjoy the view.

3\. Flying Fox: The flying fox is a thrilling zip line that sends you soaring across the valley. It’s perfect for adrenaline junkies who want to get their heart pumping.

4\. Tree Houses: The tree houses are worth checking out, as they offer a unique vantage point of the surrounding jungle. You can climb up to the tree houses and enjoy a bird’s eye view of the valley below.

5\. Jungle Swing: The Jungle Swing is another exciting ride that combines speed and height. You will be sent swinging through the jungle forest, feeling the wind in your hair and the thrill of the ride.

In conclusion, Bali Swing is an excellent way to experience the thrill of adventure while immersing yourself in the natural beauty of Bali. Whether you choose to ride the giant swing, relax in the bird’s nest, or soar across the valley on the zip line, you are sure to have an unforgettable experience. So, if you are looking for your next travel adventure, book your trip to Bali Swing today!
