# Best Resorts To Celebrate Christmas

As the holiday season approaches, the excitement of celebrating Christmas in a magical setting is something many travelers look forward to. Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or a tropical escape, the world is filled with incredible resorts that offer unforgettable holiday experiences. In this article, we explore some of the best resorts to celebrate Christmas, each offering unique festivities, luxurious accommodations, and breathtaking surroundings.

## 1. The Ritz-Carlton, Aspen, Colorado

Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, The Ritz-Carlton in Aspen is a winter wonderland perfect for a traditional Christmas celebration.

- **Location:** Aspen, Colorado, USA
- **Highlights:**
  - Ski-in/ski-out access to Aspen Highlands
  - Cozy fireplaces and elegant holiday decorations
  - Festive activities such as gingerbread house decorating and holiday-themed spa treatments
  - Proximity to Aspen's charming downtown with its boutique shops and fine dining

## 2. Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, Alberta

For those dreaming of a white Christmas, the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise offers a picturesque setting in the Canadian Rockies.

- **Location:** Lake Louise, Alberta, Canada
- **Highlights:**
  - Stunning views of the frozen Lake Louise and surrounding mountains
  - Ice skating on the lake and horse-drawn sleigh rides
  - Traditional Christmas dinner in the elegant dining room
  - Access to world-class skiing and snowboarding at Lake Louise Ski Resort

## 3. The Brando, French Polynesia

If a tropical Christmas is more your style, The Brando in French Polynesia offers an idyllic escape with a touch of luxury.

- **Location:** Tetiaroa, French Polynesia
- **Highlights:**
  - Private island resort with pristine beaches and turquoise waters
  - Eco-friendly luxury villas with private pools
  - Christmas Eve and Christmas Day feasts featuring Polynesian and international cuisine
  - Snorkeling, diving, and other water activities in the surrounding coral reefs

## 4. Gstaad Palace, Switzerland

Experience the charm of a Swiss Christmas at the Gstaad Palace, a historic resort with breathtaking alpine views.

- **Location:** Gstaad, Switzerland
- **Highlights:**
  - Elegant rooms and suites with panoramic mountain views
  - Traditional Swiss Christmas markets and festive decorations
  - Gourmet dining options, including a special Christmas menu
  - Access to Gstaad's renowned ski slopes and winter sports

## 5. Aulani, A Disney Resort & Spa, Hawaii

For a family-friendly Christmas celebration, Aulani, A Disney Resort & Spa in Hawaii offers a magical experience for all ages.

- **Location:** Ko Olina, Oahu, Hawaii, USA
- **Highlights:**
  - Disney-themed holiday events and activities
  - Beautiful beachfront location with warm weather and ocean views
  - Character meet-and-greets with Mickey, Minnie, and other Disney favorites
  - Hawaiian-inspired Christmas feasts and entertainment

## 6. The Greenbrier, West Virginia

The Greenbrier, a historic resort in the Appalachian Mountains, offers a classic Christmas experience with Southern charm.

- **Location:** White Sulphur Springs, West Virginia, USA
- **Highlights:**
  - Grand holiday decorations and a massive Christmas tree in the lobby
  - Traditional holiday events, including a Christmas Eve concert and breakfast with Santa
  - Luxurious accommodations and a world-class spa
  - Outdoor activities such as ice skating, sleigh rides, and hiking

## 7. Atlantis, The Palm, Dubai

For a luxurious and extravagant Christmas celebration, Atlantis, The Palm in Dubai offers a unique blend of opulence and festive fun.

- **Location:** Palm Jumeirah, Dubai, UAE
- **Highlights:**
  - Spectacular holiday decorations and themed events
  - Underwater suites with views of the Ambassador Lagoon
  - Christmas brunches and dinners at award-winning restaurants
  - Access to Aquaventure Waterpark and The Lost Chambers Aquarium

## 8. The Lodge at Blue Sky, Utah

Escape to the serene beauty of Utah's mountains at The Lodge at Blue Sky, where a rustic yet luxurious Christmas awaits.

- **Location:** Wanship, Utah, USA
- **Highlights:**
  - Secluded location with stunning views of the Wasatch Mountains
  - Cozy cabins and suites with wood-burning fireplaces
  - Farm-to-table dining with a festive holiday menu
  - Outdoor adventures such as horseback riding, snowshoeing, and stargazing

## 9. The Peninsula, Hong Kong

Celebrate Christmas in style at The Peninsula, Hong Kong, a luxurious urban resort with a rich history.

- **Location:** Tsim Sha Tsui, Kowloon, Hong Kong
- **Highlights:**
  - Elegant holiday decorations and a grand Christmas tree
  - Festive afternoon tea and gourmet dining experiences
  - Spectacular views of Victoria Harbour and the city skyline
  - Convenient access to Hong Kong's shopping and cultural attractions

## 10. Soneva Fushi, Maldives

For a truly unique and exotic Christmas, Soneva Fushi in the Maldives offers a barefoot luxury experience on a private island.

- **Location:** Baa Atoll, Maldives
- **Highlights:**
  - Luxurious villas with direct beach access and private pools
  - Christmas celebrations with a focus on sustainability and local culture
  - Snorkeling and diving in the UNESCO Biosphere Reserve
  - Gourmet dining with fresh, organic ingredients

## Conclusion

Whether you're seeking a snowy retreat or a sun-soaked paradise, these resorts offer the perfect setting for a memorable Christmas celebration. From traditional festivities to unique cultural experiences, each destination provides a special way to enjoy the holiday season. As you plan your Christmas getaway, consider these exceptional resorts for a holiday filled with joy, relaxation, and unforgettable memories.
