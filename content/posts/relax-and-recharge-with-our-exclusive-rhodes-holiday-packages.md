Are you looking for a holiday spot that offers a combination of relaxation, adventure and a rich historical experience? Look no further. Rhodes Island is the perfect destination for you. It offers white sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, a rich history and culture, and an array of activities to indulge in. Rhodes Island is the largest of the Dodecanese islands and is situated in the Aegean Sea near the coast of Turkey. Here, we offer exclusive holiday packages that cater to a diverse range of travelers. So no matter what you’re looking for, we’ve got you covered!

Why travel to Rhodes Island?
Rhodes Island is a popular destination for travelers from Europe and the UK because it offers an ideal combination of activities, relaxation and a rich history & culture. From pristine beaches to ancient ruins like the medieval palace of the Grand Masters or the ruined temple of Apollo at Lindos, the island offers a chance to explore Greece's ancient past. The Old Town of Rhodes City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit spot for those interested in historical architecture, winding streets, and picturesque buildings.

Most Asked Questions:
Q: What is the best time of year to travel to Rhodes Island?
A: The best time to visit is between May and October when the weather is warm and sunny, and the chance of rain is low.

Q: What type of accommodation is available for travelers?
A: There is a wide range of accommodation options available, from budget-friendly hotels and guesthouses to luxurious resorts.

Q: Do I need a visa to visit Rhodes Island?
A: If you are a citizen of the European Union, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, or Liechtenstein, you do not need a visa to visit Rhodes Island. Citizens of other countries must check the visa requirements and apply accordingly.

Q: What type of cuisine should I expect in Rhodes Island?
A: Greek cuisine is diverse and delicious. Expect to indulge in dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, tzatziki, and spanakopita. Fresh seafood is also a staple and is a must-try while visiting the island.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Rhodes Island:
1\. Visit the Old Town of Rhodes: With narrow winding streets, this UNESCO World Heritage Site offers a chance to explore medieval Europe in Greece.
2\. The beach at Lindos: Set against towering cliffs, the turquoise waters of the beach beckon travelers.
3\. Ancient Kamiros: One of the three great cities of Rhodes Island, this ancient ruin offers a glimpse into a bygone era.
4\. Hike Mount Attavyros: A challenging hike, but once at the top, you'll witness breathtaking views of the island and the surrounding sea.
5\. Tsabika Beach: A perfect spot for swimming and sunbathing.
6\. Butterfly Valley: A stunning oasis that is home to thousands of butterfly species.
7\. Filerimos Hill: A spot that not only offers incredible views of the island but is also home to an ancient monastery.
8\. The Acropolis of Rhodes: A fascinating historical site that serves as a testament to the island’s rich history.
9\. Haraki Bay: A picturesque fishing village that offers a tranquil coastal experience.
10\. The Valley of the Butterflies: Perfect for nature lovers, this lush valley is home to thousands of butterflies and is a haven of peace.
