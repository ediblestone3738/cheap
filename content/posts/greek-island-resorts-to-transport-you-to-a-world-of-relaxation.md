Greece is a popular bucket list destination for many travel enthusiasts from around the world. Boasting of picturesque landscapes, delicious cuisine, and rich history, this country is home to countless breathtaking destinations. But the Greek Islands, in particular, are on par with some of the world's best island destinations. If you're looking for a vacation that promises relaxation, tranquility, and rejuvenation, look no further than the Greek Island Resorts.

Picture this - You're lying on a pristine white sand beach as the sun dips below the horizon, your toes buried in the sand and a gentle sea breeze caressing your skin. You open your eyes after a nap under the warm Greek sun to find that the crystal-clear turquoise waters are practically at your feet. You look out towards the horizon, taking in the vistas of white-washed buildings, and picturesque coves dotted with charming fishing boats. Greek Island Resorts are quite simply, the quintessential representation of paradise.

With 227 inhabited islands to choose from, there are plenty of Greek Island resorts to transport you to a world of relaxation. Each one of them has its own unique character, so you're spoiled for choice. Our top picks include, Santorini, Mykonos, Rhodes, Zakynthos and, Crete.

Santorini – This island is considered one of the most beautiful in the Aegean Sea. Fable says that it was created by the gods with exquisite architecture, breathtaking landscapes, and stunning blue waters. It’s the perfect place for a romantic holiday, featuring a multitude of restaurants and bars with jaw-dropping views, picture-perfect sunsets and an intimate atmosphere.

Mykonos - Known for its glamorous party culture and luxury accommodations, Mykonos has a reputation for being one of the most sophisticated destinations throughout Greece. Even with its reputation for round-the-clock partying, the island also has a stress-busting atmosphere which comes from the picturesque beaches such as the gorgeous Platis Gialos, to the small idyllic towns with narrow alleys and cozy taverns.

Rhodes - Rhodes Island is located in the southeastern Aegean, and it’s popular for its rich history, turquoise waters, and vast golden-yellow beaches. Here you can find a fascinating blend of ancient history and modern luxury, with boutique hotels tucked away in green gardens, beaches lined with palm trees and crystal-clear waters and adventure-rich cathedrals and ruins steeped in history.

Zakynthos - Zakynthos is postcard-perfect and is perfect if you're looking for a destination where you can relax as you listen to the sound of the waves. This destination boasts of quirky architecture, world-class beaches, mouth-watering cuisine, and a true sense of modern Greek luxury. Here you can indulge in a heavenly countryside retreat at Peligoni Club, which sits unobtrusively above the sea, with stunning panoramic views of Zakynthos Town and the neighboring islands of Kefalonia and Ithaca.

Crete - Crete Island, the most southern island of Greece, is highly regarded for its beautiful scenery, outstanding hospitality, and of course the incredible cuisine. This island is truly a one-stop destination with everything from quiet and peaceful villages that it's famous for to vibrant party towns like Malia, Heraklion, and Chania. Crete also has many world-class beaches like Elafonisi, Balos Beach perfect for lounging in a chaise, sipping cocktails, and enjoying the beauty of the island life.

FAQs

Q1. What is the Best Way to Get to Greek Island Resorts?

A: The most common way to reach Greek Island resorts is via the airport or ferry. Flights are available from nearby cities like Athens, while ferries run between islands or from the mainland.

Q2. Can You Travel Between Islands Easily?

A: Absolutely! When you book your transportation beforehand, traveling between Greek islands are a breeze. Whether you choose to travel by sea or air, you're sure to have a comfortable and enjoyable journey.

Q3. Is Greece Safe for Travelers?

A: Greece is a relatively safe country for travelers. Like in any other travel destination, petty theft and pickpocketing are common, so always be careful and attentive.

Q4. What is the Top Thing To Do in Greek Island Resorts?

A: The top thing to do in Greek Island resorts is to relax! Islanders, and Greek people in general, believe that life is good and that living a good life involves taking things at a relaxed pace. The Greek Islands offer plenty of opportunities for rest and rejuvenation via sunbathing, horseriding, exploring ancient ruins, and even sampling the local cuisine.

1\. Visit the Panagia Hozoviotissa Monastery in Amorgos
2\. Check out the beautiful views from Thira in Santorini
3\. Explore the Palace of the Grand Masters in Rhodes
4\. Hunt for hidden beaches and head to private bays in the Cyclades.

In conclusion, Greek Island resorts offer the best of both worlds - a break from life's stressors and an opportunity to take in awe-inspiring landscapes. From the sparkling water and sandy beaches in Zakynthos to the luxurious resorts of Mykonos, the Greek Islands offer you everything you need for a relaxing vacation. With plenty of options to choose from, it's time to start getting ready for your next adventure in a world of relaxation.
