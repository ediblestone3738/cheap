Nestled in the stunning Balearic Islands is the picturesque and sophisticated Mallorca. This incredible destination boasts golden beaches, crystal clear waters, a rich culture, and breathtaking landscapes - a true paradise for weary travelers seeking to unwind and recharge.

Mallorca is dotted with some of the world's most luxurious resorts, with each offering a unique blend of opulence, serenity, and indulgence. From secluded five-star retreats overlooking the Mediterranean Sea to glamorous hotels in the heart of the city, here are some of the best places to find your ultimate sanctuary in Mallorca.

Cap Rocat

Nestled in an ancient military fortress, Cap Rocat is a truly unique retreat that combines the charms of the old world with modern luxe amenities. With a private beach, an idyllic pool, and a soothing spa, this incredible resort lets you enjoy breathtaking sea views from every corner. It's perfect for adventurous travelers who seek outdoor activities like cycling and hiking while enjoying their stay in a cozy and luxurious villa designed with natural materials.

Belmond La Residencia

Belmond La Residencia set in the heart of the Tramuntana Mountains, is undoubtedly one of the most serene resorts in Mallorca. With immaculately landscaped gardens, a world-class spa, and Michelin star dining experience, this luxurious retreat evokes the very essence of serenity and rejuvenation. Every room is classically styled with Mallorcan art and traditional furnishings to elevate the best you can get from a Mallorcan Experience.

St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort

This contemporary-style resort is located on a stunning cliff edge overlooking the sea. With its pristine beach, two majestic pools, and a world-class restaurant, St. Regis Mardavall is a gateway to paradise. The rooms are spacious, and the views are simply breathtaking. The hotel is perfectly located near activities such as golf, shopping, and sightseeing, offering the best of both worlds.

Jumeirah Port Soller

Jumeirah Port Soller is a dream come true for anyone who loves breathtaking views, luxurious accommodations, and impeccable service. With an emphasis on sustainable living, this resort offers eco-friendly activities and locally inspired cuisine. Whether you opt to explore the stunning mountains or relax in your private Jacuzzi, Jumeirah Port Soller exceeds expectations on every front.

The Lindner Hotel & Spa

This tranquil and elegant hotel is situated in the quiet town of Bendinat. Its exquisite design and serene atmosphere make it an ideal place to escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The rooms are thoughtfully furnished with natural wood details, and the spa offers a range of relaxing treatments. The restaurant serves delicious traditional Mallorcan cuisine, and the hotel's pool and terrace provide the perfect place to unwind.

Son Brull Hotel & Spa

Son Brull Hotel & Spa is located in the charming town of Pollenca, in the heart of the stunning north of Mallorca. This former monastery has been carefully renovated, and its traditional architecture seamlessly blends with modern elements. The hotel boasts a unique spa that uses natural and organic products, while the restaurant offers an exceptional gastronomic experience.

Park Hyatt Mallorca

Surrounded by a stunning golf course, Park Hyatt Mallorca is an oasis of tranquility, luxury, and indulgence. The hotel's spacious guest rooms are decorated in a traditional Mallorcan style and furnished with modern amenities. Guests can enjoy the stunning views of the mountains or the sea while dining at the hotel's restaurant or relaxing in the sprawling spa.

Castillo Hotel Son Vida

Perched high on a hill overlooking the Bay of Palma, Castillo Hotel Son Vida is a majestic castle that exudes elegance and grandeur. The property is surrounded by a luxurious golf course, and guests can enjoy stunning panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea from the hotel's balcony or private terraces. The rooms are spacious and exquisitely furnished, and the restaurant serves traditional Mallorcan cuisine paired with fine wines.

These top 8 resorts in Mallorca offer an unparalleled experience and a genuine sense of escapism that will leave you feeling refreshed and renewed.

FAQs

1\. What is the best time to visit Mallorca?

The best time to visit Mallorca is from May to October, when the weather is warm and sunny.

2\. What are the must-visit attractions in Mallorca?

There are several must-visit attractions in Mallorca, including the Caves of Drach, Bellver Castle, Palma Cathedral, the Valldemossa Charterhouse, and Serra de Tramuntana mountains.

3\. Is it easy to get around Mallorca?

Yes, it is relatively easy to get around Mallorca. You can rent a car or a bike, take a bus, or hire a taxi.

4\. What is the currency used in Mallorca?

The currency used in Mallorca is the Euro.

5\. What type of food can I expect in Mallorca?

Mallorcan cuisine is a fusion of Spanish, African, and Middle Eastern influences, and it is known for its fresh seafood, paella, ensaimadas, and sobrasada.
