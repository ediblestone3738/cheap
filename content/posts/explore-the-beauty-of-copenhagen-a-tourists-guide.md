# Explore the Beauty of Copenhagen: A Tourist's Guide

Copenhagen is one of the most beautiful cities in Europe, and it is a great destination for tourists who want to explore the beauty of the city. From its stunning architecture to its vibrant culture, Copenhagen has something for everyone. This guide will provide you with an overview of the city and its attractions, as well as tips on how to make the most of your stay.

## History of Copenhagen

Copenhagen was founded in the 10th century, and it quickly became an important trading port. The city flourished during the Middle Ages, and it was the capital of Denmark for centuries. During this time, Copenhagen was at the center of many important events, such as the Protestant Reformation and the Industrial Revolution.

## Things to Do in Copenhagen

Copenhagen is home to many attractions, such as the Little Mermaid statue, the Royal Palace, and the Tivoli Gardens. The city also has a number of museums, such as the National Museum, the Ny Carlsberg Glyptotek, and the National Gallery. Additionally, there are plenty of shops, restaurants, and bars to explore.

## Transportation

Copenhagen has an efficient public transportation system, which includes buses, trains, and metro. Additionally, the city has a number of bike lanes, making it easy to get around by bicycle. Taxis are also available, and they are a convenient way to get around the city.

## Accommodation

Copenhagen has a wide range of accommodation options, from budget hotels to luxury resorts. There are also plenty of Airbnb options available, which can be a great way to save money. Additionally, there are a number of hostels in the city, which offer basic but comfortable accommodation.
