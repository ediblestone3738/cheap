FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Greece?
A: The best time to visit Greece is from April to June and from September to November, as the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller.

Q: What is the currency used in Greece?
A: The currency used in Greece is the Euro.

Q: Do I need a visa to visit Greece?
A: If you are a citizen of the European Union or the United States, you do not need a visa to visit Greece for stays up to 90 days.

Q: What is the language spoken in Greece?
A: The official language of Greece is Greek.

Q: What are the top tourist attractions in Greece?
A: The top tourist attractions in Greece include the Acropolis of Athens, the Parthenon, the Temple of Olympian Zeus, the ancient city of Knossos, the island of Santorini, the Meteora monasteries, the Palace of Knossos, the Temple of Poseidon, and the Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes.
