# What is the Most Important Thing to Remember When Staying in a Motel?

As a traveller, you may find yourself in need of a motel stay at some point. Whether you’re on a road trip, taking a break from a long journey, or just need a place to stay for the night, motels can be a great option. But before you book, there is one very important thing to remember when staying in a motel – always ask for the exact location.

## Why Knowing the Exact Location is So Important

Knowing the exact location of the motel is important for a few reasons. First, it can help you determine if the motel is in a safe area. You can use Google Maps to scour the area and get a better idea of the surrounding neighbourhood. This way, you can make sure that you’re staying in a safe place.

Second, knowing the exact location can help you determine what restaurants and entertainment hotspots may be a short walk away. If you’re looking for a place to grab a bite to eat or a spot to spend an evening, it’s helpful to know what’s nearby.

Finally, knowing the exact location can help you determine if the motel is close to any attractions or activities you may want to visit. If you’re planning a day trip or want to explore the area, it’s important to know if the motel is close to any of the places you want to go.

## How to Find the Exact Location of a Motel

Finding the exact location of a motel is easy. When you’re searching for a motel, make sure to look for the address or a map of the exact location. You can also use Google Maps to search for the motel and get a better idea of the area.

If you’re booking a motel online, make sure to read the reviews and look for any information about the exact location. You can also call the motel directly and ask for the exact address.

## Tips for Staying Safe in a Motel

In addition to asking for the exact location of the motel, there are a few other tips to keep in mind when staying in a motel. Make sure to check the locks on the doors and windows to make sure they are secure. You should also be aware of your surroundings and keep your valuables with you at all times.

If you’re travelling with children, make sure to keep an eye on them at all times. You should also avoid walking alone at night and keep your doors locked.

## Conclusion

Staying in a motel can be a great option for travellers. But before you book, it’s important to remember to always ask for the exact location of the motel. This way, you can make sure that you’re staying in a safe area and that you’re close to any restaurants or attractions you may want to visit. It’s also important to keep in mind a few safety tips to ensure that your stay is safe and enjoyable.
