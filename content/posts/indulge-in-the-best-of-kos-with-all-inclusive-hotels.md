Indulge in the Best of Kos with All-Inclusive Hotels

Apart from the comfort and convenience of staying in an all-inclusive hotel, there are numerous tourist attractions in Kos for visitors to discover. Here are the top 10:

1\. Asklepion

Asklepion is an ancient temple dedicated to the god of healing, Asclepius. This historic site is located on a hill and offers stunning views of the island. Visitors can explore the ruins of the temple and learn about the history of medicine and healing practices in ancient Greece.

2\. Kos Town

Kos Town is the capital of the island and is a must-visit destination for anyone visiting Kos. The town boasts beautiful architecture, picturesque streets, and a rich history that dates back to the 14th century.

3\. Eleftherias Square

Eleftherias Square is located in Kos Town and is a popular gathering spot for locals and tourists alike. The square is surrounded by bustling cafes, shops, and restaurants, making it the perfect spot for people watching.

4\. Agios Stefanos Beach

Agios Stefanos Beach is located in the northwest of Kos and is one of the most beautiful beaches on the island. It features crystal-clear waters and soft sand, making it a perfect spot for swimming and sunbathing.

5\. Embros Therme

Located close to Agios Fokas, Embros Therme is a natural thermal spa that offers a unique experience for visitors. The warm water is said to have healing properties and is a great way to relax and rejuvenate after a long day of exploring.

6\. Lido Water Park

Lido Water Park is located on the outskirts of Kos Town and is a great spot for families to enjoy a fun day out. The park features water slides, pools, and a variety of other attractions that promise to keep you entertained all day.

7\. The Castle of Antimachia

Located in the village of Antimachia, The Castle of Antimachia is a historic monument that offers visitors a glimpse into the island's rich history. The castle was built in the 15th century and showcases a blend of Byzantine and medieval architecture.

8\. Hippocrates Garden Cultural Center

The Hippocrates Garden Cultural Center is located in Mastichari and offers visitors the chance to learn more about the life and teachings of Hippocrates, the father of Western medicine. The center features a garden filled with medicinal plants and herbs, as well as an interactive museum.

9\. Agios Theologos Beach

Agios Theologos Beach is located on the southwestern coast of the island and is a popular spot for windsurfers. The beach features a long stretch of golden sand and crystal-clear waters, making it a great place to soak up the sun.

10\. Kefalos Beach

Kefalos Beach is located in the village of Kefalos and is a great spot for families with kids. The beach features calm waters and soft sand, making it a great spot for swimming and playing in the sand.
