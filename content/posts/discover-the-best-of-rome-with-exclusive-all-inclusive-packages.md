Now let's take a look at the top ten tourist attractions located in Discover the Best of Rome with Exclusive All-Inclusive Packages.

1\. The Colosseum
The Colosseum is one of the most iconic landmarks in Rome, and it's a must-visit for anyone visiting the city. This ancient amphitheater is a testament to the engineering prowess of the Romans and is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the city.

2\. The Vatican Museums and Sistine Chapel
The Vatican Museums are a treasure trove of art, sculpture, and history. The Sistine Chapel is a highlight of the museum, featuring Michelangelo's famous ceiling fresco. A visit to the Vatican Museums is a must for art lovers and history buffs.

3\. The Pantheon
The Pantheon is one of the most well-preserved ancient Roman buildings in the city. It has a fascinating history, having been a temple to all gods in ancient times and later transformed into a Christian church.

4\. The Roman Forum
The Roman Forum is a vast complex of ruins that was once the heart of ancient Rome. It's a fascinating glimpse into the city's past and a must-visit for history buffs.

5\. The Trevi Fountain
The Trevi Fountain is an iconic landmark in Rome and is one of the most famous fountains in the world. The fountain is believed to grant wishes, making it a popular spot for visitors.

6\. The Spanish Steps
The Spanish Steps are a popular and picturesque spot in Rome. They offer a great view of the city and are an ideal spot for people watching.

7\. The Piazza Navona
The Piazza Navona is a large public square in Rome, home to several iconic fountains and Baroque architecture. It's a great spot to sit and soak up the atmosphere of the city.

8\. The Catacombs of Rome
The Catacombs of Rome are a fascinating underground network of tunnels and tombs. They offer a unique insight into ancient Roman burial practices.

9\. The Villa Borghese Gardens
The Villa Borghese Gardens are one of the largest public parks in Rome. The park is home to several museums, galleries, and a stunning lake. It's a great spot for a picnic or a leisurely stroll.

10\. The Trastevere District
The Trastevere District is a charming and picturesque neighborhood in Rome. It's home to narrow cobbled streets, colorful houses, trendy cafes, and great shopping.
