Escape to Paradise: A Guide to the Most Beautiful Beach Resorts in Mallorca

Mallorca, a stunning island located off the coast of Spain in the Mediterranean Sea, is well-known for its beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters. With over 300 days of sunshine per year and an average temperature of 25°C, it is no surprise that Mallorca is a popular vacation destination for travelers around the world. Planning a trip to Mallorca can be overwhelming, with an endless number of resorts and beaches to choose from. In this guide, we will explore the most beautiful beach resorts in Mallorca, providing you with all the information you need to plan the perfect escape to paradise.

FAQs

Q: When is the best time to visit Mallorca?
A: The best time to visit Mallorca is from May to October when temperatures are warm and the weather is sunny. July and August can be crowded with tourists, so it is recommended to visit in May, June or September.

Q: How do I get to Mallorca?
A: Mallorca is accessible by air or sea. The island has an international airport, Palma de Mallorca Airport, with flights from major European cities. There are also ferries from the mainland of Spain.

Q: What is the currency in Mallorca?
A: The currency in Mallorca is the Euro.

Q: What language is spoken in Mallorca?
A: The official language of Mallorca is Catalan, however, Spanish and English are widely spoken.

Q: What are the top tourist attractions in Mallorca?
A: The top tourist attractions in Mallorca include the Cathedral of Santa Maria of Palma, Bellver Castle, Serra de Tramuntana (a mountain range), Palma Aquarium, and the Caves of Drach.

Top 10 Beautiful Beach Resorts in Mallorca

1\. Belmond La Residencia
Belmond La Residencia is a stunning luxury resort located in the charming village of Deià. This hotel is set amongst lush gardens and olive groves, with breathtaking views of the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel boasts two pools, a spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel is a stone’s throw from Cala Deià, a beautiful beach with crystal-clear waters.

2\. Park Hyatt Mallorca
Park Hyatt Mallorca is a luxurious resort located in the gorgeous valley of Canyamel. The hotel offers multiple swimming pools, a spa, and four different dining options. The resort is surrounded by beautiful countryside, with easy access to numerous hiking trails. The nearest beach, Playa de Canyamel, is just a short drive away and offers crystal-clear waters and fine sand.

3\. Jumeirah Port Soller
Jumeirah Port Soller is a glamorous hotel located in the picturesque village of Port Soller. The hotel is set amongst lush gardens with stunning views of the sea and the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel features three swimming pools, a spa, and multiple dining options. The nearest beach, Es Traves, is a short walk away and is a beautiful secluded cove.

4\. Cap Rocat
Cap Rocat is a luxury resort located in a former military fort on the southwest coast of Mallorca. The hotel is set on a cliff overlooking the sea, with easy access to multiple private beaches. The hotel offers a swimming pool, spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel is perfect for guests who are looking for exclusivity and privacy.

5\. Gran Hotel Son Net
Gran Hotel Son Net is a historic 17th-century mansion located in the beautiful village of Puigpunyent. The hotel is set amongst beautiful gardens with stunning views of the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel features a swimming pool, spa, and multiple dining options. The nearest beach, Port des Canonge, is a short drive away and is a beautiful secluded cove.

6\. Hotel Illa D’Or
Hotel Illa D’Or is a beautiful resort located in the charming town of Puerto Pollensa. The hotel overlooks the stunning Bay of Pollensa and features multiple swimming pools, a spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel is just a short walk from the beach and has easy access to numerous water sports.

7\. Pleta de Mar
Pleta de Mar is a luxurious resort located on the east coast of Mallorca in the quiet village of Canyamel. The hotel is set amongst beautiful gardens and offers stunning views of the sea. The hotel features a swimming pool, spa, and multiple dining options. The resort is just a short walk from Canyamel Beach, a beautiful cove with clear waters.

8\. Hotel Es Moli
Hotel Es Moli is a charming hotel located in the beautiful town of Deia. The hotel is set amongst beautiful gardens with stunning views of the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel features a swimming pool, spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel is just a short drive from Cala Deia, a beautiful beach with crystal-clear waters.

9\. Son Brull Hotel and Spa
Son Brull Hotel and Spa is a luxurious resort located in the picturesque town of Pollensa. The hotel is set amongst beautiful gardens with stunning views of the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel features a swimming pool, spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel is just a short drive from Cala Sant Vicenç, a beautiful beach with crystal-clear waters.

10\. Gran Meliá de Mar
Gran Meliá de Mar is a luxurious resort located in the lively town of Palma. The hotel is set on a stunning cove with clear waters and easy access to multiple beaches. The hotel features a swimming pool, spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel is just a short drive from the historic center of Palma with numerous attractions and great restaurants.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Mallorca

1\. Cathedral of Santa Maria of Palma
The Cathedral of Santa Maria of Palma, also known as La Seu, is a gothic-style cathedral located in the heart of Palma. The cathedral was built in the 14th century and boasts stunning stained glass windows and a beautiful rose window.

2\. Bellver Castle
Bellver Castle is a gothic-style castle located in Palma. The castle was built in the 14th century and is admired for its unique circular design. The castle is surrounded by a beautiful park and offers stunning views of the city and the sea.

3\. Serra de Tramuntana
Serra de Tramuntana is a mountain range located in the west of Mallorca. The range offers stunning views and numerous hiking and cycling trails. The range is also home to numerous picturesque villages.

4\. Palma Aquarium
Palma Aquarium is a popular attraction located in Palma. The aquarium boasts numerous exhibits showcasing marine life from around the world, including sharks, sea turtles, and exotic fish.

5\. Caves of Drach
The Caves of Drach are located on the east coast of Mallorca and are known for their stunning underground lakes and impressive stalactites and stalagmites.

6\. Port of Soller
The Port of Soller is a beautiful port located on the west coast of Mallorca. The port is surrounded by mountains and offers stunning views. There are numerous restaurants and cafes along the waterfront.

7\. Palma’s Old Town
Palma’s Old Town is a charming area located in the heart of Palma. The area is known for its stunning architecture, narrow streets, and great shopping.

8\. Valldemossa
Valldemossa is a picturesque village located in the Tramuntana Mountains. The village is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful gardens.

9\. Cala Deia
Cala Deia is a beautiful cove located on the west coast of Mallorca. The cove offers crystal-clear waters and stunning views of the Tramuntana Mountains.

10\. Cala Sant Vicenç
Cala Sant Vicenç is a scenic beach located in the north of Mallorca. The beach offers crystal-clear waters and easy access to numerous water sports.
