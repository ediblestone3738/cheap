# Cool Places To Go During Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year, and there's no better way to celebrate than by exploring some of the world's most enchanting destinations. Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or a tropical escape, there's a perfect spot for everyone. Here are some cool places to visit during Christmas that promise unforgettable experiences.

## 1. Lapland, Finland

Lapland, the official home of Santa Claus, is a winter wonderland that offers a truly magical Christmas experience.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa himself and explore his charming village filled with festive shops and activities.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking Aurora Borealis dance across the night sky.
- **Reindeer Sleigh Rides**: Glide through snow-covered forests on a traditional reindeer sleigh.
- **Igloo Hotels**: Stay in a glass igloo and watch the stars and Northern Lights from the comfort of your bed.

## 2. New York City, USA

New York City is a classic Christmas destination, known for its dazzling lights and festive atmosphere.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Marvel at the iconic Christmas tree and go ice skating at the famous rink.
- **Fifth Avenue**: Stroll along this famous street to see the extravagant holiday window displays.
- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Enjoy a performance by the Rockettes in this beloved holiday show.
- **Central Park**: Take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the snow-dusted park.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna transforms into a fairy tale city during the Christmas season, with its elegant architecture and festive markets.

- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the traditional markets at Rathausplatz and Schönbrunn Palace.
- **Vienna Boys' Choir**: Attend a concert featuring the world-renowned choir.
- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit this stunning palace and its Christmas village.
- **Café Culture**: Warm up with a cup of Viennese coffee and a slice of Sachertorte.

## 4. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations.

- **Illuminations**: Experience the city's spectacular light displays, especially in areas like Shibuya and Roppongi.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit European-style markets at Roppongi Hills and Yebisu Garden Place.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Enjoy festive parades and decorations at this magical theme park.
- **KFC Tradition**: Join the locals in the quirky tradition of eating KFC for Christmas dinner.

## 5. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City is a charming destination that feels like stepping into a Christmas card.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the cobblestone streets adorned with twinkling lights and decorations.
- **German Christmas Market**: Enjoy traditional treats and crafts at this festive market.
- **Tobogganing**: Slide down the famous toboggan run at Terrasse Dufferin.
- **Ice Hotel**: Stay at the Hôtel de Glace, a stunning ice and snow hotel.

## 6. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's Gothic architecture and festive markets make it a captivating Christmas destination.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market and admire the towering Christmas tree.
- **Charles Bridge**: Walk across this historic bridge and enjoy the views of the city.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore the castle grounds and its holiday decorations.
- **Traditional Cuisine**: Savor Czech holiday dishes like carp and potato salad.

## 7. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes and festive traditions.

- **Yule Lads**: Discover Iceland's mischievous Christmas trolls who bring gifts to children.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters surrounded by snow-covered lava fields.
- **Northern Lights**: Take a tour to see the mesmerizing Aurora Borealis.
- **Christmas Markets**: Browse local crafts and treats at the city's markets.

## 8. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking a warm Christmas, Sydney offers sun-soaked beaches and festive events.

- **Bondi Beach**: Celebrate Christmas with a beach barbecue and a swim in the ocean.
- **Carols in the Domain**: Attend Australia's largest Christmas concert under the stars.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Take a cruise to see the city's iconic landmarks lit up for the holidays.
- **Christmas Lights**: Explore the dazzling displays in neighborhoods like Hunter Valley Gardens.

## 9. Strasbourg, France

Strasbourg is known as the "Capital of Christmas" and offers a truly enchanting holiday experience.

- **Christkindelsmärik**: Visit one of Europe's oldest Christmas markets, dating back to 1570.
- **La Petite France**: Stroll through this picturesque district with its half-timbered houses.
- **Cathedral of Notre Dame**: Admire the stunning Gothic architecture and festive decorations.
- **Alsatian Delicacies**: Taste local specialties like spiced bread and mulled wine.

## 10. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh's historic charm and festive events make it a delightful Christmas destination.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Explore the market in East Princes Street Gardens.
- **Hogmanay**: Join the New Year's Eve celebrations with fireworks and street parties.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the castle and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Winter Wonderland**: Experience the rides and attractions at the city's festive fair.

## Conclusion

Whether you're seeking a snowy escape or a sunny retreat, these cool places to go during Christmas offer something for everyone. From the enchanting markets of Europe to the vibrant celebrations in Asia and beyond, each destination provides a unique way to celebrate the holiday season. So pack your bags, embrace the festive spirit, and create unforgettable memories in these magical locations.
