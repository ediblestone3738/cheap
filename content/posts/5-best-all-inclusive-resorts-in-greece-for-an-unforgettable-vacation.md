Greece is a beautiful and diverse country with breathtaking landscapes, inviting beaches with crystal clear waters, unique historic sites, and a welcoming culture that has charmed visitors for centuries. One of the best ways to experience Greece’s beauty is through an all-inclusive resort vacation. Imagine an all-inclusive vacation with no worries about meal reservations, transport, or entertainment options. Greece is home to some of the best all-inclusive resorts that offer all that and more. Here are the 5 Best All-Inclusive Resorts in Greece for an Unforgettable Vacation.

1\. Ikos Olivia - Halkidiki

Located in the privately-owned peninsula of Halkidiki, Ikos Olivia is a luxurious resort with 22 acres of landscaped gardens and a private beach. The resort offers a selection of rooms ranging from double rooms to two-bedroom suites, all with spacious balconies overlooking the Aegean Sea. Ikos Olivia is an all-inclusive resort that offers excellent quality food and wine from its six à la carte restaurants and three bars. The resort provides a range of activities for adults and children, including sports facilities, a spa, and a kids' club. It’s also home to the Ikos Theatre, where guests can enjoy live performances, shows, and concerts.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Ikos Olivia

1\. Explore Mount Athos
2\. Discover the Sani Wetlands
3\. Take a leisurely hike in Kassandra
4\. Visit the ancient city of Olynthus
5\. Relax on Halkidiki Beach
6\. Experience traditional Greek hospitality in nearby Skopelos
7\. Visit the Sithonia peninsula
8\. Enjoy a family-friendly visit to the Aqua Paradise Waterpark
9\. Discover the hidden gem of Afytos
10\. Take a romantic stroll at Sunset Beach

FAQs about Ikos Olivia

Q: Is Ikos Olivia suitable for families with children?
A: Yes, Ikos Olivia is a family-friendly resort that provides a kids' club with trained staff to keep children entertained.

Q: Is there any dress code at the resort's restaurants?
A: Yes, the resort expects guests to dress appropriately for dinner, smart casual attire is required, and swimwear is not allowed.

Q: Does the resort have a spa facility?
A: Yes, Ikos Olivia has a spa facility with an extensive range of treatments to revitalise and rejuvenate both mind and body.

2\. MarBella Corfu - Corfu

MarBella Corfu is an all-inclusive resort located on the eastern coast of Corfu. The resort offers 388 rooms and suites, all with great views of the Ionian Sea or the resort's gardens. The resort offers its guests a choice of accommodation ranging from garden rooms to two-bedroom family suites. There are five restaurants and three bars in the resort, and guests can indulge in a range of Greek and international cuisine. The all-inclusive package includes access to the resort's spa, sports facilities, and dance classes.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in MarBella Corfu

1\. Visit the Achilleion Palace
2\. Enjoy a serene walk in the forest of Pontikonisi
3\. Relax at the Glyfada Beach
4\. Discover the history of Corfu Old Town
5\. Enjoy the natural beauty of Sidari
6\. Visit Paleo Perithia
7\. Discover the Angelokastro Castle
8\. Explore Dassia Beach
9\. Visit the Hydropolis Water Park
10\. Enjoy a day out at the Aqualand Corfu Water Park

FAQs about MarBella Corfu

Q: Does the resort have a private beach?
A: Yes, MarBella Corfu has a private beach that is accessible through a private path from the resort.

Q: What is the dress code for dinner at the resort?
A: The dress code for dinner is smart casual, and guests are expected to dress appropriately.

Q: Does the resort provide babysitting services?
A: Yes, the resort provides babysitting services upon request, so parents can enjoy an evening out.

3\. Ikos Dassia - Corfu

Located on the island of Corfu, Ikos Dassia is an all-inclusive resort that offers guests a range of accommodation options, from rooms to suites with private pools. There are five restaurants and bars that serve a range of cuisine, including Mediterranean, Italian, and French. The resort provides a range of sports activities for adults and children, including water sports, tennis, and fitness activities. Ikos Dassia also boasts a spa facility that offers guests a range of treatments to help them unwind.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Ikos Dassia

1\. Visit the Old Fortress
2\. Swim at the Canal d’Amour
3\. Explore the Corfu Museum of Asian Art
4\. Visit the Mon Repos Palace
5\. Discover the History of Corfu Old Town (Unesco Heritage)
6\. Visit Benitses Beach
7\. Visit Achilleion Palace
8\. Explore Angelokastro
9\. Discover the ruins of the ancient city of Butrint
10\. Discover the beauty of Vidos Island

FAQs about Ikos Dassia

Q: Can guests enjoy a private pool?
A: Yes, guests can enjoy a private pool if they choose to stay in one of the resort's suites.

Q: Does the resort provide airport transfers?
A: Yes, the resort provides airport transfers upon request for guests.

Q: Is the resort family-friendly?
A: Yes, Ikos Dassia is a family-friendly resort that provides a kids' club that ensures children are entertained and supervised.

4\. Blue Palace - Crete

Located on Crete's north coast, Blue Palace is an all-inclusive resort that overlooks the stunning Mirabello Bay. The resort offers a selection of 251 rooms, suites, and villas. The resort features four restaurants and three bars that serve local and international cuisine. Blue Palace is an excellent choice for families as it provides a kids' club and activities suitable for children of all ages. The resort also provides a range of sports activities, including water sports, tennis, and a fitness centre.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Blue Palace

1\. Visit the Spinalonga Island
2\. Relax at the Elounda Beach
3\. Discover the Archaeological Museum of Sitia
4\. Visit the Lasithi Plateau
5\. Explore the ruins of Lato
6\. Discover the Folklore Museum of Agios Nikolaos
7\. Visit the Palace of Knossos (Minoan Site)
8\. Visit the Preveli Beach
9\. Discover the Municipal Art Gallery of Heraklion
10\. Visit the Samaria Gorge National Park

FAQs about Blue Palace

Q: Does the resort offer free Wi-Fi?
A: Yes, the resort provides free Wi-Fi to guests.

Q: What is the dress code for dinner at the resort?
A: The dress code for dinner at the resort is smart casual, and guests are expected to dress appropriately.

Q: Is the resort suitable for guests with disabilities?
A: Yes, the resort provides accessible rooms and facilities for guests with disabilities.

5\. Lindos Imperial - Rhodes

Located on the eastern coast of Rhodes, Lindos Imperial is a large all-inclusive resort that offers 464 modern and well-equipped rooms. The resort provides a range of activities, including a water park, entertainment options, sports facilities, and a spa. Guests can enjoy a range of delicious Greek and international cuisine at the resort's restaurants and bars. The resort is particularly suitable for families with children as it provides a kids' club and a supervised children's pool.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Lindos Imperial

1\. Discover the Old Town of Rhodes
2\. Visit the Acropolis of Lindos
3\. Relax at the Tsambika Beach
4\. Discover the Valley of the Butterflies
5\. Visit the Kallithea Thermal Springs
6\. Explore the Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes
7\. Discover the modern Art Gallery of Rhodes
8\. Visit the Filerimos Monastery
9\. Take a boat ride and visit the nearby islands
10\. Visit the Ancient Kamiros

FAQs about Lindos Imperial

Q: Does the resort offer free parking?
A: Yes, the resort provides free parking for guests.

Q: Are there any age restrictions for the water park?
A: Children under 140cm are not allowed on the slides, but there are other areas and pools for younger children.

Q: Does the resort provide shuttle services to the airport?
A: Yes, the resort provides airport shuttle services upon request for guests.

In conclusion, Greece's all-inclusive resorts are perfect for families, couples, or people looking for a relaxing and worry-free vacation. All the resorts mentioned above provide a range of activities, entertainment options, excellent cuisine, and comfortable accommodations that cater to every guest's needs. By staying at one of these resorts, you will make sure you have an unforgettable and enjoyable Greek vacation.
