Mallorca, the largest of the Balearic Islands, is a sought-after vacation destination with its gorgeous beaches, stunning landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Boasting over 200 beaches, Mallorca is a beach lover’s paradise with a variety of beach resorts to choose from. In this article, we will take a look at some of the best beach resorts in Mallorca that are perfect for your next holiday escape.

1\. Port de Pollença:
Located on the northern coast of the island, Port de Pollença is a charming resort town popular for its long sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and stunning views of the Tramuntana Mountains. Visitors can enjoy strolling around the town’s narrow streets, try some of the local cuisine or visit the stunning Formentor lighthouse.

2\. Cala Millor:
Cala Millor is situated on the eastern coast of Mallorca and is one of the island’s most popular holiday destinations. The resort town boasts a long stretch of golden sand beach, crystal clear waters, and a promenade filled with shops, bars, and restaurants. One of the main attractions here is the Caves of Drach, a spectacular underground cave network with a large underground lake.

3\. Alcudia:
Located on the northern coast of Mallorca, Alcudia is a stunning coastal town known for its long stretch of sandy beach, crystal clear waters, and tranquil atmosphere. Visitors can spend their days lounging on the beach, exploring the town’s winding streets or visiting the Roman ruins of Pollentia. The town also has a great selection of restaurants, cafes, and bars to choose from.

4\. Palma Nova:
Palma Nova is a popular resort town located on the southwestern coast of the island. It boasts of three large sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and a lively promenade. Visitors can spend their days sunbathing or taking part in water sports activities like kayaking and jet skiing. There are also plenty of restaurants, cafes and bars to choose from.

5\. Playa de Muro:
Located on the eastern coast of Mallorca, Playa de Muro is a stunning beach resort known for its pristine white sandy beach, crystal clear waters, and fantastic views of the S’Albufera Natural Park. Visitors can relax on the beach or explore the numerous shops, cafes, and bars situated along the promenade.

FAQs:
Q. When is the best time to visit Mallorca?
A. The best time to visit Mallorca is from April to October when the weather is warm and sunny.

Q. What is the currency used in Mallorca?
A. The currency used in Mallorca is the Euro.

Q. What is the official language spoken in Mallorca?
A. The official language spoken in Mallorca is Spanish, but most locals also speak English.

Q. Is it possible to rent a car in Mallorca?
A. Yes, it is possible to rent a car in Mallorca. There are many car rental companies located throughout the island.

Q. Are Mallorca’s beaches crowded during peak season?
A. Yes, Mallorca’s beaches can be crowded during peak season, especially in July and August.
