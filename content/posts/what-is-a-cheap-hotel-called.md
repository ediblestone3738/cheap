# What is a Cheap Hotel Called?

Traveling on a budget can be a challenge, especially when it comes to finding a place to stay. There are a variety of different types of cheap hotels, each with its own unique name. In this article, we'll explore the different types of cheap hotels and what they are called.

## Fleahouse

A fleahouse is a type of cheap hotel that is usually found in urban areas. These hotels are typically small and often run-down, but they are usually the cheapest option in the area. Fleahouses are usually run by a single owner and may not have many amenities.

## Fleatrap

A fleatrap is another type of cheap hotel, but it is typically found in rural areas. These hotels are usually old and run-down, but they are usually the cheapest option in the area. Fleatraps are usually run by a single owner and may not have many amenities.

## Flop Joint

A flop joint is a type of cheap hotel that is usually found in urban areas. These hotels are typically small and often run-down, but they are usually the cheapest option in the area. Flop joints are usually run by a single owner and may not have many amenities.

## Run-Down Boarding House

A run-down boarding house is a type of cheap hotel that is usually found in urban areas. These hotels are typically small and often run-down, but they are usually the cheapest option in the area. Run-down boarding houses are usually run by a single owner and may not have many amenities.

## Run-Down Hotel

A run-down hotel is a type of cheap hotel that is usually found in urban areas. These hotels are typically small and often run-down, but they are usually the cheapest option in the area. Run-down hotels are usually run by a single owner and may not have many amenities.

## Conclusion

When traveling on a budget, it can be difficult to find a place to stay. There are a variety of different types of cheap hotels, each with its own unique name. Fleahouses, fleatraps, flop joints, run-down boarding houses, and run-down hotels are all types of cheap hotels that can be found in urban areas. No matter which type of cheap hotel you choose, make sure to research the hotel before booking to ensure that it meets your needs.
