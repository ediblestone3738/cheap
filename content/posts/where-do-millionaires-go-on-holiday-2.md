# Where Do Millionaires Go on Holiday?

Are you looking for the perfect holiday destination for the wealthy? Whether you’re a millionaire or just looking to experience a luxurious holiday, there are plenty of options out there. From the Palm in Dubai to Reethi Rah in the Maldives, there are plenty of places for millionaires to go on holiday. ## The Palm, Dubai

The Palm in Dubai is one of the most popular holiday destinations for millionaires. This man-made island is a luxurious paradise, with some of the most exclusive resorts in the world. From the Atlantis, The Palm to the One&Only The Palm, there’s something for everyone. The Palm is also home to some of the best restaurants, bars, and nightlife in the city.

## Reethi Rah, Maldives

The Maldives is a paradise for millionaires, and Reethi Rah is one of the most luxurious resorts in the country. Located on a private island, the resort offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean. With its private villas, world-class restaurants, and luxurious spa, Reethi Rah is the perfect place for a luxurious holiday.

## Gorilla’s Nest, Rwanda

For a unique holiday experience, millionaires can head to Gorilla’s Nest in Rwanda. This luxurious resort is located in the heart of the Virunga Mountains, and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The resort is home to a variety of activities, including gorilla trekking, bird watching, and mountain biking.

## One&Only Portonovi, Montenegro

The newest addition to the list of millionaire holiday destinations is One&Only Portonovi in Montenegro. This luxurious resort is located on the Bay of Kotor, and offers stunning views of the Adriatic Sea. The resort features a variety of activities, from sailing and kayaking to spa treatments and yoga classes.

Millionaires have plenty of options when it comes to holiday destinations. From the Palm in Dubai to Gorilla’s Nest in Rwanda, there are plenty of luxurious places to explore. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an adventure in the wilderness, there’s something for everyone.
