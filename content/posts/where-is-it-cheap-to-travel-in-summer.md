# Where is it Cheap to Travel in Summer?

Summer is a great time to get away and explore the world. But with so many places to choose from, it can be hard to know where to go and how to find the best deals. To help you out, Hopper recently analyzed the cheapest domestic destinations based on airfare and hotel rates.

## Orlando is the Cheapest Domestic Destination

Coming in at the top of the list is Orlando, Florida. With average airfare prices around $150 and average nightly hotel rates under $100, Orlando is the perfect destination for budget-minded travelers. The city is full of attractions, from the iconic Disney World to Universal Studios and SeaWorld. There are also plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy, like kayaking, fishing, and hiking.

## Ft. Myers and Tampa are Also Affordable

If you’re looking for a beach vacation, Ft. Myers and Tampa are great options. Both cities offer average airfare prices around $200 and average nightly hotel rates under $120. In Ft. Myers, you can explore the white sand beaches, take a boat tour of the mangroves, or check out the city’s art galleries and museums. In Tampa, you can visit the Tampa Bay History Center, take a tour of the Busch Gardens theme park, or explore the city’s vibrant nightlife.

## Houston and Dallas are Affordable Too

Houston and Dallas are two other cities that make the top 10 list of cheapest domestic destinations. Average airfare prices are around $250 and average nightly hotel rates are under $115. In Houston, you can visit the Space Center Houston, take a tour of the Houston Zoo, or explore the city’s vibrant art scene. In Dallas, you can visit the Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden, take a tour of the Dallas Museum of Art, or explore the city’s vibrant nightlife.

## Conclusion

Summer is a great time to get away and explore the world. With Hopper’s analysis of the cheapest domestic destinations, you can find the perfect destination for your budget. Whether you’re looking for a beach vacation, a city escape, or a theme park adventure, there are plenty of affordable options to choose from. So pack your bags and get ready to explore!
