From Beaches to Bungalows: Greece's Top All-Inclusive Resorts

Greece is known for its ancient history, picturesque coastline, and delicious cuisine. From Athens to the Greek islands, the country has become a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. One of the best ways to enjoy Greece's beauty is by staying at a top all-inclusive resort. These resorts offer everything you need for a comfortable and luxurious vacation, from breathtaking views to endless buffets. Here are Greece's top all-inclusive resorts, perfect for anyone from beach goers to bungalow lovers.

1\. Club Med Gregolimano
Club Med Gregolimano is located on the east coast of Greece, facing the Aegean Sea. It is a resort for those who want to experience the ancient history of Greece while enjoying the modern amenities of a luxurious resort. The 374 rooms are surrounded by olive trees, and the waterfront views are breathtaking. Activities at Club Med Gregolimano include sailing, water skiing, and paddleboarding. The resort offers three pools, three restaurants, and three bars. The resort also offers excursions to nearby archaeological sites.

2\. Ikos Olivia
Ikos Olivia is located on the Halkidiki peninsula and offers stunning views of the Aegean Sea. The resort is divided into two parts: the main building and bungalow-style rooms. The resort has four restaurants and four bars, and all meals and drinks are included in the price. The resort offers a kids club, water park, and sports facilities.

3\. Grecotel Olympia Riviera Resort
The Grecotel Olympia Riviera Resort is located in ancient Olympia and offers views of the Ionian Sea. The resort is spread across 500 acres and offers luxurious rooms and bungalows. The resort has five restaurants and five bars, and all meals and drinks are included in the price. Activities at the resort include tennis, beach volleyball, and water sports. The resort also offers a kids club and a spa.

4\. Porto Carras Meliton
The Porto Carras Meliton resort is located on the Halkidiki peninsula and offers stunning views of the Aegean Sea. The resort is spread across 1,700 acres and offers luxurious rooms and bungalows. The resort has five restaurants and seven bars, and all meals and drinks are included in the price. Activities at the resort include golf, tennis, and horse riding. The resort also offers a private marina and a spa.

5\. Daios Cove Luxury Resort and Villas
The Daios Cove Luxury Resort and Villas is located on the island of Crete and offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort offers luxurious rooms and villas, all with private pools or balconies. The resort has six restaurants and three bars, and all meals and drinks are included in the price. The resort offers a private beach, water sports, and a kids club. The resort also has a spa and a fitness center.

FAQs

Q: What is an all-inclusive resort?
A: An all-inclusive resort is a property that includes all meals, drinks, and activities in the room rate. This means that you do not have to worry about additional charges for food, drink or entertainment.

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts expensive?
A: All-inclusive resorts can be more expensive initially, but they often represent good value for money because you do not have to worry about additional charges for food, drink, or entertainment.

Q: Where can I find all-inclusive resorts in Greece?
A: All-inclusive resorts can be found all over Greece, but some of the most popular locations are the Halkidiki peninsula, the island of Crete, and the east coast.

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts suitable for families?
A: Yes, all-inclusive resorts are often ideal for families because they offer a range of activities and amenities for adults and children alike.

Q: Do I need to tip at all-inclusive resorts?
A: Tipping is not necessary at all-inclusive resorts, but it may be appreciated for exceptional service.
