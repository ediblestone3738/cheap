What is the Cheapest European City Break?

Are you looking for a cheap and exciting city break in Europe? Look no further than Athens, Greece! This year’s Post Office City Costs Barometer ranked Greece’s historical capital as the cheapest location for an autumn city break in Europe, and noted that prices have dropped by 15 per cent since 2021.

Athens is the perfect destination for a budget-friendly city break. With its iconic ancient ruins, bustling nightlife, and stunning Mediterranean beaches, there’s something for everyone in this vibrant city.

Let’s take a look at some of the top attractions and activities that you can enjoy in Athens on a budget.

1\. Visit the Acropolis

The Acropolis is the most iconic landmark in Athens and a must-see for any visitor. The ancient citadel is home to some of the most impressive ruins in the world, including the Parthenon, the Propylaea, and the Erechtheion. The best part is that admission to the Acropolis is free!

2\. Explore the Ancient Agora

The Ancient Agora is another important archaeological site in Athens. This was the political, commercial, and social center of the city in ancient times. Today, visitors can explore the ruins of the Temple of Hephaestus, the Stoa of Attalos, and the Temple of Apollo Patroos. Admission to the Ancient Agora is free.

3\. Visit the National Archaeological Museum

The National Archaeological Museum is one of the best museums in Athens. It houses an impressive collection of artifacts from ancient Greece, including sculptures, pottery, jewelry, and coins. Admission to the museum is free.

4\. Take a Stroll Through the Plaka

The Plaka is the oldest neighborhood in Athens and a great place to explore on foot. Here, you’ll find narrow cobblestone streets lined with shops, cafes, and restaurants. It’s the perfect place to pick up souvenirs or enjoy a meal on a budget.

5\. Enjoy the Nightlife

Athens is known for its vibrant nightlife. There are plenty of bars, clubs, and live music venues to explore. Many of them offer happy hour specials and discounts on drinks.

6\. Relax on the Beach

Athens is located on the Mediterranean Sea, so there are plenty of beaches to explore. The most popular beach is Vouliagmeni, which is known for its crystal clear waters and stunning views.

7\. Explore the Markets

Athens is home to several bustling markets, including the Central Market, the Monastiraki Flea Market, and the Athens Farmer’s Market. Here, you can find fresh produce, handmade crafts, and souvenirs at great prices.

As you can see, Athens is the perfect destination for a budget-friendly city break. With its iconic ancient ruins, bustling nightlife, and stunning Mediterranean beaches, there’s something for everyone in this vibrant city. So, why not plan your next city break in Athens? You won’t regret it!
