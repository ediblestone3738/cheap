Top 10 Tourist Attractions located in Unwind and Unplug: The Ultimate Kefalonia Retreat

1\. Myrtos Beach

Myrtos Beach is one of the most picturesque beaches in Kefalonia, with its beautiful white sand and crystal-clear waters, this beach is perfect for relaxation and swimming.

2\. The Castle of St. George

The Castle of St. George is an excellent piece of Kefalonian history, offering breath-taking views of the surrounding countryside.

3\. Melissani Cave

Melissani Cave is an underground lake with crystal clear blue-green water, in a beautiful underground cavern.

4\. Fiskardo Village

Fiskardo Village is a small harbor town, featuring picturesque buildings and traditional restaurants, perfect for a relaxed walk and a taste of the culture.

5\. Mount Ainos

Mount Ainos is the highest peak of Kefalonia, offering a picturesque view of the surrounding countryside, waterways, and picturesque forests.

6\. Assos Castle

Assos Castle is a medieval fortification, housing a small village of the same name with friendly local restaurants and shops.

7\. Drogarati Cave

Located in the area of Haliotata, Drogarati Cave is a breathtaking geological formation worth visiting for its unique formation.

8\. Lepeda Beach

Lepeda Beach offers stunning sunsets and views of the Ionian Sea.

9\. Gradakia Beach

A small and unassuming beach with crystal-clear waters offers perfect snorkeling and exploring opportunities.

10\. Skala Beach

Skala Beach is a lively, sandy beach fringed by restaurants, where tourists can take part in various water activities such as boat rentals, parasailing and jet ski rides.
