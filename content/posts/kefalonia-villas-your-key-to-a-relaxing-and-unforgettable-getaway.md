Kefalonia Villas: Your Key to a Relaxing and Unforgettable Getaway

Greece is an enticing travel destination. Its crystal-clear waters, delicious cuisine, and warm culture all capture our hearts. Nestled in the heart of the Ionian Sea, Kefalonia is a hidden gem with copious natural beauty, stunning beaches, and warm people. To experience Kefalonia’s unparalleled splendor and hospitality, book your stay at Kefalonia Villas.

Located on the western side of Kefalonia, these villas offer you the perfect gateway to a memorable vacation. Situated among the stunning scenery of olive groves, vineyards and crystal-clear blue sea, Kefalonia Villas offers a peaceful and relaxed setting for a perfect summer getaway.

Accommodation

Kefalonia Villas boasts ten fully equipped luxurious villas with state-of-the-art amenities. Each villa is uniquely designed and offers maximum privacy, ensuring that your stay is unforgettable. With villas to suit small families or large groups, you can choose between the one-bedroom, two-bedroom, three-bedroom and four-bedroom villas, all of which blend modern style with traditional Greek architecture, providing all the comforts of home for the ultimate cozy stay.

Facilities/Services

Kefalonia Villas offer impeccable facilities, making your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. Our staff is at your service 24/7, providing housekeeping and laundry services, airport transfers, and car rental service. Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a fully equipped kitchen are among some of the state-of-the-art amenities you will enjoy during your stay. You can also enjoy the villas’ swimming pool, with a panoramic sea view and ample sun loungers.

Things to do

The villas’ location provides access to many of Kefalonia’s prized attractions. The island's clear blue waters offer visitors a host of water-based activities, from water sports to sailing, boat trips, and scuba diving. One of Kefalonia's must-sees is Myrtos Beach, a crescent-shaped beach with crystal-clear waters and white sand. Another iconic destination is the Melissani Cave, once home to the god Pan, this open-air cave contains a clear-water lake surrounded by lush greenery, creating a most tranquil and picturesque scene. For panoramic views, make a visit to Mount Ainos, standing tall at 1,628 meters, for an amazing bird’s eye view of the island.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Kefalonia

1\. Myrtos Beach: It’s one of the best beaches in Greece, characterized by its rugged, cliff mountains, and pebble white sand shoreline.

2\. Melissani Cave: This iconic destination embodies everything picturesque about Kefalonia. The open-air cave on the island's east coast has a transparent blue-green lake at its center.

3\. Mount Ainos: The highest peak of the Ionian island provides a breathtaking view of the island from the bird's eye perspective.

4\. Fiskardo: Charmingly traditional northwest harbortown famous for its lively ambiance, stunning mansions, and romantic alleyway.

5\. Assos Village: Assos is a traditional and peaceful village located in the northern part of Kefalonia, where its Venetian fort adds an alluring charm.

6\. Argostoli: The hustle and bustle capital of Kefalonia, home to the island's renowned restaurants, bars, and shops.

7\. Skala Beach: The island’s picturesque south coast features this massively long stretch of beach, perfect for a refreshing swim or sunbathing.

8\. Koutavos Lagoon: The Koutavos Lagoon is a wetland near Argostoli and offers a peaceful paradise for nature lovers.

9\. Saint Gerassimos Monastery: A 16th-century monastery named after the patron saint of Kefalonia, housing his remains in a silver sarcophagus

10\. Antisamos Beach: Suitable for families and couples, a perfect blend of crystal-clear turquoise water, white pebbles, and green nature scene.

In conclusion, Kefalonia Villas offer the perfect location for travelers looking for a haven of relaxation, privacy, and natural beauty. The combination of luxurious villas, stunning location, and impeccable services and facilities provides everything you need for an unforgettable and relaxing holiday experience.
