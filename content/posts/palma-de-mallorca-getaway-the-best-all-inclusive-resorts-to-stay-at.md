Palma de Mallorca Getaway: The Best All-Inclusive Resorts to Stay At

Palma de Mallorca, the capital of the Spanish island of Mallorca, is one of the most beautiful and popular holiday destinations in the Mediterranean. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or an adventurous break, Palma de Mallorca has something to offer you. The city is renowned for its pristine beaches, stunning architectural landmarks, ancient castles, and crystal clear waters. With a plethora of all-inclusive resorts to choose from, this article highlights the best all-inclusive resorts in Palma de Mallorca that offer a luxurious and comfortable stay while enjoying the stunning surroundings.

1\. Zafiro Palace Palmanova
Located in the Palmanova area along the Cala Blanca Beach, the Zafiro Palace Palmanova is a luxurious five-star resort with excellent amenities, elegant rooms, and fantastic views of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort features an all-inclusive package that offers its guests unlimited access to its onsite restaurants serving a variety of cuisines, bars, wellness centre, and swimming pools. The Zafiro Palace is ideally situated for tourists looking to explore the sights and attractions of Palma de Mallorca.

2\. IBEROSTAR Selection Playa de Palma
This elegantly designed five-star resort is located on the Palmira Beach and offers its guests top-notch amenities that will satisfy their holiday cravings. The hotel offers all-inclusive deals, including access to its award-winning restaurants, poolside bars, and entertainment. IBEROSTAR Selection Playa de Palma provides a fantastic location for tourists wanting to experience the culture, shopping, and entertainment of Palma de Mallorca.

3\. Meliá Palma Marina
The upscale Meliá Palma Marina is situated in the heart of Palma de Mallorca's marina, giving its guests stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea, and provides easy access to the island's beaches and landmarks. The resort features a variety of all-inclusive packages, including access to its onsite bars, restaurants, spa, fitness centre, and swimming pools. The Meliá Palma Marina is ideal for couples, business travellers, and families looking to relax and unwind while enjoying the beautiful scenery of the island.

4\. HM Gran Fiesta
Located in the heart of Playa de Palma, HM Gran Fiesta is an excellent option for travellers seeking a comfortable, well-appointed stay. The hotel features a range of all-inclusive packages, including access to its restaurants, bars, swimming pools, and spa. With easy access to the beach, sports, and entertainment activities, this resort is perfect for families and couples looking for a fun-filled and memorable holiday.

5\. Be Live Collection
Be Live Collection is located right on the beautiful Cala Mayor Beach in Palma de Mallorca. The resort offers an all-inclusive package that includes unlimited access to its bars, restaurants, spa, and nightly entertainment. Be Live Collection caters to families with children, and couples looking for a romantic getaway, and provides a fantastic location for guests who want to explore the city's tourist attractions.

6\. Iberostar Cristina
The attractive Iberostar Cristina Resort is located near the Palma de Mallorca airport and features fantastic views of the Mediterranean Sea. The hotel caters to both couples and families and offers a range of all-inclusive packages that include unlimited access to its onsite restaurants, bars, swimming pools, and entertainment. The Iberostar Cristina has a prime location that makes it easy for guests to visit the island's top tourist attractions.

7\. Playa Golf Hotel
Located just a few steps away from the beach, Playa Golf Hotel offers guests spectacular views of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort is an all-inclusive hotel with excellent amenities that include access to its restaurants, bars, spa, and swimming pools. Playa Golf Hotel provides a convenient location for tourists who want to enjoy beach activities, cultural attractions, and shopping.

8\. TRYP Palma Bellver Hotel
The TRYP Palma Bellver Hotel is situated in the heart of Palma de Mallorca, overlooking the stunning Bellver Castle. The hotel offers a range of all-inclusive packages that include access to its bars, restaurants, gym, and swimming pools. This resort is perfect for business and leisure travellers who want to explore the city's landmarks, restaurants, and shopping centres.

9\. Be Smart Alea
Located in the charming town of Can Pastilla, Be Smart Alea is an all-inclusive resort ideal for a relaxed and comfortable stay. The hotel features a variety of all-inclusive packages that include access to its bars, restaurants, swimming pool, and beach club. Be Smart Alea is ideal for couples and families looking for a peaceful stay while enjoying the scenic surroundings.

10\. Gran Meliá Victoria
Gran Meliá Victoria offers guests a luxurious experience with all-inclusive packages that include round-the-clock access to its restaurants, bars, spa, and swimming pools. The hotel features elegant rooms with breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea and overlooks the stunning Palma Bay. Gran Meliá Victoria is ideal for couples and business travellers who want to relax, unwind, and explore the city's cultural attractions.
