# Best Places To Go To During Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year, and there's no better way to celebrate than by exploring some of the world's most enchanting destinations. Whether you're dreaming of a white Christmas or longing for a tropical escape, there's a perfect place for everyone. In this article, we'll explore some of the best places to visit during Christmas, each offering its own unique charm and festive spirit.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas magic. The city transforms into a winter wonderland, offering a plethora of activities and sights that capture the holiday spirit.

- **Rockefeller Center**: No Christmas in New York is complete without a visit to the iconic Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree. The massive tree, adorned with thousands of lights, is a sight to behold. Don't forget to lace up your skates and glide across the famous ice rink.

- **Fifth Avenue**: Stroll down Fifth Avenue to admire the elaborate holiday window displays at department stores like Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue. The creativity and artistry of these displays are sure to amaze.

- **Radio City Christmas Spectacular**: Experience the magic of the Radio City Christmas Spectacular, featuring the world-famous Rockettes. This dazzling show is a holiday tradition that delights audiences of all ages.

- **Central Park**: Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride through Central Park, where you can admire the snow-covered landscapes and twinkling lights.

## 2. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a city that truly comes alive during the Christmas season. With its rich history, stunning architecture, and festive markets, it's a must-visit destination for holiday travelers.

- **Christmas Markets**: Vienna is home to some of the most beautiful Christmas markets in Europe. The Rathausplatz market, set against the backdrop of the stunning City Hall, is a favorite. Enjoy mulled wine, roasted chestnuts, and handmade crafts.

- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit the Belvedere Palace for a Christmas market experience in a baroque setting. The market offers a variety of traditional Austrian foods and unique gifts.

- **Concerts and Performances**: Vienna is renowned for its classical music, and during Christmas, the city hosts numerous concerts and performances. Attend a concert at the Vienna State Opera or the Musikverein for an unforgettable experience.

- **St. Stephen's Cathedral**: Attend a Christmas mass at St. Stephen's Cathedral, a stunning Gothic masterpiece in the heart of the city.

## 3. Lapland, Finland

For a truly magical Christmas experience, head to Lapland, Finland, the official home of Santa Claus. This winter wonderland offers a unique blend of adventure and holiday spirit.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Visit Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi, where you can meet Santa himself, send postcards from Santa's official post office, and cross the Arctic Circle.

- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking Northern Lights, a natural phenomenon that illuminates the night sky with vibrant colors. Lapland offers some of the best opportunities to see this spectacular display.

- **Reindeer and Husky Safaris**: Embark on a reindeer or husky safari through the snow-covered landscapes. These guided tours offer a unique way to explore the Arctic wilderness.

- **Snow Hotels**: Spend a night in a snow hotel, where you can sleep in a room made entirely of ice and snow. It's a once-in-a-lifetime experience that you won't forget.

## 4. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo may not be the first place that comes to mind for a Christmas getaway, but the city offers a unique and vibrant holiday experience.

- **Illuminations**: Tokyo is famous for its dazzling Christmas illuminations. Visit areas like Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Roppongi to see the city aglow with millions of lights.

- **Christmas Markets**: Explore Tokyo's European-style Christmas markets, such as the Tokyo Christmas Market in Hibiya Park. Enjoy festive foods, warm drinks, and holiday shopping.

- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Experience the magic of Christmas at Tokyo Disneyland, where the park is transformed with holiday decorations, parades, and special shows.

- **Seasonal Cuisine**: Indulge in seasonal Japanese treats, such as Christmas cakes and fried chicken, which are popular holiday foods in Japan.

## 5. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City offers a charming and picturesque setting for a traditional Christmas celebration. With its cobblestone streets and historic architecture, the city exudes old-world charm.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through Old Quebec, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and admire the festive decorations and twinkling lights. The European-style architecture adds to the city's enchanting atmosphere.

- **German Christmas Market**: Visit the German Christmas Market in the heart of Old Quebec, where you can enjoy traditional German foods, crafts, and entertainment.

- **Château Frontenac**: Stay at the iconic Château Frontenac, a grand hotel that overlooks the St. Lawrence River. The hotel is beautifully decorated for the holidays and offers a cozy retreat.

- **Winter Activities**: Embrace the winter season with activities like ice skating, tobogganing, and snowshoeing. The Plains of Abraham and Montmorency Falls are popular spots for outdoor fun.

## 6. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a fairy-tale city that becomes even more magical during the Christmas season. With its stunning architecture and festive markets, it's a perfect destination for holiday travelers.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market in Old Town Square, where you can enjoy traditional Czech foods, mulled wine, and handmade crafts. The market is set against the backdrop of the beautiful Church of Our Lady before Týn.

- **Charles Bridge**: Take a stroll across the Charles Bridge, which is beautifully illuminated during the holiday season. The views of the city and the Vltava River are breathtaking.

- **Prague Castle**: Explore Prague Castle, a historic complex that offers stunning views of the city. During Christmas, the castle hosts a festive market with local foods and crafts.

- **Concerts and Performances**: Attend a Christmas concert or performance at one of Prague's historic venues, such as the Rudolfinum or the Municipal House.

## 7. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking a warm and sunny Christmas, Sydney offers a unique holiday experience with its beautiful beaches and vibrant city life.

- **Bondi Beach**: Spend Christmas Day at Bondi Beach, where you can enjoy the sun, sand, and surf. The beach is a popular spot for both locals and tourists looking to celebrate the holiday in a relaxed setting.

- **Christmas Lights**: Explore the city's Christmas light displays, including the famous Sydney Harbour Bridge and the Sydney Opera House, which are illuminated with festive colors.

- **Carols by Candlelight**: Attend a Carols by Candlelight event, a beloved Australian tradition where people gather to sing Christmas carols by candlelight. The event is held at various locations throughout the city.

- **Sydney Fish Market**: Visit the Sydney Fish Market for a unique holiday feast. The market offers a wide variety of fresh seafood, perfect for a Christmas barbecue.

## 8. Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg is home to one of the oldest and most famous Christmas markets in the world. The city's rich history and festive atmosphere make it a must-visit destination during the holiday season.

- **Christkindlesmarkt**: Explore the Christkindlesmarkt, a traditional Christmas market held in the city's main square. The market features over 180 stalls selling handmade gifts, ornaments, and delicious treats like gingerbread and mulled wine.

- **Nuremberg Castle**: Visit Nuremberg Castle, a historic landmark that offers panoramic views of the city. The castle is beautifully decorated for the holidays and hosts special events during the Christmas season.

- **Toy Museum**: Discover the Toy Museum, which showcases a vast collection of toys from different eras. The museum is a delightful place to visit, especially during the holiday season.

- **St. Sebaldus Church**: Attend a Christmas service at St. Sebaldus Church, a stunning Gothic church that dates back to the 13th century.

## 9. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique and enchanting Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes and vibrant holiday celebrations.

- **Yule Town Christmas Market**: Visit the Yule Town Christmas Market in Ingólfstorg Square, where you can enjoy Icelandic foods, crafts, and live entertainment.

- **Northern Lights**: Experience the magic of the Northern Lights, which can often be seen in Reykjavik during the winter months. The city's dark skies provide an excellent backdrop for this natural wonder.

- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the Blue Lagoon, a geothermal spa located just outside the city. The warm, mineral-rich waters are the perfect way to unwind during the holiday season.

- **Christmas Traditions**: Learn about Iceland's unique Christmas traditions, such as the 13 Yule Lads, mischievous characters who visit children in the days leading up to Christmas.

## 10. London, England

London is a city that knows how to celebrate Christmas in style. With its festive lights, holiday markets, and world-class attractions, it's a perfect destination for a holiday getaway.

- **Hyde Park Winter Wonderland**: Visit Hyde Park Winter Wonderland, a festive extravaganza featuring ice skating, rides, games, and a Christmas market. It's a must-visit for families and holiday enthusiasts.

- **Oxford Street**: Stroll down Oxford Street to see the dazzling Christmas lights and window displays. The street is a shopper's paradise, offering a wide range of stores and boutiques.

- **Covent Garden**: Explore Covent Garden, where you can enjoy street performances, holiday decorations, and a Christmas market. The area is known for its lively atmosphere and unique shops.

- **West End Shows**: Attend a West End show, where you can enjoy holiday-themed performances and classic productions. The theaters are beautifully decorated for the season, adding to the festive experience.

## Conclusion

Whether you're seeking a snowy escape or a sunny retreat, these destinations offer something for everyone during the Christmas season. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of Lapland, each place has its own unique charm and festive spirit. So pack your bags, embrace the holiday cheer, and create unforgettable memories in one of these magical destinations.
