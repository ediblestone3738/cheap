# Where is the Cheapest Place to Have a Holiday?

Are you looking for a great holiday destination without breaking the bank? Then you’ve come to the right place! We’ve compiled a list of the 13 cheapest places to go in 2022, so you can start planning your dream vacation without worrying about your budget.

## Paphos, Cyprus

Paphos, Cyprus is a great destination for a budget-friendly holiday. With its stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and Mediterranean climate, it’s no wonder why this city is a popular tourist destination. The cost of living in Paphos is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Tombs of the Kings, the Paphos Archaeological Park, and the Paphos Zoo.

## Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town, South Africa is another great destination for a cheap holiday. This vibrant city is known for its stunning beaches, iconic Table Mountain, and vibrant culture. The cost of living in Cape Town is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, the Cape of Good Hope, and the Robben Island Museum.

## Algarve, Portugal

Algarve, Portugal is a great destination for a budget-friendly holiday. With its stunning beaches, lush green hills, and Mediterranean climate, it’s no wonder why this city is a popular tourist destination. The cost of living in Algarve is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Algarve Aquarium, the Zoomarine Algarve, and the Lagos Zoo.

## Sunny Beach, Bulgaria

Sunny Beach, Bulgaria is another great destination for a cheap holiday. This vibrant city is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and great shopping. The cost of living in Sunny Beach is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Sunny Beach Amusement Park, the Action Aquapark, and the Nessebar Old Town.

## Marmaris, Turkey

Marmaris, Turkey is a great destination for a budget-friendly holiday. With its stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and Mediterranean climate, it’s no wonder why this city is a popular tourist destination. The cost of living in Marmaris is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Marmaris Castle, the Marmaris Grand Bazaar, and the Marmaris Dolphin Park.

## Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of great destinations for a budget-friendly holiday in 2022. Whether you’re looking for a beach holiday, a city break, or a cultural experience, there’s something for everyone. So, start planning your dream vacation today and take advantage of the great deals available in these 13 cheapest places to go in 2022!
