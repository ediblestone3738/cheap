What Are Short Haul Destinations?

Are you looking for a quick getaway without having to travel too far? Short haul destinations are the perfect option for you! Short haul destinations are typically located within a few hours’ flight from your home, allowing you to enjoy a holiday without having to spend too much time in transit.

If you’re looking for a short haul destination, there are plenty of options to choose from. From the sunny beaches of the Canary Islands to the ancient ruins of Egypt, there’s something for everyone. In this article, we’ll explore some of the most popular short haul destinations, so you can find the perfect spot for your next holiday.

Canary Islands

The Canary Islands are a popular short haul destination for those looking for a beach holiday. Located off the coast of Morocco, the Canary Islands are a Spanish archipelago consisting of seven main islands. With its year-round sunshine and stunning beaches, the Canary Islands are the perfect place to relax and soak up some rays. Whether you’re looking for a quiet beach escape or an action-packed adventure, the Canary Islands have something for everyone.
