Spain is a dream destination for travelers the world over, with its rich history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant culture. But if you're looking to experience the best of Spain, why not do it in style? With a range of luxury resorts scattered throughout the country, Spain offers the ultimate in relaxation and indulgence. From the sun-soaked coastlines to the charming countryside, here are the luxury resorts you must visit in Spain to unwind in style.

1\. Marbella Club, Andalusia

Located right on the beach of the Costa del Sol, this elegant resort was originally the private residence of Prince Alfonso von Hohenlohe. Today, the Marbella Club accommodates discerning guests who want to experience the glamour of the Mediterranean coast. Lush gardens, high-end restaurants, and an array of leisure activities are just some of the features that make this resort so special.

2\. Hotel Arts, Barcelona

As one of the most iconic hotels in Barcelona, the Hotel Arts boasts breathtaking views of the city's famous coastline. With its modern design and luxurious amenities, guests can experience the ultimate in comfort and sophistication. Located near some of the city's top attractions, the Hotel Arts is the perfect launchpad for exploring Barcelona.

3\. La Manga Club, Murcia

Set amid 1,400 acres of stunning countryside, this award-winning resort features luxurious villas, a world-class golf course, and a spa that offers a range of rejuvenating treatments. The La Manga Club resort is perfect for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and relax in the peace and quiet of the countryside.

4\. Finca Cortesin, Andalusia

Overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, this five-star resort combines classic Andalusian architecture with contemporary design. Nestled in the heart of the Costa del Sol, the Finca Cortesin offers luxurious amenities and personalized service to ensure guests have an unforgettable stay.

5\. Abama Golf & Spa, Tenerife

Located on the warm shores of Tenerife in the Canary Islands, the Abama Golf & Spa is a paradise for golf enthusiasts. With its 18-hole golf course designed by former Ryder Cup captain Dave Thomas and a range of other leisure activities, guests can unwind and indulge in total luxury.

6\. The Ritz-Carlton, Abama, Tenerife

The Ritz-Carlton, Abama is a stunning resort located on the island of Tenerife. Spread over 160 hectares of subtropical gardens, with gorgeous views of the Atlantic Ocean, the resort is the perfect blend of luxury and natural beauty. With a Michelin-starred restaurant, a sprawling spa, and a range of activities for all ages, this resort offers something for everyone.

7\. Kempinski Hotel Bahia, Marbella

Located in the heart of the Costa del Sol, this stunning luxury resort offers guests breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea. With a range of stylish accommodations and exceptional amenities, the Kempinski Hotel Bahia is perfect for those looking to relax in luxurious surroundings.

8\. Puente Romano Beach Resort, Marbella

Set on Marbella's exclusive Golden Mile, the Puente Romano Beach Resort offers a luxurious escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. With its beautiful gardens, gourmet restaurants, and unparalleled service, the resort offers a unique experience that will leave guests feeling completely refreshed.

9\. Barceló Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa, Alicante

Designed to offer guests a taste of southeast Asia, this luxury resort is located on the eastern coast of Spain in the beautiful city of Alicante. With its tropical gardens, stunning views, and exceptional spa facilities, the Barceló Asia Gardens Hotel is the perfect destination for those looking to unwind in style.

10\. Hotel Miró, Bilbao

Located in the heart of the city, the Hotel Miró is a luxurious and contemporary hotel that combines comfort with style. With its chic decor, gourmet restaurant, and panoramic views of the city, the Hotel Miró is a must-visit destination for those looking to experience the best of Bilbao.

FAQs

Q: What time of year is the best to visit Spain's luxury resorts?
A: The best time to visit Spain's luxury resorts is during the spring and summer months. That way you can enjoy the warm weather and sunshine.

Q: What kind of amenities can I expect from luxury resorts in Spain?
A: Luxury resorts in Spain often offer a range of amenities, including gourmet restaurants, spas, golf courses, swimming pools, and other leisure activities.

Q: Are there any family-friendly luxury resorts in Spain?
A: Many luxury resorts in Spain offer a range of activities for families, including kids' clubs, tennis courts, and swimming pools.

Q: Are luxury resorts in Spain expensive?
A: Luxury resorts in Spain can be expensive, but there are often deals and promotions available. It's always best to do your research and compare prices before booking.

Q: How do I get to Spain's luxury resorts?
A: Luxury resorts in Spain can be accessed by car, train, or plane, depending on their location. Many resorts offer transportation services to and from the airport or train station.
