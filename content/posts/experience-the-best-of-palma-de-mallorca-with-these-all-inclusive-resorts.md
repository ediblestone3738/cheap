Palma de Mallorca is a beautiful Spanish island that attracts millions of tourists all year round. The island, which is located in the Balearic Islands in the Mediterranean, boasts of a beautiful coast with sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and amazing landscapes. There are also historical and cultural landmarks, great cuisine, and an abundance of shops and nightlife entertainment. It's no wonder many travelers are interested in experiencing the best Palma de Mallorca has to offer. All-inclusive resorts provide a unique experience for tourists, making it easy for them to relax and enjoy everything the island has to offer. In this article, we will explore the top all-inclusive resorts in Palma de Mallorca, and what they have to offer.

Melia Palma Bay

If you're looking for a luxurious, all-inclusive hotel with Swiming pool and a Direct access to the Paseo Maritimo, the Melia Palma Bay should be on your list. This stylish 4-star hotel features breathtaking views of the Bay of Palma and is located just a short walk away from the historical old town of Palma. The hotel has 268 modern rooms, a rooftop terrace with an infinity pool, and a solarium. There are also plenty of dining options, including the trendy Palmitan Restaurant, which specializes in Mediterranean cuisine.

Iberostar Selection Playa de Palma

The Iberostar Selection Playa de Palma is a beautiful resort that caters to families, couples, and business travelers. Located just a few steps away from the beach, the resort offers different types of rooms to meet every need. There are three adult-only swimming pools, a beautiful garden, and lots of activities to keep guests entertained. Dining options include a buffet restaurant, a Japanese restaurant, and several bars for those looking to unwind.

Zafiro Palace Palmanova

The Zafiro Palace Palmanova is an all-inclusive resort located in the heart of Palma de Mallorca. The resort features a stunning outdoor pool area, beautiful spa, and gym facilities. There is also an indoor pool, mini-golf course, and tennis courts for guests to enjoy. The hotel's restaurant serves local and international cuisine, while the pool bar provides refreshing cocktails and snacks by the poolside.

Sentido Cala Vinas Hotel

The Sentido Cala Vinas Hotel is located in the south-west coast of Mallorca, in a quiet and exotic place. The hotel features a private beach, gardens, three swimming pools, and a terrace with stunning views. Guests can also enjoy sports activities like tennis, pool, and mini-golf. The restaurant serves local and international dishes, while the bar serves refreshing drinks throughout the day.

Sol Guadalupe

Sol Guadalupe is a beautiful hotel located near the famous Magaluf beach, famous for its nightclubs and bars. The hotel has 493 spacious and modern rooms, all fully equipped with amenities like air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and free Wi-Fi. There is also a beautiful pool area, a gym, and a spa. The hotel's restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine, while the pool bar provides drinks and light snacks.

FAQs

Q. What is the best time to visit Palma de Mallorca?

A. The best time to visit Palma de Mallorca is from April to early November. This is when the weather is warm and the island is fully open for tourism.

Q. What is the currency in Palma de Mallorca?

A. The currency in Palma de Mallorca is the Euro.

Q. Are there any all-inclusive resorts in Palma de Mallorca?

A. Yes, there are many all-inclusive resorts in Palma de Mallorca that offer a wide range of services to their guests.

Q. Is Palma de Mallorca a family-friendly destination?

A. Yes, Palma de Mallorca is a family-friendly destination, thanks to its many activities and attractions that cater to both children and adults.

Q. What is the language spoken in Palma de Mallorca?

A. The official language of Palma de Mallorca is Spanish, although English is widely spoken.
