1\. Barcelona

Barcelona is one of the most vibrant and cultural cities in Spain. The city is famous for its stunning architecture, delicious food, and lively nightlife. The main tourist attractions in Barcelona are the Sagrada Familia, the Park Guell, and the Gothic Quarter. The Sagrada Familia is a magnificent church designed by the famous artist Gaudi. The Park Guell is a beautiful park with stunning views of the city. The Gothic Quarter is an old part of the city with narrow streets and beautiful buildings.

The currency in Barcelona is Euro.

The best way to get around Barcelona is by public transport, such as metro or bus.
