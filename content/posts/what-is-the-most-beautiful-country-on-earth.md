# What is the Most Beautiful Country on Earth?

When it comes to finding the most beautiful country on earth, there is no shortage of contenders. From the lush green hills of Ireland to the crystal clear waters of the Mediterranean, there are many places around the world that can take your breath away. But when it comes to beauty, there are a few countries that stand out above the rest.

## New Zealand

When it comes to beauty, New Zealand is hard to beat. Ranked number one in scenic beauty, this small country is home to some of the most stunning landscapes in the world. From the snow-capped peaks of the Southern Alps to the lush rainforests of the North Island, New Zealand is a paradise for nature lovers. With its diverse range of landscapes, from mountains to beaches, it’s no wonder that New Zealand is ranked number eleven in best countries overall.

## Greece

Greece is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number two in scenic beauty, this Mediterranean nation is home to some of the most stunning beaches in the world. From the white-sand beaches of Mykonos to the crystal clear waters of Santorini, Greece is a paradise for sun-seekers. With its stunning coastline, ancient ruins, and vibrant culture, it’s no wonder that Greece is ranked number twenty-five in best countries overall.

## Italy

Italy is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number three in scenic beauty, this Mediterranean nation is home to some of the most stunning cities in the world. From the ancient ruins of Rome to the canals of Venice, Italy is a paradise for history lovers. With its stunning architecture, delicious cuisine, and vibrant culture, it’s no wonder that Italy is ranked number fourteen in best countries overall.

## Switzerland

Switzerland is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number four in scenic beauty, this Alpine nation is home to some of the most stunning landscapes in the world. From the snow-capped peaks of the Swiss Alps to the lush green valleys of the Rhine, Switzerland is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. With its stunning scenery, vibrant culture, and high quality of life, it’s no wonder that Switzerland is ranked number one in best countries overall.

## Spain

Spain is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number five in scenic beauty, this Mediterranean nation is home to some of the most stunning cities in the world. From the bustling streets of Barcelona to the sun-drenched beaches of the Costa del Sol, Spain is a paradise for culture lovers. With its stunning architecture, delicious cuisine, and vibrant culture, it’s no wonder that Spain is ranked number sixteen in best countries overall.

## Norway

Norway is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number six in scenic beauty, this Nordic nation is home to some of the most stunning landscapes in the world. From the snow-capped peaks of the Norwegian fjords to the lush green valleys of the Sognefjord, Norway is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. With its stunning scenery, vibrant culture, and high quality of life, it’s no wonder that Norway is a popular destination for travelers.

## Australia

Australia is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number seven in scenic beauty, this island nation is home to some of the most stunning landscapes in the world. From the white-sand beaches of the Great Barrier Reef to the lush green rainforests of the Daintree, Australia is a paradise for nature lovers. With its stunning scenery, vibrant culture, and high quality of life, it’s no wonder that Australia is ranked number seven in best countries overall.

## Ireland

Ireland is another country that is renowned for its beauty. Ranked number eight in scenic beauty, this small island nation is home to some of the most stunning landscapes in the world. From the rolling green hills of the Irish countryside to the rugged coastline of the Wild Atlantic Way, Ireland is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. With its stunning scenery, vibrant culture, and high quality of life, it’s no wonder that Ireland is ranked number eight in best countries overall.

When it comes to finding the most beautiful country on earth, there is no shortage of contenders. From the lush green hills of Ireland to the crystal clear waters of the Mediterranean, there are many places around the world that can take your breath away. But when it comes to beauty, there are a few countries that stand out above the rest. New Zealand, Greece, Italy, Switzerland, Spain, Norway, Australia, and Ireland are all contenders for the title of most beautiful country on earth. With their stunning landscapes, vibrant cultures, and high quality of life, it’s no wonder that these countries are some of the most popular destinations for travelers. So, if you’re looking for the most beautiful country on earth, you can’t go wrong with any of these stunning destinations.
