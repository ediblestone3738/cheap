# Pretty Places To Visit During Christmas

The holiday season is a magical time of year when cities and towns around the world transform into winter wonderlands. From twinkling lights to festive markets, there's no shortage of beautiful places to visit during Christmas. Whether you're looking to experience a white Christmas or simply want to soak in the holiday spirit, here are some of the prettiest places to visit during Christmas.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas magic. The Big Apple comes alive with festive cheer, offering a plethora of holiday activities and sights.

- **Rockefeller Center**: The iconic Christmas tree at Rockefeller Center is a must-see. Skating on the ice rink beneath the towering tree is a quintessential New York holiday experience.
- **Fifth Avenue**: Stroll down Fifth Avenue to see the dazzling holiday window displays in department stores like Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue.
- **Central Park**: Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride through a snow-dusted Central Park or take a leisurely walk to enjoy the serene beauty of the park in winter.

## 2. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a city that truly embraces the Christmas spirit with its charming markets and festive atmosphere.

- **Vienna Christmas Markets**: The city is home to several Christmas markets, with the most famous being the Vienna Christmas World at Rathausplatz. Here, you can find traditional Austrian crafts, delicious treats, and warm mulled wine.
- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit the Christmas Village at Belvedere Palace for a picturesque setting and a variety of unique gifts.
- **Concerts and Performances**: Vienna is known for its classical music, and during the holiday season, you can attend special Christmas concerts and performances.

## 3. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's fairy-tale architecture and cobblestone streets make it a perfect destination for a Christmas getaway.

- **Old Town Square**: The Christmas market in Old Town Square is one of the most beautiful in Europe, with a stunning Christmas tree and a variety of stalls selling handmade gifts and traditional Czech foods.
- **Charles Bridge**: Walk across the Charles Bridge for breathtaking views of the city, especially enchanting when dusted with snow.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore the historic Prague Castle and enjoy the festive decorations and events held within its grounds.

## 4. Lapland, Finland

For a truly magical Christmas experience, head to Lapland, the official home of Santa Claus.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Visit Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi, where you can meet Santa himself and cross the Arctic Circle.
- **Northern Lights**: Lapland offers one of the best chances to see the Northern Lights, a natural phenomenon that adds to the holiday magic.
- **Reindeer and Husky Safaris**: Experience a traditional reindeer sleigh ride or a thrilling husky safari through the snowy landscapes.

- **Petite France**: Wander through the charming district of Petite France, with its half-timbered houses adorned with Christmas lights.

## 6. Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg is home to one of the most famous Christmas markets in the world, the Nuremberg Christkindlesmarkt.

- **Christkindlesmarkt**: Held in the main square, this market is known for its traditional wooden stalls, gingerbread cookies, and mulled wine.
- **Nuremberg Castle**: Visit the historic Nuremberg Castle for panoramic views of the city and enjoy the festive atmosphere.
- **Toy Museum**: Explore the Toy Museum to see a collection of toys from different eras, adding a nostalgic touch to your Christmas visit.

## 7. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City offers a European-style Christmas experience with its charming old town and festive events.

- **Old Quebec**: The historic district of Old Quebec is transformed into a winter wonderland, with cobblestone streets and beautifully decorated buildings.
- **German Christmas Market**: Visit the German Christmas Market for traditional crafts, food, and entertainment.
- **Toboggan Slide**: Experience the thrill of the toboggan slide at Dufferin Terrace, a popular winter activity for both locals and visitors.

## 8. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh is a city that knows how to celebrate Christmas with style and charm.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Located in East Princes Street Gardens, this market offers a variety of festive stalls, an ice rink, and a Ferris wheel.
- **The Royal Mile**: Stroll down the Royal Mile, where you'll find beautifully decorated shops and street performers adding to the festive atmosphere.
- **Hogmanay**: Extend your stay to experience Hogmanay, Edinburgh's famous New Year's celebration, featuring fireworks, concerts, and a torchlight procession.

## 9. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations, with stunning light displays and festive events.

- **Tokyo Midtown**: Visit Tokyo Midtown for its impressive light display, featuring thousands of LED lights creating a magical winter scene.
- **Roppongi Hills**: Explore Roppongi Hills for more dazzling illuminations and a Christmas market offering international foods and gifts.
- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Experience a Disney Christmas at Tokyo Disneyland, with festive parades, decorations, and special holiday-themed attractions.

## 10. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes and festive traditions.

- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's unique Christmas folklore, including the mischievous Yule Lads who visit children during the holiday season.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the Christmas markets in Reykjavik for handmade crafts, local delicacies, and holiday cheer.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the warm waters of the Blue Lagoon, surrounded by snow-covered landscapes, for a truly magical experience.

## Conclusion

Whether you're looking to explore a bustling city or a serene winter landscape, there are countless pretty places to visit during Christmas. Each destination offers its own unique charm and festive traditions, making the holiday season a perfect time to travel and create unforgettable memories. From the twinkling lights of New York City to the enchanting markets of Europe, these destinations are sure to fill your heart with holiday cheer.
