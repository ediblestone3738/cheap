# Best Place To Travel Over Christmas Break

The holiday season is a magical time of year, filled with joy, celebration, and the spirit of giving. For many, it's also the perfect opportunity to embark on a memorable vacation. Whether you're seeking a winter wonderland or a sun-soaked escape, there are countless destinations around the world that offer unique experiences during the Christmas break. In this article, we'll explore some of the best places to travel over Christmas, each offering its own special charm and festive allure.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas magic. The city transforms into a winter wonderland, with twinkling lights, festive decorations, and a palpable sense of excitement in the air.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Witness the iconic Christmas tree and go ice skating at the Rockefeller Center rink.
- **Broadway Shows**: Catch a holiday-themed Broadway show, such as "The Nutcracker" or "A Christmas Carol."
- **Holiday Markets**: Explore the Union Square Holiday Market for unique gifts and delicious treats.
- **Window Displays**: Stroll along Fifth Avenue to admire the elaborate holiday window displays at department stores like Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue.

## 2. Lapland, Finland

For a truly enchanting Christmas experience, head to Lapland, Finland, the official home of Santa Claus. This winter wonderland offers a magical setting for families and adventurers alike.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Visit Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi and meet Santa himself.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking Northern Lights dancing across the Arctic sky.
- **Reindeer Sleigh Rides**: Enjoy a traditional reindeer sleigh ride through snow-covered forests.
- **Husky Safaris**: Experience the thrill of a husky safari across the pristine snowy landscapes.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a city that truly comes alive during the Christmas season, with its elegant architecture and rich cultural heritage providing a stunning backdrop for festive celebrations.

- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the Christkindlmarkt at Rathausplatz for handcrafted gifts and mulled wine.
- **Classical Concerts**: Attend a classical concert at the Vienna State Opera or the Musikverein.
- **St. Stephen's Cathedral**: Marvel at the Gothic architecture of St. Stephen's Cathedral, beautifully illuminated for the holidays.
- **Viennese Coffeehouses**: Warm up with a cup of Viennese coffee and a slice of Sachertorte in a traditional coffeehouse.

## 4. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations, making it an exciting destination for travelers seeking something different.

- **Illuminations**: Experience the dazzling winter illuminations at locations like Tokyo Midtown and Shibuya.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore European-style Christmas markets at Roppongi Hills and Yokohama Red Brick Warehouse.
- **Shopping**: Take advantage of holiday sales in the bustling shopping districts of Shibuya and Ginza.
- **Theme Parks**: Enjoy festive events at Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo DisneySea.

## 5. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City is a charming destination that exudes old-world charm and festive spirit during the Christmas season.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the cobblestone streets of Old Quebec, adorned with twinkling lights and decorations.
- **German Christmas Market**: Visit the German Christmas Market for traditional crafts and delicious treats.
- **Carnaval de Québec**: Experience the magic of the Winter Carnival, featuring ice sculptures and parades.
- **Château Frontenac**: Stay at the iconic Château Frontenac, a fairy-tale castle overlooking the St. Lawrence River.

## 6. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking a sun-soaked Christmas, Sydney offers a vibrant and festive atmosphere with its stunning beaches and outdoor celebrations.

- **Bondi Beach**: Celebrate Christmas Day with a beach picnic or a swim at Bondi Beach.
- **Carols by Candlelight**: Join the locals for Carols by Candlelight at the Domain, a beloved holiday tradition.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Take a cruise on Sydney Harbour to enjoy the city's iconic skyline and festive lights.
- **New Year's Eve**: Extend your stay to experience Sydney's world-famous New Year's Eve fireworks.

## 7. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a fairy-tale city that becomes even more enchanting during the Christmas season, with its medieval architecture and festive markets.

- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market at Old Town Square, complete with a towering Christmas tree.
- **Charles Bridge**: Stroll across the Charles Bridge, beautifully illuminated for the holidays.
- **Prague Castle**: Explore Prague Castle and attend a Christmas concert in St. Vitus Cathedral.
- **Traditional Cuisine**: Savor traditional Czech Christmas dishes, such as carp and potato salad.

## 8. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique and adventurous Christmas experience, with its stunning natural landscapes and vibrant holiday celebrations.

- **Northern Lights**: Chase the Northern Lights on a guided tour outside the city.
- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the Christmas markets in downtown Reykjavik for local crafts and treats.
- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters of the Blue Lagoon, surrounded by snow-covered lava fields.
- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's unique holiday folklore, featuring the mischievous Yule Lads.

## 9. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh is a city steeped in history and tradition, offering a festive and cultural Christmas experience.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Visit the European Christmas Market in East Princes Street Gardens.
- **Hogmanay**: Celebrate Hogmanay, Scotland's famous New Year's festival, with fireworks and street parties.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Explore the historic Edinburgh Castle, beautifully lit for the holidays.
- **Royal Mile**: Wander along the Royal Mile, lined with festive lights and decorations.

## 10. Cape Town, South Africa

For a unique blend of culture, nature, and sunshine, Cape Town is an ideal Christmas destination.

- **Table Mountain**: Take a cable car ride to the top of Table Mountain for panoramic views of the city.
- **Kirstenbosch Gardens**: Enjoy a picnic and live music at the Kirstenbosch Summer Sunset Concerts.
- **V&A Waterfront**: Explore the V&A Waterfront, adorned with festive lights and offering a variety of dining and shopping options.
- **Cape Winelands**: Take a day trip to the nearby Cape Winelands for wine tasting and scenic views.

## Conclusion

Whether you're dreaming of a snowy escape or a sunny retreat, the world is full of incredible destinations to explore over the Christmas break. Each of these locations offers its own unique charm and festive spirit, ensuring a memorable holiday experience for travelers of all ages. So pack your bags, embrace the holiday spirit, and embark on a Christmas adventure you'll never forget.
