## Top 5 Tourist Attractions in Puerto Rico

1\. Old San Juan: Old San Juan is a vibrant and colorful city, filled with cobbled streets and colonial architecture. There are plenty of activities to enjoy here, including sightseeing, shopping, and dining.

2\. El Yunque National Forest: El Yunque is a lush rainforest located in the northeastern part of Puerto Rico. Here you can explore the trails, take a dip in the waterfalls, and enjoy the stunning views of the Caribbean Sea.

3\. Bioluminescent Bay: Puerto Rico is home to several bioluminescent bays, where you can witness the magical sight of glowing plankton in the water.

4\. Luquillo Beach: Luquillo is one of the most popular beaches in Puerto Rico, known for its white sand and crystal-clear waters.

5\. Ponce: Ponce is a beautiful city on the southern coast of Puerto Rico. Here you can explore the colorful streets, visit the museums, and enjoy the vibrant nightlife.

## FAQs

Q: What is the best time of year to visit Puerto Rico?

A: The best time to visit Puerto Rico is from December to April, when the weather is dry and sunny.

Q: Is Puerto Rico expensive?

Q: What currency is used in Puerto Rico?

A: The official currency in Puerto Rico is the US dollar.

Q: Is Puerto Rico safe?

A: Puerto Rico is generally a safe destination, but it is always recommended to take the usual safety precautions.

Q: What is the best way to get around Puerto Rico?

A: The best way to get around Puerto Rico is by car. You can rent a car or take public transportation such as buses or taxis.
