# Where Do Most Brits Go on Holiday?

When it comes to planning a holiday, Brits have a wide range of destinations to choose from. But where do most Brits go on holiday? We’ve done the research and compiled a list of the 10 most popular holiday destinations for Brits.

## 1\. Spain

Spain has long been a favourite of British holidaymakers, and it remains the UK's number one holiday destination. From the beautiful beaches of the Costa del Sol to the vibrant cities of Barcelona and Madrid, Spain has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an action-packed city break, Spain has it all.

## 2\. France

France is another popular destination for British holidaymakers. From the chic cities of Paris and Nice to the stunning countryside of the Loire Valley and Provence, France offers a wealth of attractions. Whether you’re looking for a romantic city break or a leisurely countryside escape, France has something for everyone.

## 3\. Greece

Greece is a popular destination for Brits looking for a sunny holiday. With its stunning beaches, ancient ruins and vibrant nightlife, Greece has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an action-packed city break, Greece has it all.

## 4\. Great Britain

It may seem strange, but Great Britain is actually a popular holiday destination for Brits. From the stunning countryside of the Lake District and Scotland to the vibrant cities of London and Manchester, Great Britain has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing countryside escape or an action-packed city break, Great Britain has it all.

## 5\. Portugal

Portugal is a popular destination for British holidaymakers. From the stunning beaches of the Algarve to the vibrant cities of Lisbon and Porto, Portugal has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an action-packed city break, Portugal has it all.

## 6\. Italy

Italy is another popular destination for British holidaymakers. From the stunning beaches of the Amalfi Coast to the vibrant cities of Rome and Florence, Italy has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a romantic city break or a leisurely countryside escape, Italy has it all.

## 7\. Turkey

Turkey is a popular destination for Brits looking for a sunny holiday. With its stunning beaches, ancient ruins and vibrant nightlife, Turkey has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an action-packed city break, Turkey has it all.

## 8\. USA

The USA is a popular destination for British holidaymakers. From the stunning beaches of Florida to the vibrant cities of New York and Los Angeles, the USA has something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach holiday or an action-packed city break, the USA has it all.

## Conclusion

As you can see, there are a wide range of destinations for Brits to choose from when planning a holiday. From the stunning beaches of Spain to the vibrant cities of the USA, there’s something for everyone. So, if you’re looking for a holiday destination, why not consider one of these 10 popular destinations?

No matter where you decide to go, make sure to book your holiday in advance to get the best deals. The best time to book is usually 23 December 2022, so make sure to keep an eye out for any deals.
