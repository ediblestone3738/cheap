If you are looking for a destination to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life, Corfu is the perfect place for you. This Greek island is known for its rugged coastline, turquoise waters, and medieval castles. Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is the perfect home base to explore the beauty of Corfu. This luxury resort is located in the town of Kanoni, just a few kilometers from the bustling capital city of Corfu.

With its prime location on a wooded hillside overlooking the Ionian Sea, the Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace offers unmatched views of the ocean, mountains, and the city. Here are some of the reasons why it is the perfect base to explore the beauty of Corfu.

The Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace Offers Top-Notch Accommodations

As expected from a five-star hotel, the Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace offers a wide variety of luxurious accommodations. From single rooms to suites, all rooms come with a private balcony or terrace, some with stunning sea views. The rooms are designed to offer maximum comfort and relaxation, with high-quality linens and premium toiletries.

The hotel also features a range of facilities to make your stay as comfortable as possible, including a fantastic spa, gym, swimming pool, and an on-site restaurant that serves traditional Greek dishes alongside international cuisine.

Location Is Everything

The Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace is perfectly located, whether you want to relax or explore the island. The hotel's position at the top of a hill offers sweeping views over the Ionian Sea, and Kanoni's historic landmarks are all easily accessible on foot or by car. If you're looking to take things down a notch, the hotel is nestled amid lush greenery, and the tranquil surroundings are perfect for a leisurely stroll, jog, or bike ride.

If you're eager to explore, Corfu town is just a short drive away. The town is known for its unique blend of Italian and Greek influences and is home to some of the best restaurants, bars, and cafes on the island. Additionally, the hotel offers complimentary shuttle service to the center of Corfu, making it easy to get around.

The hotel's location also provides easy access to many of the island's most popular tourist attractions. Whether you're looking to explore the rugged coastline, hike in the mountains or discover the island's fascinating history, you'll find everything within easy reach.

A Great Variety of Restaurants and Bars at the Hotel

At Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace, you can enjoy both local and international cuisine. The hotel's main restaurant, Arcadian, serves both hot and cold dishes for breakfast and dinner, and there are a number of other restaurants to choose from, including a beachside bar and grill that serves Mediterranean cuisine.

If you're looking for something more unique, head to the hotel's rooftop bar, where you can sip cocktails and watch the sun set over the Ionian Sea. The hotel also has a poolside bar where you can grab a drink and snack while enjoying the stunning views.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Explore the Beauty of Corfu with Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace as Your Home Base

1\. Corfu Old Town - A UNESCO World Heritage Site that is known for its charming narrow streets, historic buildings, and traditional architecture.

2\. Achillion Palace - A grandiose palace that was built for the Empress of Austria, Elizabeth of Bavaria, and is known for its beautiful gardens and breathtaking views.

3\. Paleokastritsa - A picturesque village situated between two bays that is known for its crystal-clear waters and secluded beaches.

4\. Mount Pantokrator - The highest point on the island, offering breathtaking views of the island's rugged terrain, the shimmering blue of the sea, and the neighboring islands.

5\. Mouse Island (Pontikonisi) - One of the most iconic sights on the island, and a place of incredible natural beauty.

6\. Spianada Square - The most famous square in Corfu, located in the heart of the historic center and surrounded by stately buildings and cafes.

7\. Venetian Fortress - A massive fortress built in the 15th century, the Venetian Fortress is a must-visit attraction for anyone interested in the island's turbulent history.

8\. Mon Repos - A beautiful neoclassical palace that was the birthplace of Prince Philip, the Duke of Edinburgh.

9\. Kassiopi - A charming fishing village in the northeast of the island that is known for its unspoiled beaches, great food, and vibrant nightlife.

10\. Canal D'Amour - A natural grotto located in the northwest of the island that is known for its unique rock formations and the crystal-clear waters that surround it.

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Corfu?

A: The best time to visit Corfu is between May and October, when the weather is warm and sunny. July and August can be quite busy, so if you prefer a quieter experience, consider visiting in May, June, or September.

Q: What is the currency in Corfu?

A: The currency in Corfu is the Euro.

Q: What is the distance between Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace and Corfu town?

Q: Is Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace suitable for families?

Q: Is there a beach near Hotel Corfu Holiday Palace?
