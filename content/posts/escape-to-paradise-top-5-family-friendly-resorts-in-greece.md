Escape to Paradise: Top 5 Family-Friendly Resorts in Greece

Greece has been a popular tourist destination for decades, known for its ancient history, stunning beaches, and delicious cuisine. But it’s also a great destination for families with plenty of resorts that cater to parents and their children. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach vacation or a more adventurous escape, these top 5 family-friendly resorts in Greece are sure to please.

1\. Paradise Beach Resort, Kos Island

Located on the beautiful island of Kos, the Paradise Beach Resort offers a range of family-friendly activities and amenities. The resort’s spacious rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, and a kitchenette. Families can enjoy the resort’s three swimming pools, one of which includes a water slide, as well as a children’s playground and a mini club.

The resort also offers a range of activities for families, including beach volleyball, tennis, and scuba diving. Children can take part in the mini club’s arts and crafts activities, while parents can relax with a massage at the resort’s spa. The Paradise Beach Resort is also located near many of Kos’s top tourist attractions, including the ancient city of Kos and the Hippocrates Garden Cultural Center.

2\. Porto Carras Meliton, Halkidiki

Located on the stunning Halkidiki Peninsula, the Porto Carras Meliton is a family-friendly resort with amenities and activities for all ages. The resort’s rooms feature balconies with stunning sea or mountain views, as well as air conditioning and satellite TV.

Families can enjoy the resort’s private beaches, six swimming pools (including a children’s pool), and a range of water sports. The resort’s mini club offers arts and crafts, games, and sports for children aged 4-12, while older children can take part in the resort’s tennis and basketball courts. Parents can take advantage of the resort’s spa and fitness center.

The Porto Carras Meliton is also located near many of Halkidiki’s top tourist attractions, including the ancient city of Olynthos and the Petralona Cave.

3\. Grecotel Daphnila Bay Thalasso, Corfu

Located on the beautiful island of Corfu, the Grecotel Daphnila Bay Thalasso is a family-friendly resort with stunning views of the Ionian Sea. The resort’s spacious rooms are equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV, and can accommodate families of up to five people.

Families can enjoy the resort’s private beach, as well as the two swimming pools (including a children’s pool) and a range of water sports. The resort’s mini club offers arts and crafts, games, and a playground for children aged 4-12, while older children can take part in the resort’s tennis and basketball courts. Parents can relax in the resort’s spa and fitness center.

The Grecotel Daphnila Bay Thalasso is also located near many of Corfu’s top tourist attractions, including the historic center of Corfu Town and the Achilleion Palace.

4\. Minos Palace Hotel & Suites, Crete

Located on the beautiful island of Crete, the Minos Palace Hotel & Suites is a family-friendly resort with stunning views of the Mirabello Bay. The resort’s spacious rooms feature air conditioning and satellite TV, and many also come with private swimming pools or outdoor Jacuzzis.

Families can enjoy the resort’s private beach, as well as the two swimming pools (including a children’s pool) and a range of water sports. The resort’s mini club offers arts and crafts, games, and a playground for children aged 4-12, while older children can take part in the resort’s tennis and basketball courts. Parents can relax in the resort’s spa and fitness center.

The Minos Palace Hotel & Suites is also located near many of Crete’s top tourist attractions, including the ancient city of Knossos and the town of Agios Nikolaos.

5\. Ikos Olivia, Halkidiki

Also located on the Halkidiki Peninsula, the Ikos Olivia is a family-friendly resort with a range of activities and amenities. The resort’s spacious rooms feature air conditioning and satellite TV, and many also come with private pools or outdoor Jacuzzis.

Families can enjoy the resort’s private beach, as well as the four swimming pools (including a children’s pool) and a range of water sports. The resort’s mini club offers arts and crafts, games, and a playground for children aged 4-12, while older children can take part in the resort’s tennis and basketball courts. Parents can relax in the resort’s spa and fitness center, or take part in the resort’s gourmet dining experiences.

The Ikos Olivia is also located near many of Halkidiki’s top tourist attractions, including the ancient city of Stageira and Ouranoupoli.
