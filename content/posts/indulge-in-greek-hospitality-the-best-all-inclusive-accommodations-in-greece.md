Greece is one of the most popular European destinations. It is known for its spectacular sea, rich cultural heritage, and exquisite Mediterranean cuisine. Greece is home to thousands of dazzling islands, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, and dazzling natural wonders. People visit Greece to relish its idyllic beaches, excellent hospitality, and charming lifestyle.

Indulging in Greek hospitality is a unique experience, which can be best enjoyed at all-inclusive accommodations. These accommodations offer guests unparalleled comfort, convenience, and hospitality. From cozy boutique hotels to luxurious resorts, Greece has some of the best all-inclusive accommodations in Europe.

In this travel article, we will explore the best all-inclusive accommodations in Greece and the top tourist attractions located near them.

Sani Resort

Sani Resort is a luxurious all-inclusive accommodation located on the Kassandra peninsula in Northern Greece. This resort has everything that guests need for an exclusive holiday experience. The resort features several swimming pools, a private beach, a yacht marina, and numerous dining options. Guests can choose from several room categories, including suites and villas.

The Sani Resort is located close to several tourist attractions, including the ancient city of Olynthos, the Petralona cave, and the Sithonia peninsula.

FAQs:

Q: What is the nearest airport to Sani Resort?
A: The nearest airport is Thessaloniki International Airport, located approximately 45 minutes drive away.

Q: Does Sani resort offer a shuttle service to the airport?
A: Yes, Sani Resort offers a shuttle service to and from the airport at an additional charge.

Q: Does Sani resort offer water sports activities?
A: Yes, Sani Resort offers a wide range of water sports activities, including windsurfing, water skiing, and diving.

Top attractions near Sani Resort

1) Petralona Cave: This cave is located approximately 20 minutes drive away from Sani Resort. It is famous for its stalagmites and stalactites and is open to visitors.

2) Ancient Olynthos: This ancient city is located approximately 30 minutes drive away from Sani Resort. It is home to impressive ancient ruins, including a theater and a marketplace.

3) Ierissos Beach: This sandy beach is located approximately 20 minutes drive away from Sani Resort. It is a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing.

Costa Navarino

Costa Navarino is a luxurious all-inclusive accommodation located on the Peloponnese peninsula in southern Greece. It is part of a larger residential area that includes four hotels, several restaurants, shops, and recreational facilities. Guests can choose from several room categories, including suites and villas.

The resort is located near several archaeological sites, including the Palace of Nestor, the Temple of Apollo Epicurius, and Ancient Olympia.

FAQs:

Q: What is the nearest airport to Costa Navarino?
A: The nearest airport is Kalamata International Airport, located approximately 35 km away.

Q: Does Costa Navarino offer a shuttle service to the airport?
A: Yes, Costa Navarino offers a shuttle service to and from the airport at an additional charge.

Q: What recreational facilities are available at Costa Navarino?
A: Costa Navarino offers several recreational facilities, including an 18-hole golf course, tennis courts, and a water park.

Top attractions near Costa Navarino

1) Palace of Nestor: This ancient palace is located approximately 30 minutes drive away from Costa Navarino. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and features numerous preserved frescoes and architectural elements.

2) Voidokilia Beach: This crescent-shaped beach is located approximately 20 minutes drive away from Costa Navarino. It is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in Greece and is ideal for swimming and sunbathing.

3) Ancient Olympia: This ancient site is located approximately 60 minutes drive away from Costa Navarino. It is home to the original Olympic Games and features impressive ancient ruins, including the Temple of Zeus.

St. Nicolas Bay Resort Hotel & Villas

St. Nicolas Bay Resort Hotel & Villas is a luxurious all-inclusive accommodation located on the Gulf of Mirabello in eastern Crete. The resort features several swimming pools, a spa, a private beach, and numerous dining options. Guests can choose from several room categories, including suites and villas.

The resort is located near several tourist attractions, including the Island of Spinalonga, the Palace of Knossos, and the Lassithi Plateau.

FAQs:

Q: What is the nearest airport to St. Nicolas Bay Resort?
A: The nearest airport is Heraklion International Airport, located approximately 45 km away.

Q: Does the resort offer a shuttle service to the airport?
A: Yes, St. Nicolas Bay Resort offers a shuttle service to and from the airport at an additional charge.

Q: What dining options are available at the resort?
A: St. Nicolas Bay Resort features several dining options, including a seafood restaurant, a Mediterranean restaurant, and a beach bar.

Top attractions near St. Nicolas Bay Resort

1) Island of Spinalonga: This historic island is located approximately 20 minutes by boat from St. Nicolas Bay Resort. It is a former leper colony and is home to several preserved historic buildings.

2) Palace of Knossos: This ancient palace is located approximately 90 minutes drive away from St. Nicolas Bay Resort. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and features impressive ancient ruins.

3) Lassithi Plateau: This beautiful plateau is located approximately 60 minutes drive away from St. Nicolas Bay Resort. It is home to several charming villages, including Psychro, and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Indulging in Greek hospitality is a unique and unforgettable experience. Whether you're looking for luxury or boutique, Greece has some of the best all-inclusive accommodations in Europe.
