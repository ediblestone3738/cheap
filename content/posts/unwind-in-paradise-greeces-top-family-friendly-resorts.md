When it comes to naming an exotic vacation destination, Greece tops the list undoubtedly. This southeastern European country has everything – from breathtaking landscapes to ancient mythology, from modern-day adventure to never-ending fun. However, if you are traveling with your family and looking for comfort, safety, and doing everything together, finding the right resort is a lot of work. Nevertheless, we’ve got your back! Here are the top family-friendly resorts in Greece that promise to offer a relaxing and exciting family vacation, while still capturing the beauty of Greece’s splendid sights.

1\. Elounda Bay Palace

Nestled by the aquamarine waters of the Cretan Sea, Elounda Bay Palace is an upscale resort that never fails to impress visitors. With several pools and kids' clubs, this resort is all about keeping your kids entertained while allowing adults to unwind. The accommodations are fit for royalty with all necessary amenities, breathtaking views, and excellent service. The resort’s beachfront location and its evening entertainment will make your stay more lively and enjoyable.

2\. Costa Navarino

Located amidst the stunning Peloponnese region is the Costa Navarino resort. This resort boasts vast natural beauty and basks in history with its cultural exhibits and archaeological sites. The resort has two luxurious hotels with playgrounds, kids clubs, sandy beaches, and an array of excursions, making it the best place to create unforgettable family memories.

3\. Ikos Olivia

A newcomer in the resort industry, Ikos Olivia is a treat for tourists, including families. Located near the picturesque Halkidiki peninsula, this all-inclusive resort offers guests access to three private beaches, swimming pools, a kid's club, and recreational activities, including paddleboarding and tennis. The resort’s delectable food, spacious rooms, and sleek design will leave you wanting more.

4\. Sani Beach

Sani Beach is nestled between the Corinthian Gulf and Mount Olympus, offering an unbeatable combination of splendid scenery and endless fun. This all-inclusive resort has marshaled low-rise buildings and bungalows amidst lush green gardens with something for everyone - from toddlers to teenagers. Younger kids will enjoy the kids' club and playgrounds, while teenagers will enjoy the beach parties and night-time entertainment. Adults, too, have access to amenities like the luxurious spa, various water sports, and picturesque hiking trails.

5\. Stella Palace Resort and Spa

This resort is all about luxurious relaxation coupled with fun-filled activities for kids and adults alike. Stella Palace Resort and Spa boast a beachfront location and an all-inclusive package that suits all your family vacation requirements. The resort’s kid's club has various features, including a waterpark with slides, a cinema, and a playground. Additionally, adults have access to spa services, yoga classes, and a myriad of recreational and sports activities.

6\. Domes Zeen Chania

A beautiful desaturated resort located on Crete Island, Domes Zeen Chania adds a touch of elegance and modernity to your family vacation. The resort’s spacious suites, breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea, beaches, pools, and kids' clubs are unmatched. The resort also offers self-contained residences with multiple bedrooms, kitchens, and living and dining rooms, providing ample space and privacy for families.

7\. Grecotel Corfu Imperial

Set against the emerald hillsides of Corfu's Ionian Coastline, Grecotel Corfu Imperial boasts a family-friendly environment with exceptional amenities. Accommodations are modern, spacious and feature balconies or patios. The kid's club caters to younger kids, while teenagers enjoy a teen-only club. The beachfront location, various swimming pools, water sports, and evening entertainment ensure that no one gets bored.

8\. The Westin Resort Costa Navarino

The Westin Resort Costa Navarino reflects the region's spacious landscape and offers deluxe accommodations and facilities to suit everyone. With a golf course, a fitness studio, a spa, and a kids' club with educational programs, kids can learn and have fun simultaneously. The resort is also known for its locally sourced cuisine, stunning views, and recreational activities like hiking and cycling.

9\. Blue Palace, a Luxury Collection Resort and Spa

Blue Palace is located in the charming town of Elounda, which offers visitors a welcome escape from the mundaneness of everyday life. The resort is nestled amidst olive groves and open-air restaurants and offers its guests private pools, a mini club, and the chance to swim in crystal-clear waters or bask in the sun on the resort’s private beach. The rooms are comfortable with modern aesthetics and luxurious amenities.

10\. Asterion Hotel

Asterion Hotel offers guests a perfect blend of Cretan tradition and modern luxury. The resort has three private beaches with shallow waters, suitable for young children, various swimming pools, a waterpark, and more. The resort’s animation team offers various shows and performances for all ages to keep you all entertained. The facilities are exceptional, with stylish rooms that give you a homely feeling away from home.

FAQs

1\. What Kind of Food Should I Expect in Greece?

Greece is famous for its Mediterranean cuisine. Foodies will have a wide range of delicacies to try, including tomato fritters, moussaka, and souvlaki. Cyprus and Greece also have similar precious civilisations and tasteful cuisine.

2\. Is It Safe to Travel with Kids to Greece?

Greece is one of the safest countries in Europe, and it is generally safe to travel with your kids. However, as with any travel, there are a few precautions to take into consideration, such as staying hydrated, using sunscreens, and ensuring your kids do not wander off.

3\. What Is the Best Time to visit Greece?

Greece is sunny for the most part of the year, and its climate is generally mild. However, the best time to visit will depend on the activities you want to indulge in - for example, excursions are best from May to October, while winter sports lovers should visit during the winter months.

4\. How Can I Get Around Greece with My Family?

Greece has a well-developed transportation network with everything from rental cars, taxis to boats and ferries - this makes it easy to get around with your family. Additionally, many resorts offer transportation services for their guests.

5\. What Activities Can My Family and I Participate in in Greece?

There are plenty of activities to participate in Greece, including snorkelling, swimming, sightseeing, exploring historical sites, hiking, and engaging in water sports such as kayaking, paddle boating, and more.
