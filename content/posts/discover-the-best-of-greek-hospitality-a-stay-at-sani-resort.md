Discover the Best of Greek Hospitality: A Stay at Sani Resort

Greece is known for its stunning Mediterranean coastline, pristine beaches, lush countryside and warm hospitality. Here, guests can indulge in a relaxing vacation that will leave them feeling rejuvenated and recharged.

Accommodation

Sani Resort caters to every need of its guests - be it couples, families, or solo travellers. The resort offers a wide range of accommodation options ranging from standard and deluxe rooms to spacious suites and villas that come with private pools. All accommodations are designed with utmost attention to detail, ensuring that your stay is as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. The interiors feature elegant furnishings, modern amenities, and panoramic views of the ocean or garden.

Dining

Sani resort offers guests an exceptional dining experience with its wide range of restaurants, bars, and cafes. Each restaurant serves up a unique blend of Mediterranean and international cuisine using locally sourced ingredients. From Michelin-starred dining at Katsu to healthy organic meals at the Garden Theatre, there's something for everyone. The resort's wine cellar boasts an impressive selection of over 250 labels of international and Greek wines, ensuring that wine enthusiasts can indulge their passions.

Activities

Sani Resort boasts an array of activities to ensure guests have an enjoyable and active stay. The resort's sports centre offers various sports facilities such as basketball and tennis courts, football fields, and beach volleyball. Watersports lovers can enjoy a range of activities such as windsurfing, sailing, and kayaking. The resort also has an exclusive sailing academy where guests can learn the art of sailing. Kids are not left out as the resort has its own mini club that provides a range of activities for children aged between four months to 17 years.

Spa

Sani Resort's spa is a tranquil and peaceful haven where guests can experience the ultimate relaxation. The spa offers an unparalleled range of treatments such as massages, facials, body wraps, and hydrotherapy. The spa's treatments are carefully curated to help restore the balance of body, mind, and spirit. The therapists use only carefully selected natural products to ensure that guests leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Discover the Best of Greek Hospitality: A Stay at Sani Resort

1\. Explore the ancient city of Olynthus - this archaeological site is known for its preserved mosaics and artifacts from the fourth century BC.

2\. Visit Mount Athos - one of the most sacred places in the Greek Orthodox religion, this mountain is home to over 20 monasteries.

3\. Take a stroll around the picturesque village of Afytos - this traditional village has narrow cobbled streets and stunning views of the ocean.

4\. Relax on the pristine beaches of Kassandra peninsula - the peninsula boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in Greece.

5\. Visit the Petralona Cave - this cave is home to some of the oldest human remains found in Europe.

6\. Take a boat tour around the Halkidiki peninsula - explore the region's hidden coves, secluded beaches, and crystal-clear waters.

7\. Taste the local cuisine - Halkidiki is known for its fresh seafood, olive oil, and honey, making it a food lover's paradise.

8\. Visit the Ancient Stagira - the birthplace of the famous philosopher Aristotle, this ancient city is steeped in history.

9\. Spend the day at Sani Marina - an exclusive marina with luxurious yachts, designer boutiques, and elegant bars and restaurants.

10\. Take a day trip to Thessaloniki - Greece's second-largest city, known for its historic monuments, museums, and lively street markets.
