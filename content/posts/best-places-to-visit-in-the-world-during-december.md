# Best Places To Visit In The World During December

December is a magical month, filled with festive cheer, winter wonderlands, and tropical escapes. Whether you're looking to embrace the holiday spirit in a snowy paradise or escape the cold on a sun-drenched beach, the world offers a plethora of destinations to satisfy your wanderlust. Here are some of the best places to visit in December, each offering unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is a quintessential December destination, where the holiday spirit comes alive in grand style.

- **Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree**: Witness the iconic Christmas tree lighting at Rockefeller Center, a tradition that dates back to 1933.
- **Ice Skating**: Glide across the ice at the famous rink in Central Park or Bryant Park.
- **Holiday Markets**: Explore the festive holiday markets at Union Square and Columbus Circle for unique gifts and treats.
- **Broadway Shows**: Catch a holiday-themed Broadway show to experience the magic of New York's theater scene.

## 2. Lapland, Finland

For a true winter wonderland experience, Lapland in Finland is the ultimate destination.

- **Northern Lights**: Marvel at the mesmerizing aurora borealis dancing across the Arctic sky.
- **Santa Claus Village**: Visit the official hometown of Santa Claus in Rovaniemi and meet the man himself.
- **Reindeer Safaris**: Embark on a reindeer safari through the snow-covered forests.
- **Igloo Hotels**: Stay in a glass igloo and gaze at the stars from the comfort of your bed.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna transforms into a fairy tale city during December, with its stunning architecture and festive atmosphere.

- **Christmas Markets**: Stroll through the enchanting Christmas markets at Rathausplatz and Schönbrunn Palace.
- **Classical Concerts**: Enjoy a classical concert at the Vienna State Opera or Musikverein.
- **Viennese Coffeehouses**: Warm up with a cup of traditional Viennese coffee and a slice of Sachertorte.
- **Belvedere Palace**: Visit the Belvedere Palace to admire its baroque architecture and art collections.

## 4. Sydney, Australia

For those seeking sun and sand, Sydney offers a vibrant December experience in the Southern Hemisphere.

- **Sydney Harbour**: Celebrate New Year's Eve with a spectacular fireworks display over Sydney Harbour.
- **Bondi Beach**: Soak up the sun and surf at the iconic Bondi Beach.
- **Sydney Opera House**: Attend a performance or take a guided tour of this architectural masterpiece.
- **Taronga Zoo**: Explore the diverse wildlife at Taronga Zoo, with stunning views of the city skyline.

## 5. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo in December is a blend of traditional and modern festivities, offering a unique cultural experience.

- **Illuminations**: Experience the dazzling winter illuminations at locations like Shibuya and Roppongi Hills.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit the European-style Christmas markets at Tokyo Skytree and Yokohama Red Brick Warehouse.
- **Shopping**: Enjoy holiday shopping in the bustling districts of Shibuya and Ginza.
- **New Year's Celebrations**: Participate in traditional Japanese New Year's celebrations, including temple visits and mochi making.

## 6. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City offers a charming and historic setting for a December getaway, with its cobblestone streets and festive decorations.

- **Old Quebec**: Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Old Quebec, adorned with twinkling lights and holiday decor.
- **Winter Carnival**: Experience the Quebec Winter Carnival, one of the largest and oldest winter festivals in the world.
- **Ice Hotel**: Stay at the Hôtel de Glace, a unique ice hotel built entirely from snow and ice.
- **Tobogganing**: Enjoy a thrilling toboggan ride at Terrasse Dufferin, overlooking the St. Lawrence River.

## 7. Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town offers a warm and sunny escape in December, with its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture.

- **Table Mountain**: Take a cable car ride to the top of Table Mountain for panoramic views of the city and coastline.
- **Cape Winelands**: Explore the picturesque vineyards and indulge in wine tastings in Stellenbosch and Franschhoek.
- **Beaches**: Relax on the beautiful beaches of Camps Bay and Clifton.
- **Robben Island**: Visit the historic Robben Island, where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned.

## 8. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a city of fairy tales, with its medieval architecture and festive charm during December.

- **Prague Castle**: Explore the majestic Prague Castle, overlooking the city.
- **Charles Bridge**: Stroll across the iconic Charles Bridge, adorned with holiday lights.
- **Old Town Square**: Visit the Christmas market in Old Town Square, with its towering Christmas tree and festive stalls.
- **Czech Cuisine**: Savor traditional Czech dishes like roast pork and trdelník, a sweet pastry.

## 9. Maldives

For a tropical paradise in December, the Maldives offers pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters.

- **Overwater Bungalows**: Stay in a luxurious overwater bungalow with direct access to the ocean.
- **Snorkeling and Diving**: Discover the vibrant marine life and coral reefs through snorkeling and diving excursions.
- **Spa Retreats**: Indulge in a relaxing spa treatment with ocean views.
- **Private Island Resorts**: Experience the ultimate in privacy and luxury at a private island resort.

## 10. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik is a gateway to Iceland's natural wonders, offering a unique December adventure.

- **Blue Lagoon**: Soak in the geothermal waters of the Blue Lagoon, surrounded by snow-covered landscapes.
- **Golden Circle**: Explore the Golden Circle, including the geysers of Geysir, Gullfoss waterfall, and Thingvellir National Park.
- **Northern Lights Tours**: Join a guided tour to chase the elusive northern lights.
- **Christmas Celebrations**: Experience Icelandic Christmas traditions, including the 13 Yule Lads.

## Conclusion

December is a month of contrasts, offering destinations that cater to both winter enthusiasts and sun seekers. Whether you're drawn to the festive lights of a bustling city, the serene beauty of a snowy landscape, or the tranquil shores of a tropical island, there's a perfect December destination waiting for you. Embrace the spirit of the season and create unforgettable memories in some of the best places the world has to offer.
