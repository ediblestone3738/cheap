# Vacation Places To Go For Christmas

As the holiday season approaches, the excitement of planning a Christmas getaway fills the air. Whether you dream of a white Christmas surrounded by snow-capped mountains or a tropical escape with sandy beaches, there are countless destinations to consider. In this article, we explore some of the best vacation places to go for Christmas, each offering unique experiences and festive cheer.

## 1. New York City, USA

New York City is synonymous with Christmas magic. The Big Apple transforms into a winter wonderland, with iconic landmarks adorned in festive decorations.

- **Rockefeller Center**: Witness the famous Christmas tree lighting and enjoy ice skating at the iconic rink.
- **Fifth Avenue**: Marvel at the elaborate holiday window displays and shop for gifts at world-renowned stores.
- **Radio City Music Hall**: Experience the Rockettes' Christmas Spectacular, a beloved holiday tradition.
- **Central Park**: Take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the snow-dusted park.

## 2. Lapland, Finland

For a truly enchanting Christmas experience, visit Lapland, the official home of Santa Claus.

- **Santa Claus Village**: Meet Santa himself and explore his workshop.
- **Northern Lights**: Witness the breathtaking Aurora Borealis dancing across the night sky.
- **Reindeer Sleigh Rides**: Glide through snowy forests on a traditional reindeer sleigh.
- **Igloo Hotels**: Stay in a glass igloo and gaze at the stars from the comfort of your bed.

## 3. Vienna, Austria

Vienna exudes old-world charm and elegance, making it a perfect destination for a classic Christmas celebration.

- **Christmas Markets**: Explore the city's numerous markets, such as the Vienna Christmas Market at Rathausplatz.
- **St. Stephen's Cathedral**: Attend a Christmas concert in this stunning Gothic cathedral.
- **Vienna State Opera**: Enjoy a festive performance of a classic opera or ballet.
- **Schönbrunn Palace**: Visit the palace's Christmas market and admire the beautifully decorated grounds.

## 4. Quebec City, Canada

Quebec City offers a European-style Christmas experience with its cobblestone streets and historic architecture.

- **Old Quebec**: Wander through the charming streets adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations.
- **German Christmas Market**: Discover unique gifts and enjoy traditional treats like mulled wine and gingerbread.
- **Tobogganing**: Slide down the famous toboggan run on Dufferin Terrace for an exhilarating winter activity.
- **Ice Hotel**: Stay at the Hôtel de Glace, a stunning ice hotel built anew each year.

## 5. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Christmas celebrations, with dazzling illuminations and festive events.

- **Tokyo Disneyland**: Experience the park's Christmas Fantasy event with themed parades and decorations.
- **Roppongi Hills**: Admire the stunning light displays and enjoy holiday shopping.
- **Ginza**: Explore the upscale shopping district and its beautifully decorated storefronts.
- **Christmas Markets**: Visit European-style markets offering seasonal treats and handcrafted gifts.

## 6. Sydney, Australia

For a warm and sunny Christmas, Sydney offers a vibrant holiday atmosphere with plenty of outdoor activities.

- **Bondi Beach**: Celebrate Christmas with a beach barbecue and a swim in the ocean.
- **Sydney Harbour**: Enjoy a Christmas cruise with stunning views of the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
- **Carols in the Domain**: Join thousands for a festive evening of carol singing in the park.
- **Taronga Zoo**: Visit the zoo for special holiday events and meet the native wildlife.

## 7. Prague, Czech Republic

Prague's fairy-tale setting makes it a magical destination for a Christmas vacation.

- **Old Town Square**: Explore the Christmas market and admire the towering Christmas tree.
- **Charles Bridge**: Stroll across the historic bridge, beautifully illuminated for the holidays.
- **Prague Castle**: Visit the castle grounds and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Traditional Czech Cuisine**: Savor festive dishes like carp and potato salad.

## 8. Reykjavik, Iceland

Reykjavik offers a unique and adventurous Christmas experience with its stunning natural landscapes.

- **Blue Lagoon**: Relax in the geothermal waters surrounded by snow-covered lava fields.
- **Golden Circle**: Explore Iceland's famous natural wonders, including geysers and waterfalls.
- **Christmas Village**: Visit the Hafnarfjörður Christmas Village for local crafts and festive treats.
- **Yule Lads**: Learn about Iceland's mischievous Christmas trolls and their holiday traditions.

## 9. Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh's historic charm and festive spirit make it an ideal destination for a Christmas getaway.

- **Edinburgh Christmas Market**: Browse the stalls for unique gifts and enjoy seasonal food and drinks.
- **Hogmanay**: Celebrate New Year's Eve with Scotland's famous Hogmanay festival.
- **Edinburgh Castle**: Visit the iconic castle and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Royal Mile**: Explore the historic street adorned with festive lights and decorations.

## 10. Zurich, Switzerland

Zurich offers a picturesque setting for a traditional Swiss Christmas with its snow-covered landscapes and cozy atmosphere.

- **Christkindlimarkt**: Visit the indoor Christmas market at Zurich Main Station, featuring a stunning Swarovski tree.
- **Bahnhofstrasse**: Stroll along the famous shopping street, beautifully lit with holiday lights.
- **Lake Zurich**: Take a scenic boat ride on the lake and enjoy the winter scenery.
- **Swiss Fondue**: Warm up with a traditional Swiss fondue in a cozy chalet.

## 11. Nuremberg, Germany

Nuremberg is home to one of the world's most famous Christmas markets, offering a quintessential German holiday experience.

- **Nuremberg Christkindlesmarkt**: Explore the market's stalls filled with handcrafted ornaments and delicious treats.
- **Nuremberg Castle**: Visit the historic castle and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
- **Gingerbread**: Sample Nuremberg's famous gingerbread, a holiday favorite.
- **Toy Museum**: Discover the history of toys and enjoy festive exhibits.

## 12. Aspen, USA

Aspen offers a luxurious and snowy Christmas escape in the heart of the Rocky Mountains.

- **Skiing**: Hit the slopes at one of Aspen's world-class ski resorts.
- **Snowmass Village**: Enjoy holiday events and activities in this charming mountain village.
- **Après-Ski**: Relax with a warm drink by the fire after a day on the slopes.
- **Aspen Art Museum**: Explore contemporary art exhibits in a stunning architectural setting.

## 13. Bruges, Belgium

Bruges is a fairy-tale city that comes alive with festive cheer during the Christmas season.

- **Bruges Christmas Market**: Wander through the market in the historic city center, surrounded by medieval architecture.
- **Ice Sculpture Festival**: Marvel at intricate ice sculptures created by artists from around the world.
- **Canal Cruise**: Take a boat tour through the city's picturesque canals.
- **Belgian Chocolate**: Indulge in delicious Belgian chocolates and other sweet treats.

## 14. Salzburg, Austria

Salzburg, the birthplace of Mozart, offers a charming and musical Christmas experience.

- **Salzburg Christmas Market**: Explore the market in the historic Residenzplatz and Domplatz.
- **Silent Night Chapel**: Visit the chapel where the famous carol "Silent Night" was first performed.
- **Hohensalzburg Fortress**: Enjoy panoramic views of the city from this historic fortress.
- **Mozart's Birthplace**: Tour the museum dedicated to the famous composer.

## 15. Cape Town, South Africa

For a unique and sunny Christmas, Cape Town offers stunning landscapes and vibrant culture.

- **Table Mountain**: Take a cable car ride to the top for breathtaking views of the city.
- **V&A Waterfront**: Enjoy holiday shopping and festive events at this popular destination.
- **Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens**: Attend a Christmas concert in the beautiful gardens.
- **Cape Winelands**: Explore the nearby wine regions and enjoy wine tastings.

## Conclusion

Whether you prefer a snowy retreat or a sun-soaked escape, these vacation places offer unforgettable Christmas experiences. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of Lapland, each destination brings its own unique charm and festive spirit. As you plan your holiday getaway, consider these magical locations to make your Christmas truly special.
