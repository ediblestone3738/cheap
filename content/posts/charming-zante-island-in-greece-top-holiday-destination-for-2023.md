Located in the Ionian Sea, just a short distance from the coast of mainland Greece, lies the charming island of Zante, also known as Zakynthos. Part of the Ionian Islands, Zante is a popular tourist destination due to its stunning natural beauty, crystal clear waters, and rich history. With its long list of attractions and activities, Zante is set to be one of the top holiday destinations for 2023.

Zante has something for everyone- picturesque beaches, historic landmarks, traditional villages, and a vibrant nightlife. Whether you’re looking to relax, explore or party, Zante is the perfect destination for your next getaway.

FAQs:

Q: How to get to Zante?
A: You can fly directly to Zakynthos International Airport which is located on the southeastern part of the island. Alternatively, you can take a ferry from Greece’s main port of Piraeus, or from the ports of Killini and Kyllini in the Peloponnese.

Q: What is the best time to visit Zante?
A: The best time to visit Zante is during the months of May to October, where the weather is warm and sunny, with temperatures ranging between 25-30°C.

Q: What currency is used in Zante?
A: Euro (€)

Q: What are the famous traditional products and specialties of Zante?
A: Olive oil, wine, honey, fruits, vegetables, nougat cake, mandolato

Q: What is Navagio Beach?
A: Navagio Beach, also known as Shipwreck Beach, is a secluded cove on the northwestern part of the island. It is one of the most popular attractions of Zante and is famous for its turquoise waters and white sandy beach, which is located at the base of towering limestone cliffs.

Top 10 tourist attractions of Zante:

1) Navagio Beach: As mentioned above, this is one of the most popular attractions of Zante. The beach can only be accessed by boat, but visitors can take in the stunning views from an observation deck located a short distance away.

2) Keri Caves: Located on the southwestern part of the island, the Keri Caves are a must-see attraction for anyone visiting Zante. Visitors can explore the caves by boat or take a walking tour.

3) Laganas Beach: This is the largest beach on the island and is a popular destination for party-goers due to its numerous bars and nightclubs located nearby. However, Laganas is also a great spot for families, as it offers plenty of sun loungers and water sports activities.

4) Marathonisi Island: Located just a short boat ride away from Laganas, Marathonisi Island is a small, uninhabited island that is famous for its crystal clear waters, sea turtles, and natural beauty.

5) Zakynthos Town: The capital of the island, Zakynthos Town, is a charming place to explore. The town features a mix of Venetian and British architecture, and visitors can wander the cobblestone streets, visit museums, shops and restaurants.

6) Alykanas Beach: Located on the northwestern part of the island, Alykanas is a quieter alternative to Laganas Beach. The beach is surrounded by lush greenery and features plenty of water sports activities and beach bars.

7) Blue Caves: Located on the northern part of the island, the Blue Caves are a series of impressive sea caves that are only accessible by boat. Visitors can explore the caves and take a dip in the crystal blue waters.

8) Porto Limnionas: This is a stunning cove located in the western part of the island that is surrounded by towering cliffs. The turquoise waters are perfect for swimming and snorkeling and there are plenty of restaurants and beach bars located nearby.

9) Gerakas Beach: Located on the southeastern part of the island, Gerakas Beach is a beautiful, sandy beach that is ideal for families. The beach is part of the Zakynthos Marine Park, which is home to the Caretta Caretta sea turtle.

10) Agios Sostis: A small island located just a short distance from Laganas. Visitors can access the island by walking over a narrow bridge. The island features beautiful scenery and a few restaurants and bars.

In conclusion, Zante is a charming island that offers something for everyone. With its beautiful beaches, natural wonders, and rich history, Zante is set to be one of the top holiday destinations for 2023. So, pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable adventure!
