The History and Evolution of Edinburgh Zoo

Edinburgh Zoo, nestled at the foot of the picturesque Corstorphine Hill, holds a storied past that stretches back to 1913. With the ambitious aim of exhibiting wildlife from around the globe, it quickly gained popularity among locals and tourists alike.
