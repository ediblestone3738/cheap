Where is the Cheapest Place to Have a Holiday?

Are you looking for a great holiday destination without breaking the bank? Then you’ve come to the right place! We’ve compiled a list of the 13 cheapest places to go in 2022, so you can start planning your dream vacation without worrying about your budget.

Paphos, Cyprus

Paphos, Cyprus is a great destination for a budget-friendly holiday. With its stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and Mediterranean climate, it’s no wonder why this city is a popular tourist destination. The cost of living in Paphos is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Tombs of the Kings, the Paphos Archaeological Park, and the Paphos Zoo.

Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town, South Africa is another great destination for a cheap holiday. This vibrant city is known for its stunning beaches, iconic Table Mountain, and vibrant culture. The cost of living in Cape Town is quite low, making it an ideal destination for those looking to save money. Additionally, the city is home to many attractions, such as the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, the Cape of Good Hope, and the Robben Island Museum.
