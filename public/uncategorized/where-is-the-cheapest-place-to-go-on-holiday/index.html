Where is the Cheapest Place to Go on Holiday?

Are you looking for a great holiday destination that won’t break the bank? Look no further! There are plenty of amazing destinations around the world that offer great value for money. Here are some of the best and cheapest places to go on holiday.

Thailand

Thailand is a popular destination for backpackers and holidaymakers alike. It offers idyllic islands, a rich culture, beach-huts, tantalising cuisine and plenty of adventures. All of this is available at incredibly low prices. Thailand is the perfect destination for those looking for a budget-friendly holiday. From the bustling cities of Bangkok and Chiang Mai to the beautiful beaches of Koh Samui and Phuket, Thailand has something for everyone.

South Africa

South Africa is a great destination for budget travellers. It offers stunning scenery, vibrant cities, world-class beaches and plenty of adventure activities. South Africa is also home to some of the best wildlife viewing in the world. From the Kruger National Park to the Garden Route, there are plenty of opportunities to spot some of the country’s incredible wildlife.

Vietnam

Vietnam is an amazing destination for budget travellers. From the bustling cities of Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City to the stunning beaches of Nha Trang and Phu Quoc, there is something for everyone. Vietnam is also home to some of the best street food in the world. You can sample delicious dishes for just a few dollars.

Uruguay

Uruguay is a great destination for budget travellers. It offers stunning beaches, vibrant cities, and plenty of adventure activities. From the colonial city of Colonia del Sacramento to the stunning beaches of Punta del Este, Uruguay has something for everyone.

Cuba

Cuba is a great destination for budget travellers. It offers stunning beaches, vibrant cities, and plenty of adventure activities. From the colonial city of Havana to the stunning beaches of Varadero, Cuba has something for everyone.

Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a great destination for budget travellers. It offers stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and plenty of adventure activities. From the cobbled streets of the Old Town to the stunning Charles Bridge, Prague has something for everyone.

Greece

Greece is a great destination for budget travellers. It offers stunning beaches, vibrant cities, and plenty of adventure activities. From the stunning islands of Santorini and Mykonos to the vibrant cities of Athens and Thessaloniki, Greece has something for everyone.

Guatemala

Guatemala is a great destination for budget travellers. It offers stunning scenery, vibrant cities, and plenty of adventure activities. From the ancient Mayan ruins of Tikal to the stunning Lake Atitlan, Guatemala has something for everyone.

No matter what your budget is, there is a great holiday destination for you. From Thailand to Guatemala, there are plenty of amazing and affordable destinations to choose from. So what are you waiting for? Start planning your next holiday today!
