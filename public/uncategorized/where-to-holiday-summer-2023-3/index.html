Where to Holiday Summer 2023?

Summer is the perfect time to get away and enjoy a well-deserved break. With the summer of 2023 fast approaching, it’s time to start planning your holiday. There are so many amazing destinations to choose from, so it can be hard to decide where to go. To help you out, we’ve compiled a list of the top summer holidays in July 2023/2024.

Corfu, Greece

Corfu is a stunning Greek island located in the Ionian Sea. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, lush green landscapes, and vibrant nightlife. Corfu is a great destination for families, couples, and solo travellers alike. During the summer months, the island is alive with activity, with plenty of water sports, beach bars, and restaurants to explore. There are also plenty of historical sites to visit, such as the Old Town of Corfu and the Achilleion Palace.

Costa Blanca, Spain

The Costa Blanca is a stunning stretch of coastline in the south-eastern region of Spain. It’s known for its white sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and picturesque villages. During the summer months, the Costa Blanca is a popular destination for sun-seekers and beach-lovers. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as water sports, boat trips, and hiking. The Costa Blanca is also home to some of Spain’s best golf courses, so it’s a great destination for golfers.

Costa del Sol, Spain

The Costa del Sol is a popular holiday destination located in the south of Spain. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and world-class golf courses. During the summer months, the Costa del Sol is a great destination for sun-seekers and beach-lovers. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as water sports, boat trips, and hiking. The Costa del Sol is also home to some of Spain’s best golf courses, so it’s a great destination for golfers.

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a stunning Indonesian island located in the Indian Ocean. It’s known for its lush green landscapes, stunning beaches, and vibrant culture. During the summer months, Bali is a great destination for families, couples, and solo travellers alike. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as surfing, snorkelling, and diving. Bali is also home to some of the world’s best yoga retreats, so it’s a great destination for yogis.

California, USA

California is a stunning US state located on the west coast. It’s known for its stunning beaches, vibrant cities, and world-famous attractions. During the summer months, California is a great destination for families, couples, and solo travellers alike. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as surfing, hiking, and wine tasting. California is also home to some of the world’s best theme parks, so it’s a great destination for thrill-seekers.

Ibiza, Balearic Islands, Spain

Ibiza is a stunning Spanish island located in the Balearic Islands. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and world-famous clubs. During the summer months, Ibiza is a great destination for party-goers and music-lovers. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as boat trips, water sports, and beach bars. Ibiza is also home to some of the world’s best clubs, so it’s a great destination for clubbers.

Italian Lakes, Italy

The Italian Lakes are a stunning region located in northern Italy. It’s known for its beautiful lakes, stunning scenery, and world-class cuisine. During the summer months, the Italian Lakes are a great destination for families, couples, and solo travellers alike. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as boat trips, water sports, and hiking. The Italian Lakes are also home to some of the world’s best restaurants, so it’s a great destination for foodies.

French Riviera, France

The French Riviera is a stunning stretch of coastline located in the south of France. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant cities, and world-famous attractions. During the summer months, the French Riviera is a great destination for families, couples, and solo travellers alike. There are plenty of activities to enjoy, such as boat trips, water sports, and beach bars. The French Riviera is also home to some of the world’s best casinos, so it’s a great destination for gamblers.

No matter where you decide to go for your summer holiday in 2023, you’re sure to have an amazing time. With so many amazing destinations to choose from, you’re sure to find the perfect holiday for you. So start planning your summer holiday now and get ready for an unforgettable experience.
