Where Do Millionaires Go on Holiday?

When it comes to luxury holidays, millionaires have a range of exclusive destinations to choose from. From the Maldives to Rwanda, these exclusive holiday spots offer an unparalleled level of luxury and relaxation. Here, we take a look at some of the most popular millionaire holiday destinations, as well as the latest addition to the scene.

The Palm, Dubai

Dubai is known for its opulence and luxury, and The Palm is no exception. This exclusive resort is situated on a man-made island, offering guests the ultimate in relaxation and luxury. With its private beaches, world-class restaurants, and stunning views, The Palm is the perfect destination for those looking for a luxurious getaway.

Reethi Rah, Maldives

The Maldives is one of the most sought-after holiday destinations for millionaires. Reethi Rah is one of the most exclusive resorts in the country, offering guests a truly unique experience. With its private beaches, stunning villas, and world-class restaurants, Reethi Rah is the perfect place to relax and unwind.

Gorilla’s Nest, Rwanda

For those looking for a unique holiday experience, Gorilla’s Nest in Rwanda is the perfect destination. This exclusive resort offers guests the chance to experience the beauty of Rwanda’s rainforest, as well as the opportunity to observe the endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat.

One&Only Portonovi, Montenegro

2020 sees the launch of One&Only Portonovi, in Montenegro. This exclusive resort is situated on the stunning Bay of Kotor, offering guests unparalleled views of the Adriatic Sea. With its world-class restaurants, luxurious villas, and private beaches, One&Only Portonovi is the perfect destination for those looking for a truly luxurious holiday experience.

Conclusion

For millionaires looking for a luxurious holiday experience, there are a range of exclusive destinations to choose from. From the Maldives to Rwanda, these destinations offer an unparalleled level of luxury and relaxation. The latest addition to the scene is One&Only Portonovi, in Montenegro, which is sure to be a popular destination for those looking for a truly unique holiday experience.
