Where do Most Brits Go on Holiday?

As the world slowly begins to open up again, many of us are looking forward to taking a much-needed holiday. For Brits, the question of where to go is an important one. To help you decide, we’ve compiled a list of the 10 most popular holiday destinations for Brits.

Spain

Spain has long been a favourite of British holidaymakers, and it remains the UK’s number one holiday destination. With its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and excellent food, it’s no wonder that Spain is so popular. From the Costa del Sol to the Balearic Islands, there’s something for everyone in Spain.

France

France is another popular destination for British holidaymakers. With its stunning scenery, delicious cuisine, and rich culture, it’s no wonder that France is so popular. From the stunning beaches of the Cote d’Azur to the snow-capped peaks of the Alps, France has something for everyone.

Greece is another popular destination for British holidaymakers. With its stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and delicious cuisine, it’s no wonder that Greece is so popular. From the beautiful islands of the Aegean Sea to the bustling streets of Athens, Greece has something for everyone.

Turkey is another popular destination for British holidaymakers. With its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine, it’s no wonder that Turkey is so popular. From the beautiful beaches of the Mediterranean to the bustling streets of Istanbul, Turkey has something for everyone.
