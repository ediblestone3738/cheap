Where is the Cheapest Place to Fly to in Europe This Summer?

Are you looking for the best deals on flights to Europe this summer? If so, you’re in luck. Europe is full of amazing destinations, and with the right research, you can find great deals on flights to get you there. One of the best places to fly to in Europe this summer is Reykjavik, Iceland.

Why Reykjavik, Iceland?

Reykjavik, Iceland is a great destination for those looking for a budget-friendly vacation in Europe. For most people in the US, this is the cheapest European city to fly to. Depending on the time of year and where you’re flying from, you can expect to pay anywhere between $260-$550 roundtrip on Icelandair.

What to Do in Reykjavik

Reykjavik is a great destination for those looking for a mix of culture, nature, and adventure. There are plenty of activities to keep you busy, from exploring the city’s vibrant nightlife to taking in the breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

One of the best things to do in Reykjavik is to explore the city’s many museums and galleries. The National Museum of Iceland is a great place to start, as it offers a comprehensive overview of the country’s history and culture. Other popular museums include the Reykjavik Art Museum, the Saga Museum, and the Icelandic Phallological Museum.

If you’re looking for outdoor activities, Reykjavik has plenty to offer. From whale watching to horseback riding, there are plenty of ways to explore the city’s stunning natural landscape. The city is also home to a number of thermal pools, which are perfect for relaxing after a long day of sightseeing.

Where to Stay in Reykjavik

Reykjavik is home to a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. If you’re looking for a more affordable option, there are plenty of hostels and guesthouses to choose from. For those looking for a more luxurious stay, there are plenty of high-end hotels in the city, as well as a number of Airbnb options.

When to Visit Reykjavik

The best time to visit Reykjavik is during the summer months, from June to August. During this time, the days are long and the weather is mild, making it the perfect time to explore the city and its surroundings.

Conclusion

Reykjavik, Iceland is a great destination for those looking for a budget-friendly vacation in Europe. With its vibrant culture, stunning natural landscape, and wide range of accommodation options, it’s the perfect place to visit this summer. With the right research, you can find great deals on flights to get you there. So what are you waiting for? Book your trip to Reykjavik today!
