From the bustling cities of Bangkok and Chiang Mai to the stunning beaches of Koh Samui and Phuket, there’s something for everyone. Accommodation is cheap, food is plentiful, and there are plenty of activities to keep you busy. Plus, you can easily find flights to Thailand from many countries around the world.
