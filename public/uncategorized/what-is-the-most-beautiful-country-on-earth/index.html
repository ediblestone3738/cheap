What is the Most Beautiful Country on Earth?

When it comes to finding the most beautiful country on earth, there is no shortage of contenders. When it comes to beauty, New Zealand is hard to beat. Ranked number one in scenic beauty, this small country is home to some of the most stunning landscapes in the world. From the snow-capped peaks of the Southern Alps to the lush rainforests of the North Island, New Zealand is a paradise for nature lovers.
