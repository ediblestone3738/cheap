5 Cheap European Beach Holidays for 2022

Are you looking for a cheap beach holiday in Europe for 2022? With the world slowly reopening after the pandemic, now is the perfect time to start planning your next beach getaway. From sunny Bulgaria to the beautiful Algarve in Portugal, here are five of the best and cheapest beach holidays in Europe for 2022.

Sunny Beach, Bulgaria

Sunny Beach is a popular holiday destination on the Black Sea coast of Bulgaria. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, lively nightlife, and excellent value for money. In fact, the cost of holiday essentials in Sunny Beach is just £30.68, making it one of the cheapest beach holidays in Europe.

Marmaris, Turkey

Marmaris is a stunning beach resort in southwestern Turkey. It’s a great choice for those looking for a mix of relaxation and adventure, with plenty of activities such as diving, sailing, and horse riding. The cost of holiday essentials in Marmaris is £44.15, making it a great value beach holiday.

Algarve, Portugal

The Algarve is one of the most popular beach holiday destinations in Europe. With its stunning coastline, beautiful towns, and excellent food, it’s no wonder why. The cost of holiday essentials in the Algarve is £49.87, making it a great choice for those looking for a budget-friendly beach holiday.

Costa del Sol, Spain

The Costa del Sol is one of the most popular beach holiday destinations in Spain. With its beautiful beaches, lively nightlife, and excellent value for money, it’s a great choice for those looking for a budget-friendly beach holiday. The cost of holiday essentials in the Costa del Sol is £51.63, making it a great choice for those looking for a cheap beach holiday in Europe.

Paphos, Cyprus

Paphos is a stunning beach resort in southwestern Cyprus. It’s a great choice for those looking for a mix of relaxation and adventure, with plenty of activities such as diving, sailing, and horse riding. The cost of holiday essentials in Paphos is £53.11, making it a great value beach holiday.

So, if you’re looking for a cheap beach holiday in Europe for 2022, these five destinations are all great choices. From the stunning beaches of Bulgaria to the beautiful Algarve in Portugal, you’re sure to find the perfect beach holiday for you.

Happy travels!
