Which European Country is Cheapest?

Living in Europe is a dream for many people, but the cost of living can be a deterrent. Fortunately, there are several European countries that offer a great quality of life at an affordable price. Here is a list of the top six cheapest European countries for expats.

Portugal

Portugal is a great choice for expats looking for an affordable place to live. This country is famous for its good food, beautiful beaches, a relaxed lifestyle, and affordable living. Portugal has a low cost of living, with a monthly budget of around $1,500 for a single person. Rent is also quite affordable, with an average of $500 per month for a one-bedroom apartment in the city center.
