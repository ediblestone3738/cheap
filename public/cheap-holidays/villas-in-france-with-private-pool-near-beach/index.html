FAQs

1. Are there any restrictions in accessing the beach?

Guests can access the beach easily from most villas. However, it is important to check with the villa owner since some beaches in France are private, and charges may apply.

2. What are the general rates of rental villas in France?

The usual rate for villas in France starts from €2000 a week to over €40,000 for high-end villas.

3. Is breakfast included in the rental of private villas?

Most villas do not include breakfast in the rental price. However, guests can request for a cook or chef who can prepare breakfast and other meals for an additional fee.

4. Do villas in France allow pets?

Most villas do not allow pets, and it is essential to check with the villa owner before booking.

5. Is it easy to reach villas in France?

France has an extensive network of public transportation, including trains and buses, making it easier for guests to travel to villas. Private transportation can also be arranged for guests upon request.
