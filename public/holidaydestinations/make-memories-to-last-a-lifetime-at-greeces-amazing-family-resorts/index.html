Make Memories to Last a Lifetime at Greece's Amazing Family Resorts

Apr 5, 2023

[ad_1] Greece is a popular destination for families for a good reason; it is a treasure trove of ancient wonders, stunning scenery, crystal-clear waters, delectable cuisine, and fun-filled family vacation experiences. Families are not only attracted to Greece for its rich cultural history but also for its beautiful sea-view resorts that provide the perfect backdrop for a memorable family vacation. For any family looking to unwind, relax, and make beautiful memories, Greece has an amazing array of hotels and resorts. In this article, we will highlight the best family resorts that will make your trip unforgettable.

Top Family Resorts in Greece

1. Costa Navarino, Messinia Costa Navarino is not just a hotel but a luxurious family resort, which has won many international awards. Set in a beautiful location, it offers guests the chance to experience Greek hospitality and indulge in outdoor activities such as golf, tennis, and cycling.

2. Sani Resort, Halkidiki Sani Resort is located next to a lush green forest that compliments the beautiful sea view. It has high-end facilities, including spas and a range of restaurants. Its beautiful beach is perfect for families to unwind and relax.

3. Domes of Elounda, Crete Domes of Elounda is a fantastic family retreat located in Crete. It offers a refreshing blend of modern and ancient Greece, with its stunning beach views and beautiful surroundings.

4. Ikos Olivia, Halkidiki Ikos Olivia offers a five-star experience with a concierge service that caters to your every need. Its family-friendly approach is a breath of fresh air, making it a perfect vacation spot for parents and kids.

5. St Nicolas Bay Resort, Crete St Nicolas Bay Resort offers magnificent sea views and a plethora of activities to keep families entertained throughout their stay. With its sophisticated and luxurious ambiance, it is a great choice for those looking for high-end family resorts.

6. The Westin Resort, Costa Navarino The Westin Resort, Costa Navarino offers a unique family vacation experience with its Adventure Park that keeps kids engaged throughout their stay. The resort’s facilities range from beautifying spas to seven diverse dining experiences that offer a wide range of flavors to suit all taste buds.

7. Eagles Palace, Halkidiki Eagles Palace is a perfect example of a family-friendly hotel that caters to all age groups. The hotel offers a kids’ club that entertains children between 4 and 12 years of age, and babysitting services for parents who want to explore Halkidiki.

8. Port Palace, Corfu Port Palace is a beachfront resort that offers great views, modern rooms, and a delightful dining experience. Its facilities include a kids’ club and playground, making it an excellent vacation spot for families with children.

9. Santo Maris Oia Luxury Suites and Spa, Santorini Santo Maris Oia Luxury Suites and Spa offer the perfect family vacation experience in Santorini with beautiful views of the Aegean Sea. The resort’s architecture and design provide a beautiful blend of modern and traditional styles that will enchant your senses.

10. Danai Beach Resort, Halkidiki Danai Beach Resort is a member of the Luxury Collection of Hotels and Resorts. It offers a range of accommodation options, including lofts, suites, and villas, all overlooking the beautiful sea view. The resort’s facilities include a kids’ club, tennis court, and excellent dining options, all perfect for families.

FAQs

1. What is the best time to visit Greece? The best time to visit Greece is during the spring months, from April to June, and in the fall months, from September to November. The weather is great, and there are fewer tourists during these months.

2. Is traveling to Greece with kids safe? Yes, it is safe to travel to Greece with kids, and most family-friendly resorts provide safe and secure environments for both adults and children.

3. What are some of the best activities to do with kids in Greece? There are many great activities to do with kids in Greece, such as visiting historical sites, hiking, swimming, and indulging in outdoor sports like cycling and tennis.

4. Is Greece an expensive travel destination? While some areas in Greece can be expensive, there are many affordable family resorts available that cater to all budgets.

5. What are the best family resorts in Greece? The best family resorts in Greece include Costa Navarino, Sani Resort, Domes of Elounda, Ikos Olivia, St Nicolas Bay Resort, The Westin Resort, Eagles Palace, Port Palace, Santo Maris Oia Luxury Suites and Spa, and Danai Beach Resort.
