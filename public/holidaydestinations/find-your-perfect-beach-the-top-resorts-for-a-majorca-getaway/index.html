Located off the eastern coast of Spain is the beautiful Mediterranean island of Majorca. This Spanish paradise boasts stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, scenic mountain ranges, and a range of world-class resorts scattered across the island.

For anyone seeking a relaxing beach holiday, Majorca is the perfect place to visit. The island has a wide range of high-end resorts, each offering unique accommodations, amenities, and experiences. So, whether you are looking for a family-friendly holiday or a romantic getaway, Majorca has it all. In this article, we will explore some of the top resorts in Majorca, perfect for a memorable beach holiday.

1. Belmond La Residencia

The Belmond La Residencia is a luxurious hotel situated on the idyllic Mediterranean island of Majorca, situated in the mountainous north-west region of Deia.

The resort boasts a historic and vintage charm, offering guests an authentic Mediterranean escape. It features elegant rooms, suites and villas, all designed with a modern twist and offering stunning panoramic views of the mountain ranges and coastline. With two outdoor swimming pools, tennis courts, a gym, a first-class spa, and numerous dining options, there is never a dull moment at Belmond La Residencia.

2. Hotel Illa d’Or

Located in the charming town of Puerto Pollensa, Hotel Illa d’Or is an elegant, family-run hotel situated on a scenic peninsula overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

The hotel boasts 120 rooms and suites, all of which are extremely comfortable and beautifully designed to maximize the stunning views of the sea or gardens. The resort has a private beach, swimming pool, and numerous dining options. It is also known for its award-winning cuisine and outstanding service.

3. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa

Jumeirah Port Soller is a 5-star hotel situated on the cliffside of Soller Bay, one of the most beautiful bays on the island of Majorca.

The resort boasts luxurious accommodations and amenities and is perfect for anyone looking for a more exclusive and private getaway. It features exceptional facilities such as a fine dining restaurant, an infinity pool, a private spa, and a host of activities such as mountain biking, kayaking, and hiking.

4. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort

The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a luxurious 5-star resort set in a stunning location overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

The resort boasts an elegant and modern design, featuring 130 spacious guest rooms and suites, all featuring private balconies with stunning views of the sea. The resort also has a luxurious spa, an infinity pool, and numerous dining options, ensuring guests are never bored.

5. Park Hyatt Mallorca

The Park Hyatt Mallorca is a 5-star resort located in the peaceful Canyamel Valley, nestled between stunning beaches, pristine forests, and the stunning Llevant Mountains.

The resort boasts an authentic Mallorcan village design, featuring traditional stone walls, terracotta roofs, and lush gardens. It features 142 guest rooms, suites, and villas, all featuring private outdoor terraces or balconies that offer breathtaking views of the Mediterranean or the surrounding hills. Park Hyatt Mallorca also includes a luxury spa, a fitness center, and numerous dining options.

FAQs:

Q. What is the best time to visit Majorca?

A. The best time to visit Majorca is between April and September, when the weather is sunny and warm.

Q. Is it easy to get around Majorca?

A. Yes, it’s easy to get around Majorca. Renting a car is a great way to explore the island, and there are also local buses and taxis available.

Q. What is the currency of Majorca?

A. The currency of Majorca is the Euro.

Q. What language is spoken in Majorca?

A. The official languages of Majorca are Spanish and Catalan.

Q. How safe is Majorca for tourists?

A. Majorca is a very safe tourist destination, with low crime rates and a friendly local community.
