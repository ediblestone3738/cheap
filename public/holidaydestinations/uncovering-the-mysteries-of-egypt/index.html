Q: What is the best time to visit Egypt?

A: The best time to visit Egypt is from October to April. During this time, temperatures are milder and rainfall is minimal.

Q: What is the currency used in Egypt?

A: The currency used in Egypt is the Egyptian pound (EGP).

Q: What is the best way to get around Egypt?

A: The best way to get around Egypt is by train. The country has an extensive railway network that connects major cities and tourist attractions.

Q: What type of food is served in Egypt?

A: Egyptian cuisine is a mix of Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, and African flavors. Popular dishes include koshari (a combination of lentils, rice, and macaroni), ful medames (a dish of boiled fava beans), and kofta (grilled meatballs).
