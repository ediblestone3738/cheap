A Family Oasis in Greece: The Best Resorts to Create Unforgettable Memories

Apr 12, 2023

[ad_1] Greece is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and beautiful beaches. It is also a popular destination for families who want to create unforgettable memories. A family oasis in Greece can provide everything that a family needs for a fun-filled and relaxing vacation. From luxurious resorts to outdoor activities, Greece has it all. Here are the best resorts to create unforgettable memories in Greece, along with information on top tourist attractions and frequently asked questions.

Best Resorts in Greece for Families

1. Sani Resort – Located in Halkidiki, the Sani Resort is a luxury resort that has everything a family could need. From beautiful beaches to top-notch restaurants, this resort has it all. Kids will love the Mini Club, which offers activities such as tennis, art classes, and cooking lessons. The resort also has a teen club for older kids.

2. Ikos Resorts – With four locations in Greece, Ikos Resorts offers luxury accommodations that are perfect for families. There are plenty of activities for kids, including sports, dance classes, and cooking lessons. Parents can indulge in a spa treatment or a gourmet meal while the kids are entertained.

3. Domes of Elounda – Located in Crete, the Domes of Elounda is a luxurious resort that caters to families. Kids will love the adventure park, which includes zip-lining and rock climbing. There are also plenty of activities for adults such as yoga classes and wine tastings.

4. Portogrande – Located on the island of Lemnos, Portogrande is a family-friendly resort that offers activities for kids of all ages. There are organized activities for younger kids, while older kids can enjoy water sports and other outdoor activities.

5. Sivota Diamond Spa Resort – Located in the Epirus region of Greece, the Sivota Diamond Spa Resort offers luxurious accommodations and plenty of activities for families. Kids will love the water park, while parents can relax in the spa or enjoy a gourmet meal.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Greece for Families

1. Acropolis – Located in Athens, the Acropolis is a must-see attraction for families. This ancient citadel includes the iconic Parthenon and other historic monuments.

2. Santorini – This beautiful island is known for its stunning views and beautiful sunsets. Families can enjoy swimming and water sports, as well as exploring the island’s ancient ruins.

3. Mykonos – This island is known for its vibrant nightlife and beautiful beaches. Families can enjoy shopping, dining, and exploring the island’s historic landmarks.

4. Delphi – This ancient site is home to the famous oracle of Apollo. Families can explore the ruins and learn about the history of ancient Greece.

5. Meteora – This stunning rock formation is home to six monasteries that were built in the 14th century. Families can explore the monasteries and enjoy the stunning views.

6. Corfu – This beautiful island is known for its beaches and historic landmarks. Families can enjoy swimming, hiking, and exploring the island’s ancient ruins.

7. Olympia – This ancient site is home to the original Olympic Games. Families can explore the ruins and learn about the history of the games.

8. Crete – This island is known for its beautiful beaches and ancient ruins. Families can enjoy swimming, water sports, and exploring the island’s ancient landmarks.

9. Rhodes – This island is known for its beautiful beaches and historic landmarks. Families can enjoy swimming, hiking, and exploring the island’s ancient ruins.

10. Zakynthos – This beautiful island is known for its stunning beaches and beautiful landscapes. Families can enjoy swimming, hiking, and exploring the island’s historic landmarks.

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Greece for families?

A: The best time to visit Greece is during the spring or fall when the weather is mild and crowds are low.

Q: What is the currency in Greece?

A: The currency in Greece is the Euro.

Q: Do I need a visa to visit Greece?

A: Visitors from the US and most European countries do not need a visa to visit Greece for stays up to 90 days.

Q: Is Greece a safe country for families?

A: Greece is generally a safe country for families. However, as with any destination, it is important to use common sense and take precautions to stay safe.

Q: What are some popular activities for families in Greece?

A: Popular activities for families in Greece include swimming, hiking, exploring ancient ruins, and enjoying water sports.

By visiting the best resorts and top tourist attractions, families can experience the beauty and culture of this incredible country.
