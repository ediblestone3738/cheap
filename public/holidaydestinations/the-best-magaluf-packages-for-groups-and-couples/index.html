FAQs

  1. What is the best time to visit Magaluf?

The best time to visit Magaluf is between May and September when the weather is warm and dry, and the resort is buzzing with tourists.

  1. Is Magaluf safe?

Magaluf is generally safe, but visitors should take precautions to avoid pickpocketing and avoid drinking to excess, which can lead to risky behavior.

  1. What is the legal drinking age in Magaluf?

The legal drinking age in Magaluf is 18.

  1. Is Magaluf only for partygoers?

No, Magaluf offers a range of activities and accommodations suitable for families, couples, and solo travelers.

  1. Can I visit Magaluf in the offseason?

Yes, you can visit Magaluf in the offseason, but many of the bars, clubs, and restaurants may be closed, and the resort may be quieter.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Magaluf

  1. Katmandu Park Located in the heart of Magaluf, Katmandu Park is an amusement park that offers a range of attractions, from 4D rides to virtual reality experiences and live shows.

  2. Western Water Park Western Water Park is a water park with thrilling slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers. The park has a Western-themed atmosphere, with cowboys, saloons, and even a mini ranch for kids.

  3. Palma Nova Beach Palma Nova Beach is located just a short walk from Magaluf and offers an array of water sports, parasailing, and boat trips.

  4. BCM Planet Dance BCM Planet Dance is known as the biggest nightclub in Magaluf, with top DJs from all around the world playing to a packed house.

  5. House of Katmandu House of Katmandu is an interactive museum in Magaluf, where visitors can explore the world of science fiction and fantasy through virtual reality and immersive exhibitions.

  6. Golf Fantasia Golf Fantasia is a mini-golf course with three 18-hole courses, each designed around a different theme: Garden, Jurassic, and Fantasia.

  7. Cova de’s Coloms Located just outside Magaluf, Cova de’s Coloms is a natural cave system that’s been open to visitors since the 1950s.

  8. Marineland Mallorca Marineland Mallorca is an aquatic park featuring a dolphin show, sea lion show, and a range of marine animals.

  9. Pirates Adventure Dinner Show The Pirates Adventure Show is a dinner theater that combines acrobatics, pyrotechnics, and a swashbuckling storyline.
