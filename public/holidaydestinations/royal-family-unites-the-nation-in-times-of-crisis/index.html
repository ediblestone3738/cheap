Top 10 Tourist Attractions

1. Buckingham Palace: The royal residence of Queen Elizabeth II is arguably the most iconic building in London and a must-visit for tourists.

2. Windsor Castle: Home to the British monarchy for over 900 years, Windsor Castle is the oldest and largest inhabited castle in the world.

3. Kensington Palace: Another royal residence, Kensington Palace is famously the childhood home of Queen Victoria and Princess Diana.

4. Buckingham Palace Changing of the Guard: A popular tourist attraction, the Changing of the Guard ceremony takes place outside Buckingham Palace, and features guards marching to military music.

5. The Tower of London: A historic castle located on the north bank of the River Thames, the Tower of London has served as a royal palace and a prison throughout history.

6. The Royal Pavilion: Located in Brighton, the Royal Pavilion was once a seaside retreat for the Prince Regent and features fantastic Indian-inspired architecture.

7. The Palace of Holyroodhouse: The official Scottish residence of the British monarch, the Palace of Holyroodhouse is located at the end of the Royal Mile in Edinburgh.

8. Balmoral Castle: Set deep in the Scottish Highlands, Balmoral Castle has been a favorite retreat of the Royal Family since the reign of Queen Victoria.

9. The Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew: A beautiful and expansive botanical garden featuring exotic plants, the Royal Botanic Gardens is a popular tourist destination.

10. The Royal Observatory Greenwich: A UNESCO World Heritage site, the Royal Observatory Greenwich is the official starting point of every time zone in the world and is home to the famous Prime Meridian Line.
