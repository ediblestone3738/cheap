Nestled on the Kassandra Peninsula in Halkidiki, Greece, the luxurious Sani Resort is a hidden gem that offers the perfect blend of tranquillity, natural beauty, and indulgence. Known for its stunning azure waters and pristine golden sand, Sani Resort is a favourite among seasoned travellers who seek a peaceful, rejuvenating vacation. With its gorgeous beaches, five-star amenities, and unparalleled hospitality, Sani Resort is one of Greece’s best-kept secrets.

Accommodation

Sani Resort offers a variety of luxurious accommodation options that cater to all tastes, budgets, and preferences. The resort has seven hotels, including the Sani Beach Hotel, Sani Club, Porto Sani, Sani Dunes, Sani Asterias, Sani Marina, and Sani Maison. Each hotel is uniquely designed and offers a range of rooms, suites, and villas.

The rooms at Sani Resort are equipped with all the modern amenities and offer stunning views of the Aegean Sea or the resort’s lush gardens. The suites are spacious and feature private terraces or balconies. The villas are luxurious and come with their own private pools and gardens, making them perfect for families or groups of friends.

Dining

Sani Resort has a diverse range of dining options that cater to all palates. The resort has over 20 restaurants, cafes, and bars, offering a traditional Greek dining experience, as well as cuisines from around the world. The restaurants at Sani Resort use only the freshest ingredients to create wholesome, healthy meals that are bursting with flavour.

Activities

Sani Resort offers a wide range of activities for guests of all ages. The resort has four private beaches, each with its own unique charm. Guests can enjoy beach volleyball, watersports, swimming, and sunbathing. The resort also has a variety of pools, including an infinity pool, a heated pool, and a lagoon pool.

If you’re looking for something more active, Sani Resort has a range of outdoor activities, including guided hikes and bike tours. The resort also has a world-class tennis academy, a basketball court, and a football pitch.

For those who want to unwind and relax, Sani Resort has a sophisticated spa and wellness centre. The resort offers a range of massages, facials, and body treatments that use only the highest quality products. The spa also has a sauna, a steam room, and a heated indoor pool.
