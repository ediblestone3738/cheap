[ad_1] Getaway to Greece with Your Family: Unmissable Resorts to Visit

Greece is a perfect getaway destination for families who want to enjoy fun-filled activities, explore historical sites, and make unforgettable memories. With its pristine beaches, picturesque landscapes, and ancient architectural masterpieces, Greece has something for everyone. From the stunning views of Santorini to the idyllic beaches of Crete, there are many resorts you can consider when visiting Greece with your family. Here are some of the unmissable resorts you should visit on your Greek family getaway.

1. Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa, Crete

Nestled in a private cove, the Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa offers luxurious accommodation for families. With its private beach, four swimming pools, and a water park for kids, this resort guarantees a memorable vacation. It also has easy access to ancient sites such as Knossos Palace.

2. Blue Palace, A Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Crete

The Blue Palace is a family resort with a breathtaking view of the Aegean Sea. It has world-class amenities such as private pools, a private beach, a fitness center, and spacious family rooms. Children can enjoy playing in the kids’ club or explore the resort’s many activities like water sports or horseback riding.

3. Sani Resort, Halkidiki

Sani Resort is located in a 1000-acre natural reserve and features five-star luxury accommodation suitable for families. With its white-sand beaches, three marinas, water sports activities, and children’s clubs, this resort ensures that your family will never run out of activities to do.

4. Ikos Olivia, Halkidiki

The Ikos Olivia resort is located in a beautiful setting surrounded by olive trees on the Halkidiki peninsula. It features a private beach, four swimming pools, and extensive facilities for children, including a kids’ club, cr√®che, and sports activities.

5. Electra Palace, Rhodes

The Electra Palace is a luxurious family resort located on the beautiful island of Rhodes. With its private beach, several swimming pools, restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues, this resort guarantees a memorable stay for the whole family.

6. Sheraton Rhodes Resort, Rhodes

The Sheraton Rhodes Resort is an excellent choice for families who want to experience Rhodes’ splendor. It has spacious and elegant rooms, a private beach, several swimming pools, and activities that cater to children and adults alike.

7. Costa Navarino, Peloponnese

The Costa Navarino resort is located on the western coast of the Peloponnese peninsula, where you can explore ancient sites with your family. The resort features luxurious accommodation, private beaches, and plenty of activities for children of all ages.

8. Eagles Palace, Halkidiki

Eagles Palace is a five-star resort situated in a stunning location on the Halkidiki peninsula, with a private beach and breathtaking views. It has spacious family rooms, a children’s club, several swimming pools, and activities for the whole family.

9. Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas, Crete

The Daios Cove luxury resort and villas are located on a hillside overlooking the stunning Vathi beach in northeast Crete. With its private beach, five swimming pools, and activities such as sailing, windsurfing, and horseback riding, this resort guarantees an unforgettable vacation.

10. Amanzoe, Argolis

Located in the Argolis region of Greece, the Amanzoe resort offers luxury accommodation and privacy for families. With its private beach, infinity pool, and children’s club, the resort is perfect for a relaxing family vacation.

FAQs

1. How much does it cost to stay in a resort in Greece?

The cost of staying in a resort in Greece depends on the location, time of the year, and level of luxury. Prices range from $200 to $1500 per night.

2. Is it safe to travel to Greece with your family?

Greece is a generally safe destination for families, but it is always best to take precautionary measures, such as avoiding isolated areas and late-night walks.

3. What are the best months to visit Greece with your family?

The best months to visit Greece with your family are between April and June and from September to November, as the weather is mild, and there are fewer crowds.

4. Do resorts in Greece offer all-inclusive packages?

Yes, many resorts in Greece offer all-inclusive packages that include meals, drinks, and activities. These packages can be an affordable and convenient option for families.

5. Can non-European Union citizens enter Greece without a visa?

Citizens of the US, Canada, Australia, and many other countries can enter Greece without a visa if they stay for less than 90 days.
