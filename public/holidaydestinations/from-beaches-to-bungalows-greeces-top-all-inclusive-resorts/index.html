FAQs

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts expensive? A: All-inclusive resorts can be more expensive initially, but they often represent good value for money because you do not have to worry about additional charges for food, drink, or entertainment.

Q: Where can I find all-inclusive resorts in Greece? A: All-inclusive resorts can be found all over Greece, but some of the most popular locations are the Halkidiki peninsula, the island of Crete, and the east coast.

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts suitable for families? A: Yes, all-inclusive resorts are often ideal for families because they offer a range of activities and amenities for adults and children alike.

Q: Do I need to tip at all-inclusive resorts? A: Tipping is not necessary at all-inclusive resorts, but it may be appreciated for exceptional service.
