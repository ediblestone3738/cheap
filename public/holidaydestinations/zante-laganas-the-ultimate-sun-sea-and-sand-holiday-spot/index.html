[ad_1] Zante Laganas: The Ultimate Sun, Sea and Sand Holiday Spot

Zante Laganas, located on the southern end of the Greek island of Zakynthos, is a premier travel destination. Its long sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters and sunny skies make it an ideal location for a sun, sea and sand holiday. Here, we will explore the attractions of Zante Laganas, the must-see spots around the area, and answer some of the most frequently asked questions about this picturesque town.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Zante Laganas

1. Laganas Beach - This 9km sandy beach is the main attraction of the town. Here, you will find plenty of sunbeds and umbrellas for rent, as well as a variety of beach bars and restaurants. It also serves as a nesting site for the endangered loggerhead sea turtles.

2. Keri Caves - These natural rock formations can be found at the southern end of Zakynthos. Visitors can take a boat tour to see the stunning inlets and crystal-clear waters that are perfect for swimming and diving.

3. Marathonisi Island - Located just off the coast of Laganas, Marathonisi is a small, uninhabited island that is home to beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can take a boat tour from Laganas beach to explore the island and enjoy a swim.

4. Zakynthos Marine Park - The park was established to protect the loggerhead sea turtles that nest on the island’s beaches. Visitors can learn about the turtles’ habitat and conservation efforts at the park’s visitor center.

5. Bohali Hill - A small hill overlooking Zakynthos town that provides beautiful views of the island and Ionian Sea. Here, visitors can enjoy a meal or a refreshing drink at one of the hilltop cafes or restaurants.

6. Zakynthos Water Village - The water park located near Sarakinado village is perfect for a fun day out with family or friends. With slides, wave pools and numerous other water attractions, it is a must-see spot for visitors of all ages.

7. Byzantine Museum - An exhibition of Byzantine art and artifacts located in Solomos Square in Zakynthos town. Visitors can explore the permanent collection or attend one of the museum’s temporary exhibitions.

8. Shipwreck Beach (Navagio) - This world-famous beach, located on the northwestern coast of Zakynthos, is known for its stunning scenery and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can take a boat tour or hike down the cliffs to reach the beach.

9. Solomos and Kalvos Museum - The museum, located in Zakynthos town, showcases the works of two famous Greek poets, Dionysios Solomos and Andreas Kalvos. Visitors can explore their lives and literary contributions through exhibits and artifacts.

10. Blue Caves - Located on the northern coastline of Zakynthos, these natural caves are a popular spot for swimming, snorkeling and diving. The blue waters and natural arches create a stunning and unique landscape.

FAQs

Q: How do I get to Zante Laganas?

A: The easiest way to reach Zante Laganas is by flying into Zakynthos International Airport (ZTH) and taking a taxi or rented car to the town.

Q: What is the best time to visit Zante Laganas?

A: The best time to visit Zante Laganas is during the summer months, from June to September, when the weather is warm and sunny with low chances of rain.

Q: Is it safe to swim in the sea around Zante Laganas?

A: Yes, the sea around Zante Laganas is generally safe for swimming, but visitors should be aware of the sea currents and follow the lifeguard’s warnings.

Q: Are there any cultural events or festivals in Zante Laganas?

A: Yes, there are several cultural events and festivals in Zante Laganas throughout the year, including the Zakynthos Carnival, the Greek Easter celebrations, and the Zakynthos International Music Festival.

Q: Can I rent a car or a scooter in Zante Laganas?

A: Yes, there are several car and scooter rental agencies in Zante Laganas that offer a variety of vehicles for rent.

In conclusion, Zante Laganas is the ultimate holiday spot for anyone seeking to bask in the sun, swim in crystal clear waters, and enjoy a relaxed atmosphere. With the above-mentioned top tourist attractions, travelling tips and FAQs, visitors are guaranteed to have a memorable experience on this beautiful Greek island. [ad_2]
