Whether you’re a solo traveler looking for an adventure or couples and families seeking relaxation and fun, Kos all-inclusive holidays offer everything you need for a memorable break.

From its striking beaches to its captivating archaeological sites, the island of Kos will leave you bewitched. Its cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and warm hospitality make it one of the most sought-after destinations for a Greek holiday. Here are the top reasons to visit and book a Kos all-inclusive holiday.

Beaches

Kos has some of the most stunning beaches in Greece. There are over 70 beaches to choose from, each with its unique charm. The island’s west and south coast are the most popular spots to sunbathe, swim and indulge in a plethora of water activities. The beaches of Mastichari, Kardamena, and Tigaki are among the best in Kos. You can take a day trip to the nearby islands of Rhodes, Kalymnos, and Symi if you’d like to explore further.

Ancient sites and history

Kos boasts of rich history and ancient sites that attract millions of visitors every year. It was home to one of the seven wonders of the ancient world- the “Asklepion,” the most renowned healing temple in Greece. In Kos town, you’ll find a vast range of ruins, museums, and galleries that tell the story of the island’s past. It’s a perfect place if you’d like to learn about ancient Greece and its systems.

Outdoor activities

Kos offers a myriad of activities for those looking for an active holiday. You can hike up the hills or mountain bike through the forest trails. Other activities include golfing, horseback riding, and scuba diving. The island’s warm climate and diverse terrain provide an ideal environment for outdoor activities, making it a must-visit destination for adventure enthusiasts.

Nightlife

Kos has an alluring nightlife that draws party animals from all over Europe. From beach bars to lively clubs, there is no shortage of entertainment spots on this island. If you’re looking to experience the Greek party scene, head to Kardamena, where you’ll find some of the liveliest bars and nightclubs.

Cultural experiences

Kos offers a fantastic opportunity to experience the Greek culture firsthand. You can stroll through the streets of Kos town and discover the traditional architecture, taste the local delicacies, and enjoy the island’s rich culture. It’s an experience guaranteed to make you appreciate Greek traditions.
