Majorca is a beautiful and diverse island located in the Mediterranean Sea, which offers visitors an incredible mix of culture, history, beautiful beaches, and stunning landscapes. If you’re planning a luxury vacation to Majorca, you’re in luck. The island is home to some of the best resorts in Europe, offering visitors world-class facilities and services, as well as stunning locations. Here’s a rundown of the top resorts in Majorca that offer the ultimate in luxury living.

Belmond La Residencia

Belmond La Residencia is an exclusive luxury resort located in Deia, one of the most beautiful and picturesque villages on the island. It’s undoubtedly one of the most luxurious resorts in Majorca, boasting beautifully decorated rooms, suites, and private villas with stunning views of the sea and mountains. The resort has two swimming pools, a spa, an art gallery, a tennis court, and two restaurants that serve delicious locally sourced food.

Castillo Hotel Son Vida

Castillo Hotel Son Vida is a five-star luxury resort located in a stunning 13th-century castle situated on a hilltop overlooking the bay of Palma. The resort offers visitors an unrivalled experience, with beautifully decorated rooms, a spa, fitness centre, golf course, and three restaurants, all of which offer excellent service and delicious food.

St. Regis Mardavall Resort

The St. Regis Mardavall Resort is a luxurious five-star resort located in Puerto Portals. The resort is set in tranquil and beautiful surroundings and offers visitors breathtaking views of the Mediterranean. The resort has a beautiful pool, a luxurious spa, a fitness centre, and two restaurants that offer delicious cuisine, including a Michelin-starred restaurant.

Cap Rocat

Cap Rocat is an exceptional luxury resort located in an ancient fortress situated on a cliff overlooking the sea. The resort offers guests historic facilities, including stunning rooms and private villas, some with private pools, which are all beautifully designed and furnished. The resort has a spa, a fitness centre, and two restaurants that serve beautiful Mediterranean cuisine.

Hotel Son Brull

Son Brull Hotel is a beautiful luxury hotel located in the heart of the beautiful island of Majorca. The hotel is set in an old monastery and offers visitors beautiful rooms, a luxurious spa, and beautiful gardens. Visitors to Son Brull can enjoy scenic walks, beautiful beaches, mountains, and world-class restaurants.

FAQs – Most Asked Questions about Luxury Living in Majorca

What is the best time to travel to Majorca? The best time to visit Majorca is from late May to September because the weather is warm and sunny, and you can enjoy the island’s beautiful beaches and outdoor activities.

What is the currency of Majorca? The official currency of Majorca is the Euro.

Do I need a visa to visit Majorca? If you’re an EU citizen, you don’t need a visa to travel to Majorca. If you’re not an EU citizen, you may need a visa, depending on your country of origin.

What is the official language of Majorca? The official language of Majorca is Spanish, but English and German are widely spoken in tourist areas.

What is the best way to get to Majorca? The easiest way to get to Majorca is by plane. The island has one airport, Palma de Mallorca Airport, which is served by many international airlines.
