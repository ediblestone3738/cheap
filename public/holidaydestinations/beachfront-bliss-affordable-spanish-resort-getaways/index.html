Moreover, the resort is situated near some of the most famous tourist attractions in Spain. From historical landmarks to modern museums, there’s something for every type of traveler. Some of the must-visit attractions include:

1. La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona One of the most famous landmarks in Barcelona, the Sagrada Familia is an awe-inspiring cathedral that has been a work in progress since 1882.

2. The Alhambra, Granada A UNESCO World Heritage site, the Alhambra is a fortress that dates back to the ninth century. Its distinctive Moorish architecture and stunning gardens make it an unforgettable attraction.

3. Park Guell, Barcelona Designed by the renowned architect Antoni Gaudi, Park Guell is a whimsical and colorful park that features intricate mosaics and stunning views of the city.

4. The Aqueduct of Segovia Built during the Roman Empire, the Aqueduct of Segovia is a marvel of ancient engineering. It is still standing today, nearly 2,000 years after its construction.

5. The Prado Museum, Madrid One of the most famous museums in the world, the Prado Museum boasts an impressive collection of European art, including works by Velazquez, Goya, and Rubens.

6. The Guggenheim Museum, Bilbao Designed by world-renowned architect Frank Gehry, the Guggenheim Museum is a masterpiece of modern architecture. Its collections focus on contemporary art.

7. The Royal Palace, Madrid The official residence of the Spanish Royal Family, the Palace boasts an impressive collection of art and artifacts, as well as stunning architecture and gardens.

8. The Park of the Sun and the Moon, Menorca Located in the heart of the island of Menorca, this vast park offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and is home to some ancient archaeological treasures.

9. The Aquarius Ecomuseum, Valencia One of the largest aquariums in Europe, the Aquarius Ecomuseum houses over 500 different species of marine life and offers an extraordinary opportunity for visitors to experience the ocean first-hand.

10. San Lorenzo Beach, Gijon San Lorenzo Beach is a stunning, white sand beach that stretches for over two kilometers. Located in the city of Gijon, it is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing.
