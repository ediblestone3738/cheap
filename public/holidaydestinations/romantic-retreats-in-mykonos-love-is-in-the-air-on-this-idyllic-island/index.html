FAQs:

1. How do I get to Mykonos?

There are two main ways to get to Mykonos. The first is by plane, with several airlines offering flights to the island from major European cities. The second is by ferry, with regular ferry services departing from Athens and other Greek islands.

2. What is the best time to visit Mykonos?

The best time to visit Mykonos is between May and September, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, this is also the peak tourist season, so be prepared for crowded beaches and busy streets.

3. What is the currency used in Mykonos?

The currency used in Mykonos is the Euro.

4. Is English widely spoken in Mykonos?

Yes, English is widely spoken in Mykonos, especially in tourist areas.

5. What are some must-see tourist attractions in Mykonos?

Some of the must-see tourist attractions in Mykonos include the iconic windmills, the charming Little Venice, the ancient island of Delos, and the stunning beaches of Super Paradise and Elia.
