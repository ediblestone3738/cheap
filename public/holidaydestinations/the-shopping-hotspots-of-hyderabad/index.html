The Shopping Hotspots of Hyderabad

Mar 10, 2023

[ad_1] Hyderabad is known for its rich history, culture, and cuisine. But did you know that it is also a shopper’s paradise? The city is a treasure trove of shopping hotspots that offer everything from traditional handicrafts to modern fashion. In this article, we will take you through the best shopping places in Hyderabad that you must not miss.

Charminar Bazaar Charminar Bazaar, located around the iconic Charminar monument, is a popular shopping destination in Hyderabad. The bazaar is dotted with small shops and stalls that sell clothes, jewellery, perfumes, and handicrafts. The place is also known for its street food, especially the biryani, which is a must-try.

Laad Bazaar Laad Bazaar, known for its colourful bangles and jewellery, is a narrow and crowded street located near Charminar. The bangles sold here are made of lacquer, which is why the place is called Laad Bazaar, which means “lacquer bazaar” in Hindi. You can also find traditional Hyderabadi pearls and wedding accessories at this market.

Nampally Market Nampally Market is one of the oldest and busiest markets in Hyderabad. The market is divided into different sections, such as vegetable, meat, and clothing. You can find everything from fresh produce and spices to traditional clothes and footwear. The market also has a section dedicated to books and stationary.

Hyderabad Central Mall Hyderabad Central Mall is a popular shopping mall located in the heart of the city. The mall has a wide range of stores that sell clothing, footwear, accessories, and electronics. You can also find international brands such as Zara, Mango, and H&M here. The mall also has a food court and a cinema.

Forum Sujana Mall Forum Sujana Mall is one of the largest malls in Hyderabad, located in Kukatpally. The mall has over 300 stores that sell everything from fashion to electronics. You can also find a variety of international brands, such as Forever 21, Marks & Spencer, and Sephora. The mall also has a multiplex cinema and a food court.

GVK One Mall GVK One Mall, located in Banjara Hills, is a high-end shopping mall that caters to luxury shoppers. The mall has stores that sell designer clothing, jewellery, and accessories. You can find international brands such as Gucci, Louis Vuitton, and Jimmy Choo here. The mall also has a fine dining restaurant and a spa.

Inorbit Mall Inorbit Mall, located in Cyberabad, is another popular shopping mall in Hyderabad. The mall has a range of stores that sell fashion, beauty, and electronics. You can also find international brands such as Aldo, H&M, and Forever New here. The mall also has a food court, a gaming zone, and a multiplex cinema.

Begum Bazaar Begum Bazaar is a popular wholesale market for textiles, groceries, and spices. The market is located in the Old City and is famous for its wholesale prices. You can find everything from silk sarees and dress materials to spices and dry fruits here. The market is also home to some of the city’s oldest sweet shops.

Shilparamam Village Shilparamam Village is a cultural and craft village located in Madhapur. The village has a range of stores that sell traditional handicrafts, clothing, and accessories. You can find beautiful hand-made pottery, jewellery, and paintings at this village. The place also has cultural shows and food stalls.

Abids Road Abids Road is a busy street located in the heart of the city that is known for its street shops, bookstores, and restaurants. The street is dotted with shops that sell clothes, electronics, and accessories. You can also find some of the oldest and most famous bookstores in the city here.

FAQs: Q. What are the best things to buy in Hyderabad? A. Hyderabad is known for its pearls, bangles, handicrafts, and spices. You can also find beautiful silk sarees and dress materials here.

Q. What is the best time to go shopping in Hyderabad? A. The best time to go shopping in Hyderabad is during the cooler months from October to February.

Q. Can I bargain at the markets in Hyderabad? A. Yes, bargaining is common at most markets in Hyderabad. However, it is not advisable to do so at fixed-price shops and malls.

Q. Is it safe to visit the shopping hotspots in Hyderabad? A. Yes, Hyderabad is relatively safe for tourists. However, it is always advisable to be cautious and aware of your surroundings.
