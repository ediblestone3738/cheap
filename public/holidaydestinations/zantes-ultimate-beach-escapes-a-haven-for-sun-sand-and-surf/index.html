Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Zante’s Ultimate Beach Escapes

1. Navagio Beach (Shipwreck Beach): One of the most photographed beaches in Greece and the most famous beach of Zante’s Ultimate Beach Escapes. The beach exists because of a shipwreck that happened in 1980 when Panayiotis, a smuggler’s boat full of contraband cigarettes, ran aground. The beach boasts a long stretch of golden sand and an exquisite view of the turquoise sea.

2. Gerakas Beach: Located on the southern coast of the island, Gerakas Beach is a nesting spot for the endangered Loggerhead sea turtles. Visitors can enjoy the sun and sand while watching this miracle of nature unfold.

3. Kalamaki Beach: Located in the bay of Laganas, Kalamaki Beach is a delightful spot for sunbathing and swimming. It is also one of the few beaches in Zante’s Ultimate Beach Escapes that offers sun loungers and umbrellas for rent.

4. Tsilivi Beach: Tsilivi Beach is a lively beach with a vast range of water sports, beach bars and clubs, and other recreational activities. Visitors can indulge in different beach activities while enjoying the crystal-clear waters and the sun

5. Marathonisi Island: This peaceful island is known as the ‘Turtle Island,’ a habitat of the caretta-caretta turtles. Visitors can take a boat tour around the island and enjoy the stunning marine life.

6. Makris Gialos Beach: Makris Gialos Beach in Zante’s Ultimate Beach Escapes is known for its crystal blue waters and a combination of pebbles and sand. The beach offers plenty of water sports activities, and visitors can enjoy a view of the mountains while sunbathing.

7. Porto Zoro Beach: This beach is characterized by its crystal-clear turquoise waters and an excellent spot for snorkelling. Visitors can take a swim, sunbathe and relax beneath the shade of the trees lining the beach’s edge.

8. Blue Caves: The Blue Caves are located on the northernmost tip of Zakynthos. Visitors can take a boat ride and explore the blue waters and the unique natural caves.

9. Alykes Beach: Alykes Beach is a family-friendly beach located in Zante’s Ultimate Beach Escapes. The reef protects the shallow water from high swells, making it suitable for young children.

10. Limnionas Beach: Located in northern Zakynthos, this beach is a popular choice for snorkelling enthusiasts. The beach also has a taverna serving delicious Greek dishes.
