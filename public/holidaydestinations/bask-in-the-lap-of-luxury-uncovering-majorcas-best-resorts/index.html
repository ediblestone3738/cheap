The island of Majorca in the Mediterranean is renowned for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant culture. It’s no wonder that it has become one of Europe’s top holiday destinations, particularly among luxury travelers. Whether it’s indulging in fine dining, world-class spa treatments or enjoying a round of golf, Majorca has something to offer every kind of traveler. To make the most of the island’s luxurious offerings, we’ve rounded up Majorca’s top resorts.

1. Belmond La Residencia: Set among the Tramuntana Mountains, this world-famous resort is a must-visit for luxury travelers. It’s a haven of peace and tranquility and features world-class amenities, including a spa and Michelin-starred restaurant.

2. Jumeirah Port Soller: Nestled in a tranquil cove among stunning mountain terrain, this lavish resort offers guests unparalleled views of the Mediterranean. With fine dining, a spa, and excellent service, this is the perfect place to unwind and recharge.

3. Castillo Hotel Son Vida: Built in the 13th-century, this stunning castle has been meticulously restored to its former glory. Today, it’s one of Majorca’s most luxurious resorts, offering guests the quintessential Mediterranean experience.

4. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort: With its striking architecture and incredible sea views, this resort is a true gem on the island. Guests can enjoy world-class dining, unbeatable spa treatments, and top-tier services.

5. Cap Rocat: This former military fortress has been transformed into one of Majorca’s most exclusive resorts. Offering sweeping sea views, luxurious villas and impeccable service, it’s the perfect place for couples seeking a romantic getaway.

6. Park Hyatt Mallorca: This elegant resort is located in the peaceful Canyamel Valley, offering stunning mountain views and easy access to some of Majorca’s most beautiful beaches. Guests can expect top-tier amenities, including a world-class spa and Michelin-starred restaurant.

7. Hotel Maricel & Spa: This boutique hotel boasts breathtaking sea views and exceptional service. With its minimalist, contemporary design, it offers a serene and luxurious escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

8. Gran Hotel Son Net: A stunning 17th-century mansion, this hotel has been beautifully restored into a luxurious resort. Nestled in the heart of the Tramuntana Mountains, it’s the perfect place to enjoy the island’s natural beauty in style.

9. Sheraton Mallorca Arabella Golf Hotel: Located just a stone’s throw away from three world-class golf courses, this resort is the ultimate destination for golf enthusiasts. With spacious rooms, a stunning pool, and a multitude of sporting activities, it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind.

10. Hospes Maricel: This romantic hotel is located in a historic 15th-century building, offering guests the ultimate in luxury and comfort. With its stunning sea views and Mediterranean-inspired decor, it’s the ideal place for a romantic getaway.
