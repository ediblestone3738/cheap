Sandy Beaches and Greek Hospitality: Discover Greece’s Best Family Resorts

When it comes to family holidays, there are endless options to choose from. However, few destinations can compare to the beauty and warmth of Cyprus. Located in the eastern Mediterranean, Cyprus attracts tourists for its sandy beaches, year-round sunshine, and ancient historical sites.

Whether you are looking for a relaxed, all-inclusive resort or an adventurous holiday, Cyprus offers something for everyone. Here are some of the best family resorts in Cyprus to help you make the most of your holiday.

1. Coral Bay Resort

Coral Bay Resort is a popular option for families looking for a beach-front resort. Located in Paphos, this resort offers a private beach, an outdoor pool with a children’s section, water sports, and entertainment facilities for guests.

2. Louis Phaethon Beach Resort

The Louis Phaethon Beach Resort is another popular option for families. Located in Paphos, this resort offers a private beach, an indoor and outdoor pool, water slides for children, and a wide range of activities for both adults and children.

3. Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos

The Olympic Lagoon Resort in Paphos is an all-inclusive resort that has something for everyone. This resort features a private beach, eight swimming pools, water slides, and a range of sports facilities, including tennis courts, mini-golf, and soccer fields.

4. Atlantica Aeneas Resort & Spa

Located in Ayia Napa, the Atlantica Aeneas Resort & Spa is another excellent option for families. This resort boasts a private beach, a water park with slides, an indoor and outdoor pool, and various sports facilities.

5. Nausicaa Beach

Nausicaa Beach is a family-owned hotel located in Protaras. The hotel’s private beach is ideal for swimming and snorkeling, and guests can also enjoy an outdoor pool, a children’s pool, and an excellent restaurant.

FAQs:

1. What is the best time to visit Cyprus?

The best time to visit Cyprus is from mid-April to mid-October when the weather is warm and sunny. However, if you want to avoid the crowds, consider visiting during the off-season when the weather is still mild and pleasant.

2. Do you need a visa to visit Cyprus?

If you are a citizen of the European Union, you do not need a visa to visit Cyprus. However, if you are from a non-EU country, you may need to apply for a visa before your trip.

3. What is the currency of Cyprus?

The currency of Cyprus is the euro.

4. Is Cyprus expensive?

Cyprus is generally considered to be an affordable destination, especially when compared to other European countries.

5. What is the official language of Cyprus?

The official languages of Cyprus are Greek and Turkish, although English is widely spoken.
