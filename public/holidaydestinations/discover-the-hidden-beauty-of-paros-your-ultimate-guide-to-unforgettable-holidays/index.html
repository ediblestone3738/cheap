Paros is a small yet beautiful island in the Aegean Sea, located in the Cyclades group of Greek islands. Despite its small size, the island is one of the most beautiful and popular travel destinations in Greece. Paros is a place where ancient culture meets modern life. The beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and picturesque landscapes make it an ideal spot for an unforgettable vacation.

In this guide, we will explore everything you need to know about discovering the hidden beauty of Paros, from the best places to stay, to the top tourist attractions and activities that you should not miss.

Where to Stay?

Paros has a diverse range of accommodation options available to suit all budget categories. Whether you’re looking for a luxurious villa, budget-friendly hostels or charming guesthouses, Paros has something for everyone.

1. Luxury Villas: If you want to experience the ultimate luxury during your stay in Paros, then opt for a luxury villa. There are an array of stylish villas available on the island, offering breathtaking views of the coastline and first-class amenities.

2. Charming Guesthouses: One of the best ways to experience the local culture and hospitality on the island is to stay in a traditional guesthouse. These charming properties are typically located in the heart of the old town, providing an authentic insight into the local lifestyle.

3. Budget-Friendly Hostels: If you’re looking for budget-friendly stays, then Paros has an array of hostels available. These hostels typically offer clean beds and shared amenities to travelers looking for affordable accommodation options.

What to Do?

Paros has a plethora of activities and attractions to explore during your stay on the island. Here are some must-visit places and activities on the island that you should not miss.

1. Visit the Beaches: Paros is famous for its stunning beaches with crystal blue waters. Head to Golden Beach or Santa Maria Beach to enjoy the soft sand and calm waters.

2. Go Hiking: Paros has many hiking trails that provide breathtaking views of the island. Trail No.1 in Marpissa Village and Trail No.2 in Lefkes Village are popular among travelers.

3. Learn to Windsurf: Paros is known as one of the best windsurfing destinations in Greece. The island’s strong winds and calm waters make it the ideal spot for both beginners and pros.

4. Historical Sites: If you’re interested in ancient history, then Paros has a range of historical sites to visit. These include the Paros Archaeological Museum, the Church of Ekatontapiliani, and the ancient quarry of Marathi.

5. Boat Tours: A boat tour around the island is an ideal way to take in the stunning scenery of Paros. Head to Naoussa, where you can board a boat tour taking you to the neighboring islands of Naxos, Mykonos or Santorini.

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Paros?

A: The best time to visit Paros is between May and October. During this period, the weather is warm, and the island is dotted with colorful flowers and greenery.

Q: How do I get to Paros?

A: Paros can be reached by ferry from Pireaus port, Rafina port or from other Aegean Islands including Mykonos and Santorini.

Q: What are the best places to visit in Paros?

A: The best places to visit in Paros are:

- Golden Beach - Santa Maria Beach - Parikia - Naoussa - Lefkes Village - Marpissa Village - Antiparos Island - Paros Archaeological Museum - The Church of Ekatontapiliani - Panagia Ekatontapiliani Monastery

Q: Is Paros expensive?

A: Although Paros is a popular tourist destination, food, and lodging prices are still affordable, making it a great destination for budget-friendly travel.

Q: What are the best water activities to do in Paros?

A: The best water activities to do in Paros are:

- Windsurfing - Kitesurfing - Water Skiing - Jet Skiing

Conclusion

Paros is an exceptional destination that combines stunning natural landscapes, a rich culture and a relaxed pace of life. The island will provide you with endless opportunities for adventure, relaxation, and culture. From the fantastic beaches to the historical sites, Paros offers something for everyone.
