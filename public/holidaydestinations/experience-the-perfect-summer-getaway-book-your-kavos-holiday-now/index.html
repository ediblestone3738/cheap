Experience the Perfect Summer Getaway: Book Your Kavos Holiday Now!

Apr 27, 2023

[ad_1] Summer is the perfect time to unwind and enjoy a well-deserved break from the daily grind. If you’re looking for an ideal summer getaway, look no further than Kavos! This charming town located on the southern coast of the island of Corfu in Greece is the perfect destination for those seeking relaxation, beach fun, and a lively nightlife scene. In this travel article, we’ll explore why Kavos should be your go-to for a perfect summer holiday.

Why Kavos Is Ideal for Summer Breaks

Kavos has something to offer everyone. Its sandy beaches are a major draw, and the crystal-clear waters will beckon you for a dip. Choose from the miles-long, sandy Kavos Beach, and the quieter, small cove of Arkoudilas Beach. If you’re an adventurer, try your luck with the water sports available like parasailing, jet-skiing, and kayaking. And when you’re ready for some rest and relaxation, enjoy a refreshing cocktail or a delicious Greek dish at the numerous beach bars and restaurants.

Besides soaking in the sun and sea, Kavos also offers a bustling nightlife scene. The town has a range of bars and clubs that come alive after dark. You’ll find everything from casual watering holes to thumping dance floors. But even with its party scene, Kavos is still suitable for families and more relaxed travelers. It’s easy to avoid the clubs and enjoy quieter resorts at a slightly removed location.

Kavos is also ideal for those interested in nature and history. You can admire the picturesque countryside and olive groves by bike or on foot, and explore the nearby traditional villages for a taste of local life. History buffs can take a day trip to Corfu Old Town, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where you can explore the Venetian architecture and Greek ruins.

FAQs for a Kavos Holiday

Can I travel to Kavos without a passport? No, you’ll need a passport to travel to Kavos. Greece is part of the Schengen Zone, which requires a valid passport for all travelers.

What is the best time to visit Kavos? Kavos is an ideal summer destination, so the best time to visit is between June and September when its weather is warm and sunny.

What is the currency used in Kavos? Kavos and Greece, in general, use the Euro (EUR) as their currency.

Do I need a visa to travel to Kavos? You don’t need a visa to travel to Kavos if you’re traveling with a UK or EU passport or visa. If you’re a non-EU citizen, check with the Greek embassy or consulates to confirm if you need a visa.

What should I pack when going to Kavos? Pack plenty of sun protection like hats, sunglasses, and sunscreen as well as comfortable and light clothes. Pack beachwear like swimwear and shorts, and some comfortable walking shoes for exploring. Include a light jacket, cardigan, or shawl for the cooler evenings and visitants may want to bring mosquito repellent.
