Mykonos, Greece has rightfully earned its reputation as a party destination, but if you’re looking for relaxation and luxury accommodations, there are plenty of all-inclusive resorts that offer just that. Whether you’re traveling solo, as a couple, or with a group of friends, Mykonos resorts are the perfect getaway for any type of relaxation.

From stunning beaches to top-notch amenities, Mykonos resorts offer a variety of ways to unwind and relax. Here’s the ultimate guide to top all inclusive resorts in Mykonos:

1. St. John Mykonos Hotel

The St. John Mykonos Hotel is an all-inclusive resort that’s located only three kilometers from Mykonos town, and a short distance from a beautiful beach. The hotel features a fitness center, an outdoor pool, an Italian restaurant, and a bar.

The rooms are tastefully decorated and include air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and satellite TV. The hotel also offers an airport shuttle, free parking, and a 24-hour reception.

2. Palladium Boutique Hotel

The Palladium Boutique Hotel is a luxurious and all-inclusive resort, located in Psarou beach, Mykonos. It is just 4 km from Mykonos town and offers a range of top-notch amenities.

The hotel has a fitness center, a spa, an outdoor pool, two restaurants, and three bars. The rooms are elegantly designed, air-conditioned, and equipped with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a mini bar. The hotel also provides free shuttle service to the beach.

3. Myconian Imperial Resort

The Myconian Imperial Resort is a grand all-inclusive resort built on a cliff overlooking Elia Bay, which is just 12 km from Mykonos town. The resort offers a variety of swimming pools, spa services, and restaurants with stunning views of the sea.

The rooms are decorated with luxury furnishings and are equipped with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a mini bar. The resort also offers an airport shuttle, butler services, and a 24-hour reception to assist guests with any needs.

4. Petasos Beach Resort & Spa

The Petasos Beach Resort & Spa is an all-inclusive resort located in Platis Gialos beach, just 3 km from Mykonos town. It offers accommodation in rooms, suites, or villas with private pools.

The resort has two outdoor swimming pools, a spa, a bar, and a restaurant overlooking the sea. The rooms have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a mini bar. The resort also offers an airport shuttle, car rental, and free parking.

5. Royal Myconian Resort & Villas

The Royal Myconian Resort & Villas is located in Elia beach, just 12 km from Mykonos town. The all-inclusive resort offers a range of amenities and picturesque views of the Aegean Sea from the outdoor pool.

The rooms are furnished with luxury decor and have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a mini bar. The resort also offers airport shuttle service, car rental, and a 24-hour reception.

FAQs:

Q: What is the best time to visit Mykonos?

A: The best time to visit Mykonos is from mid-May to mid-September, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the peak season can be crowded and expensive, so visiting in the off-season may be more enjoyable for those who want to relax and unwind.

Q: Do Mykonos resorts offer airport transportation?

A: All the resorts mentioned in this guide offer airport transportation, either for an additional fee or included in the price of the room.

Q: Are Mykonos resorts suitable for families?

A: Yes, many Mykonos resorts offer amenities and services suitable for families, such as children’s pools and babysitting services. However, you should check with the individual resort to be sure.
