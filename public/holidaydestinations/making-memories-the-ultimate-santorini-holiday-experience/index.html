FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Santorini? A: The best time to visit Santorini is during the months of April-June and September-November, when the weather is mild, and the crowds are not as intense.

Q: How do I get to Santorini? A: Santorini can be accessed by either a flight or a ferry. There are direct flights to Santorini from Athens, and several other European destinations. The port of Santorini is located in the town of Fira, and ferries run regularly to and from the island.

Q: What are the best tourist attractions in Santorini? A: Santorini has plenty of beautiful tourist attractions, including the Red Beach, Oia, the Archaeological Museum of Thera, Akrotiri, and the Venetian Castle of Pyrgos.

Q: What is Megalochori known for? A: Megalochori is known for its traditional architecture, small alleys, and hidden gems. The village is located in the center of the island, surrounded by vineyards, and it’s perfect for a relaxing and peaceful holiday.
