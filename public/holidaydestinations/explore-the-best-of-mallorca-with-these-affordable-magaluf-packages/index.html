Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Mallorca

1. Palma Cathedral: The cathedral is an iconic landmark of Mallorca with its Gothic architecture and stunning sea views.

2. Serra de Tramuntana: The mountain range offers breathtaking landscapes, including valleys, reservoirs, and winding roads.

3. Cuevas del Drach: The underground caves of Drach are a natural wonder with stunning stalactites and an underground lake.

4. Port de Soller: The picturesque port town is nestled between the mountains and the sea and offers stunning views and a relaxed atmosphere.

5. Es Trenc beach: The stunning white-sand beach is one of the most beautiful and undeveloped beaches on the island.

6. Deia: The charming village of Deia offers a glimpse of traditional Mallorcan life and is home to many artists and writers.

7. Puerto Pollensa: The scenic town offers a wide range of water sports, cafes, and restaurants, and a laid-back atmosphere.

8. Valdemossa: The village is known for its picturesque streets, stunning mountain views, and the famous Cartuja de Valldemossa monastery.

9. Cap de Formentor: The northernmost point of the island offers stunning views of the sea, cliffs, and lighthouse.

10. Castell de Bellver: The historic castle in Palma is a unique circular fortress that offers stunning views of the Bay of Palma and the city.
