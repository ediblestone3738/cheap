Top 10 tourist attractions in Rhodes

1. The Acropolis of Lindos - an ancient citadel and UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring incredible views and historic ruins.

2. The Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes – a medieval fortress that served as a palace for the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes.

3. Tsambika Beach - an uncrowded beach with crystal clear waters and golden sands.

4. The Valley of the Butterflies - a lush and green valley with stunning butterflies.

5. The Ancient Stadium of Rhodes – an ancient stadium from 3rd century BC.

6. Lindos village - a picturesque village with traditional white washed houses.

7. The Medieval Old Town of Rhodes – a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring narrow streets and stunning architecture.

8. The Seven Springs - a natural oasis with seven fresh water springs and a lake.

9. The Farma of Rhodes - an agricultural farm that provides organic produce and a peaceful setting.

10. The Archaeological Museum of Rhodes - a museum that showcases the island’s rich history and artefacts.
