Tips for Visitors to Disneyland Paris

Here are few tips for visitors to be mindful before embarking on a journey to Disneyland Paris that will help you have a more enjoyable and less stressful visit:

1. Purchase tickets in advance: By visiting the official Disneyland Paris website, you can purchase tickets in advance and avoid long queues at the entrance to the park. 2. Be prepared for long queues: Arrive early and set aside time for waiting in line because some of the popular rides can have waiting times of over an hour. 3. Plan your visit ahead: Disneyland Paris offers convenient services like the Disney FASTPASS which entitles you to bypass queues, but it’s a limited exclusive service that must be booked beforehand, so plan it ahead on which rides you want to utilize this service. 4. Bring comfortable footwear: Disneyland Paris is vast and in a day you’ll be covering most of the ground. Comfortable shoes that are easy to walk will keep you going till the end of the day. 5. Take advantage of extra magic hours: Disneyland offers early access to the park to its guests, so start your day early and take advantage of the extra hour available to guests in the morning. 6. Be mindful of the season: The crowds will differ depending on the season, be mindful of national holidays and vacation periods. Winter can be cold so be prepared for it if visiting during those times.

FAQs about Disneyland Paris Q: Are pets allowed in Disneyland Paris? A: No, pets are not allowed for safety and hygiene reasons.

Q: What is the age limit for Disneyland Paris? A: There is no age limit to visit Disneyland Paris, it’s a fun escape with something for everyone of all ages.

Q: Is Disney’s FASTPASS free? A: Yes, it is a free service you can enjoy but limited to only one ride at a time to pass the queue.

Q: Are there any Baby Care Centers in Disneyland Paris? A: Yes, baby centers are available with qualified staff and services like baby food, changing rooms, baby products, nursing stations, etc.

Q: Can I buy Disney Electronic Gift Cards, and where? A: Yes, Disney Electronic Gift Cards are available for purchase online, and at several physical Disney Stores or in the parks.
