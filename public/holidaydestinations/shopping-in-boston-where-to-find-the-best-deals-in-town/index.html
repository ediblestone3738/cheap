[ad_1] Shopping in Boston: Where to Find the Best Deals in Town

Boston, Massachusetts is a popular tourist destination known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and vibrant neighborhoods. While tourists flock to the city for its fascinating tourist attractions, little do they realize that the city is also a mecca for shopping enthusiasts. From luxury brands to bargain finds, Boston has something for everyone. If you’re in town for some retail therapy, here’s a guide to where you can find the best deals in town.

Headings:

1. Downtown Crossing

2. Newbury Street

3. Copley Place

4. Prudential Center

5. Faneuil Hall Marketplace

6. CambridgeSide

7. Wrentham Village Premium Outlets

8. Assembly Row

9. Legacy Place

10. The Street Chestnut Hill

Downtown Crossing

Downtown Crossing is Boston’s premier shopping district with various retail stores, boutiques, and big-name shopping centers. It is a bustling hub of shopping in the heart of Boston, where you can find everything from jewelry to clothing, home decor, and electronics. The iconic Macy’s department store offers a vast selection of quality, name-brand items at affordable prices.

Newbury Street

Newbury Street is the shopping haven for fashionistas with its high-end boutiques, art galleries, and restaurants. It is a trendy street with a cosmopolitan feel that attracts fashion buyers from all over the world. You can find top designer brand names, such as Chanel, Valentino, and Gucci, as well as offbeat local boutiques, like Bobby From Boston – renowned for its vintage menswear.

Copley Place

Copley Place is the ultimate shopper’s paradise and is located in the heart of the Back Bay neighborhood. It is home to over 75 designer stores, including some of the most recognizable names like Neiman Marcus, Jimmy Choo, and Tiffany & Co. It offers a luxurious shopping experience packed with state-of-the-art facilities.

Prudential Center

Prudential Center is one of the best shopping destinations in Boston and offers an excellent mix of local and national brands. Its retailers include Saks Fifth Avenue, Sephora, and Lord & Taylor, among others. The center’s Skywalk Observatory, located on the top floor, provides a breathtaking 360-degree panoramic view of the city.

Faneuil Hall Marketplace

Faneuil Hall Marketplace is an indoor/outdoor shopping center located in downtown Boston. At Faneuil Hall, you can shop for clothing, footwear, books, and other souvenirs. Some famous stores at the Marketplace include Banana Republic, Ann Taylor, and Victoria’s Secret. Also, visit the nearby Quincy Market, where food vendors offer a variety of snacks and meals.

CambridgeSide

CambridgeSide is Cambridge’s only fully enclosed mall offering various shopping options from fashion, jewelry, food, beauty, and more. Popular stores include Victoria’s Secret, Urban Outfitters, and Apple. As you shop, enjoy the panoramic views of the Charles River.

Wrentham Village Premium Outlets

If you’re looking for great shopping deals, Wrentham is the place to go. The town is home to the Wrentham Village Premium Outlet, which features over 170 brand-name stores. You can find amazing deals on brands like Adidas, Kate Spade, and Puma. The mall is located about 30 minutes south of Boston.

Assembly Row

On the banks of the Mystic River, sits the Assembly Row shopping district, featuring outlet stores, local boutiques, bars, and restaurants. The highlight of the shopping district is the Assembly Row outlet mall offering up to 70% discount on designer collections. You can get rid of your shopping withdrawal symptoms by visiting the outlet stores, such as Nike and Bose.

Legacy Place

Legacy Place is a premium shopping destination located in Dedham, Massachusetts. With brands like Container Store, Sephora, and Anthropologie, It caters to the needs of the city’s fashion-savvy population.

The Street Chestnut Hill

The Street Chestnut Hill is a high-end shopping destination located in the Chestnut Hill neighborhood of Boston. It features an upscale collection of shops, including Tiffany & Co., Lululemon, and Il Casale Cucina Campana.

FAQs

1. Are there any shopping centers in Boston that offer free parking?

Most shopping centers offer parking, but it’s not always free. For example, at the Wrentham Village Premium Outlets, you can park your car for free.

2. Are there any outlet malls located in Boston?

Yes, Boston is home to several outlet malls, including Wrentham Village Premium Outlets and Assembly Row.

3. What is the best time to go shopping in Boston?

The best time to go shopping in Boston is during weekdays, as weekends can be busy.

4. Do stores in Boston charge sales tax?

Yes, Massachusetts imposes a 6.25% sales tax on most items.

5. Are there any stores that offer student discounts in Boston?

Yes, some stores offer student discounts in Boston. For instance, Ann Taylor offers a 15% discount to students with a valid ID.
