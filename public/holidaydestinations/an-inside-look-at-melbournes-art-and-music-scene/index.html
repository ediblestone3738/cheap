Melbourne is known for its thriving art and music scene. The city is home to many renowned galleries, theatres, and music venues that attract visitors from all over the world. From street art to architecture, Melbourne has something for every art lover.

With a diverse cultural landscape, Melbourne’s art and music scene is a reflection of its creative and innovative people. The city has spawned some of Australia’s finest artists, musicians, and performers, with many international acts also calling Melbourne their home.

One of Melbourne’s most iconic art institutions is the National Gallery of Victoria. The gallery has an extensive collection that spans thousands of years, showcasing the best of Australian and international art. Visitors can view everything from contemporary art to Renaissance paintings.

Melbourne is also home to some of the best theatres in the country. The Arts Centre Melbourne is a must-visit destination for theatre enthusiasts. The centre has several theatres that showcase both local and international productions. The State Theatre is a popular venue that hosts world-renowned ballet and opera performances.

Music lovers will also have a great time in Melbourne. The city boasts a vibrant live music scene, with various venues hosting gigs and concerts every night of the week. One of the most iconic live music venues is The Corner Hotel in Richmond. The venue has hosted some of the world’s top musicians and has become a favourite amongst locals and tourists alike.

Melbourne’s street art is another form of artistic expression that is hard to miss. The city’s laneways are adorned with colourful murals, graffiti, and street art. Hosier Lane and AC/DC Lane are two of the most popular destinations for street art enthusiasts.
