Best neighborhood for nightlife:

The best neighborhood for nightlife in Boston is undoubtedly the Fenway-Kenmore area. This neighborhood is home to several colleges and universities, which means there are plenty of bars catering to the college crowd. But there are also plenty of more sophisticated options, such as Eastern Standard, which offers craft cocktails, and The Hawthorne, which is known for its extensive whiskey selection. The area is also home to Fenway Park, which means it’s a great place to hang out before or after a game.

FAQs:

Q: What is the legal drinking age in Boston? A: The legal drinking age in Boston, as in the rest of the United States, is 21.

Q: What is the dress code for Boston nightclubs? A: Most Boston nightclubs have a dress code of “smart casual,” which means no sneakers or athletic wear.

Q: Are Boston nightclubs open late? A: Boston nightclubs typically close at 2am, but some may stay open later on weekends.

Q: How do I get around Boston at night? A: Boston has a public transportation system known as the MBTA, which includes buses and trains that run late into the night. Taxis and ride-share services such as Uber and Lyft are also readily available.

Q: Are there any rooftop bars in Boston? A: Yes, there are several rooftop bars in Boston, including Lookout Rooftop at The Envoy Hotel, Legal Harborside, and Sky Lounge at Yotel Boston.
