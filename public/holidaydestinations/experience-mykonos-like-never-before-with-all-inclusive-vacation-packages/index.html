Top 10 tourist attractions located in Experience Mykonos Like Never Before with All Inclusive Vacation Packages

1. Little Venice

Little Venice is one of the most picturesque areas in Mykonos, It is located in the southwest part of the town and is a must-visit for anyone visiting the island. The colorful buildings, crystal-clear waters, and stunning harbor views are a perfect spot for photo opportunities.

2. Windmills

The windmills of Mykonos are one of the island’s most recognizable landmarks, and for good reason. Visitors can take a guided tour of the windmills or just appreciate the impressive 16th-century structures from a distance. Either way, the windmills should be on your list of things to see in Mykonos.

3. Mykonos Archaeological Museum

The Archaeological Museum of Mykonos is a small yet impressive museum featuring ancient artifacts and archaeological finds from the Cycladic period, dating back several centuries. It is a perfect place to learn more about the island’s rich history and culture.

4. Paraportiani Church

The Paraportiani Church is one of the most fascinating and unique religious structures in Europe. This church is a stunning architectural masterpiece located in the Kastro area of Mykonos. A visit to Mykonos is incomplete without seeing this impressive church.

5. Delos Island

Delos Island is a small island located just a short boat ride from Mykonos. It is a must-visit for anyone interested in history or archaeology. The island was once the center of the ancient world and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

6. Super Paradise Beach

Super Paradise Beach is a prime location for party lovers visiting Mykonos, it is known for a luxurious atmosphere and a bustling beach-side club. Its stunning blue waters and white sandy beaches are the perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing or relaxing with friends.

7. Panagia Paraportiani

Panagia Paraportiani is a charming little church situated in the heart of Mykonos town. It is a popular spot for photographers and tourists alike and its architecture is reminiscent of the island’s Cycladic style.

8. Psarou Beach

Psarou beach, an ideal location to relax, unwind and take in the famous Greek sunshine. It is famous for its crystal-clear waters and glamourous beach clubs.

9. Tropicana Beach Club

Tropicana Beach Club located in Paradise Beach, is a top nightlife destination for those visiting Mykonos. It’s known for its lively atmosphere, great music and dance-floor.

10. Cavo Paradiso

Cavo Paradiso is another must-visit for partygoers visiting Mykonos. It’s one of the most famous night clubs in Greece and is located right on the beach. The club has played host to some of the best DJs in the world and is known for its stunning views.
