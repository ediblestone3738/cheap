Mallorca, also known as Majorca, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Spain. This beautiful island offers stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and picturesque landscapes that attract millions of tourists every year. However, if you’re looking for a luxurious getaway, then Mallorca has some of the finest resorts to offer.

Mallorca’s resorts offer the ultimate beachfront experience, making it the perfect destination for those looking to unwind and relax. Withworld-class amenities and exceptional service, these luxurious resorts provide an indulgent experience. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway or a relaxing family vacation, Mallorca’s finest resorts cater to every need.

Here are some of the top resorts to experience the ultimate beachfront getaway at Mallorca’s finest resorts:

1. Belmond La Residencia

Belmond La Residencia is a luxurious resort located in Deià, at the foothills of the Tramuntana Mountains. This resort offers breathtaking views of the Mediterranean and the charming village of Deià. The 5-star hotel features elegantly designed rooms and suites with private terraces, four restaurants, an outdoor swimming pool, and a world-class spa.

2. Castillo Hotel Son Vida

Castillo Hotel Son Vida is a majestic castle that has been converted into a luxurious hotel. This resort is located in Palma de Mallorca, overlooking the Bay of Palma. The 5-star hotel offers luxuriously furnished rooms and suites with private balconies, four restaurants and bars, a world-class spa, and an 18-hole golf course.

3. Cap Rocat

Cap Rocat is a former military fortress turned into a luxurious retreat. This resort is located in Cala Blava, on the southern coast of Mallorca. The 5-star hotel boasts beautifully designed rooms and suites, two restaurants and bars, an outdoor infinity pool, and stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.

4. Park Hyatt Mallorca

Park Hyatt Mallorca is a luxurious resort located in the northeast of Mallorca, in the valley of Canyamel. This 5-star hotel offers elegantly designed rooms and suites, three restaurants and bars, a world-class spa, and an 18-hole golf course.

5. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort

St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a luxurious resort located in Costa d’en Blanes, overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. The 5-star hotel offers elegantly designed rooms and suites, an award-winning spa, a private marina, and two outdoor swimming pools.

6. Sheraton Mallorca Arabella Golf Hotel

Sheraton Mallorca Arabella Golf Hotel is a luxurious hotel located in the southwest of Mallorca, near the capital city of Palma. The 5-star hotel offers beautifully designed rooms and suites, three golf courses, a world-class spa, and two outdoor swimming pools.

7. Gran Hotel Son Net

Gran Hotel Son Net is a luxurious 5-star hotel located in the village of Puigpunyent, at the foothills of the Tramuntana Mountains. This hotel offers elegantly designed rooms and suites, a world-class spa, an outdoor swimming pool, and a private tennis court.

8. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa

Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa is a luxurious resort located on the northwest coast of Mallorca, overlooking the Tramuntana Mountains and the Mediterranean Sea. The 5-star hotel offers elegantly designed rooms and suites, three restaurants and bars, a world-class spa, and two outdoor swimming pools.

9. Gran Meliá de Mar

Gran Meliá de Mar is a luxurious 5-star hotel located on the beachfront of Illetas, near the capital city of Palma. The hotel offers elegantly designed rooms and suites, three restaurants and bars, a world-class spa, and an outdoor swimming pool.

10. Hotel Formentor, a Royal Hideaway Hotel

Hotel Formentor, a Royal Hideaway Hotel is a luxurious 5-star hotel located in the northeast of Mallorca, in the stunning Pollença Bay. The hotel offers beautifully designed rooms and suites, two restaurants and bars, a world-class spa, and an outdoor swimming pool.
