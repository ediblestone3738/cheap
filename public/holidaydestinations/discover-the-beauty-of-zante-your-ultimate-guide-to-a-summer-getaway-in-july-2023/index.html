[ad_1] Discover the Beauty of Zante: Your Ultimate Guide to a Summer Getaway in July 2023

Looking for the perfect summer getaway spot where you can enjoy sun, sea, and sand? Look no further than the stunning island of Zante! Nestled in the Ionian Sea, Zante is a picturesque Greek island, adorned with lush greenery and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Admired by travelers from all over the world, this dreamy island promises to leave you breathless with its charm, history, and natural beauty.

Whether you are looking for a peaceful escape or a wild adventure, Zante has something for everyone. From exploring its ancient ruins and taking on epic water sports to savoring delicious local cuisine and basking under the Mediterranean sun, this enchanting island has got you covered.

To help you plan the ultimate summer getaway of July 2023, we have brought together the best things to see, experience, and enjoy on Zante. So, pack your bags, book your tickets, and let’s discover the beauty of Zante together.

Getting there

The easiest way to get to Zante is by taking a flight to Zakynthos International Airport (ZTH). Many airlines offer regular flights to Zante from various cities across Europe and beyond. Once you arrive, you can take a taxi or bus to your accommodation, which is usually not too far away, as the island is rather small.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Zante is from May to September, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the sea is perfect for swimming. July is the peak tourist season, so make sure to book your accommodation and tours in advance to avoid disappointment.

Accommodation

Zante has a wide range of accommodation options to suit all tastes and budgets. Whether you prefer a luxurious resort or a charming guesthouse, you will find it all here. Some popular areas to stay in Zante include Zakynthos Town, Alykanas, and Laganas.

Things to see and do

1. Navagio Beach

No trip to Zante is complete without a visit to the iconic Navagio Beach, also known as “Shipwreck Beach.” Located on the north-west coast of the island, this breathtaking stretch of white sand and turquoise waters is framed by towering cliffs and a rusting shipwreck that is visible from the shore. You can reach the beach by boat or by hiking down a steep pathway called “the Stairs of Neda.”

2. Blue Caves

Discover the magic of the Blue Caves, a natural wonder of Zante that is found on the island’s north coast. These sea caves are known for their unique blue hues, which are created by the reflection of sunlight on the water and the surrounding white rocks. You can explore the caves by boat, which is a truly unforgettable experience.

3. Zakynthos Town

Take a stroll through Zakynthos Town, the capital city of Zante, and get lost in its charming streets and beautiful architecture. The town boasts a rich history and culture, evident in its museums, churches, and monuments. The Venetian Castle, St. Dionysios Church, and Solomos Square are some of the must-see attractions in the town.

4. Laganas Beach

If you are looking for a more lively beach experience, head over to Laganas Beach, one of the most popular and buzzing beaches on the island. This long stretch of golden sand and clear waters is filled with beach bars, water sports activities, and vibrant nightlife. It’s also a nesting site for the endangered loggerhead sea turtle, so make sure to follow the rules and regulations in the area.

5. Marathonissi Island

Escape the crowds and explore the peaceful and untouched beauty of Marathonissi Island. This small island, located on the south of Zante, is a nature reserve where you can observe various species of flora and fauna, including sea turtles and monk seals. You can take a boat tour from Zakynthos Town to reach Marathonissi and enjoy its pristine beaches and turquoise waters.

6. Water sports

Get your adrenaline pumping and try out some water sports activities in Zante. From windsurfing and water skiing to banana boating and jet skiing, there’s something for everyone. You can find plenty of water sports providers on Laganas Beach and other popular beaches on the island.

7. Local cuisine

Savor the authentic flavors of Zante by indulging in its local cuisine. The island is famous for its seafood, olive oil, and wine. Some of the must-try dishes include stuffed squid, moussaka, and traditional Greek salad. You can find local eateries and tavernas in different parts of the island.

FAQs

Q: Is it safe to travel to Zante in July 2023?

A: The safety of travel depends on the current global situation and the guidelines set by the authorities. Make sure to check the travel advisories and protocols before booking your trip.

Q: What is the currency used in Zante?

A: The currency used in Zante is the Euro.

Q: Is it easy to get around Zante?

A: Yes, Zante is a small island, and it’s easy to navigate by car, taxi, or local bus. You can also explore the island on foot or by bike.

Q: Can I drink tap water in Zante?

A: It’s recommended to drink bottled water in Zante to avoid any health risks.

Q: Are there any dress codes to follow in Zante?

A: Zante is a relatively laid-back island, but it’s advisable to dress modestly when visiting churches and other religious sites.

Conclusion

Zante is a heavenly island that promises to steal your heart with its natural beauty, rich history, and warm hospitality. From exploring its stunning beaches and sea caves to diving into its local culture and cuisine, you will find plenty of reasons to fall in love with this Greek gem.
