[ad_1] Explore Greece in Style: Book Your Stay at One of These All-Inclusive Resorts

Greece, the cradle of western civilization, has been one of Europe’s most popular tourist destinations for many years. Known for its ancient ruins, picturesque islands, and delicious cuisine, it has everything that a traveler could want. But, with so much to see and do, planning a trip to Greece can be overwhelming. That’s where all-inclusive resorts come in. By booking a stay at one of these resorts, you can focus on enjoying your vacation while everything else is taken care of for you. Here are some of the best all-inclusive resorts in Greece that will make your trip a truly memorable one.

  1. Ikos Dassia, Corfu

If you’re looking for a luxurious all-inclusive experience, look no further than Ikos Dassia. This stunning resort is located on the beautiful island of Corfu and boasts everything from fine dining to a private beach. With its crystal clear waters and sandy shores, it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind.

  1. Club Med Gregolimano, Evia

Club Med is known for providing its guests with the ultimate vacation experience, and their resort in Gregolimano is no exception. Situated on the island of Evia, it offers a range of activities, including sailing, windsurfing, and beach volleyball. And, with all meals and drinks included, you won’t have to worry about a thing.

  1. Grecotel Olympia Riviera Resort, Peloponnese

Located on the Peloponnese peninsula, the Grecotel Olympia Riviera Resort is a paradise for lovers of water sports. With two private beaches and a range of activities, including kayaking and windsurfing, you’ll never be bored. And, when you’re ready to relax, you can enjoy a treatment at the resort’s spa.

  1. Amada Colossos Resort, Rhodes

The Amada Colossos Resort is a family-friendly all-inclusive that offers everything from water parks to movie theaters. It’s located on the island of Rhodes and makes the perfect base for exploring the island’s many attractions.

  1. Ikos Olivia, Halkidiki

Another Ikos resort makes the list, this time in the region of Halkidiki. The Ikos Olivia offers a range of activities, including tennis and yoga, and boasts a private beach. And, with seven restaurants to choose from, you’ll never go hungry.

  1. Mitsis Blue Domes, Kos

Kos is a popular destination for families, and the Mitsis Blue Domes offers the perfect family-friendly all-inclusive experience. With its own water park and a range of activities, including tennis and archery, there’s something for everyone. And, when you’re ready to relax, you can enjoy a treatment at the resort’s spa.

  1. Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas, Crete

For a truly luxurious all-inclusive experience, book a stay at the Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas. Located on the island of Crete, it offers a range of activities, including water sports and tennis. And, with its own private beach, you’ll never have to leave the resort.

  1. Robinson Club Kyllini Beach, Peloponnese

The Robinson Club Kyllini Beach is located on the western coast of the Peloponnese and offers a range of activities, including horseback riding and beach volleyball. And, with its own private beach and spa, it’s the perfect place to unwind after a day of exploring.

  1. Club Med Kefaluka, Kos

Club Med’s second resort on the list is located on the island of Kos and offers a range of activities, including sailing and yoga. And, with all meals and drinks included, you won’t have to worry about a thing.

  1. Ikos Oceania, Halkidiki

Rounding out the list is the Ikos Oceania, another stunning all-inclusive resort in Halkidiki. With its Michelin-starred restaurant and private beach, it’s the perfect place to indulge in some luxury.

FAQs

Q: What’s included in an all-inclusive resort? A: All-inclusive resorts typically include meals, drinks, and activities. Some may also include spa treatments or excursions.

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts expensive? A: Prices vary depending on the resort and the time of year, but all-inclusive resorts can be more expensive than traditional hotels.

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts family-friendly? A: Many all-inclusive resorts are family-friendly and offer activities and entertainment for children.

Q: Are all-inclusive resorts located near tourist attractions? A: Many all-inclusive resorts are located near tourist attractions, but it’s always a good idea to research your chosen resort’s location before booking.
