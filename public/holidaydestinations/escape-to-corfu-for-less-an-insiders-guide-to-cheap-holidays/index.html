Corfu is a Greek island located in the Ionian Sea, known for its crystal-clear waters, olive groves, and beautiful beaches. If you are looking for a budget-friendly vacation destination, then Corfu is the perfect pick for you. The stunning island of Corfu offers a wide range of affordable holiday options and is an ideal place for budget-conscious travelers. To help you make the most of your budget holiday, we have put together this insider’s guide to cheap holidays in Corfu.

1. When is the best time to visit Corfu?

The best time to visit Corfu is from April to early October. During these months, the weather is warm, and you can enjoy the sun, sea, and the many outdoor activities available on the island. However, if you want to save money, you can visit Corfu during the off-season, which is from November to March, but be prepared for cooler temperatures and the occasional rain.

2. How to get to Corfu on a budget?

To get to Corfu on a budget, look for budget airlines that fly to the island. Ryanair, Wizz Air, and EasyJet are all budget airlines that offer flights to Corfu from major European cities. The earlier you book your flights, the cheaper they will be, so plan in advance. Another way to save money is to choose flights that depart and arrive mid-week instead of the weekends.

3. Where to stay in Corfu?

Corfu offers a wide range of budget-friendly accommodation options, from hostels to budget hotels and self-catering apartments. Sidari, Paleokastritsa, and Kavos are some of the best places to stay in Corfu if you’re on a budget. These areas offer affordable accommodation and are close to the island’s popular tourist destinations.

4. How to get around Corfu on a budget?

If you are on a budget, the best way to get around Corfu is by using public transport. Buses run from the capital city of Corfu to all the major tourist destinations on the island. Another budget-friendly option is to rent a scooter or a bike. You can find rental shops all over the island, and the prices are affordable.

FAQs

1. How much money should I expect to spend on a budget holiday in Corfu? Ans. A budget holiday in Corfu can cost anywhere from €50 to €100 per day, including accommodation, food, and activities.

2. Is it safe to travel to Corfu? Ans. Yes, Corfu is a safe destination to visit. The locals are friendly, and the island is well-developed for tourism.

3. Do I need a visa to visit Corfu? Ans. If you are a citizen of a European Union member state or a member of the European Economic Area, you do not need a visa to visit Corfu. Citizens of certain other countries can stay in Greece for up to 90 days without a visa.

4. What is the currency of Corfu? Ans. The official currency of Corfu (and Greece) is the euro.

5. Is it easy to find vegetarian and vegan food in Corfu? Ans. Yes, it is relatively simple to find vegetarian and vegan food in Corfu. Most restaurants offer vegetarian options, and there are a few vegan-friendly restaurants on the island as well.
