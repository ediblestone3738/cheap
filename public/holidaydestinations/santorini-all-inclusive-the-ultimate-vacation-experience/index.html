Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Santorini All-Inclusive

1. Caldera: The caldera is Santorini’s top attraction, offering visitors breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea and the famous Santorini sunset.

2. Oia Village: Oia is one of the most picturesque villages in Santorini, with its whitewashed houses, blue domed churches, and narrow streets.

3. Red Beach: The Red Beach is a unique red sand beach located near Akrotiri, and is a popular spot for swimming and snorkeling.

4. Akrotiri Archaeological Site: Akrotiri is an ancient city that was destroyed by a volcanic eruption almost 3600 years ago. Visitors can explore the ruins of this ancient city and learn about its history and culture.

5. Wine Tasting: Santorini is famous for its wine, and a wine tasting tour is a must-do activity. Visitors can explore the vineyards and taste the unique flavors of Santorini’s volcanic wines.

6. Perissa Beach: Perissa Beach is a black sand beach with crystal-clear waters, located on the south-east side of Santorini. This beach is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

7. Fira Town: Fira is the capital of Santorini and is a bustling town with a variety of restaurants, bars, and shops. Visitors can explore the town’s narrow streets and alleyways and enjoy the vibrant nightlife.

8. Amoudi Bay: Amoudi Bay is a small fishing village on the northwestern coast of Santorini. Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood and traditional Santorini dishes while admiring the stunning views of the bay.

9. Santo Winery: Santo Winery is a winery located in Pyrgos, offering panoramic views of the island and the Aegean Sea. Visitors can taste a variety of wines while enjoying the stunning view.

10. Nea Kameni: Nea Kameni is a volcanic island located in the middle of the caldera. Visitors can hike to the island’s summit, where they can experience the most amazing views of the Aegean Sea and the surrounding islands.
