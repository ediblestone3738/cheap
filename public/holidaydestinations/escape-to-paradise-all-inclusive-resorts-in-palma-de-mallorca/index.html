[ad_1] Escape to Paradise: All-Inclusive Resorts in Palma de Mallorca

Palma de Mallorca, the largest city of the Balearic Islands, is an idyllic location ideal for a relaxing holiday escape. The city is famous for its crystal-blue Mediterranean waters, sandy beaches, and its vibrant history and culture. Palma de Mallorca is a favorite destination among tourists thanks to its sunny weather, friendly atmosphere, and stunning scenery.

And what better way to enjoy all this than in an all-inclusive resort that caters to all your vacation needs? Palma de Mallorca has an abundance of all-inclusive resorts that offer visitors high-end resort experiences from luxurious amenities, scrumptious restaurants, and thrilling entertainment venues.

In this article, we’ll take a look at the top Escape to Paradise: All-Inclusive Resorts in Palma de Mallorca and where to find them.

1. Iberostar Selection Llaut Palma

The Iberostar Selection Llaut Palma is a modern and luxurious all-inclusive resort featuring 186 rooms, all furnished to high standards with spacious balconies and spectacular sea views. A 24-hour all-inclusive service makes dining at the resort effortless, where guests can enjoy international cuisine or local Mallorcan delicacies in any of the six restaurants accompanied by premium drinks and handpicked wines.

The resort’s amenities include a large outdoor pool, an indoor pool, a fitness center, and a spa. The Iberostar Selection Llaut Palma is only a short walk to the beach, and there are plenty of water-based activities to indulge in, such as snorkeling or paddleboarding.

2. Lindner Golf Resort Portals Nous

Tucked away amidst luscious green hills and facing the shimmering blue ocean, the Lindner Golf Resort Portals Nous is a perfect vacation destination for golf aficionados. This all-inclusive resort is located in the stunning town of Portals Nous, just minutes from a quaint marina and a gentle stroll to the beach.

The resort features 118 superbly-appointed rooms, all of which come with a luxurious balcony or terraces overlooking the nearby golf course. There are two restaurants in the resort, a terrace café, and the Sunset Lounge, a lounge-bar that serves drinks, cocktails, and light dishes.

The Lindner Golf Resort Portals Nous’ extensive spa and wellness center feature a sauna, steam room, indoor pool, and outdoor swimming pool. As for golfers looking to improve their game, there’s an on-site Golf Academy offering private lessons.

3. Iberostar Bahia de Palma

Located on the seafront in Playa de Palma, a six-kilometer stretch of sandy beach is the magnificent Iberostar Bahia de Palma. The resort features 400 air-conditioned rooms, all with heating, satellite TV, and free Wi-Fi.

Pick and choose from a variety of buffet-style options or indulge in a la carte dining in one of the restaurants. The resort has vast gardens and a heated outdoor swimming pool with sun loungers perfect for relaxation. Moreover, the resort’s location in Palma de Mallorca means visitors can venture out to explore the city and partake in shopping or cultural tours.

4. FERGUS Style Palmanova

The FERGUS Style Palmanova is a chic all-inclusive resort for adults, located next to Palmanova’s beachfront. The resort is ideally situated just 20 minutes from Palma city and close to local attractions and leisure activities.

The resort features 93 stylishly designed rooms, some with a private Jacuzzi and fantastic views of the Mediterranean. The hotel’s all-inclusive package allows visitors to have their fill of food and drink in the in-house restaurant or poolside bar.

Guests are offered access to the resort’s rooftop infinity pool, luxury spa, and a gym with stunning views. The FERGUS Style Palmanova is an ideal choice for those looking for a romantic getaway that offers plenty of excitement.

5. H10 Lanzarote Princess

Situated five minutes from Playa Blanca’s beach and the promenade, the H10 Lanzarote Princess is an idyllic resort in southern Lanzarote. The resort’s 410 rooms all come with a balcony, private bathroom, and satellite TV.

The H10 Lanzarote Princess is an all-inclusive resort, boasting a selection of six bars that serve a variety of drinks and refreshments. As for dining options, there is an extensive buffet restaurant serving a selection of international and local cuisine.

The resort also features two outdoor swimming pools, a fitness center, a playground, and a variety of daytime and evening entertainment programs.

FAQs

1. What is the best time of year to visit Palma de Mallorca?

The best time to visit Palma de Mallorca is during the summer months from June to September, where the weather is likely to be the most pleasant with long days of sunshine.

2. Is Palma de Mallorca expensive?

Palma de Mallorca can be an expensive holiday destination, though there are many good value all-inclusive resorts available.

3. What is the local currency in Palma de Mallorca?

The currency used in Palma de Mallorca is the Euro.

4. Is the drinking water safe in Palma de Mallorca?

Tap water in Palma de Mallorca is safe to drink, but bottled water is readily available, making it an ideal choice for travelers wanting to err on the side of caution.

5. Do I need a Visa to visit Palma de Mallorca?

Visitors from most countries, including the USA, Canada, and Australia, do not need a visa to visit Palma de Mallorca for stays of up to 90 days.
