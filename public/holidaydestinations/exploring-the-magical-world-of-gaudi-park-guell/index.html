History of Gaudi Park Guell

Gaudi Park Guell was designed by the renowned Spanish architect Antonio Gaudi in the late 19th century. Gaudi envisioned a park that was a fusion of nature and architecture, and he created a unique and beautiful design that incorporated his signature style. The park was completed in 1914, but it wasn’t until the 1980s that it was officially declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
