The Best Greek Islands to Visit with Your Kids this Summer!

Apr 6, 2023

[ad_1] Greece is known for its beautiful islands that are perfect for a summer holiday. From sandy beaches to crystal clear waters, the Greek islands offer everything that is needed for a fun-filled family vacation. With so many islands to choose from, it can be challenging to decide which ones to visit. That’s why we have rounded up the best Greek islands to visit with your kids this summer.

1. Crete The largest island in Greece, Crete, is a popular destination for families. With its beautiful beaches, interesting history, delicious food, and warm weather, Crete has something for everyone. The island’s family-friendly resorts offer a range of activities and amenities, making it an excellent place for parents to relax while their kids are entertained.

2. Santorini Famous for its beautiful sunsets and stunning views, Santorini is a must-visit destination in Greece. While it may not be the best island for young children, older kids will love exploring the island’s volcanic landscape, taking a boat tour of the caldera, and visiting the island’s many beaches.

3. Naxos Naxos is the largest island in the Cyclades and is perfect for families looking for a relaxing holiday. With its sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and traditional villages, Naxos has plenty to offer. Families can enjoy a range of activities such as hiking, windsurfing, and exploring the island’s many historical sites.

4. Corfu Located in the Ionian Sea, Corfu is a beautiful island with a rich history and culture. Families will love exploring the island’s many beaches, hiking trails, and water parks. Corfu also has several family-friendly resorts that offer a range of activities and amenities.

5. Rhodes Located in the Aegean Sea, Rhodes is a popular island for families. The island is known for its beautiful beaches and interesting historical sites, including the medieval Old Town of Rhodes. Families can also enjoy a range of outdoor activities such as scuba diving, hiking, and horseback riding.

6. Mykonos Known for its cosmopolitan nightlife, Mykonos is also a great destination for families. The island’s beaches are ideal for kids with shallow waters and soft sand, and many of the island’s resorts offer a range of child-friendly amenities.

7. Paros Paros is a great island for families who love watersports. The island’s beaches are perfect for swimming, windsurfing, and kiteboarding. Paros also has several family-friendly resorts that offer a range of activities and amenities.

8. Skiathos Located in the Sporades, Skiathos is a beautiful island with stunning beaches and crystal clear waters. Families can enjoy a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, windsurfing, and snorkeling. The island’s many family-friendly resorts offer a range of activities and amenities, making it an excellent place for a relaxing family holiday.

9. Zakynthos Known for its beautiful beaches and stunning blue caves, Zakynthos is a popular destination for families. The island’s many resorts offer a range of activities and amenities, including water sports, horseback riding, and hiking.

10. Milos Milos is a beautiful island that is ideal for families who love adventure. The island’s rugged landscape is perfect for hiking and exploring, and families can also enjoy a range of water sports such as snorkeling and scuba diving.

FAQs:

Q: What is the best time to visit the Greek islands with kids? A: The best time to visit the Greek islands with kids is between June and September, when the weather is warm and the sea is calm.

Q: What are some kid-friendly activities to do in the Greek islands? A: Some kid-friendly activities to do in the Greek islands include visiting water parks, taking boat tours, visiting historical sites, and enjoying water sports.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions:

1. Acropolis of Athens 2. Caldera in Santorini 3. Palace of Knossos in Crete 4. Old Town of Rhodes 5. Mykonos Windmills 6. Shipwreck Beach in Zakynthos 7. Parthenon in Athens 8. Delphi Theatre 9. Navagio Beach in Zakynthos 10. Lalaria Beach in Skiathos

In conclusion, the Greek islands offer a range of family-friendly activities and beautiful scenery. From the sandy beaches of Naxos to the stunning views of Santorini, there is something for everyone. Whether you want to relax or explore, there is an island that will suit your family’s needs.
