[ad_1] Majorca has long been a favourite destination for travellers looking to get their perfect beach getaway, and for good reason. With its stunning coastline, crystal-clear waters and golden sands, it’s easy to understand why Majorca’s top resorts are so popular. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway, a family holiday or simply some time to relax in the sun, Majorca has something for everyone.

If you’re considering a trip to Majorca and are looking for the perfect beach getaway, then you’re in luck. Here, we take a closer look at the island’s top resorts and explore what makes them so special.

Palma

The vibrant capital of Majorca, Palma is a great place to start your beach getaway. This beautiful city is home to a wide range of cultural attractions, including the iconic Cathedral of Santa Maria, the Royal Palace of La Almudaina and the Bellver Castle. Alongside its rich history and beautiful architecture, Palma also boasts a stunning coastline, with some of the island’s best beaches just a short drive away.

Magaluf

Famous for its lively nightlife and beautiful beaches, Magaluf is a popular destination for visitors looking to let their hair down and have fun. The town’s main beach is a long stretch of golden sand that’s perfect for sunbathing, swimming and watersports. Magaluf is also home to a range of bars, restaurants and nightclubs, making it a great choice for those looking for some fun in the sun.

Alcudia

Located in the northeast of the island, Alcudia is famous for its beautiful historic old town and stunning beaches. The town’s main beach is a long stretch of white sand that’s perfect for families, with a shallow bay that’s perfect for paddling. Alcudia is also home to a range of historic attractions, including the ancient Roman city of Pollentia and the impressive Sant Jaume church.

Calvia

Located in the southwest of the island, Calvia is a popular destination for visitors looking for a more relaxed beach getaway. The town is home to a range of beautiful beaches, including the popular Santa Ponsa beach, which is perfect for families. Calvia is also surrounded by stunning natural scenery, with the stunning Tramuntana Mountains just a short drive away.

FAQs

What’s the best time of year to visit Majorca for a beach getaway?

The best time of year to visit Majorca for a beach getaway is between May and September, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, be prepared for crowds and higher prices during peak season.

What beaches should I visit in Majorca?

Some of the top beaches to visit in Majorca include Cala Millor, Cala d’Or, Playa de Muro, and Es Trenc. Each offers something different, from pristine white sand to crystal-clear waters and great watersport opportunities.

Is Majorca expensive?

Majorca can be expensive, especially during peak season. However, there are many budget-friendly options available, such as small hotels, guesthouses or self-catering apartments.
