Find out which Island in Greece is Best for Families!

Apr 16, 2023

[ad_1] Greece is a popular destination for families who are looking for a mix of beautiful beaches, culture, and history. With so many islands to choose from, it can be difficult to decide which one is best suited for your family. In this article, we will take a look at some of the most family-friendly islands in Greece, and what makes them stand out.

1. Corfu

Corfu is one of the most popular islands in Greece and for good reason. With a mix of Venetian and Byzantine architecture, the island has a unique charm that families will enjoy. The coastline is dotted with beautiful beaches, and the crystal-clear waters are perfect for swimming and snorkeling. Families can also enjoy water sports, hiking, and cycling. For a taste of history and culture, visit the Old Town of Corfu, where you can explore the narrow streets, visit the museums, and sample local food.

2. Crete

The largest island in Greece, Crete has something to offer every family. The island boasts a rich history, with ancient ruins and museums to explore. Crete is also home to some of the best beaches in Greece, including Elafonissi and Balos, which are perfect for families with young children. The island is also known for its delicious cuisine, with an abundance of fresh seafood, vegetables, and cheese.

3. Rhodes

Rhodes is a popular destination for families who are looking for a blend of history and beach fun. The island has a rich history, with ancient ruins, medieval castles, and museums to explore. The coastline is lined with beautiful beaches, and families can enjoy a range of water sports, including windsurfing and jet skiing. For a fun day out, visit the Valley of the Butterflies, where you can see thousands of colorful butterflies in their natural habitat.

4. Zakynthos

Zakynthos is a beautiful island with picturesque beaches and crystal-clear waters. Families can enjoy a range of water sports, including snorkeling and diving, and explore the island’s rocky coastline. For a fun day out, visit the Blue Caves, where you can take a boat ride through the impressive cave formations.

5. Naxos

Naxos is a tranquil island that is perfect for families who are looking for a more relaxed holiday. The island boasts beautiful beaches, including Agios Prokopios and Agia Anna, which are perfect for swimming and sunbathing. Families can also enjoy hiking, cycling, and visiting the island’s historic sites, including the Temple of Apollo and the Venetian Castle.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. When is the best time to visit Greece with the family?

The best time to visit Greece with the family is during the shoulder seasons, between April-June and September-October. This is when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. July and August are the busiest and hottest months, with temperatures often exceeding 30Â°C.

2. What are some of the best beaches in Greece for families?

Some of the best beaches in Greece for families include Elafonissi and Balos in Crete, Agios Prokopios and Agia Anna in Naxos, and Navagio and Laganas in Zakynthos.

3. What are some family-friendly activities to do in Greece?

Family-friendly activities in Greece include visiting ancient ruins and museums, exploring the islands on foot or by bicycle, trying water sports such as snorkeling and diving, and enjoying local cuisine.

Each island has its own unique charm and appeal, so it’s important to choose one that suits your family’s interests and needs. Whether you decide to visit Crete for its ancient ruins and stunning beaches, or Corfu for its Venetian architecture and local cuisine, you’re sure to have an unforgettable family holiday in Greece.
