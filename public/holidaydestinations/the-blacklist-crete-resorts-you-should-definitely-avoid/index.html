However, the island is not immune to resorts that do not live up to the standards that travelers expect. In this article, we have compiled a list of Crete resorts that you should avoid in order to ensure that your vacation is enjoyable and stress-free.

List of Crete Resorts to Avoid

1. The Lyttos Beach Hotel

The Lyttos Beach Hotel is located in Hersonissos, one of the most popular areas of Crete for tourists. However, despite its prime location, the hotel has received numerous negative reviews from travelers. Visitors have reported issues with cleanliness, rude staff, and a lack of basic amenities such as hot water and functioning air conditioning.

2. The Bella Beach Hotel

The Bella Beach Hotel is another Hersonissos resort that travelers should avoid. Guests have complained about outdated rooms, poor hygiene standards, and a lack of customer service. Some visitors have also reported being overcharged for basic services such as laundry and airport transfers.

3. The Creta Maris Beach Resort

Located in the popular tourist town of Hersonissos, the Creta Maris Beach Resort has been the subject of numerous complaints from guests. Travelers have reported issues with poor food quality, unclean rooms, and a lack of basic facilities such as working elevators.

4. The Star Beach Village & Water Park

The Star Beach Village & Water Park is a popular resort among families, but it has received numerous negative reviews from guests. Visitors have complained about the resort’s poor hygiene standards, rude staff, and lack of security measures.

5. The Aquila Porto Rethymno

The Aquila Porto Rethymno is located in the popular town of Rethymno, but it has received numerous negative reviews from travelers. Guests have reported issues with poor customer service, outdated rooms, and a lack of basic amenities such as hot water and functioning air conditioning.

FAQs

Q: Are there any resorts in Crete that are worth visiting? A: Yes, there are many resorts in Crete that offer excellent accommodations and amenities. Some popular options include the Elounda Mare Hotel, the Blue Palace Resort & Spa, and the Daios Cove Luxury Resort & Villas.

Q: What should I do if I’ve already booked a room at one of these resorts? A: If you have already booked a room at a resort on this list, it is best to contact the hotel and express your concerns. You may be able to cancel your reservation or switch to another room at the resort.

Q: How can I avoid booking a room at a bad resort in the future? A: It is always a good idea to read reviews from previous guests before booking a room at a resort. Websites such as TripAdvisor and Booking.com allow travelers to read unbiased reviews from real guests.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Crete

1. The Palace of Knossos - This ancient palace is a must-visit attraction for history buffs.

2. Samaria Gorge - This stunning gorge offers some of the best hiking in Crete.

3. Elafonisi Beach - This hidden gem is known for its pink sand and crystal-clear waters.

4. Heraklion Archaeological Museum - This museum houses countless artifacts from ancient Crete.

5. Sissi - This charming fishing village offers a glimpse into traditional Cretan life.

6. Spinalonga Island - This former leper colony offers stunning views and rich history.

7. The Old Town of Chania - This town is known for its charming Venetian architecture and picturesque harbor.

8. St. Titus Church - This ancient church is a must-visit for those interested in religious history.

9. The Lassithi Plateau - This scenic plateau offers stunning views and a peaceful escape from the tourist crowds.

10. The Venetian Fortress of Rethymno - This fortress offers a glimpse into Crete’s fascinating past and stunning views of the surrounding area.

Conclusion

While Crete is a beautiful and welcoming destination, it is important to be cautious when booking a room at a resort. By avoiding the resorts on our blacklist and doing your research before booking, you can ensure that your vacation is an enjoyable and stress-free experience.
