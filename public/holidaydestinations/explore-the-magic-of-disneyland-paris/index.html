Disneyland Paris is a world-class, family-oriented amusement resort located in Marne-la-Vallée, France. It is the only Disney resort located in Europe and attracts millions of visitors every year from all over the world. Here is a detailed guide of everything you need to know before visiting Disneyland Paris.

Getting There

Disneyland Paris is easily accessible by various modes of transportation. Visitors can travel to the resort via bus or train, with regular shuttle bus services from the two main international airports in Paris: Charles de Gaulle and Orly. If you are traveling by car, the resort is located approximately 32 km east of Paris and is served by the A4 motorway.

Accommodation

Disneyland Paris is a fully integrated resort with a wide range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. The resort features seven themed hotels inspired by popular Disney characters such as Mickey Mouse, Sleeping Beauty, and Toy Story, as well as numerous partner hotels located nearby.

Park Tickets

Park tickets can be purchased online, at the entrance of the park, or via the Disneyland Paris app. The park offers a range of ticket options, including single-day tickets, multi-day tickets, and annual passes. It is recommended to purchase tickets in advance to save money and time.

Park Layout

Disneyland Paris is split into two parks – Disneyland Park and Walt Disney Studios Park – each with its own unique attractions and experiences. Disneyland Park is divided into four themed lands: Fantasyland, Adventureland, Frontierland, and Discoveryland. Each themed land features its own attractions, restaurants, and shops.

Walt Disney Studios Park is dedicated to the art of filmmaking and features themed areas such as Front Lot, Toon Studio, and Production Courtyard. The park is home to popular attractions such as Crush’s Coaster, Ratatouille: The Adventure, and Twilight Zone Tower of Terror.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions

There are a plethora of attractions at Disneyland Paris, ranging from thrilling roller coasters to enchanting shows and parades. Here are the top 10 tourist attractions located in Disneyland Paris:

1. Big Thunder Mountain Railroad – a thrilling roller coaster set in the Wild West.

2. Pirates of the Caribbean – a boat ride adventure through a Caribbean village filled with pirates and hidden treasures.

3. Ratatouille: The Adventure – a 4D ride based on the popular Pixar film, Ratatouille.

4. It’s a Small World – a boat ride that takes visitors on a journey around the world, with hundreds of animatronic dolls singing one of Disney’s most iconic songs.

5. The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror – a thrilling elevator drop ride inspired by Rod Serling’s television series.

6. Disney Illuminations – a spectacular nightly show featuring fireworks, projections, and music.

7. Peter Pan’s Flight – a family-friendly ride that takes visitors on a magical journey to Neverland.

8. Space Mountain: Mission 2 – a thrilling roller coaster set in outer space, featuring loops, drops, and high-speed turns.

9. Star Tours: The Adventures Continue – an interactive 3D flight simulator ride that takes visitors on a journey through the Star Wars galaxy.

10. Indiana Jones et le Temple du Péril – a high-speed roller coaster set in an ancient temple.

FAQs

Q: How much does it cost to visit Disneyland Paris?

A: The cost of tickets varies depending on the type of ticket purchased and the time of year. Single-day tickets start at €89, multi-day tickets start at €189, and annual passes start at €259.

Q: What are the opening hours of Disneyland Paris?

A: The park is typically open from 10 am to 11 pm, but hours may vary depending on the season.

Q: Are there any restrictions on the rides at Disneyland Paris?

A: Yes, some rides have height restrictions and others may not be suitable for visitors with certain medical conditions. It is recommended to check each ride’s restrictions before queuing.

Q: Are there any special events held at Disneyland Paris?

A: Yes, Disneyland Paris hosts various events throughout the year, including Halloween and Christmas-themed celebrations, Star Wars Nights, and numerous others.

Q: Can I bring food and drink into the park?

A: Yes, visitors are allowed to bring food and drink into the park. There are also numerous restaurants and food outlets throughout the park.

Conclusion

Disneyland Paris is a magical destination that promises fun, excitement, and memories that will last a lifetime. With its multitude of attractions, shows, and entertainment, visitors of all ages will find something to enjoy at the park. Whether you’re a Disney aficionado or simply looking for a fun day out, Disneyland Paris is not to be missed. [ad_2]
