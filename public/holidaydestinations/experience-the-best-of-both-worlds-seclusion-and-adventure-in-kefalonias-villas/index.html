Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Kefalonia’s Villas

1. A visit to the Melissani Cave The Melissani Cave is a breathtaking cave that is located near the village of Karavomilos. The cave is filled with crystal clear waters, which create a magical effect when the sunlight penetrates through the ceiling. Visitors can enjoy a boat ride on the turquoise waters and marvel at the different rock formations.

2. Assos Village Assos Village is another must-visit attraction located in Kefalonia’s Villas. The village is a small and picturesque fishing village that is surrounded by lush greenery and beautiful beaches. The village offers visitors an opportunity to explore the traditional houses, enjoy fresh seafood, and enjoy a unique cultural experience.

3. Fiskardo Village Fiskardo Village is a beautiful and historic village located in northern Kefalonia. The village has several Venetian buildings, which adds to its unique charm. Visitors can explore the village, eat at traditional taverns, and enjoy beautiful beaches.

4. Mount Ainos National Park The Mount Ainos National Park is a beautiful and unique destination located in Kefalonia’s Villas. The park is home to the highest mountain on the island, which is Mount Ainos. Visitors can go hiking, camping, and enjoy beautiful vistas of the surrounding forests.

5. Myrtos Beach Myrtos Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Kefalonia’s Villas. The beach is located on the north-west coast of the island and offers visitors crystal clear waters, beautiful scenery, and a peaceful environment. Visitors can swim, sunbathe, and enjoy a picnic on the beautiful sandy beach.

6. Lighthouse of Saint Theodoroi The Lighthouse of Saint Theodoroi is a unique attraction located in Kefalonia’s Villas. The lighthouse is located near the village of Argostoli and offers visitors beautiful views of the surrounding coastline. Visitors can explore the lighthouse and learn about its history.

7. Drogarati Cave The Drogarati Cave is another breathtaking cave located in Kefalonia’s Villas. The cave is filled with stunning stalactites and stalagmites, which create a magical and eerie atmosphere. Visitors can explore the cave and take stunning photographs.

8. Skala Village Skala Village is a beautiful village located in southern Kefalonia. The village is known for its beautiful beaches, which offer visitors crystal clear waters and a serene environment. Visitors can explore the village, enjoy fresh seafood, and enjoy a relaxing day at the beach.

9. Agia Efimia Village Agia Efimia Village is a beautiful and charming village located in Kefalonia’s Villas. The village has several traditional houses and beautiful beaches that offer visitors a unique cultural and natural experience. Visitors can explore the village, eat at traditional taverns, and enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and kayaking.

10. Koutavos Lagoon The Koutavos Lagoon is a beautiful and unique attraction located in Kefalonia’s Villas. The lagoon is located near the town of Argostoli and offers visitors beautiful views of the surrounding coastline. Visitors can take a boat ride on the lagoon and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
