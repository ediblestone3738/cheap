Mykonos is the epitome of a luxurious vacation destination with its pristine sandy beaches, traditional white-washed buildings, crystal-clear seas, and world-renowned nightlife. This Greek island is a favorite among celebrities, jet setters, and luxury-seekers from around the world. And the best way to enjoy your stay here? Opting for an all-inclusive package.

All-inclusive holidays are designed to give you an all-inclusive experience where all your meals, drinks, and activities are included in the package. With an inclusive vacation, you need not worry about the cost of dining, excursions or any hidden fees, which means you can indulge in the luxurious escapes, Mykonos has to offer without worrying about the bill.

Why opt for an all-inclusive vacation in Mykonos?

Travelling to Mykonos for an all-inclusive vacation is a wise choice for numerous reasons. Firstly, the island is famous for its cuisine that includes fresh seafood, Mediterranean rustic dishes, and traditional Greek recipes. With an all-inclusive package, you don’t have to stress about the dining cost as three meals and snacks are included in the package.

Secondly, the island of Mykonos is a hub of activities, from watersports to yoga classes, horse riding to helicopter tours. With an all-inclusive package, you can enjoy activities that come with the package and still have the option of adding extra activities. You can enjoy a relaxing day by the pool, have a complimentary massage to loosen up, or join in on an Island hopping boat tour without worrying about the cost.

Thirdly, indulging in an all-inclusive vacation means you can disconnect from the regular day-to-day activities and let yourself be pampered by top-end services. With an all-inclusive package, all these expenses have been factored into the cost of your stay.

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about All-Inclusive Vacations in Mykonos, along with their answers.

FAQs

Q: WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF AN ALL-INCLUSIVE VACATION PACKAGES?

A: An all-inclusive vacation package means you don’t have to keep track of the cost of food, drinks, accommodation, and some activities. At check-in, everything is taken care of, and this not only makes the vacation stress-free, but also helps you keep to a budget while experiencing luxurious and relaxing moments.

Q: HOW MANY HOURS OF EXCEPTIONAL SERVICES ARE OFFERED WITH THE ALL-INCLUSIVE PACKAGE?

A: The exceptional services offered with an all-inclusive package varies from hotel to hotel. However, typically, 24-hour room service is included in the package, which means you can order whatever food you need even after-hours. The drinks service is unlimited and available all day long.

Q: WHAT ARE THE EXTRA ACTIVITIES I CAN ENJOY WHILE ENJOYING AN ALL-INCLUSIVE VACATION IN MYKONOS?

A: The extra activities include yacht tours, guided city tours, horse-riding, water sports, entrance fees to museums and historical sites, and a range of therapies such as massages and spa treatments.

Q: CAN I UPGRADE ROOMS WHEN BOOKING AN ALL-INCLUSIVE VACATION PACKAGE?

A: Upgrading rooms is possible at most of the hotels in Mykonos offering all-inclusive packages. However, the cost of upgrading differs according to the hotel and room type.

Q: HOW ARE THE ALL-INCLUSIVE PACKAGES PRICED?

A: All-inclusive vacation packages are priced based on several factors such as the length of stay, room type, and extra activities. When booking an all-inclusive package, it’s important to thoroughly read through the package inclusions, check the room type you are booking, and review all the extras included in the package.

Now that we have covered the common questions about all-inclusive packages let’s explore the top ten tourist attractions located on the island.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions Located on Indulge in Luxury: All Inclusive Vacations in Mykonos:

1. Mykonos Windmills: These windmills are unique to the island and set the perfect backdrop for a memorable photo.

2. Little Venice: Little Venice is one of the most picturesque spots of Mykonos town. The charming neighborhood is home to pretty houses that have balconies hovering over the picturesque sea.

3. Psarou Beach: A relaxing day spent soaking under the sun on this sandy beach with excellent restaurants is inevitable for visitors.

4. Panagia Paraportiani: From a distance, the whitewashed church seems crooked and seen from closer, the marvel of its build is astonishing. Its name translates to “Our Lady of the Side Gate” and is one of the most iconic churches on Mykonos Island.

5. Delos Island: An excursion to Delos island is an absolute must! The island of Delos is reputed to be the birthplace of the twin gods Apollo and Artemis, and the excavations uncover an entire city that dates back to 2000 BC.

6. Mykonos Town: This Cycladic town is one of the most attractive in Greece, with dozens of golden-sanded beaches and deep blue sea, lovely shops and restaurants, and meticulously preserved houses and churches.

7. Archaeological Museum of Mykonos: The museum showcases a myriad of artifacts excavated from Delos Island.

8. Little Venice bars: Little Venice bars are some of the most quaint in Greece. They are built to give a feeling of being on water, with their balconies hanging over the sea.

9. Agios Sostis Beach: This beach is one of the most secluded beaches of Mykonos Island, without any organized facilities or restaurants along its coastline.

10. Matoyianni Street: Last on our list is the incredible Matoyianni Street. It winds its way through the heart of Mykonos town, with charming shops along the way.
