When it comes to the dress code on a P&O Mediterranean cruise, it’s all about comfort and style. During the day, you can wear casual clothes, such as shorts, t-shirts, and sandals. 4. Do I need a visa for a P&O Mediterranean cruise?

5. Can I bring my own alcohol onboard a P&O Mediterranean cruise?
