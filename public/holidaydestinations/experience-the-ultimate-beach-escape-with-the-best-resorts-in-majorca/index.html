If you’re looking for the ultimate beach escape, look no further than the beautiful island of Majorca! Located in the Mediterranean Sea, Majorca is one of the most popular destinations in Europe for beach lovers, and it’s not hard to see why. With miles of stunning sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, charming villages, and world-class resorts, Majorca is a true paradise on earth. Here are some of the best resorts in Majorca where you can experience the ultimate beach escape.

1. Belmond La Residencia

Located in the picturesque village of Deià, the Belmond La Residencia is a stunning five-star hotel that’s surrounded by mountains and the Mediterranean Sea. The hotel features luxurious rooms and suites, two outdoor swimming pools, a world-class spa, and two restaurants that serve delicious local and international cuisine. With its romantic ambiance and breathtaking views, the Belmond La Residencia is a perfect choice for couples looking for a romantic getaway.

2. Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa

Perched on the cliffs of the charming village of Port Soller, the Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa offers unparalleled views of the Mediterranean Sea. The hotel features spacious and stylish rooms and suites, two outdoor swimming pools, a world-class spa, and three restaurants that serve delicious local and international cuisine. With its stunning location and luxurious amenities, the Jumeirah Port Soller Hotel & Spa is a perfect choice for a family vacation.

3. Gran Hotel Son Net

Located in the heart of the island, the Gran Hotel Son Net is a restored 17th-century mansion that’s surrounded by a lush garden and the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel features spacious and elegant rooms and suites, an outdoor swimming pool, a spa, and a restaurant that serves delicious local cuisine. With its peaceful location and historic charm, the Gran Hotel Son Net is a perfect choice for a relaxing getaway.

4. St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort

Nestled between the lush greenery and the turquoise waters of the Mediterranean Sea, the St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a luxurious five-star hotel that’s perfect for a romantic escape. The hotel features spacious and elegantly decorated rooms and suites, three outdoor swimming pools, a world-class spa, and three restaurants that serve delicious local and international cuisine. With its romantic setting and luxurious amenities, the St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort is a perfect choice for a honeymoon or anniversary trip.

5. Castell Son Claret

Located in the serene countryside of the island, the Castell Son Claret is a restored 19th-century castle that’s surrounded by a lush garden and the Tramuntana Mountains. The hotel features spacious and beautifully decorated rooms and suites, an outdoor swimming pool, a spa, and two restaurants that serve delicious local and international cuisine. With its stunning location and historic charm, the Castell Son Claret is a perfect choice for a romantic or family getaway.
