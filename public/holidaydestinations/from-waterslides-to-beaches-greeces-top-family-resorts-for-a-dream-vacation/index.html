From waterslides to beaches, here are Greece’s top family resorts for a dream vacation.

1. Asterias Beach Hotel, Crete

The Asterias Beach Hotel is located on the beautiful island of Crete. It overlooks the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea and has a private sandy beach. The resort offers a range of activities for children, including a children’s pool, mini-golf, and a playground.

2. Club Katrin Hotel & Bungalows, Kos

The Club Katrin Hotel & Bungalows is situated on the Greek island of Kos. It has a large swimming pool with water slides and a mini-club for children. There are also tennis courts, a mini-golf course, and a playground.

3. Blue Lagoon Princess, Halkidiki

The Blue Lagoon Princess is located in the stunning Halkidiki peninsula. It has a private beach and a range of activities for children, including a children’s pool, a mini-club, and a playground. There are also tennis courts, a gym, and a spa for adults.

4. Sani Resort, Halkidiki

The Sani Resort is an all-inclusive resort located on the Halkidiki peninsula. It has a private beach, a children’s pool, and a mini-club. There are also a range of activities for adults, including golf, tennis, and hiking.

5. MarBella Corfu, Corfu

The MarBella Corfu is a luxury resort located on the island of Corfu. It has a private beach and a range of activities for children, including a children’s pool, a mini-club, and a playground. There are also tennis courts, a gym, and a spa for adults.

6. Aqua Bay Suites, Zakynthos

FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Greece? A: The best time to visit Greece is from April to June and from September to November, as the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller.

Q: What is the currency used in Greece? A: The currency used in Greece is the Euro.

Q: Do I need a visa to visit Greece? A: If you are a citizen of the European Union or the United States, you do not need a visa to visit Greece for stays up to 90 days.

Q: What is the language spoken in Greece? A: The official language of Greece is Greek.

Q: What are the top tourist attractions in Greece? A: The top tourist attractions in Greece include the Acropolis of Athens, the Parthenon, the Temple of Olympian Zeus, the ancient city of Knossos, the island of Santorini, the Meteora monasteries, the Palace of Knossos, the Temple of Poseidon, and the Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes.
