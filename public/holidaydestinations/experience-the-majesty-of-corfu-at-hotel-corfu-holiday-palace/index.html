Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Corfu

1. Achilleion Palace - built by Empress Elisabeth of Austria, the palace boasts beautiful gardens and neoclassical architecture that showcases an Austro-Hungarian and Greek fusion.

2. Old Town of Corfu - a UNESCO World Heritage site with narrow alleys, beautiful old buildings, quaint cafes, and unique boutiques to explore.

3. Paleokastritsa Beach - one of the most beautiful beaches in the area, the beach is surrounded by a picturesque setting of olive groves, cypresses, and dramatic rock formations.

4. Mount Pantokrator - the highest mountain on the island, with a beautiful panoramic view of the sea and picturesque scenery.

5. Vlacherna Monastery - a small island that is most fa mously known for the monastery that sits atop a rocky outcropping.

6. Canal D’Amour - a scenic spot with unique rock formations that have been created by the sea and the wind for centuries.

7. Saint Spyridon Church - the patron saint of the island, the church is a beautiful example of a Byzantine-style church that has been well-preserved over the centuries.

8. Aqualand Water Park - a fun-filled destination for families with a variety of water rides, swimming pools, and slides.

9. Corfu Golf Club - a beautiful golf course located near the Ropa Valley, surrounded by olive groves and a beautiful view of the sea.

10. Kassiopi Village - a lovely village with a rich history, beautiful scenery, and an idyllic port that is perfect for a lazy afternoon stroll.
