Kos is an island in the Aegean Sea, which is a part of the Dodecanese archipelago in Greece. The island measures 290 square kilometers and has a population of approximately 33,000. For centuries, Kos has been a tourist attraction because of its natural beauty, beaches, climate, and historical sites. Today, the island has become home to several all-inclusive hotels that offer luxury, comfort, and a first-class holiday experience.
