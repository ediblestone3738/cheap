Discovering the Cuisine of New Delhi

New Delhi is a city of vibrant colors, aromas, and flavors. From street food to fine dining, the city offers a wide variety of cuisines that will tantalize the taste buds of all types of travelers. Whether you’re looking for traditional Indian dishes or something more modern, you can find it in New Delhi.

Street Food

If you’re looking for a truly authentic experience, you’ll want to sample the street food of New Delhi. From chaat stands to kathi rolls, there’s something for everyone. Popular street food dishes include pav bhaji, aloo tikki, and bhel puri. Many of these dishes are vegetarian, so they’re perfect for those who are looking to cut down on their meat consumption.

Fine Dining

New Delhi also offers a number of fine dining options. From traditional Indian restaurants to international cuisine, you’ll find something to suit your tastes. Popular restaurants include Bukhara, a north Indian restaurant, and Threesixty, an Italian restaurant.

Regional Cuisine

New Delhi is home to a number of regional cuisines. From the Mughlai cuisine of the north to the coastal flavors of the south, there’s something for everyone. Popular dishes include tandoori chicken, biryani, and dal makhani.

Sweets

No trip to New Delhi is complete without sampling some of the city’s famous sweets. From jalebi to rasmalai, there’s something for everyone. Be sure to try some of the local specialties such as kulfi and kheer.

FAQs

Q: What type of cuisine is available in New Delhi?

A: New Delhi offers a wide variety of cuisines, from street food to fine dining. You can find traditional Indian dishes, regional cuisines, and international cuisine.

Q: Are there any vegetarian options?

A: Yes, there are many vegetarian options available in New Delhi. From street food to fine dining, you’ll find something to suit your tastes.

Q: Are there any sweets I should try?

A: Yes, New Delhi is known for its sweets. Popular dishes include jalebi, rasmalai, kulfi, and kheer.
