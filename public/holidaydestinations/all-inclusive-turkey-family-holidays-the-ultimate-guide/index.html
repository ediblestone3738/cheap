All-Inclusive Turkey Family Holidays: The Ultimate Guide

Introduction

Are you searching for a perfect family holiday destination that has everything you need in one place? Look no further than Turkey! With its stunning beaches, rich culture, and family-friendly resorts, Turkey is the perfect destination for an all-inclusive family holiday.

In this guide, we’ll share with you the best all-inclusive resorts for families in Turkey, things to do with kids, planning tips, and more. So, pack your bags, grab your family, and let’s dive into the ultimate guide to all-inclusive Turkey family holidays!

Best All-Inclusive Resorts for Families in Turkey

  1. Rixos Premium Belek

Located in the beautiful Belek region, the Rixos Premium Belek is a perfect all-inclusive resort for families. The resort features a private beach, a water park, and plenty of activities to keep kids of all ages entertained. The accommodation options range from standard rooms to family suites, ensuring that there is something for everyone.

The dining options at Rixos Premium Belek are diverse, with 8 different restaurants offering a range of cuisines from Turkish to Italian. There are also 9 bars serving drinks and snacks throughout the day.

Activities at the resort include water sports, tennis, mini-golf, and a kids’ club. There’s also an impressive spa and wellness center for adults to enjoy.

The Rixos Premium Belek is a top-rated resort on TripAdvisor, with guests praising its stunning location, family-friendly amenities, and excellent service.

  1. Titanic Beach Lara

The Titanic Beach Lara is another fantastic all-inclusive resort for families in Turkey. The resort is located on a private beach in the Lara region and features a water park with multiple slides and pools. The accommodation options range from standard rooms to family suites, with some rooms featuring direct access to the pool.

Dining options at the Titanic Beach Lara include 6 different restaurants serving a range of international cuisines, as well as a number of bars serving drinks and snacks throughout the day.

Activities at the resort include water sports, tennis, volleyball, and a kids’ club. There’s also a spa and wellness center for adults to enjoy.

The Titanic Beach Lara is highly rated on TripAdvisor, with guests praising its family-friendly amenities, excellent service, and stunning location.

  1. Hillside Beach Club

Located on the beautiful Fethiye coastline, the Hillside Beach Club is a stunning all-inclusive resort perfect for families. The resort features a private beach and three swimming pools, including a kids’ pool with water slides. The accommodation options range from standard rooms to family suites, some with sea views.

Dining options at the Hillside Beach Club are varied, with 4 different restaurants serving a range of international and local cuisine. There are also a number of bars serving drinks and snacks throughout the day.

Activities at the resort include water sports, tennis, volleyball, and a kids’ club. There’s also a spa and wellness center for adults to enjoy.

The Hillside Beach Club is highly rated on TripAdvisor, with guests praising its stunning location, family-friendly amenities, and excellent service.

Things to Do with Kids in Turkey

Turkey is a fantastic destination for families, with plenty of activities and attractions to keep kids of all ages entertained. Here are some of the top things to do with kids in Turkey:

  1. Beaches and Water Activities

Turkey is known for its beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters, making it the perfect destination for water activities. Whether it’s swimming, snorkeling, or kayaking, there’s something for everyone.

  1. Historical Sites and Museums

Turkey has a rich history, and there are plenty of historical sites and museums to explore.

  1. Theme Parks

Turkey has several theme parks that are perfect for families, including the Land of Legends in Antalya and the Vialand Theme Park in Istanbul. These parks offer a range of rides and attractions for kids of all ages.

  1. Turkish Bath

A Turkish bath, or hammam, is a traditional spa experience that is perfect for families. Kids will love the warm marble, steam, and massage, and it’s a great way to relax and unwind together.

  1. Hot Air Balloon Ride

For an unforgettable experience, take a hot air balloon ride over the stunning Cappadocia region. Kids will love the adventure and the breathtaking views.

Planning Tips for an All-Inclusive Turkey Family Holiday

When planning an all-inclusive family holiday to Turkey, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  1. Choose the right resort

Choose a resort that caters specifically to families, with amenities like kids’ clubs, pools, and activities.

  1. Research the location

Research the location of your resort to make sure it’s in a safe and convenient area with easy access to activities and attractions.

  1. Pack appropriately

Be sure to pack plenty of sunscreen, hats, and other protective gear for your kids, as the Turkish sun can be quite intense.

  1. Consider hiring a car

Consider hiring a car to explore the surrounding areas and attractions, as public transportation may not be as convenient.

Conclusion

All-inclusive family holidays to Turkey are the perfect way to relax, unwind, and make memories with your loved ones. From stunning beaches and water activities to historical sites and theme parks, there’s something for everyone in Turkey. So, start planning your all-inclusive family holiday to Turkey today, and get ready to experience the vacation of a lifetime!
