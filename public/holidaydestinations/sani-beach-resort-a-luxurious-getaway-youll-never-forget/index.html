Apr 19, 2023

[ad_1] Nestled on the stunning shores of the Aegean Sea, Sani Beach Resort is a luxurious getaway that blends impeccable service, world-class amenities, and natural beauty to offer guests an unforgettable experience.

From its sprawling pristine beaches to its lushly landscaped gardens, this resort effortlessly combines style and comfort to create a haven of tranquility that will leave you feeling relaxed and rejuvenated.

Whether you’re traveling solo, as a couple or with your entire family, there is something for everyone at Sani Beach Resort. Here’s a closer look at what this enchanting destination has to offer.

Accommodation

Sani Beach Resort boasts an impressive 480 rooms and suites, each elegantly appointed with modern amenities and breathtaking views of the sea or gardens. Choose from standard rooms, family suites, and luxurious villas, each with its own private pool and garden.

Food and Drink

At Sani Beach Resort, dining is an experience in itself, thanks to the vast array of restaurants and bars that cater to diverse tastes and palates. From Greek and Mediterranean cuisine to international flavors, there’s a dining option for every craving.

The resort is home to over 20 restaurants and bars, including the gourmet Ammos Restaurant, the casual al fresco Grill By The Pool, and the family-friendly Ouzerie, where you can savor authentic Greek dishes.

Pools

For those looking to cool off, the resort offers a variety of pools, including its main infinity pool, which overlooks the Aegean Sea. The resort also has a heated indoor pool for those cooler months or for guests looking for a more private experience.

Activities

With a wealth of activities on offer, guests can stay as active or as relaxed as they choose. From an array of water sports, such as jet skiing and windsurfing, to tennis courts, mini-golf, and a state-of-the-art wellness center, there’s something for everyone at this luxurious resort.

For children, there are numerous activities such as a kids club, a snorkeling academy, and a football academy. Teens also have their own space with an exclusive teen zone featuring a range of activities and events.

Spa

For total relaxation, the MySpa at Sani Beach Resort offers an extensive range of treatments that will leave you feeling pampered from head to toe. The spa features a sauna, steam room, and an indoor heated pool for ultimate relaxation.
