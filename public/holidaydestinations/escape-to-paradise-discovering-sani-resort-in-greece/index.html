Greece is a heaven on earth with magnificent landscapes, clear waters, and a relaxed atmosphere. It’s a place where you can unwind, recharge and reconnect with nature. Sani Resort, located in the Halkidiki region, is a premium destination that offers breathtaking vistas of the Aegean Sea, stunning beaches, and luscious forests. Here’s a detailed travel guide on Escape to Paradise: Discovering Sani Resort in Greece.

Getting to Sani Resort

Sani Resort is located in Halkidiki, Northern Greece, which is the perfect destination to escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. If you’re coming from Athens, the nearest airport is Thessaloniki. From Thessaloniki, you can take a private transfer or rent a car to reach Sani Resort, which is approximately 50 miles away.

Accommodation

Sani Resort offers unique, luxurious accommodation options that cater to the diverse needs of guests. The resort has several hotels that are themed with lively colors and designs that embody a classic Mediterranean vibe.

Sani Beach

Sani Beach is the perfect oasis for travelers looking for a relaxing stay in Greece. It boasts panoramic views of the sea, private sandy beaches, and beautifully landscaped gardens. There are 395 rooms spread across eight floors, all of which are spacious and well-appointed with modern amenities. Sani Beach offers different room categories, including deluxe garden rooms, junior suites, and family suites, among others.

Sani Dunes

Sani Dunes is a new addition to the Sani Resort, offering luxurious, elegantly furnished guestrooms with world-class amenities. The rooms are spacious and offer guests breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. The hotel has 136 rooms and suites, all designed to embody a classic modern Mediterranean vibe.

Sani Club

Sani Club offers a luxurious holiday experience with its 212 contemporary-styled rooms and suites. The rooms are spacious and well-appointed with all modern amenities. Sani Club offers several room categories, including deluxe rooms, junior suites, and family suites.

Sani Asterias

Sani Asterias is a boutique hotel that offers a unique stay experience. This small and intimate hotel has only 48 suites, each designed with elegance and sophistication. The hotel’s suites come with a private pool, a terrace, and stunning views of the Aegean sea.

Dining

Sani Resort offers its guests over 24 dine-out options, including restaurants, bars, and delis. The restaurants offer cuisines from around the world, including Greek, Italian, French, Asian, and Turkish cuisine. Beverages are available at the resort’s bars, which offer a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

Some of the best restaurants in Sani Resort include:

- Water Restaurant - serves fresh seafood with unparalleled ocean views. - Katsu Restaurant - a Japanese-inspired restaurant that offers an extensive selection of sushi and sashimi. - Psaroyannos Ouzeri - offers traditional Greek taverna-style dining experiences.

Activities

Sani Resort offers an array of activities to keep visitors entertained throughout their stay. The resort offers on-site tennis courts, beach volleyball courts, and fitness centers that are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment. Additionally, the resort offers activities like:

- Watersports - you can enjoy paddle boarding or take a scuba diving course - Hiking and Biking - you can explore the surrounding hills and forests on the resort’s hiking trails or rent a bike to cycle through the resort’s paths. - Yacht Excursion - you can hire a yacht to explore the beauty of the Aegean Sea.

FAQs

Q. Is Sani Resort child-friendly? A. Yes, Sani Resort offers a variety of child-friendly activities and facilities, including the Sani Creche, Mini Club, and Teen Club programs.

Q. Do I need to be vaccinated to visit Greece? A. Currently, Greece requires visitors to present proof of vaccination to enter the country.

Q. Is Sani Beach wheelchair accessible? A. Yes, Sani Beach offers wheelchair access and rooms equipped with accessible features.

Q. Does Sani Resort offer Wi-Fi? A. Yes, Sani Resort offers complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the resort.

Q. Does Sani Resort offer airport transfers? A. Yes, Sani Resort offers airport transfers at an additional cost.
