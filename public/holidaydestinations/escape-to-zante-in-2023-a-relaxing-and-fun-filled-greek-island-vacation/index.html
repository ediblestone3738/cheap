FAQs

Q. When is the best time to visit Zante? A. The best time to visit Zante is between May and September, when the weather is warm and sunny.

Q. How do I get to Zante? A. You can fly to Zante from Athens or other major European cities. You can also take a ferry from the mainland or other islands in the Ionian Sea.

Q. What is the currency in Zante? A. The currency in Zante is the Euro.

Q. Do I need a visa to visit Zante? A. If you are a citizen of the EU, you do not need a visa to visit Zante. If you are from another country, check with the Greek Embassy in your country.

Q. What language do they speak in Zante? A. The official language in Zante is Greek, but English is widely spoken, especially in tourist areas.

Top 10 Tourist Attractions in Escape to Zante in 2023

1. Navagio Beach Also known as Shipwreck Beach, Navagio Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Zante. It is home to a shipwreck that washed up on the beach in 1980 and has since become a popular tourist attraction.

2. Blue Caves The Blue Caves are a network of sea caves on the north-east coast of Zante. The caves are famous for their crystal clear waters and the blue reflection caused by the sunlight.

3. Marathonisi Island Marathonisi Island is a small uninhabited island located off the southern coast of Zante. The island is known for its beautiful beaches and crystal clear waters, and is a popular spot for snorkeling.

4. Keri Caves Keri Caves are a network of sea caves in the south-west of Zante. The caves are a popular spot for scuba diving and snorkeling, and are home to a variety of marine life.

5. Solomos Museum The Solomos Museum is located in Zakynthos Town and is dedicated to the life and work of the poet Dionysios Solomos, who wrote the Greek national anthem.

6. Keri Lighthouse Located on the southern tip of Zante, the Keri Lighthouse is a popular spot for watching the sunset. The lighthouse was built in 1925 and offers panoramic views of the surrounding area.

7. Tsilivi Water Park Tsilivi Water Park is a popular water park located on the east coast of Zante. It offers a variety of water slides, pools and other attractions for all ages.

8. Zakynthos Town Zakynthos Town is the capital and main port of Zante. It is a charming town with narrow streets, traditional tavernas and beautiful architecture.

9. Blue Caves Snorkeling Trip Take a snorkeling trip to the Blue Caves and explore the underwater world of Zante. The tour includes snorkeling gear and a guide to show you the best places to snorkel.

10. Alykes Beach Alykes Beach is a long sandy beach located on the north-east coast of Zante. It is a popular spot for sunbathing and swimming, and offers a variety of water sports activities.
