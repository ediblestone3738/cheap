What’s the Cheapest Way to Book a Hotel? 15 Tips to Help You Save Money

Traveling can be expensive, but there are plenty of ways to save money on your hotel bookings. From using credit cards with travel cash back perks to booking in advance with free cancellation, there are many ways to get the best deal on your hotel stay. Here are 15 tips to help you book the cheapest hotels and save money on your next trip.

1. Use Credit Cards With Huge Travel Cash Back Perks

Using a credit card with travel cash back perks is one of the best ways to save money on your hotel bookings. Many credit cards offer rewards points or cash back on travel purchases, so make sure to check the details of your card before booking. You can often get up to 5% cash back on your hotel bookings, which can add up to big savings over time.

2. Sign Up To Hotel Booking Engines Loyalty / Rewards Programs

Many hotel booking engines, such as Expedia and Booking.com, offer loyalty and rewards programs that can help you save money on your hotel bookings. Signing up for these programs can get you access to exclusive discounts and deals, as well as points that can be used for future bookings.

3. Download Hotel Booking Smartphone Apps

Downloading hotel booking apps is another great way to save money on your hotel bookings. Many apps offer exclusive discounts and deals that you won’t find anywhere else. Plus, you can often get access to last-minute deals and discounts that can help you save even more.

4. Check Prices Directly With Hotels

Checking prices directly with hotels is another great way to save money on your hotel bookings. Many hotels offer discounts and deals that you won’t find on hotel booking engines, so it’s always worth checking directly with the hotel before booking.

5. Book In Advance With Free Cancellation

Booking in advance with free cancellation is one of the best ways to save money on your hotel bookings. Many hotels offer discounts for booking in advance, and if you book with a free cancellation policy, you can always cancel your booking if you find a better deal.

6. Book In Advance With Non-Refundable Rate

Booking in advance with a non-refundable rate is another great way to save money on your hotel bookings. Many hotels offer discounts for booking in advance, and if you book with a non-refundable rate, you can often get an even bigger discount.

7. Look For Special Offers

Look for special offers when booking your hotel. Many hotels offer discounts for booking multiple nights, as well as special offers for certain dates or times of year. Make sure to check the hotel’s website or contact them directly to find out about any special offers.

8. Look For Package Deals

Look for package deals when booking your hotel. Many hotels offer package deals that include flights, car rentals, and other travel services. These package deals can often save you money on your hotel bookings, so make sure to check for them before booking.

9. Book During Off-Peak Times

Booking during off-peak times is another great way to save money on your hotel bookings. Many hotels offer discounts for booking during off-peak times, such as weekdays or during the winter months.

10. Look For Last-Minute Deals

Look for last-minute deals when booking your hotel. Many hotels offer discounts for last-minute bookings, so make sure to check for them before booking.

11. Look For Coupon Codes

Look for coupon codes when booking your hotel. Many hotels offer discounts for booking with a coupon code, so make sure to check for them before booking.

12. Use Hotel Comparison Sites

Use hotel comparison sites to compare prices and find the best deals. Many hotel comparison sites, such as Hotels.com and Trivago, offer discounts and deals that you won’t find anywhere else.

13. Look For Discounts For Groups

Look for discounts for groups when booking your hotel. Many hotels offer discounts for groups, so make sure to check for them before booking.

14. Look For Discounts For Military Personnel

Look for discounts for military personnel when booking your hotel. Many hotels offer discounts for military personnel, so make sure to check for them before booking.

15. Book On The Right Day

Booking on the right day can help you save money on your hotel bookings. Many hotels offer discounts for booking on certain days of the week, so make sure to check for them before booking.

These are just a few of the many ways to save money on your hotel bookings. By following these tips, you can make sure you get the best deal on your next hotel stay. Happy travels!
