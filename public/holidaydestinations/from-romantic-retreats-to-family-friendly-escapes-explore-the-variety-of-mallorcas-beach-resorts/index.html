But beyond the beaches, Mallorca has so much more to offer. From charming, romantic retreats to family-friendly escapes, there’s a beach resort for everyone on this beautiful Balearic Island.

Romantic Retreats

Cala Ratjada is the perfect destination for couples looking for a romantic getaway. The resort town is located on the northeastern coast of Mallorca and boasts stunning beaches, crystal clear water, and plenty of romantic activities for couples to enjoy. Take a stroll along the harbor, watch the sunset from the lighthouse, or explore the town’s quaint streets and shops. There are plenty of romantic restaurants and bars to choose from, and the nightlife is a perfect mix of relaxed and lively.

Port de Sóller is another romantic destination that’s popular with couples. The resort town is located on the western coast of the island and is surrounded by mountains and water. The beach is secluded and quiet, perfect for couples who want to enjoy some quality time together. Take a romantic stroll along the promenade, have a leisurely lunch at one of the waterfront restaurants, or take a dip in the clear blue water.

Family-Friendly Escapes

Alcúdia is a popular destination for families thanks to its long, sandy beach and abundance of activities for children. The resort town is located on the northeastern coast of Mallorca and has a wide range of attractions, including a water park, go-karting track, and mini-golf course. There are plenty of family-friendly restaurants and shops, and the nightlife is relatively quiet, making it a great place for families with young children.

Cala Millor is another family-friendly escape that’s popular with tourists. The resort town is located on the eastern coast of Mallorca and has a long, sandy beach that’s perfect for families. The beach has plenty of activities for children, including water sports and beach games. There are also plenty of restaurants and shops that cater to families, and the nightlife is lively but not too rowdy.

FAQs

Q: What is the water temperature in Mallorca? A: The water temperature in Mallorca varies depending on the season. In the summer months, the water temperature can reach up to 26°C (79°F), while in the winter months, it can drop to around 13°C (55°F).
